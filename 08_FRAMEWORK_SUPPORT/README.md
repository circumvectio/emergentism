This lane translates and stress-tests Emergentism. Source authority remains in
the kernel, ontology, formal system, axiology, and methodology owners.

## Non-negotiable boundaries

- Evidence does not transfer merely because two domains share a shape.
- Correspondence is not derivation.
- A selected seven-row lens is not a universal census of reality.
- Models may recommend; they do not manufacture authorization or consequences.
- Archive history remains legible but does not compete with active owners.
- Emergentism has no venture or product premise.
