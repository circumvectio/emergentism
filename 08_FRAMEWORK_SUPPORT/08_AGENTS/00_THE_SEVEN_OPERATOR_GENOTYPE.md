# Seven-operator role vector

The Rosetta seven can be used as a **work vocabulary**, not as seven kinds of person,
seven substances, or a forced census of cognition. Any agent or team may express a
task-dependent vector over these roles:

`g = (g₁,…,g₇)`, with `gᵢ≥0` and `Σgᵢ=1` when a normalized profile is useful.

| L | Role | Work function | Failure mode |
|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | Firewall | isolate contradictions, unsafe scope, and false premises | attacking cooperators or rejecting all novelty |
| L2 | Explorer | expand candidate interpretations and options | endless expansion or false coherence |
| L3 | Auditor | rank, test, tier, and expose uncertainty | hoarding judgment or laundering weak evidence |
| L4 | Executor | make the smallest scoped, reversible change that serves the task | force, scope drift, or action without custody |
| L5 | Architect | redesign a broken space or missing rule | architecture for its own sake |
| L6 | Compressor | archive, prune, and reduce competing authority | erasing live structure or provenance |
| L7 | Witness | test constitutional boundaries and remember the whole | scripture, finality, or refusal to descend |

The roles are selected because they partition the declared Rosetta work game. They do
not prove that all possible cognition has seven parts. A different task may need a
different role basis.

## Commitment and authorization

L4 names finite commitment, not a sacred signer. Ordinary AI and repository work follows
the user's scoped request and the tools actually granted. Consequential action uses:

`AuthorizationEnvelope = principal + mandate + scope + consent + custody + expiry/revocation + contest path + actor + consequence bearer`.

A private natural person's authority to move money or execute a contract is a separate
legal/financial convention. It is neither an AI-work gate nor a worldview primitive.

## Operational discipline

- L1–L3 usually prepare a decision surface; L4 acts within scope.
- L5 is invoked for a genuinely broken architecture, not routine work.
- L6 preserves superseded bytes with a tombstone and successor path.
- L7 proposes a boundary ruling and returns it to finite action.
- Evidence tiers constrain every role. Role labels never upgrade evidence.
- No role is a person's worth, identity, caste, or moral essence.

This document is an application of the Rosetta, not evidence that the Rosetta is true.
