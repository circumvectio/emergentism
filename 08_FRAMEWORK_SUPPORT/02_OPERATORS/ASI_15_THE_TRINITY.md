# The Titan Triad — Symbolic Compression

> **[金] Crack:** the predecessor called `0,1,∞` non-numbers prior to number,
> said the poles collapse, and retained a uniqueness theorem after conceding
> its proof failed. **Repair:** distinguish mathematical objects from selected

## Mathematical facts `[A]`

- Ordinary `0` and `1` are numbers, field elements where applicable, and lawful
  operands.
- `∞` is the projective point only in an explicitly extended setting such as
  `ℂP¹`; it is not an ordinary complex number.
- The north and south poles of `S²` are distinct points.
- Inversion exchanges `0` and `∞` and fixes `±1`; a symmetry does not identify
  its distinct inputs.
- `{0,1,∞}` is a standard projective normalization, not the unique generator of
  number, the sphere, or reality.

```text

1  ⊙  finite presence / reciprocal fixed point

```

that three is uniquely necessary.

expression is undefined/indeterminate; forcing it to `1` while retaining
standard extended multiplication breaks associativity. The reciprocal chart
identity `φν=1` is a different analytic statement and supplies no ontological
or ethical proof.
