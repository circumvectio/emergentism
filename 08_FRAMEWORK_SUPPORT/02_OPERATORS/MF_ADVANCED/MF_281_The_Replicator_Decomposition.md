# MF-281: THE REPLICATOR DECOMPOSITION

> **Legacy numbering note:** MF-330 carries the later Force Chain number. This
> stable intake path remains for provenance; new claims must defer to active
> semantic owners.

> **[金] Kintsugi seam.** The former paper claimed two multiplicative
> replicator stacks derive `P_node=ΦV`, located informational replicators in D5,
> and identified the product with technology and electromagnetic “fire.” None
> of those steps was derived. This successor keeps the decomposition as a
> comparative, falsifiable model.

## 1. Two separate modeling choices

### Selected stack `[I]`

```text
Genotype → Epigenotype → Phenotype → Extended Phenotype
         → Memotype → Egregoreotype
```

This is a chosen six-layer analysis, not a necessary sequence in every system.
The layers may overlap, interact bidirectionally, or fail to instantiate.

### Selected node aggregator `[I/C]`

For normalized nonnegative factors, Emergentism declares:

```text
P_node := C×(Φ,V) = ΦV
```

inside a broader monotone conjunctive family satisfying
`C(0,V)=C(Φ,0)=0`. Product, minimum, harmonic mean, and Cobb–Douglas forms are
not interchangeable and can rank cases differently. The reciprocal-chart
identity `φν=1` does not force the node product.

The stack does not derive the aggregator, and the aggregator does not prove the
stack.

## 2. Correct modality

All concrete genes, epigenetic marks, organisms, artifacts, environments,
texts, rules, institutions, model tokens, ranking events, actions, and receipts
are D4 actual.

Some D4 actual traces encode or represent D5 merely possible alternatives. A
memotype is therefore not “in D5”; it is an actual carrier-pattern whose
content may concern possible worlds.

```text
MemotypeTrace:D4(actual) ── represents ──▶ Alternatives:D5(possible)
SelectionEvent:D4(actual) ── reweighted-by ──▶ Trace:D4(actual)
```

## 3. Egregoreotype as candidate trace

The last layer qualifies only when a persistent shared D4 trace:

1. survives carrier turnover;
2. measurably reweights later selection under intervention;
3. exhibits recurrent objective-like bias;
4. carries visible substrate costs with named payers and beneficiaries.

Together with persistence, these are the five mandatory conditions. They do
not presume consciousness, personhood, an autonomous objective, or literal
energy feeding. The collective may remain fully substrate-dependent.

## 4. A bounded decomposition hypothesis `[C]`

A study may ask whether measured features usefully separate into two
operational families:

- `V`: current usable means such as bodily capacity, resources, skills,
  access, and time;
- `Φ`: current model coherence and foresight relevant to ranking alternatives.

That is a measurement hypothesis, not an ontological division into material
and informational hemispheres. Environment and extended phenotype may affect
both families; this is an interaction to model, not a reason to multiply the
same variable twice.

## 5. Prospective comparison

An independently governed, preregistered test should:

1. define validated indicators without reusing retired study data as evidence;
2. compare one-, two-, and higher-factor measurement models;
3. test product, minimum, harmonic, Cobb–Douglas, and additive aggregators;
4. publish ranking counterexamples and calibration error;
5. ablate proposed Egregoreotype traces and measure later selections;
6. separate exploratory model choice from confirmatory evaluation.

Evidence for two latent factors would not by itself prove a product. Product
superiority on one outcome would not prove a universal ontology.

## 6. Kill criteria

The proposal fails or narrows if:

- the proposed indicators do not form stable, discriminable factors;
- a rival aggregator predicts as well or better out of sample;
- high values on one factor reliably compensate for near-zero on the other;
- the stack adds no intervention-sensitive prediction beyond lower-level
  variables;
- the Egregoreotype candidate fails persistence, turnover, intervention,
  recurrence, or cost tests;
- any result depends on treating D5 content as an actual entity.

## 7. What survives

The replicator decomposition is a research grammar for locating carriers,
traces, represented alternatives, selection effects, and costs. It is not a
derivation of `P_node`, a proof of strong emergence, or a physical force map.
