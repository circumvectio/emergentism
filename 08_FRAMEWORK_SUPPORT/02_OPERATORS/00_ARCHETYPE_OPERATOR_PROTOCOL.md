## 1. Type boundary

An operator token names an **actual D4 action or posture**. An actual agent may
use a D4 model token to represent and rank **D5 merely possible** alternatives.
Nothing here is an entity in D5. An archetype name never creates authorization,
performs an act, or manufactures an outcome.

The chart `phi*nu=1` is analytic on its declared open domain. `P_node:=Phi*V`
is a selected normalized conjunctive model. Neither fact yields operator names,
sevenfold necessity, moral status, an attractor, or ontology.

## 3. Description is not judgment

`etaObserved` may report a declared extraction relation. `etaObserved=0` is
necessary but insufficient for strict syntropy and cannot certify consent,
fairness, stability, or hidden-third-party safety.

Bearer-complete Justice is assessed independently:

- identify every materially affected individual and sustaining whole;
- expose payer, beneficiary, custody, and irreversible cost;
- validate principal, mandate, scope, actor, consent, revocation, and contest;
- distinguish commitment, performed action, and environmental outcome receipts;
- keep reversibility and exit available wherever physically and legally possible;
- do not offset a harmed bearer with aggregate gain.

A giving-labeled move can coerce, create dependency, or destroy its payer. A
taking-labeled move can be a consented exchange or a proportionate defense. The
name never decides. Strict syntropy requires both focal durable potentials to
rise under the complete Justice envelope. Voluntary sacrifice is a separate
costly class, never syntropy by relabeling.

## 4. Demon and god analogy

The demon/god polarity is an `[I]` comparison between short-horizon ego-potential
maxing and durable collective-potential maxing. It is useful only if the bearer
ledger remains visible. A collective that demands an individual's destruction
is not made divine by calling itself the whole; an individual is not evil for
surviving. The comparison dies when it becomes a person-ranking or excuses
unreceipted harm.

## 5. Collective traces

Repeated coordination can create an actual D4 trace field. Call it an
Egregoreotype candidate only if persistence, carrier turnover, intervention
effect, recurrent objective-like bias, and visible substrate costs are all
observed. The label presumes neither consciousness nor personhood. Candidacy is
descriptive; Justice remains a separate normative test.

Ritual may be studied as repeated synchronization. Sacrifice must always name
who pays, whether consent is informed and revocable, what becomes irreversible,
who benefits, and whose option cone contracts.

## 6. Deployment receipt

Before using any operator label, record:

```text
actual situation -> modeled alternatives -> authorization assessment
-> commitment receipt -> performed action receipt
-> environmental outcome receipt -> map/selector update
```

If the label hides a bearer, substitutes myth for evidence, or transfers proof
between domains, discard the label and describe the action plainly.

## Kill criteria

Retire or narrow this protocol if its users cannot reliably distinguish actions
from persons, D4 tokens from D5 content, measurement from morality, or selected
vocabulary from empirical law; or if a smaller language supports better
prediction and accountability.
