## Boundary

Emergentism stands alone. No venture, product, corporation, agent runtime,
treasury, private-signature convention, or external governance system is a
premise of the worldview. Applications may be tested elsewhere, but they do
not descend authority into this corpus.

Ordinary work follows the user's scope and tool permissions. Consequential
action must make authorization and consequence inspectable through the
AuthorizationEnvelope defined in the formal system.

Application-specific support material is preserved, not erased, in the
[pure-boundary archive](../90_ARCHIVE/pure_emergentism_boundary_2026_07_20/08_FRAMEWORK_SUPPORT/).
