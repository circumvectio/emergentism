# Paradox dissolutions

These documents test whether a paradox weakens when its hidden type error,
register shift, observer boundary, or missing degree of freedom is made explicit.
They are lenses, not automatic proofs that an ancient problem has been solved.

## Reading rule

For each proposed dissolution identify:

1. the original paradox in its strongest form;
2. the exact premise or type distinction being changed;
3. what follows inside the Emergentist model;
4. what remains unresolved outside it;
5. the evidence tier and kill criterion.

The D3 quantum-state / D4 record calculus is load-bearing only for that selected
register crosswalk. Quantum interpretations and Bloch–Burri correspondences are
removable and non-load-bearing. Everett is a no-collapse relative-state
formulation; actualization language is interpretation-specific; neither is an
added spacetime dimension.
