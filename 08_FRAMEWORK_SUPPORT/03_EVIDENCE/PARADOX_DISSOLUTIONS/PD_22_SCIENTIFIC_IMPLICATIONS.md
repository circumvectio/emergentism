# PD-22 — Scientific Implications After the D3 Repair

## 1. What remains unsolved

Quantum theory and general relativity are extraordinarily successful in their
tested domains, and a complete empirically confirmed theory of quantum gravity
remains open. Emergentism does not solve that problem, derive a measurement
interpretation, or show that the theories occupy different literal dimensions.

## 2. The only licensed register crosswalk

- **D3 — quantum state:** a density operator `ρ` together with a declared
  measurement context supplies Born probability distributions. Valid D3 states
  may carry both position and momentum distributions; uncertainty follows from
  noncommuting observables.
- **D4 — causal actuality:** laboratories, fields, apparatuses, interactions,
  attempted measurements, outcomes, and durable records are actual physical
  carriers.
- **D5 — counterfactual possibility:** an actual D4 agent may represent, rank,
  and select among possible contents. D5 is not a synonym for quantum
  superposition.

The operational D3→D4 interface `μ₃` distinguishes probability assignment from
an actual run and record. It is formally reducible as a stochastic interface.
It is not wave-function collapse, an interpretation of measurement, or evidence
for ontic strong emergence. `μ₄` is the separate D4→D5 counterfactual-capacity
hypothesis; its evidence field remains unassessed.

General relativity describes actual spacetime structure and therefore has D4
applications. Quantum theory also describes actual physical systems through
D3 states and D4 preparations and records. The registers type aspects of an
account; they do not partition physics into noninteracting worlds.

## 3. What the scaffold can contribute

The scaffold offers a type checker:

1. do not confuse a probability distribution with a realized record;
2. do not let a D4 token inherit the modality of the D5 possible content it
   represents;
3. do not mistake an interpretation-specific actualization story for an extra
   spacetime dimension; and
4. do not infer a physical bridge law from a Rosetta correspondence.

This may clarify questions. It becomes scientific only if it yields a novel,
preregistered discriminator that survives comparison with established accounts.
No such discriminator is presently calibrated.

## 4. Boundary and kill criteria

The note fails if it claims that register assignment replaces the mathematical
work of quantum gravity, if `μ₃` is identified with fundamental collapse, or if
standard quantum states with momentum distributions are rejected. A future
successful theory of quantum gravity would constrain applications of the
scaffold but would not, by itself, confirm or refute the scaffold's bookkeeping
use.
