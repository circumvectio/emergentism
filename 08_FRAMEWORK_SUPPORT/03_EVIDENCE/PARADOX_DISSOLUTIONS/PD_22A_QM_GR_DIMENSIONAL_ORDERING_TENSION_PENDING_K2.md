# PD_22A · Where does quantum sit relative to gravity?

> This note does not resolve quantum gravity, and it corrects any reading that
> says the framework does. PD_22 already reframes the QM/GR *incompatibility* as a
> possible dimensional level-confusion `[I/C]`, with falsifiers and no claimed
> observable. This note flags that canon is **internally inconsistent about the
> ordering itself**, and offers a candidate fix on the register pattern this

## 1. The contradiction (hand-verified)

- **PD_22 (`:46–47`, `:57–58`):** GR = **D4** (actuality); QM = **D5** (possibility);
  collapse = the μ-limit, **D5→D4**. Here **quantum sits *above* gravity.**
- **Live K2 intuition (2026-07-12):** "quantum is a 3D object; gravity only starts
  existing at 4D." Here **quantum sits *below* gravity.**
- Both agree **gravity = D4.** They disagree, by two rungs, on QM.

This is not a paraphrase drift; it is two genuine, opposite orderings, each with
real physics behind it (below).

## 2. Both readings are motivated

- **Quantum-above-gravity (PD_22):** the *measurement/collapse* intuition — QM is
  the space of possibility (D5), GR the actualized spacetime (D4), and collapse
  descends possibility into actuality (D5→D4 = χ-commitment). `[I/C]`
- **Gravity-above-quantum (K2):** the *emergent-gravity / holography* intuition —
  spacetime and gravity are not fundamental but **emerge** from a deeper quantum
  substrate (Jacobson's derivation of Einstein's equations from horizon
  thermodynamics; Verlinde/Padmanabhan entropic gravity; and above all AdS/CFT,
  where a boundary **quantum** theory *without* gravity, in `D` dimensions,
  generates a bulk **gravity** theory in `D+1` — quantum below, gravity one
  dimension up). `[C]`

Neither is crank; they are two live, respected directions. The framework has been
speaking both without noticing — the same fault, exactly, that receipts 105–107
found and fixed for D4/D5.

## 3. Candidate reconciliation — two registers, gravity as the pivot (`[C]`, not asserted)

The reconciliation is *grounded, not manufactured*: canon **already** places
quantum superposition at two rungs. **Rule 3's D4 row** reads D4 as "time appears"
*and* "**the potential register (pre-collapse superposition)**"; **PD_22** reads
QM=possibility as **D5**. That is the register-indexing of receipts 105–107
("possibility" is D4 on the emergence axis, D5 on the action axis) showing up in
the physics register.

So "quantum" may be **two objects**:

| "Quantum" as | Register / axis | Rung | Physics reading |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Substrate** — pre-temporal gauge/vacuum, before a time-arrow | emergence / μ (up) | **below D4** | emergent gravity: substrate → spacetime crystallizes |
| **Superposition** — the space of what-could-be | action / χ (down) | **D5** | measurement: possibility → collapses to actuality |

And **gravity = D4 is the pivot both readings share** — the rung where time and
causal direction *appear*. It emerges *up* from the substrate; it is the
collapse-target *down* from superposition. Quantum is below it as substrate and
above it as possibility. One structure, two registers — the D4/D5 axis-fusion
generalized.
