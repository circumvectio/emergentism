## 2. Mirror Score

| Pair | Score | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| L3/L5 | strong | Market price and planned optimization are the canonical decentralized/centralized allocation mirror. |
| L2/L6 | partial | Both are non-price forms, but one binds through personal obligation and the other releases through structural commons. |
| L1/L7 | weak | Both are outside normal accounting, but scarcity extraction and transcendent gift are not functional inverses without framework reading. |
| L4 | partial / useful | Mechanism design is a real mediating field, but its L4 placement is selected by the framework. |

---

## 3. Failed / Partial Appendix

- **L7 is the weakest cell.** It should never be used as evidence for historical economic evolution.
- **L1 risks overclaim.** Subsistence is not always extraction. Some foraging economies are high-cooperation systems under ecological constraint.
- **L6 may belong partly to L4.** Ostrom-style commons governance is institution design, not pure property dissolution.
- **Historical sequence is not linear.** Economic forms layer. L1, L2, L3, L4, L5, and L6 coexist in modern societies.
