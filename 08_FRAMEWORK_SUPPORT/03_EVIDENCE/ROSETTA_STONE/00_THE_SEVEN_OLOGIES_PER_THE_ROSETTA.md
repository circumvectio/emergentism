# The Seven -ologies — reversible Rosetta crosswalk

> **[金] Kintsugi seam — 2026-07-20.** The former body treated a selected
> seven-row correspondence as a generated causal chain, as evidence that the
> sevenfold corpus was vindicated, and at points as a derivation of Justice
> from an equatorial chart position. None of those inferences follows. This
> repair keeps the useful navigation table and makes it reversible.

## What this surface is

The table below is a **chosen index** between seven Emergentism folders and
seven philosophical questions. It is useful when a reader wants to translate
one inquiry into the vocabulary of another. The row order does not assert that
one discipline causes, generates, ranks, or completes the next.

| Row label | Selected discipline | Guiding question |
|---|---|---|
| L1 | Teleology | What ends or directions are being proposed? |
| L2 | Epistemology | What can be known, and by what warrant? |
| L3 | Methodology | How will the claim or practice be tested? |
| L4 | Axiology | What is being valued, by whom, and at whose cost? |
| L5 | Cosmology | What large-scale structure is being modeled? |
| L6 | Ontology | What is claimed to exist, and in what sense? |
| L7 | Theology | What ultimate or boundary language is being used? |

This is the folder order currently used by the corpus `[S]`. The association
between those folders and the seven Rosetta rows is interpretive `[I]`. A
different domain may need fewer, more, overlapping, or differently ordered
categories without contradicting Emergentism.

## What this surface does not establish

- It does not generate a causal chain from social role to way of knowing,
  reasoning style, discipline, political regime, or equation.
- It does not prove that nature, minds, cultures, or sciences are sevenfold.
- It does not make mythic names, social castes, or boundary symbols into kinds
  of persons, levels of human worth, moral identities, or causal agents.
- It does not derive Justice from a row label, balance point, or any other
  chart fact.
- It does not transfer the evidence tier of one column to another.

The labels sometimes called gods, demons, or Titans elsewhere are optional
symbolic roles in a selected grammar. They are not people, castes, moral
essences, developmental ranks, or evidence for this crosswalk.

## Reversibility and test contract

For a domain `d`, a proposed projection `rho_d` is admissible only if it names:

1. the domain-native constructs before assigning Rosetta labels;
2. the alternatives to the seven-row partition;
3. the independent observations, dependencies, and missing evidence;
4. the prediction that differs from rival partitions; and
5. the result that would make the projection be withdrawn.

A failed projection is removed without changing the E1-E10, formula, or
Justice owners. If deleting the row labels leaves the domain analysis intact,
the Rosetta has served as a lens. If the analysis collapses merely because the
labels are removed, the mapping was doing unearned explanatory work.

**Exit:** use the seven rows when they clarify a comparison; put them down when
they force the territory.
