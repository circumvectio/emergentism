# Rosetta Stone corpus map

The Rosetta is a translation instrument. It projects a selected topology across
domains so that similarities, inversions, and missing cells become visible. It
does not transfer proof, upgrade evidence, or establish that every tradition
natively contains seven levels.

## Owners

- [Master Rosetta](00_THE_MASTER_ROSETTA.md) — the principal table.
- [Rosetta protocol](00_THE_ROSETTA_PROTOCOL.md) — translation discipline.
- [Replicator crosswalk](ROSETTA_REPLICATOR.md) — genotype through
  Egregoreotype, held at explicit tier.
- [Computation](ROSETTA_COMPUTATION.md), [psychology](ROSETTA_PSYCHOLOGY.md),
  [music](ROSETTA_MUSIC.md), [mythology](ROSETTA_MYTHOLOGY.md), and other
  family columns — domain-specific projections.
- [Row studies](D_SERIES_ROWS/) and [domain studies](D_SERIES_DOMAINS/) — local
  elaborations that remain downstream of the Master and source disciplines.

## Translation contract

For a projection `rho_domain`, report:

1. the source relation being projected;
2. the target-domain evidence and native vocabulary;
3. the proposed correspondence;
4. mismatches and omitted degrees of freedom;
5. evidence tier;
6. a kill criterion.

A round trip may preserve topology while losing semantics. That loss must be
declared. A fit discovered after choosing the rows is not independent evidence
for the row count.

## Boundary

The active Rosetta contains Emergentist correspondences only. Former product,
organization, financial, and agent-runtime projections remain available as
historical applications in the
