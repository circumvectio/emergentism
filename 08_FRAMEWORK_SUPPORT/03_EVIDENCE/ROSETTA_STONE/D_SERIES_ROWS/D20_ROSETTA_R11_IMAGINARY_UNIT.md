## 1. Exact mathematical floor `[A]`

In the complex plane,

```text
i²=-1
|i|=1
z ↦ iz  rotates the plane by π/2
z ↦ i²z=-z  rotates the plane by π
```

These are ordinary complex-algebra facts. They do not require curved geometry.
A full plane rotation is `2π`, so two multiplications by `i` are a half-turn,
not a journey once around a sphere.

Under standard stereographic projection to the Riemann sphere `ℂP¹≅S²`, the
entire unit circle `{z∈ℂ:|z|=1}` maps to the equator. The point `i` is one point
on that circle; it is not the equator itself. General Möbius transformations
map generalized circles to generalized circles, but a full spherical rotation
does not generically send a point to its antipode.

## 2. Reciprocal chart remains a different object `[A/S]`

On the selected open positive-real meridian,

```text
θ∈(0,π)
φ=cot(θ/2)
ν=tan(θ/2)
P∞=φν=1
B=2/(φ+ν)≤1
```

At `θ=π/2`, `φ=ν=1` and `B=1`. This is a coordinate identity and score on a
declared one-parameter chart. It does not identify `i` with `P∞`, make `i` a
conversion operator between D4 and D5, or turn the chart identity into a
conserved physical quantity.

## 3. Titan sovereignty `[S/I]`

The Titan vocabulary is a metalinguistic three-seat display:

```text
•     ⊙     ○
0_T   1_T   ∞_T
```

`TitanFrame:Type_Meta`; no arithmetic operation accepts its terms. The display
is therefore operator-free. Ordinary complex multiplication by `i` acts on
complex numbers, not on Titan seats. Projective `∞_P` occurs only in an
explicitly named projective structure and is not the horizon-facing Titan term
`∞_T`.

## 4. The correspondence that survives `[I/C]`

The Rosetta may use `i` as an **analogy for an orthogonal descriptive
coordinate**: a reminder that one coordinate system may need another axis to
represent what a one-dimensional projection omits. This can generate questions
about subjective and systemic awareness `[I]`.

It does not establish any of the following:

- that `i` is consciousness, the present, or a witness;
- that complex multiplication performs quantum measurement or collapse;
- that D4 and D5 are perpendicular axes in a physical space;
- that a corpus callosum implements complex multiplication;
- that the Hard Problem is solved;
- that similar diagrams constitute convergent empirical validation.

Any stronger consciousness proposal is a separately specified `[C]` model. It
must name variables, mechanism, observables, rival explanations, prediction,
and kill criterion. Removing that proposal leaves every mathematical statement
above unchanged.
