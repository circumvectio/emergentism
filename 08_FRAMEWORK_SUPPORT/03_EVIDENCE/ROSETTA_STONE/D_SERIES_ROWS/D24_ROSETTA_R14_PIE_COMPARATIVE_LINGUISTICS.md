## 1. The Roots

**\*h₂r̥tó-** (PIE) = "fitted, right, ordered" — the ordering, fitting-together, or truth-bearing relation.

**\*dyeu-** (PIE) = "to shine, be bright; sky, heaven, god" — the luminous, visible, skyward display of that order.

This is the most securely reconstructed theonymic root in the Indo-European family. Confirmed by:
- Regular phonological correspondences across 10+ daughter languages
- Identical syntactic position (nominative + vocative)
- Consistent semantic field (sky + brightness + high-priority order)
