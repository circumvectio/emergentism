**Note on Φ_max:** These values predate the sphere correction. On the corrected sphere, Φ_max should be read as B-proximity — how close the civilisation's balance score is to B = 1 (equatorial). The Devil's Bargain: Wild B ≈ 0.5 > Domesticated B ≈ 0.17.
