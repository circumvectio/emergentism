- The [Settled Canon Registry](../../../00_META/00_SETTLED_CANON_REGISTRY.md)
  and dimension-first owners define the worldview.

- Seven -ologies are questions; they are not D-rungs.

  owner document.

All other pages in this folder are mapping data, historical experiments, or
domain projections at their stated tiers. If one conflicts with the Master
Rosetta or a dimension owner, it is non-authoritative evidence of an earlier
claim state. Its value is auditability, not precedence.

## Success and failure

Internal fit means a declared mapping is coherent. External support requires a
preregistered out-of-sample discriminator, independent coding, explicit rivals,
and visible non-fits. A mapping that can be rescued by arbitrary recoding fails.
