# The Master Rosetta — Translation and Audit Protocol

Rosetta is a method for asking whether two domains share a useful relational
pattern. It is not a D-register, an ontology, an empirical result, or a machine
for turning analogy into proof.

## 1. Type contract

For a source claim `c` in domain `A`, a Rosetta projection is a partial map

\[
\rho_{A\to B}:c_A\mapsto \widehat c_B
\]

that preserves explicitly named relations. The image `ĉ_B` is a candidate
translation. It inherits neither the source's truth nor its evidence tier.

Every projection must declare:

1. source and target domains;
2. the relation claimed invariant;
3. source provenance and independence;
4. information discarded by the translation;
5. native cardinality before normalization;
6. target prediction or practical use;
7. evidence tier and kill criterion.

If a round trip is claimed, `ρ_{B→A}(ρ_{A→B}(c))` must state what is preserved
and what is lost. Visual resemblance is never enough.

## 2. Non-transfer laws

- A theorem in `A` does not become a theorem in `B`.
- Repeated rows do not become independent observations when one interpreter
  selected or normalized them.
- A seven-row fit does not establish that a source domain natively has seven
  stages.
- A correspondence cannot define `D0…D6`, `μ₀…μ₄`, `b₆`, or `r₆`.
- A Rosetta result cannot promote `[I]` or `[C]` to `[S]` or `[A]`.
- Failed mappings and non-fits remain in the record.

## 3. Relation to the dimension-first canon

The reality scaffold is owned elsewhere: `D0` boundary, `D1` distinction,
`D2` configuration, `D3` quantum state, `D4` causal actuality, `D5`

domain *through* those types but cannot alter them.

## 4. Audit sequence

1. **Inventory:** record the source's native terms and count before mapping.
2. **Independence:** disclose inheritance, diffusion, shared authorship, and
   framework-generated extensions.
3. **Translate:** define `ρ` and the proposed invariant.
4. **Countermap:** seek rival maps and material the selected rows omit.
5. **Discriminate:** preregister an observation on unseen material that differs
   from chance, flexible coding, or a rival grammar.
6. **Kill or retain:** retain at `[I]` only while its discriminator and failure
   record remain visible.

A table that merely admits a coherent coding has demonstrated compressibility,
not external truth.

## 5. Selected seven-row example

The familiar chain

```text
Varṇa → Pramāṇa → Reasoning → -ology → Regime → Equation
```

is a selected reading order, not a causal law. Varṇa and regime are historical
analogies, never identities or rankings of persons; the seven -ologies are
cross-cutting philosophical questions, not D-rungs; equations do not evidence
the rows they annotate. Each cell remains `[I]` unless independently supported
inside its own domain.

## 6. Pass, fail, and kill

A mapping passes an internal audit only when its invariant is explicit, its
non-fits remain visible, and a rival cannot explain the same material with less
freedom. It earns external standing only through preregistered out-of-sample
discrimination by independent raters.

The Rosetta claim is killed if flexible recoding can rescue every failure, if
native source counts must be hidden, or if its apparent convergence disappears
under independent coding.
