# Failed Mappings — Domains That Do Not Fit the 7-Stage Structure

**Date:** 2026-04-25
**Status:** Canonical honesty surface
**Purpose:** Document domains examined for Rosetta inclusion that failed the 7-stage test. This document strengthens the framework by demonstrating epistemic discipline.
**Evidence tier:** [I] for documented stage counts; [S] for structural assessment; [I] for interpretive notes
**Depends on:** [`05_NON_WEIRD_SWEEP_2026_04_25.md`](05_NON_WEIRD_SWEEP_2026_04_25.md), [`00_SELECTION_METHODOLOGY.md`](00_SELECTION_METHODOLOGY.md)
**Rule:** No domain is "wrong" for not fitting. The framework's claim is that *some* domains converge on 7 stages. The absence of convergence in other domains is data, not refutation.

## 0. Why Failed Mappings Matter

The strongest move an empirical framework can make is to **report its own negative results.**

If the Rosetta only includes domains that fit, it is a file-drawer exercise — the pattern-matching equivalent of only publishing successful trials. If the Rosetta includes domains that were examined and failed, it becomes a research instrument.

**This document is the immune system.** It is Kālī cutting false coherence to recover viability.

## 1. Daoist 內丹 (Internal Alchemy) — FAILED 7-Stage Test

| Attribute | Finding |
|---|---|
| **Tradition** | Daoist internal alchemy (nèidān) |
| **Canonical stage count** | 3 stages (refining essence→qi→spirit→void) or 5 stages (Quanzhen school) |
| **7-stage versions** | Exist in some later syncretic lineages but are interpretive elaborations, not canonical |
| **Why it failed** | The foundational texts articulate 3 or 5 stages, not 7. The 7-stage versions are secondary. |
| **What DOES map** | The operator grammar (jīng→qì→shén→xū→Dao as coherence-viability gradient) holds regardless of stage count. |
| **Verdict** | **7-stage claim FAILS. Operator-grammar claim HOLDS.** |
| **Source basis** | Quanzhen lineage texts; scholarly consensus (Kohn, Komjathy) |

## 3. Sub-Saharan African Traditions — FAILED 7-Stage Test

| **Verdict** | **7-stage claim FAILS across surveyed Sub-Saharan traditions.** |
| **Source basis** | Griaule 1948; van Beek 1991; Kagame; Ellis 1829 |

## 7. Chemistry (Periodic Table) — FAILED Functional Mapping Test

| Attribute | Finding |
|---|---|

| **Why it failed** | While there are 7 periods, the functional content of chemical periods does not map to the operator geometry. Period 4 contains transition metals (high chemical complexity) — a possible L4 analog — but Period 1 (H, He) is not a "boundary condition" in any meaningful sense parallel to L1/Kali. |
| **What DOES map** | Period 4 as "center" is suggestive (transition metals are the most chemically versatile). But the rest of the mapping is forced. |
| **Verdict** | **7-stage claim PARTIALLY HOLDS numerically but FUNCTIONALLY FAILS.** The number 7 appears, but the rows are not functional mirrors. |
| **Source basis** | IUPAC periodic table; standard chemistry |

## 10. What This Means for the Framework

### The honest claim

> **The 7-stage initiatory structure converges in Indo-European and Mediterranean traditions. Other traditions express coherence-viability gradients through different counts (3, 5, 8, 10, 16, varying) or non-graded functional structures. The operator grammar (4 mixed-sign Gods + 3 same-sign Executive boundary frames) is structurally exhaustive and appears across all surveyed traditions, but the specific 7-stage articulation is empirically Indo-European/Mediterranean, not universal.**

### Framework status

| Claim | Status After Failed Mapping Survey |
|---|---|
| Universal 7-stage structure | **NARROWED** to Indo-European/Mediterranean |
| Operator grammar exhaustive | **HOLDS** — no counter-example found |
| φ·ν = 1 on S² generates pattern | **[C] unchanged** — not directly tested by this survey |
| Cross-cultural validity | **WEIRD bias confirmed** — non-IE traditions show non-7 structures |
| Rosetta Stone falls? | **NO** — the framework survives by honest scope-narrowing |

### The kill criterion is NOT triggered

The Rosetta's kill criterion (from [`04_DEEP_RUMINATION_2026_04_25.md`](04_DEEP_RUMINATION_2026_04_25.md)):

1. **A domain requires a fundamentally different number of levels** — PARTIALLY OBSERVED. Multiple domains have non-7 counts. But these are not "fundamentally different" in a way that breaks the operator grammar. They are different resolutions of the same surface.
2. **The operator grammar proves non-exhaustive** — NOT OBSERVED.
3. **The equator is not a global minimum** — NOT OBSERVED.

**Verdict:** The framework survives. It is **narrowed**, not falsified.
