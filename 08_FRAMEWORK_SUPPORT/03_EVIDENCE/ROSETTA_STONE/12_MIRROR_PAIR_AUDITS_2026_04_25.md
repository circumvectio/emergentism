## 0. Mirror Law

Mirror pairs test the S² claim more sharply than ordinary sequence matching.

| Pair | Balance | Required inversion |
|---|---:|---|
| L3/L5 | B = sqrt(3)/2 | Productive building vs systematic holding. |
| L2/L6 | B = 1/2 | First relational contract vs axiomatic release of contract. |
| L1/L7 | B -> 0 | Boundary pressure from below vs boundary closure from above. |

If a column only rises linearly, it may still be useful, but it is not an S² projection. The audit question is always:

```text
same functional kind, opposite vector?
```

## 1. L3/L5 Audit: Productive Building / Systematic Holding

**Row sentence:** L3 builds capability inside a concrete system; L5 holds the whole system as an ordered architecture. Both are high-balance rows. L3 is outward-productive; L5 is inward-systematic.

| Economics | Market exchange / price | Planned-information economy | strong | [13_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_ECONOMICS_2026_04_25.md](13_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_ECONOMICS_2026_04_25.md) | If planning is not functionally inverse to decentralized price allocation. |

## 2. L2/L6 Audit: Relational Contract / Axiomatic Release

**Row sentence:** L2 forms the first relational bond that prevents raw collapse; L6 releases the bond's false necessity. Both sit at half-balance. L2 says "we are bound"; L6 says "the binding is not ultimate."

| Column | L2 cell | L6 cell | Mirror score | Source basis | Kill criterion |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Operator | Kali cuts false coherence | Śiva dissolves all form | strong | Operator grammar | If lower negation and upper dissolution collapse into the same function. |
| Varna | Śūdra labourer | Sādhu renunciant | strong | Social-function mapping | If bodily service and bodily renunciation cannot be paired as inverse disciplines. |
| Pramana | Comparative Analysis / analogy | First Principles / non-apprehension | strong | Vedantic epistemic taxonomy | If positive comparison and absence-knowledge are not accepted as inverse knowing. |
| Politics | Democracy | Anarchy | fail / partial | Plato plus framework extension | Plato treats democracy and anarchy as a continuum, not mirror. |
| Economics | Gift/social debt | Commons/post-scarcity | partial | [13_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_ECONOMICS_2026_04_25.md](13_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_ECONOMICS_2026_04_25.md) | If commons does not function as structural dissolution of price/debt. |
| Music | Folk/pentatonic/call-response | Atonality/drone/ambient | partial | [14_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_MUSIC_2026_04_25.md](14_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_MUSIC_2026_04_25.md) | If the pairing is merely "simple before" vs "complex after," not inverse simplicity. |
| Neuroscience | Limbic/social-emotional | DMN/witness/meta-awareness | fail / testable alternate | [15_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_NEUROSCIENCE_2026_04_25.md](15_COLUMN_CELL_AUDIT_NEUROSCIENCE_2026_04_25.md) | If DMN is not trans-limbic affect-observer but ordinary self-narrative machinery. |

**Verdict:** L2/L6 is clean in formal and Sanskrit columns, partial in cultural/diagnostic columns, and weak in Plato/neuroscience. It is the best early-warning mirror: when it fails, the column is probably linear or selected after the fact.

**Repair path:** pre-register L6 as the inverse of L2 before sourcing. If source-domain L6 cannot be found, keep the L6 cell interpretive or remove it from evidence-weighted tables.
