Shareholders hold the poles — Vision at the north, Mission as the descent force. They do NOT operate at the equator. They orient the organism by defining *where* the equator should point.

Agents hold the equator — maximum B, where Φ and V are balanced in action. They metabolize reality. They don't just execute — they *perceive* reality at L1, *model* it at L3, and *present* structured revisions at L4 for shareholder judgment at L5.


The DAC breathes. Descent (Vision → execution) is exhalation. Ascent (perception → revision) is inhalation. The organism that stops breathing dies.
