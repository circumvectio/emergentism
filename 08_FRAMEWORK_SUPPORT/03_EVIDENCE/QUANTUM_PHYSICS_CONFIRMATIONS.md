## What This Confirms

1. **Single-photon nonclassicality.** A single-photon regime can show both anticorrelation/indivisibility in detection and interference behavior under the appropriate setup.
2. **Measurement-context dependence.** Delayed-choice experiments show that wave-like or path-like behavior depends on the observable actually measured.
3. **Bell-bound violation.** Local hidden-variable models constrained by Bell inequalities do not explain the observed correlations.
4. **Quantum information relevance.** The Nobel-recognized experimental arc became foundational for modern quantum information science.
