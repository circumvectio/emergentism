### C2. BALANCE FUNCTION CANONICALIZED

**Problem:** B defined as sin(θ) in P22/P24 but as 2/(φ+ν) in F31.

**Resolution:** **B = sin θ** is canonical. This is the sphere correction. The old formula 2/(φ+ν) is an approximation that only agrees at the equator.

**Derivation:** On S² with φ = cot(θ/2), ν = tan(θ/2): B = sin θ = 2·sin(θ/2)·cos(θ/2) = 2/(φ + 1/φ) = 2/(ν + 1/ν).

**Affected documents:** Sphere Health Dashboard, all L-level tables, all B-value references.

### C3. P = 1 EVERYWHERE (The Sphere Correction)
