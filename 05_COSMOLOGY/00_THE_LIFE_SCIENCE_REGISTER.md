## Why This Document Exists

Biology already has language for several local appearances of what the framework groups under `Teleological Force`.

That is not a problem.

It is a refinement rule.

When Emergentism speaks to life science, it should not behave as if every relevant phenomenon is unnamed until the framework arrives. The biologically responsible move is:

1. name the nearest established vocabulary first
2. state where the framework is extending or unifying that vocabulary
3. label the stronger theory as theory

This document makes that translation canonical.

## The Three Biological Layers

### Layer A: Established Biological Language [S]

These are already live scientific vocabularies:

- **Teleonomy** -- end-directed behavior in living systems without requiring cosmic intention
- **Autopoiesis** -- self-producing, boundary-maintaining organization
- **Homeostasis / allostasis** -- regulation of viable ranges under stable and changing conditions
- **Interoceptive regulation** -- sensing and acting on internal bodily state
- **Niche construction / organism-environment feedback** -- living systems partially shape the conditions under which they persist

Emergentism should treat these as the first translation layer for biology-facing work.

### Layer B: Emergentist Synthesis [I]

`Teleological Force` is the framework's unifying interpretive name for the family resemblance across those biological phenomena:

- living systems maintain themselves
- regulation is not random but viability-directed
- organisms do not merely react; they preserve conditions for continued agency
- embodied intelligence is organized around staying in a viable band

This is a legitimate theory move.

It is not yet established biology.

### Layer C: Frontier Theory [C]

These are allowed as theory submission, but they may not be presented as settled biology:

- `F5` as a **literal Standard Model-style fifth physical interaction**
- **retrocausal** or future-to-past selection
- **systemic awareness-collapse** claims as biology rather than interpretation
- organismic regulation as proof of **cosmic volition**

The framework may propose these.

The framework may not pretend biology has already confirmed them.

## First-Person Evidence in Scientific Register

The life-science bridge does not require science to become mystical.

It requires a narrower and more disciplined move:

- trained first-person report can be treated as data
- that data should be structured, compared, and constrained by physiology, behavior, and method
- direct experience is not the enemy of science; undisciplined inflation is

The closest live scientific bridges are:

- **neurophenomenology**
- **contemplative neuroscience**
- **bodily self-systemic awareness research**
- **interoceptive systemic awareness research**
- **computational phenomenology**

So when the framework uses `Pratyakṣa` in biology-facing work, the correct translation is:

> **disciplined first-person disclosure, treated as structured phenomenological data rather than private revelation**

That does not prove the full metaphysics.

It does make the framework's evidence architecture more scientifically legible.

## Writing Rules for Biology-Facing Documents

### Rule 1: Biology First

If a claim can be stated in the language of:

- teleonomy
- autopoiesis
- homeostasis or allostasis
- interoceptive regulation
- self-maintaining organization

state it that way before introducing `Teleological Force`.

### Rule 2: Theory Submission Means Explicit Extension

When the framework moves beyond current biology, say so directly:

- "The framework proposes..."
- "A stronger interpretation is..."
- "This is submitted as theory, not as established biology."

### Rule 3: Do Not Smuggle Frontier Claims Through Biology

No biology-facing document may present the following as already biologically established:

- literal Standard Model-style fifth-interaction language
- retrocausal selection
- systemic awareness-causes-collapse claims
- cosmic will inferred directly from organismic regulation

Those remain higher-tier interpretations or conjectures.

### Rule 4: Myth Comes After Mechanism

If a biology-facing document also uses `Demiurge`, `Dyēus`, `Hidden Hand`, or other mythic names:

1. establish the biological register first
2. mark the mythic language as downstream interpretation
3. do not use mythic personification as evidence

## What the Framework Is Actually Claiming in Biology

The minimal biologically legible claim is:

**Living systems are not well described as passive matter alone. They are organized around preserving viable self-maintaining constraint structures.**

The stronger Emergentist claim is:

**The family resemblance across teleonomy, autopoiesis, allostasis, interoception, and niche construction reflects a deeper teleological regularity that the framework names `Teleological Force`.**

The frontier claim is:

**This regularity may ultimately prove to be deeper than current biology or even current physics allows.**

That sequence preserves honesty without shrinking ambition.

---

## What Survives if the Frontier Theory Fails

If biology fully explains purposive-looking organization without a literal Standard Model-style fifth physical interaction, without retrocausality, and without systemic awareness-collapse:

- teleonomy still survives
- autopoiesis still survives
- homeostatic and allostatic regulation still survive
- interoceptive and action-selection frameworks still survive
- Emergentism can contract to a structural synthesis over these domains

What dies is not the observation that life is viability-directed.

What dies is the stronger metaphysical inflation of that observation.
