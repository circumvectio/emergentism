# The Dyadic Coupling Law — Kintsugi Successor

> **[金] Crack:** the predecessor derived good and evil from `φν=1`, equated
> balance with goodness, and treated `η=0` as a sufficient moral proof.
> **Repair:** the chart describes; the Justice postulate evaluates; receipts
> test what happened.

## 1. Analytic chart facts `[A]`

For `θ∈(0,π)`, define

\[
\phi=\cot(\theta/2),\qquad \nu=\tan(\theta/2).
\]

Then

\[
\phi\nu=1,\qquad \phi+\nu\ge2,
\]

with equality in the second relation at `φ=ν=1`. These are consequences of
the chosen reciprocal chart. They are not empirical conservation laws,
ontological proofs, or ethical imperatives.

## 2. Coupling model `[I/C]`

Let `C(Φ,V)` be a normalized, monotone conjunctive aggregator satisfying

\[
C(0,V)=C(\Phi,0)=0.
\]

Emergentism selects the normalized product instance

\[
P_{node}:=C_\times(\Phi,V)=\Phi V
\]

for its canonical compression. The product expresses the chosen AND-class
intuition that foresight/coherence and embodied means/viability are jointly
necessary. It is not uniquely forced; minimum, harmonic, Cobb–Douglas, and
other conjunctive aggregators can rank states differently.

“Goodness is the bond” remains an `[I]` metaphor for the declared commitment
to durable mutual option-capacity. It is not a theorem about every real dyad.

## 3. Justice is not a chart coordinate

`etaObserved=0` means only that a declared protocol observed no extraction at
its stated boundary, horizon, resolution, and uncertainty. It is necessary for
the Emergentist Justice predicate, but never sufficient.

Justice also requires:

- every materially affected bearer, payer, and beneficiary;
- accountable authorization, consent, and custody;
- truthful costs and consequence-bearing;
- reversibility where possible, Grace Exit, and a contest path;
- prospective non-destruction and retrospective receipts.

The canonical directional predicates live in
[`Objective Morals and Ethics`](../04_AXIOLOGY/02_VALUE_THEORY/00_OBJECTIVE_MORALS_AND_ETHICS.md):

```text
morals:   individual → whole
ethics:   whole → individual
syntropy: both durable potentials rise under Justice
```

Aggregate gain cannot compensate for destroying either side or a hidden third
bearer. Voluntary sacrifice is a distinct costly class and is never strict
syntropy.

## 4. Optional correspondences

Masculine/feminine, yin/yang, Śakti, mythic, political, biological, and
psychological dyads are Rosetta projections `[I]`. A projection must preserve
types, tiers, rivals, and kill criteria; resemblance transfers no proof.

The law's honest core is therefore modest and useful: test whether jointly
necessary capacities remain coupled, and judge consequential action only with
the full Justice envelope and outcome receipt.
