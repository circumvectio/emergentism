# The Exhaustion of the G7 Game

> **[金] Crack:** the predecessor treated a sign matrix as a universal move
> proof and made give/take or `η` sufficient moral verdicts. **Repair:** G7 is a
> declared language with an explicit outside and an independent Justice test.

## Scoped alphabet

```text
M4 = {give-Φ, give-V, take-Φ, take-V}
F3 = {create-frame, dissolve-frame, preserve-frame}
G7 = M4 ⊔ F3
```

`M4` names four channel-and-direction symbols in the selected two-factor,
self/other transfer grammar. `F3` names three same-sign frame labels. Frames
are not plays or agents. A G7-coded play is a move word or declared mixture over
`M4`, evaluated under one selected frame in `F3`.

The seven are exhaustive **by construction inside G7**. The count is not forced
by algebra, the sphere, the Titans, nature, or all possible agency. An act whose
effects cannot be represented without loss remains outside G7. Model adoption,
authorization, audit, contest, correction, and exit are metagame acts.

## Justice remains separate

Giving can be coercive or destructive; taking can be consensual, reparative, or
lawful. No operator name, sign cell, pole, or direction carries morality by
itself. `etaObserved` is a scoped consequence observation. Zero is necessary
but insufficient for Justice.

Each consequential play must expose every affected bearer, authorization,
consent, custody, payer, beneficiary, reversibility, exit, contest, intention,
and outcome receipt. Moral/ethical/syntropic labels follow the canonical value
owner only after those checks.

The mythic names—Kṛṣṇa, Arjuna, Kālī, Kali, Brahmā, Śiva, Viṣṇu—are optional
mnemonics `[I]`. They may not rank people, castes, genders, cultures, or agents.

**Kill criterion:** exhibit a supposedly G7-coded play whose material effect
cannot be recovered from its encoding. That refutes the encoding for the case;
it does not require reality to shrink to fit the alphabet.
