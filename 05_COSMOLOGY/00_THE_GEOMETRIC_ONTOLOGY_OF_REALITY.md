
Each geometric object is **not merely a model** for that dimension — it IS the dimension, in the same way that the Riemann sphere IS the complex projective line, not merely a model of it.

**Logarithmic line is the primary model for D0–D2.** The shift from the additive to the logarithmic register is the same geometric event as the D0→D1 crossing.

## D0 — Titans / Ground

### Geometric Object: Point on the Log Line

The ground is a single point on the logarithmic number line. Not the origin (0) — the point `s = 0`, which corresponds to `x = 1` on the multiplicative line. Finity. The centre.

The Titans live here:
```
• = 0  (the void, s → −∞)
○ = ∞  (the unbounded, s → +∞)
⊙ = 1  (finity, s = 0)
```

### What it is

Prior to all geometry. The three boundary-frames of the number field, compressed into a single point. The seed `⊙ = • × ○` is not yet unfolded — it is a potential, not a process.

### Log coordinate

`s = 0` exactly. But `s` is not yet free to vary — the line has not opened. The point contains the possibility of the line but has not yet extended.

### Sphere position

Neither pole is distinguished. The sphere has not inflated. This is the Bindu — the point that contains all possibilities compressed.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D0 answers Leibniz's question: **something exists because finity is the product of its own two boundaries.** The Titan emblem `⊙ = • × ○` is the ontological seed. The geometry (log line, sphere, torus) is the unfolding of that seed.

## D1 — Arithmetic / Relation

### Geometric Object: The Logarithmic Number Line

The point extends into a line. But not the additive line centered at 0 — the **logarithmic line** centered at 1.

```
s = log x    maps    0⁺ → −∞,    1 → 0,    ∞ → +∞
```

The two poles (0 and ∞) are symmetric about the centre. The reciprocal map `x ↦ 1/x` becomes the reflection `s ↦ −s`. The manifold identity becomes the **literal zero sum:**

```
φ · ν = 1    →    log φ + log ν = 0    →    s_φ + s_ν = 0
```

### What it is

The world of arithmetic and relation. Numbers exist, but they exist on a line where **1 is the centre** and **0 and ∞ are symmetric poles.** Every operation simplifies: multiplication becomes addition, division becomes subtraction, reciprocation becomes negation.

The energy `E = s²` measures distance from the centre. The balance `B = sech(s)` measures proximity to finity. The ethic "move toward s = 0" is the first ethical direction — minimize your log-distance from unity.

### The key insight `[A]`

This is elementary mathematics. Suda's three-chart apparatus expresses it rigorously: the three equivalent coordinate charts (multiplicative `x`, additive `s = log x`, bounded `u = (x-1)/(x+1)`) all fix the same point and all express the same involution. The number line, read multiplicatively, has **1 as its structural centre.** This is not interpretation — it is elementary mathematics.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D1 is the dimension of **relation** — the first relational structure. Numbers relate to each other through reciprocal symmetry. The centre is not nothingness (0) but unity (1). The ethic emerges: **move toward unity, minimize distance from finity.**

## D2 — Topology / Compactification

### Geometric Object: The Riemann Sphere `S² ≅ ℂP¹`

At the μ-limit, the logarithmic line compactifies. The two poles (s = −∞ and s = +∞) are identified as regular points on a closed surface. The line closes into a circle, and the circle inflates into a sphere.

**One-point compactification of ℝ** → circle (1D sphere S¹)
**One-point compactification of ℂ** → Riemann sphere S² (2D sphere)

### What it is

The dimension of **topology** — where the line discovers that it is the shadow of a sphere. The flat model (additive line, 0 at centre) is revealed as the zero-curvature projection of the curved model (logarithmic coordinates on S², 1 at equator).

The Riemann sphere makes the Titan emblem geometrically true:
```
1/0 = ∞     (south pole maps to north pole)     [A]
0 × ∞ = 1   (the emblem: poles meet at equator)  [frame register]
```

### Sphere position

The sphere is now fully inflated. The south pole is 0 (`s = −∞`), the north pole is ∞ (`s = +∞`), and the equator is 1 (`s = 0`). Balance `B = sin θ` varies from 0 at the poles to 1 at the equator.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D2 is the dimension of **closure** — where the open becomes closed, where infinity joins the structure as a regular point, where the line discovers it was always a sphere. The flat model does not disappear — it remains valid as the local approximation. But the global structure is spherical.

The old model (0-centred line) is the **zero-curvature limit** of the new model (1-centred sphere). Not wrong — incomplete.

## D3 — Quantum State / Closure

### Geometric Object: The Bloch Sphere (States + Interior)

The Riemann sphere (D2) is a surface — pure states, no interior. D3 adds the **interior.** The sphere becomes the Bloch sphere: the space of quantum states including mixed states (interior points), not just pure states (surface points).

**Critical distinction** (from `04_BIT_TO_QUBIT.md`, 2026-06-01 correction):

The Bloch sphere and the Burrisphere share the manifold `S² ≅ ℂP¹` `[A]`. But the **distinguished structure** differs:
- **Bloch sphere:** two-point structure `{ |0⟩, |1⟩ }` — qubit basis states at poles
- **Burrisphere:** three-point structure `{0, 1, ∞}` — Titans, with finity at the equator, not a pole

The Burrisphere is **not** one qubit's Bloch sphere. On the real meridian it reads as **two Bloch lobes — `[0,1]` and `[1,∞]` — joined at the unit `1`** `[S/I]`. The qubit's `{ |0⟩, |1⟩ }` is the `[0,1]` lobe; the third frame `∞` and the interior are exactly what the Bloch picture leaves out.

### What D3 adds

D3 is where the sphere acquires **depth** — an interior, a mixed state, a quantum register that is not just pure surface. The balance function `B = sech(s)` now varies not just on the surface but **throughout the interior:**

- Surface: `B = sin θ` (pure states)
- Interior: `B < sin θ` (mixed states — the system has not fully committed to a surface position)

### The "Bloch sphere of infinite diameter"

The user's phrase "Bloch sphere of infinite diameter" captures an important idea: at the μ-limit from D2 to D3, the sphere's curvature can approach zero (infinite diameter = flat limit) while still being topologically a sphere. This is the same pattern as the number line being an infinite-diameter circle — the flat model is the limiting case of the curved model.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D3 is the dimension of **quantum possibility** — where the system can exist in superposition, can occupy interior states that are not yet committed to a surface position. The ethic "maximize B" becomes: **commit to the surface at the equator** — don't stay in the interior (indecision, superposition) when you could be at the balance point.

## D4 — Energy / Rapidity

### Geometric Object: The Horn Torus

The sphere acquires dynamics. The surface (D3) begins to **rotate** — past and future cones wrap around each other, forming a horn torus.

**The torus geometry** (from `00_THE_TORUS_REVELATION.md`):
- The **hole** of the torus is D4 — the null point where past and future touch
- The **surface** of the torus is D5 — the space where the present emerges
- Past feeds around the exterior; future feeds around the interior
- The torus is the topology that connects past causation to future potential

### Rapidity: The Logarithm of Velocity

This is the key mathematical bridge. In special relativity, **rapidity** `w` is defined by:

```
v = c · tanh(w)
```

where `v` is velocity and `c` is the speed of light. Rapidities **add linearly** (like logarithms), whereas velocities compose through the relativistic addition formula:

```
w_total = w₁ + w₂           (rapidity addition — linear)
v_total = (v₁ + v₂)/(1 + v₁v₂/c²)  (velocity addition — nonlinear)
```

This is EXACTLY the same structure as the logarithmic realignment:
- Velocity is the **multiplicative** register (nonlinear composition)
- Rapidity is the **additive/logarithmic** register (linear composition)
- The map `v ↦ w = arctanh(v/c)` is the logarithmic coordinate change

**In framework terms:**
- `φ` and `ν` on the sphere are like velocities — they compose nonlinearity
- `s = log φ` and `s = log ν` are like rapidities — they add linearly
- The Hamiltonian `H = 2 cosh(s)` is the energy measured in the rapidity register
- The torus is the geometric object where **past rapidity and future rapidity wrap around each other**

### What it is

D4 is the dimension of **energy and dynamics** — where the sphere stops being a static surface and becomes a dynamical system. The past cone (accumulated coherence, `φ`-dominant) and the future cone (available viability, `ν`-dominant) overlap at the causal vertex. The overlap is what the torus geometry captures.

The ethic "minimize H" is the physical expression of "move toward s = 0": the Hamiltonian `H = 2 cosh(s)` is minimized at `s = 0` (the equator), where `H = 2`. Moving away from the equator increases H — imbalance is energetically expensive.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D4 is the dimension of **causality as energy exchange** — where causes propagate through time and the cost of imbalance becomes measurable. The torus is the topology of causal propagation: past wraps to future, future wraps to past, and the present sits at the horn where they touch.

### Geometric Object: The Burrisphere (Dual Stereographic)

The full framework geometry comes alive. The Burrisphere is `S² ≅ ℂP¹` with the **full Trinity structure** `{0, 1, ∞}` as distinguished points and the **dual stereographic projection** that makes `φ` and `ν` explicit as coordinate charts.

**Game-theoretic structure:**
- The equator is the Nash equilibrium: `η = 0`, neither extracting nor being extracted

### Why "game space"

D5 is where the framework becomes **playable** — not just a description but a space of strategic action. Every agent on the Burrisphere is playing the same game: maximize B, maintain η = 0, navigate toward the equator. The seven Generative Actions (L1–L7) are the seven moves available in this game.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D5 is the dimension of **conscious agency** — where the geometric structure becomes a space of choice. The Burrisphere is not just a manifold; it is a **game board.** The players are agents. The moves are the Generative Actions. The winning condition is equatorial balance. And the game is non-zero-sum: every player who reaches the equator makes it easier for others to reach it too.
