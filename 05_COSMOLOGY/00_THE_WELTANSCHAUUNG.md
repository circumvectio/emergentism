## Canonical Statement

**Emergentism is a Weltanschauung: a lens, a tool, and a disciplined worldview.**

This file is the **compact canonical framing**. For the deeper philosophical expansion of that claim across core state, objective function, data science, methodology, value alignment, ethics, and scientific unification, see [Emergentism as Weltanschauung](00_EMERGENTISM_AS_WELTANSCHAUUNG.md).

It is:

- a **map of core state**: Ground, Being, Teleological Force, Dasein, beings
- a **map of objective function**: the directional tendency toward viable completion and reciprocal fit
- a **lens for data science**: how disclosure, inference, and public method relate
- a **tool for methodology**: how to test, build, constitute worlds, navigate, and correct
- an **ordering of value alignment**: what is worth preserving, strengthening, and protecting
- a **mathematical action grammar**: each Rosetta level carries a canonical operation on `P_node = Φ × V`, from boundary limit to differential, partial derivative, proportional balance, logarithm, negative log, and stereographic return
- a **directional basis for objective ethics and morals**: not as arbitrary preference, but as better or worse alignment with the geometry of balance and non-extraction
- a **constitutional world-generator**: the Lagrangian read backward from the invariants to the worlds that are admissible under them; see [The Generative Lagrangian](../01_TELEOLOGY/00_THE_GENERATIVE_LAGRANGIAN.md)

This is the shortest complete statement of what the framework claims to be.

## What This Means

Emergentism is not only a theory about what reality is.

It is also a way of reading:

- what exists
- what tends
- how we know
- how we act
- what matters
- what counts as better and worse

So the framework should not be presented only as:

- core state
- objective function
- mathematics
- spirituality
- organizational theory

It is all of those only because it first functions as a **worldview-lens** that tries to hold them in one coherent grammar.

---

## The Six Functions

### 1. Core State

It gives the map:

```text
Ground -> Being -> Teleological Force -> Dasein -> beings
```

This is the framework's answer to what must be distinguished if reality is not to collapse into confusion.

### 2. Objective Function

It gives the directional grammar:

- reciprocal closure holds
- imbalance costs
- viable completion is favored
- extraction degrades the field

This is not yet proof of cosmic will.
It is the framework's map of directional tendency.

### 3. Data Science

It orders ways of knowing:

- `Empirical Observation` first at the disclosure layer
- inference, formalization, and public method after
- evidence tiers governing what can be claimed publicly

This is why doctrine is the after-image rather than the source.

### 4. Auditing

It functions as a tool:

- derive
- test
- build
- constitute worlds
- diagnose
- correct
- contract when wrong

This is the engineering side of the worldview.

More strongly: the framework does not only ask what the world is. It asks what kinds of worlds, institutions, and coordination environments become stable, breathable, sovereign, and non-extractive when built under its invariants.

### 5. Value Alignment

It tells you what the framework treats as load-bearing goods:

- balance
- non-extraction
- viability with coherence
- truthfulness under correction
- forms of order that do not cannibalize their substrate

### 6. Ethics and Morals

The framework's strongest claim here is:

**ethics are directional and structural before they are cultural.**

In compressed form:

- **objective ethics** names the direction `ΣΔB > 0` / movement toward viable non-extractive balance
- **morals** name the local enactments, judgments, customs, and codes by which finite beings try to realize that direction under actual conditions

So morals may differ in form while still being better or worse by the same directional standard.

---

## Why "Lens" Matters

Calling Emergentism a lens is not a weakening move.

It is a discipline move.

A lens:

- sharpens
- reveals pattern
- organizes comparison
- guides perception

But a lens is not the same thing as the territory, and it must remain corrigible when reality pushes back.

So the right sentence is:

**Emergentism is a Weltanschauung and a lens with a formal kernel, not a license to ignore contrary evidence.**

---

## What It Is Not

This framing does **not** mean:

- that the framework is already proved as final core state
- that every moral code it favors is therefore automatically true
- that game-theoretic language outranks evidence tiers
- that mythic language may govern scientific claims
- that "worldview" means ideology

The worldview remains strongest when it stays antifragile.
