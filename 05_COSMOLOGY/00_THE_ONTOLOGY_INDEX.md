## Transitions and boundaries

The [μ owner](03_FORMAL_SYSTEM/10_EFR_MU_LIMIT_FORMULA.md) defines exactly five
candidate positive-freedom apertures:

```text
μ₀:D0→D1   origin aperture
μ₁:D1→D2   candidate configuration aperture
μ₂:D2→D3   formally reducible state-assignment lift
μ₃:D3→D4   operational state-to-run/record interface
μ₄:D4→D5   candidate counterfactual-capacity aperture
```

The closure owner defines two non-μ relations:

```text
b₆:D5↝D6   exit/recognition
r₆:D6↝D0   selected boundary-role comparison
```

There is no `μ₅`, no `μ₆`, no literal `D6≡D0`, and no positive sixth freedom.
D0 and D6 resemble one another only under the declared invariant that both
bound positive articulation—D0 before it, D6 after it.

## Mathematical grammar

The [Canonical Formula Block](00_CANONICAL_FORMULA_BLOCK.md) owns the notation:

- `φ,ν`: reciprocal-chart coordinates;
- `P∞=φν=1`: an analytic chart identity;
- `B=2/(φ+ν)`: a chart balance score;
- `Φ`: a present D4 measurement of D5 option-field quality or foresight;
- `V`: D4 usable means;
- `P_node=ΦV`: the selected normalized conjunctive model, not a uniquely
  derived law of the world.

The chart does not derive ontology, ethics, quantum mechanics, or empirical
conservation. The Titans are selected symbolic roles; ordinary `0` and `1`
remain operands, and projective `∞` belongs only to a declared extended
structure.
