# THE η CONVERSION MAP

One letter, three registers, two different zeros. This document is the bridge
the corpus has cited as missing since 2026-05-04. It states each register from
its verified source, gives the lawful conversions, and — the part that does the
real work — states what **cannot** be converted and why.

**The rule this map enforces:** never write bare `η` in a claim that could be
read in more than one register. Write `η_move`, `η_ratio`, or `η_domain`.

---

## 1 · The three registers, from source

### 1a · `η_move` — the constitutional fence (action register)

Source: `40_THE_LOGARITHMIC_REALIGNMENT.md` §3 (verbatim): *"The live game /
constitutional register is `η_move`: `η_move = 0` names reciprocal
non-extraction, while `η_move > 0` names extraction or closure."*

- **Type:** a binary/ordinal fence on an *action or standing arrangement*.
- **Zero means:** the fence holds — no sustained extraction.
- **This is the R1 non-extraction rule.** It is a *rule*, not a measurement.
- Tier: `[S]` as constitutional structure; the vow itself `[I]`.

### 1b · `η_ratio` — the log-accounting diagnostic (measurement register)

Source: `40_THE_LOGARITHMIC_REALIGNMENT.md` §3 (verbatim):

```
η_ratio ~ |s_extraction| / |s_contribution|
```

with the source's own readings: `< 1` movement toward the equator; **`= 1`
trophic balance**; `> 1` movement away; `→ ∞` pure extraction ("ground
negation").

- **Type:** a nonnegative real; a *diagnostic*, not a rule.
- **Break-even is 1, not 0.** `η_ratio = 0` would mean zero extraction term —
  a stronger and *different* condition than balance.
- Undefined at `(0,0)` — a domain may assign a reporting convention, never an
  analytic value (`30_OPERATIONAL_DEFINITIONS.md` §4).
- Tier: `[A]` as arithmetic on declared inputs.

### 1c · `η_domain` — the per-study statistic (empirical register)

Source: `30_OPERATIONAL_DEFINITIONS.md` §5 (verbatim): *"There is no
context-free universal `η` meter."* A study must first declare six inputs —
**bearer set · depleted resource/option · counterfactual baseline · payer and
beneficiary · custody/consent/reversibility/exit · time horizon and
uncertainty** — and *"only then may it define a nonnegative extraction
statistic `η_domain`. The normative constraint `η_domain = 0` is a declared
Justice condition, not an empirical theorem and not sufficient for justice by
itself."*

- **Type:** a study-scoped statistic; changes when any of the six inputs change.
- **Zero means:** the *chosen* justice condition of that study is met.
- Tier: `[D/C]` per study.

## 2 · The lawful conversions

**C-1 · `η_ratio` → `η_move` (diagnostic evidence for the fence).** `[S]`
A *sustained* `η_ratio > 1` over a declared horizon, against a declared
baseline, is **evidence of** `η_move > 0` for that arrangement. It is evidence,
not identity: the fence judges the *arrangement*; the ratio measures one
accounting window. Formally, the honest bridge is directional:

> sustained `η_ratio > 1` ⟹ prima-facie `η_move > 0` (rebuttable by the six
> declared inputs — e.g. consent, reversibility, or a baseline error).
> `η_ratio ≤ 1` in one window ⟹ **nothing** about `η_move` (a window is not a
> horizon).

**C-2 · `η_domain` → `η_move` (measurement instantiates the fence).** `[S]`
`η_move` for a concrete case is *operationalised by* an `η_domain` whose six
inputs are declared and defensible. `η_domain = 0` under an honest declaration
is what "the fence holds, here" *means* empirically. The conversion inherits
every weakness of the declaration — six contestable inputs, so `η_domain = 0`
is never more certain than its worst input.

**C-3 · `η_domain` ↔ `η_ratio` (two statistics, one study).** `[A]`
Within a single study whose declared inputs identify log-extraction and
log-contribution terms, `η_ratio` may be computed *as* that study's `η_domain`
(or alongside it). Then and only then do the two numbers live on one chart —
because the declaration, not the letter, is what put them there.

## 3 · The non-conversion theorems (where the map says NO)

**N-1 · The zeros do not translate.** `η_move = 0` (fence holds) corresponds to
`η_ratio = 1` (balance), **not** `η_ratio = 0`. Any sentence equating "η = 0"
across these registers commits an off-by-one at the exact point the constitution
cares about. Source, verbatim: *"Do not compare the two thresholds as if they
were the same number"* (`40:165`). `[S]`

**N-2 · No window-to-verdict promotion.** A single-window `η_ratio` — however
extreme — never *is* an `η_move` verdict. The fence is a claim about a
sustained arrangement; promotion requires the horizon to be declared and
crossed. `[S]`

**N-3 · No study-free fence claims.** "This system has `η = 0`" with no
declared bearer set, baseline, and horizon is not a claim in any register — it
is the *paraphrase drift* the corpus convicted on 2026-07-10. The fence without
a declaration is a vow; a vow is `[I]` and must say so. `[S]`

**N-4 · No aggregation across bearers.** The extraction law's verified form is
per-bearer (*"no scalar sum across incommensurable bearers is licensed"*). No
register of η may be summed over bearers into one number that then claims the
fence — `η_domain` is per-declaration, and a declaration with a laundered sum
is not honest. `[S]`

**N-5 · η is not K\*.** The retired token `K*` fused this family with selection
complexity and Kolmogorov complexity — different units, no conversion exists.
See `00_KSTAR_DISAMBIGUATION.md`; the identity `A7 = K* = 0` is FG-4. `[S]`

## 4 · Worked reading (one arrangement, three registers)

A marketplace charges a 2% fee on transactions.

- **`η_domain`:** declare the six inputs (bearers = sellers; resource = margin;
  baseline = the no-platform counterfactual including discovery costs; payer =
  seller; custody/exit = seller may leave freely; horizon = 3 years). If the
  fee ≤ the value the platform demonstrably creates for sellers against that
  baseline, the study's `η_domain = 0` holds *for that declaration*.
- **`η_ratio`:** compute log-extraction (fees taken) against log-contribution
  (value created) per window. Ratio hovering at 1 = trophic balance; a drift
  above 1 across windows is the early-warning signal.
- **`η_move`:** the constitutional question — is the *arrangement* one of
  sustained extraction? Answered by C-1/C-2: sustained ratio-drift plus a
  defensible declaration showing net depletion ⟹ the fence is breached; the
  fee must fall or the arrangement must end. One bad quarter proves nothing
  (N-2); a slogan proves nothing (N-3).

## 5 · What this map does not do

It does not decide which application-specific number a zero-extraction rule
may wear, does not touch retired ambiguous aliases, and does not upgrade any study. It
is a translation table with refusals — the conversions in §2 are the only ones
licensed, and §3 is the part to cite when someone equates the zeros.
