# 25. The Steel Thread — Kintsugi Tombstone

This stable path preserves the provenance of an April 2026 attempt to join
mathematical chart facts, game definitions, biological interpretation, and
empirical claims into one “steel” derivation. The attempt is **not** current
authority.

## The crack

The original thread silently changed claim types. Among other things, it treated
a selected projective chart as uniquely forced, treated a coordinate identity as
a world constraint, promoted defined payoffs into universal cooperation laws,
identified a zero-valued chart expression with zero physical maintenance cost,
and treated the now-retired GFS study as a prospective tier-upgrade mechanism.
Those steps do not follow from the mathematics.

## What survives

On the named chart, for `θ∈(0,π)`,
`φ=cot(θ/2)`, `ν=tan(θ/2)`, `φν=1`, and `B=sin θ≤1` with equality at
`θ=π/2`. These are `[A]` analytic facts. Any interpretation of `B` as
flourishing, any transfer to independently measured `Φ` and `V`, and any claim
about physical forces or consciousness requires its own bridge, evidence tier,
prediction, and kill criterion.

## Kill criterion

This tombstone may be replaced only by a derivation in which every arrow is
typed, every nondefinitional bridge is independently evidenced, and no empirical
failure is converted into support for an unchanged higher-level claim.
