### Probability fence

For a normalized state `ψ` over a domain and measurable event `A`, a valid
illustrative probability statement is

\[
\mathbb P_\psi(A)=\int_A |\psi(s)|^2\,ds,
\qquad o\sim\mathbb P_\psi.
\]

One samples an outcome from the probability measure. One does not sample from
the normalization scalar `∫|ψ|² ds = 1`. The analytic identity
`φν = 1` in the reciprocal chart is not the Born rule and supplies no quantum
probabilities.
