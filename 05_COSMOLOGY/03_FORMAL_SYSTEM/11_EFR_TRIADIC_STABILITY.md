# TRIADIC STABILITY — KINTSUGI TOMBSTONE

> **[金] Kintsugi seam — uniqueness was assumed, not proved.** The former
> document claimed that one primitive is sterile, two are unstable, three are
> uniquely stable, and every `N≥4` system is redundant. Its case split begged
> the question, counted relations as new primitives when convenient, and used
> false group-theoretic claims. The original text remains recoverable from Git
> blob `a26b33761b1df597f7fa6477374ef1fd9fd709ec`; it is not live authority.

## What broke

The legacy argument fails by counterexample and invalid inference:

1. A one-generator system can have arbitrarily rich derived structure; calling
   every such structure “the same type” merely defines emergence away.
2. Commutativity does not collapse a two-generator algebra to monism, and a
   non-commutativity relation is not automatically a third primitive.
3. Four-element and larger closed structures need not contain a redundant
   primitive. Finite groups such as `ℤ₄` and `ℤ₅` already defeat the claimed
   counting argument; `ℤ₅` has no proper nontrivial subgroup.
4. Closure of `{0,∞}` under inversion does not force adjoining `1`; inversion
   exchanges `0` and `∞`, while `±1` are independent fixed points.
5. `⊙=•×○` and `1=0×∞` are emblems, not field arithmetic or derivations of a
   unique ontology.

Therefore no theorem here establishes that reality, number, action, or the
D-register scaffold must be triadic or sevenfold. The former `[S]` proof claim
is retracted.

## What survives

Emergentism may select `{0,1,∞}` as a useful projective normalization and
symbolic frame `[I]`. It may also compare that selected triad with genuine
Möbius or projective facts, provided the comparison does not upgrade the
selection into necessity.

The active boundaries are:

- ordinary `0` and `1` remain numbers and operands;
- `∞` is a projective point in the declared extended setting;
- the Titan roles are selected, not forced;
- D0–D6 is a selected interpretive scaffold;
- `r₆:D6↝D0` is an interpretive non-`μ` return, not literal identity;
- no triadic claim derives `η=0`, Power-Max, Justice, or empirical emergence.

## Evidence and kill criterion

- `[A]` Specific algebraic or projective statements may be proved inside their
  declared structures.
- `[I]` The Titan correspondence is an Emergentist modeling choice.
- `[C]` Any claim that the triad predicts a real system requires independent
  evidence and a discriminator.

**Kill criterion:** any live citation that calls the triad unique, irreducible,
forced, or the mathematical foundation of ethics fails this tombstone.
