A brand consultant would say "the brand should evolve with the company." For Skyzai, **the brand cannot evolve faster than the constitution**:

- A visual refresh that softens evidence (e.g., glassy receipts) would violate A7 — not allowed.
- A voice update that uses "guaranteed" — not allowed (η=0 has no guarantees, only mechanisms).
- A logo simplification that removes the schematic structure of `Zero-Sum Resolution Equation` — not allowed; the schematic IS the equation.
- A theme expansion to "playful" mode for marketing — not allowed if it weakens evidence presentation. (Three themes is enough; the trade-offs between them are constitutional.)

Brand evolution is constrained by constitutional evolution. The brand changes when the constitution changes — and the constitution changes only through K2 signing the change as a constitutional amendment, which by K2 doctrine is extremely rare.
