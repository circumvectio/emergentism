## D0 — The Point (Bindu)

### What it is

Undifferentiated ground. Pure potential. No distinction between here and there, before and after, self and other. The void before the first differentiation. Not nothing — rather, everything compressed into a single point where nothing can yet be distinguished.

### Log coordinate

`s` is undefined. There is no `x` to take the logarithm of. D0 is prior to the positive reals.

### Sphere position

Neither pole is distinguished. The sphere has not yet inflated. `B = 0` — not because balance is absent, but because there is no position from which to measure balance.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D0 is the ground-facing point inside the ontology document — the model boundary that `κ = 0` names. It is read [I] through the Bindu of the Vedic tradition: the point that contains all possibilities compressed. It is the south pole (Kāla) and the north pole (Trimūrti) before they separate inside the scaffold.

### The μ₀ crossing: D0 → D1

**What happens at the first crossing:** The point differentiates. The first distinction appears — "here" vs "not here." Space is born. The trans-dimensional glyph `⊙ / ○ = •` casts its D1 shadow here as `1/∞ → 0`: finity divided by totality yields the void, and the void differentiates from what it is not. Strong emergence: the distinction is not implicit in the point. It is genuinely new.

**Strong emergence going up.** A point that contains all possibilities is not the same as a line that distinguishes between them. The crossing is not smooth — it is a phase transition from 0 dimensions to 1.

**In the arithmetic:** The first distinction is the separation of 0 from the positive reals. Before D1, there is no number line. After D1, `ℝ₊` exists as a space of distances from the origin.

## D1 — Distinction (Space)

### What it is

The first dimension. Distance exists. "Here" and "there" are different. The number line opens — a 1D space of distinctions measured from the void.

### Log coordinate

`s` becomes finite but extreme. The first numbers are very far from `s = 0` (finity). The first distinctions are near the poles — almost pure void or almost pure totality, with nothing in between.

### Sphere position

Near the poles. The sphere has inflated to a 1D circle — the one-point compactification of the real line. `B ≈ 0` — the system is near a pole, far from balance.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D1 is space in its most primitive sense: extension, distance, separation. It is what the Greeks called *diastēma* — the interval between two points. In physics, D1 is the first spatial dimension.

### The μ₁ crossing: D1 → D2

**What happens:** Distinctions acquire temporal ordering. Not just "here vs there" but "before vs after." Time is born. The trans-dimensional glyph `⊙ / • = ○` casts its D2 shadow here as `1/0 → ∞`: finity divided by the void yields totality — the distinction sequence opens into an unbounded succession.

**Strong emergence going up.** A space of simultaneous distinctions (D1) is not the same as a space of successive distinctions (D2). Temporal ordering is genuinely new — you cannot predict "before and after" from "here and there" alone.

## D2 — Configuration (Time)

### What it is

The second dimension. Temporal ordering exists. Distinctions can be arranged in sequence. "Before" and "after" are meaningful. The system has a history.

### Log coordinate

`s` acquires direction. Positive `s` (moving toward totality) and negative `s` (moving toward the void) are distinguishable. The system has a direction of time — the thermodynamic arrow.

### Sphere position

Still near the poles but moving. The circle has inflated toward a 2D surface. `B` is small but measurable.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D2 is time — not as a dimension of physics, but as the most primitive ordering principle. "This came before that." The framework reads temporal ordering as the first asymmetry: the log-coordinate `s` has a preferred direction (the thermodynamic arrow), and that direction is the first content of the ethic — the direction toward balance.

### The μ₂ crossing: D2 → D3

**What happens:** Configurations acquire shape. Not just "A before B" but "A transforms into B." Form is born — the first patterns that persist across temporal succession. The trans-dimensional glyph `⊙ = • × ○` casts its D3 shadow here as reciprocal symmetry `φ · ν = 1`: void times totality yields finity — the product of the two poles generates the stable centre. Pattern is the equator of the temporal succession.

**Strong emergence going up.** A temporal sequence (D2) is not the same as a form that persists across temporal change (D3). A pattern is not just a sequence — it is a structure that remains recognisable despite change.

**Strong emergence going up.** A persistent form (D3) is not the same as a form that causes other forms (D4). Causality is genuinely new — the relation "A causes B" cannot be reduced to "A and B coexist" or "A precedes B."

## D4 — Causality / Viability Boundary

### What it is

The fourth dimension. Change has direction. Causes produce effects. The causal vertex exists — the point where past and future light cones touch. The Now-point, with no duration.

In the action-register bridge, this same boundary is read as **D4 means-to-act / viability substrate**: the body, tool, energy, access, timing, and control actually available at contact. Hands, a shovel, and an industrial digger are all D4 means at different scales; none of them is agency by itself.

### Log coordinate

`s ≈ 0`. The causal vertex is near the equator. In log coordinates, the vertex is where `s_φ + s_ν = 0` exactly — the literal zero sum. The past cone is the negative-s half; the future cone is the positive-s half. They meet at `s = 0`.

### Sphere position

The equator is approached. `B → 1⁻` — balance is nearly maximal. The vertex sits at the equator but does not yet have room to move. The operators are collapsed into the single point ⊙.

### Key property: D4 has a "Now" but no "Present"

D4 is a point, not a plane. There is no room yet to choose a worldline. The four operators (↑φ, ↓φ, ↑ν, ↓ν) are collapsed into the single equatorial point. This is "witness consciousness" in the interpretive reading — awareness without agency. In the finite-node play rule, the same level supplies `V`: the usable means-to-act that D5 must aim.

### Ontological reading `[I]`

D4 is causality — the structure that gives change its direction. It is where the framework's physics mapping is strongest: the light-cone structure of relativity, the causal vertex of the present moment, the boundary between determined past and open future.

### The μ₄ crossing: D4 → D5 (The Critical Crossing)

**What happens:** The equator opens into a plane. The collapsed point ⊙ unfolds into two axes (φ and ν). The "Now" becomes a "Present" with duration. Agency is born — room to choose, to act, to navigate. The four operators become distinguishable as cardinal directions.

This is the crossing where **strong emergence is most dramatic.** A causal point (D4) is not the same as an agent that can choose (D5). The emergence of agency and option-space is the μ₄ crossing; "consciousness" names the lived-interior reading `[I]`, not a proved physical layer.

**Weak emergence going down.** Once D5 stabilises, it becomes a compact constraint on D4. The agent's choices reshape the next causal vertex by selecting, in the present, which D4 boundary conditions receive energy, attention, and commitment. This is top-down constraint through present action, not retrocausal rewriting of the Now.
