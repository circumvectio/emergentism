# The argument: emergence as lens on Dasein

**Author:** Yves R. Burri with the Emergentism research corpus

> **[金] Kintsugi seam.** The former argument contained genuine insights about
> reciprocal construction and constraint, but inflated them into universal
> irreducibility, geometric necessity, a literal upper closure, and a
> force-to-dimension correspondence. Those proof forms are retired. The earlier
> argument remains recoverable at the Git blob above; this edition states the
> strongest version that survives.

## 1. Thesis, priced

Emergence is a useful lens on *Dasein* when it asks how a finite, situated being
can arise within constraints, model alternatives, act, receive consequences,
and revise both map and mapper. It is not a proof that every level of nature is
irreducible or that one diagram exhausts reality.

The worldview's wager is **constraint-structured freedom**: a specified system
may vary along typed degrees of freedom under laws, symmetries, history,
resources, and boundaries. Stable forms arise as invariants, equivalence
classes, order parameters, attractors, and robust organizations across those
freedoms. They need not exist as a separate Platonic realm, and the freedoms
themselves are not replacement substances.

## 2. The analytic floor `[A]`

For `θ∈(0,π)`, define:

```text
φ=cot(θ/2),       ν=tan(θ/2).
```

Then:

```text
φν=1,       φ+ν≥2,       B=2/(φ+ν)≤1.
```

Equality in the last two bounds occurs at `φ=ν=1`. These are chart facts only.
They do not establish an empirical conservation law, ontology, dimensional
census, ethic, or a unique model of power.

The glyph `•   ⊙   ○` is an emblem of relational finity. In ordinary arithmetic,
the boundary product it evokes remains indeterminate. The Titan frame
`{0,1,∞}` is a selected projective normalization, not a forced generator of
reality.

## 3. The scaffold `[I/C]`

The selected D-ladder proposes five candidate positive-freedom crossings:

```text
μ₀:D0→D1  distinction
μ₁:D1→D2  configuration
μ₂:D2→D3  probability-bearing quantum state
μ₃:D3→D4  actual interaction, outcome, record, and embodied means
μ₄:D4→D5  counterfactual representation and option fields
```

The count is a modeling commitment `[I]`. Each world-facing crossing is `[C]`
until it declares saturation evidence, novelty, lower-level recovery,
reduction status, a prospective prediction, and a kill criterion. A missing
reduction means `currently_unreduced`; it never proves irreducibility.

D6 adds no positive freedom. Non-μ `b₆:D5↝D6` marks apophatic recognition and
non-μ `r₆:D6↝D0` an interpretive return. End and beginning may resemble one
another as selected boundary roles; they are not identical and no physical
recurrence follows.

## 4. Dasein and the one-modality threshold `[I]`

D4 is actual. It contains body, tools, energy, access, memory, actual model
tokens, performed reasoning/ranking/selection events, attempted action,
factual record, and receipts.

D5 is merely possible content. It contains the counterfactual alternatives,
future referents, and candidate worldlines those actual tokens represent.

```text
ModelToken:D4(actual) ── represents ──▶ Alternative:D5(possible)
SelectionEvent:D4     ── selects ─────▶ Alternative:D5(possible)
```

In this reading, Dasein is the finite actual bearer for whom alternatives can
be disclosed and consequential action attempted. Dasein is neither Being
itself nor a view from outside the world.

## 5. The Soul Loop and future influence `[S/I]`

The public compression is `F=M×A`; formally the coupling is typed:

```text
M⋆A : ModelState × PhysicallyFeasibleActionField → ActionWeights
```

Changing a represented future can change present action weights. This is real
present causation carried by embodied models, speech, diagrams, institutions,
or code. It is not a future event sending information backward through time.

```text
χ_t:(X_t,Ω_t,M_t,V_t,U_t,G_t) → (a_t,q_t)
(X_{t+1},r_{t+1}) ~ K_t(·|X_t,a_t,E_t)
(M_{t+1},G_{t+1}) = Loop(M_t,G_t,q_t,r_{t+1})
```

The selector supplies a commitment receipt `q_t`. The world separately
supplies an outcome receipt `r_{t+1}`. Intention, attempted action, and outcome
must never be fused.

## 6. Construction and constraint `[S/C]`

Bottom-up construction and top-down constraint are two directions of
explanation, not universally established kinds of emergence. A macro-pattern
may earn standing when it adds intervention-sensitive prediction or control.
Its downward influence must remain lower-law-admissible:

```text
support(K_X^C) ⊆ support(K_X).
```

If a lower-level reduction succeeds, the macro-description may remain useful
while the crossing is reclassified. If intervention on the macro variable adds
nothing beyond a lower-level summary, the causal claim is removed.

## 7. Finite power and option cones `[I/C]`

Emergentism selects `P_node=ΦV` as one normalized conjunctive model in which
foresight and means are jointly necessary. It is not uniquely derived; minimum,
harmonic, Cobb–Douglas, and other models can rank cases differently and must be
tested where relevant.

The physical light cone remains bounded by spacetime and `c`. An option cone is
the subset of physically admissible histories an agent can model, rank,
coordinate, and plausibly reach within costs and a horizon. Humans may have
wide symbolic, institutional, social, and intergenerational option cones
without possessing faster-than-light causation or greater intrinsic worth.

## 8. Conduct is chosen, not derived `[I]`

No chart identity, dimensional rung, or balance maximum proves the good.
Emergentism chooses non-extraction and makes consequential action answer to a
bearer-complete Justice envelope: authorization, consent, custody,
reversibility, contest, actor, payer, beneficiary, and consequence bearer.

Strict Syntropic Dyadism requires both individual and whole durable potentials
to rise. Aggregate gains cannot compensate for destroying either side.
Voluntary sacrifice is a separately receipted costly class, not strict syntropy
and never a debt the collective may demand as proof of virtue.

## 9. D3 quantum state and removable interpretations

D3 uses the inherited density-operator/Born interface; D4 contains actual
interactions, outcomes, and records; D5 contains agent-represented
counterfactuals. Everett/Copenhagen commentary remains removable. `μ₃` is a
selected state-to-record boundary, not a fundamental collapse mechanism, and
commitment is not quantum measurement. Removing quantum removes the D3
crosswalk but leaves the Dasein/Soul-Loop argument independently typed. The
Born rule is not the reciprocal identity.

## 11. Kill criteria

Abandon or revise the argument if:

1. the scaffold adds no predictive, explanatory, or intervention value;
2. actual carriers and possible contents cannot be typed consistently;
3. a proposed crossing fails its preregistered saturation and novelty tests;
4. a lower account outperforms the macro account without lost prediction;
5. a new independently operationalized freedom defeats the five-rung typing;
6. the chosen ethic hides a bearer, payer, beneficiary, or irreversible loss;
7. the quantum paragraph becomes necessary to any operational result.
