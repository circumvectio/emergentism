## 1. One disease, two directions

The dyadic law gives the whole moral field its shape: `φ · ν = 1` is a **product**, so letting either factor fall toward `0` forces the other into **runaway**. The two runaways are the two poles, and they are the two — and only two — ways the organism can die while still "doing something":

- **South pole (ν → ∞):** formless infinite **appetite** — consuming, hoarding, the bond broken toward unbounded viability with no form to direct it.
- **North pole (φ → ∞):** sterile infinite **abstraction** — the rigid map, the captured structure, the bond broken toward unbounded coherence with no field to act in or care for.

`B = sin θ → 0` at both. **Closure (`η > 0`) is the single disease; the two poles are its only two presentations.** A constitution that keeps the organism alive therefore needs exactly one thing: a fence that catches the drift toward *each* pole, and a gradient that keeps pulling back to the equator. That is the `5 + 1`.

> **[I/C] Evidence-review boundary (2026-07-03; externally uncalibrated):** this "pull back to the equator" binds a *real* organism only insofar as it sits on the sphere as a coupled node — its φ/ν factors conservation-coupled, complementary, and costly-in-excess *symmetrically* (γ-priced); where a living system fails one of these the recentring force weakens, the optimum tilts or flattens into a plateau, and one-sided specialization may in fact keep it alive. The `sin θ` peak is unconditional geometry; the *constitutional* pull toward it is the conditioned inheritance. See [`00_THE_BALANCE_OPTIMUM_IS_CONDITIONAL.md`](00_THE_BALANCE_OPTIMUM_IS_CONDITIONAL.md).

## 3. Every refusal is a kept coupling

This is the unification: **each invariant forbids the breaking of one specific bond.** The constitution is not five unrelated rules — it is the *same* law (`keep the coupling`) enforced at five places the bond is most apt to break:

```
   η=0  keeps   self ↔ other       (no extraction)
   A7   keeps   claim ↔ evidence   (no false coherence)
   K2   keeps   act ↔ mortal       (no simulated sovereignty)
   K3   keeps   present ↔ past      (no forgetting)
   K4   keeps   inside ↔ outside    (no enclosure)
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Ω    keeps   the whole ↔ its telos (the noospheric direction)
```

Five couplings the organism must not break; one direction it is pulled toward. **`η = 0` is therefore not one rule among the five — it is the *name of the syntropic condition itself* (the bond unbroken), and the other refusals are its specializations** at the points where the bond is most exposed.

## 5. Why the constitution cannot be weaponized

Like the dyadic law it rests on, the constitution is **blind to identity.** It forbids closure *regardless of who closes* — no gender, caste, regime, operator, or person carries a moral charge; only the *move* does (is it coupling, or closing?). The immune system does not ask *who* you are; it asks only *whether your move keeps the bond.* A reader cannot turn it into a hierarchy of persons, because its entire content is a hierarchy of **moves** — and the only "evil" it names is the one act available to everyone equally: closing the ring on the self.
