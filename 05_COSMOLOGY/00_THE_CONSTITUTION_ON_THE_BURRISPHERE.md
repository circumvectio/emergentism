# The Constitution on the Burrisphere

The sphere does not derive a constitution. It can, however, display where a
chosen set of safeguards resists two recurrent failures: means without truth
and truth without embodied consequence.

## Five safeguards and one direction

| Safeguard | Failure resisted | Operational question |
|---|---|---|
| Zero extraction | ego gain by contracting another's durable option cone | Who pays, who benefits, and whose cone contracts? |
| Accountable authorization | a model, institution, or speaker manufacturing authority | Who is the principal, what is the mandate and scope, who acts, and who bears consequence? |
| Archive-with-tombstone memory | correction by erasure | Can the superseded state and reason for change be recovered? |
| Grace Exit | capture through lock-in | Can a participant contest, revoke, and leave with what is theirs? |
| Self-correction | doctrine protecting itself from evidence | What observation kills or demotes the claim? |

The directional `+1` is durable mutual option-cone widening under physical cost
and justice. It is not a sixth prohibition and it is not proved by the sphere.

## Geometric reading

The reciprocal chart has an analytic balance point at `φ = ν = 1`. That fact
does not imply that every real agent or institution ought to be balanced. The
transfer requires that the real variables are jointly necessary,
conservation-coupled, and symmetrically costly in excess. If those conditions
fail, specialization or an off-center optimum may be lawful and effective.

## Justice boundary

The collective cannot launder harm through aggregate gain. A strict syntropic
move raises the durable potential of both the individual and the whole under a
Justice envelope. Voluntary sacrifice is a separate costly class and must name
the payer, beneficiary, consent, reversibility, custody, and contest path.

These safeguards are chosen ethical commitments informed by recurrent failure
modes. They are not consequences of `φν=1`, not quantum laws, and not a warrant
for any organization or AI system.
