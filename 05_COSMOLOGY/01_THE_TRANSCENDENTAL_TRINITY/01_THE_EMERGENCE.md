# THE EMERGENCE

## D0 → D1 without forced genesis

> **[金] Kintsugi seam.** The former narrative made an angle move before a
> geometry existed, inflated a point into a sphere, generated the unit, and
> treated the animation as cosmogenesis. That construction was circular. The
> repair separates analytic chart motion from candidate emergence in reality.

## 1. Construction is not creation

After a sphere, projection convention, and coordinate domain have been chosen,
varying `θ` traces positions in that model. At `θ=π/2`, the reciprocal
coordinates satisfy `φ=ν=1`. This is an analytic consequence of the definitions,
not the birth of arithmetic `1`, a physical event, or evidence that D0 produced
`S²`.

The projective point `∞` belongs to a selected compactification. Ordinary
division by zero remains undefined in the real or complex field; a projective
point does not turn `1/0` into ordinary arithmetic.

## 2. The candidate μ-crossing

Emergentism uses `μ₀` as a label for the candidate passage from one
ground-facing object role to a first positive distinction. The sovereign Titan
seats describe that boundary from the metalanguage; they are not three D0
inhabitants:

```text
D0  one ground-facing object role
μ₀  candidate crossing
D1  distinction
```

This label is `[I/C]`, not a theorem from geometry. `μ₀` is the **origin
aperture**, so it does not claim that a prior positive freedom saturated. Its
record must instead name the D0 boundary role, the proposed first distinction,
the recovery attempt, reduction status, evidence tier, prediction, and kill
criterion. For `μ₁…μ₄`, a valid crossing record additionally names the lower
register's system boundary, saturation statistic, and threshold. If the alleged
new freedom is recovered from the declared lower model, the crossing is weak
emergence; the scaffold can remain useful without claiming irreducibility.

## 3. One modality throughout

The scaffold uses one fixed type distinction:

- **D4:** causal actuality—embodied carriers, model tokens, selections,
  commitments, actions, and records;
- **D5:** merely possible counterfactual content represented and ranked by an
  actual D4 system.

No possible D5 content acts by itself. An actual agent may represent an option,
commit it through D4 means, and receive a separate D4 outcome receipt.

## 4. No literal return cycle

Five candidate positive-freedom crossings are labeled `μ₀…μ₄`. The later
boundary edge `b₆:D5→D6` is not `μ₅` and opens no new positive freedom. The
interpretive edge `r₆:D6⇝D0` names only a boundary-role comparison:

```text
D6 ≠ D0
r₆ is not identity, recurrence, or general self-similarity
r₆ is not a physical cycle
the end does not become the beginning
```

Practices of release or return may project onto this topology `[I]`; they do not
prove cosmological recurrence, awareness survival, or identity through death.

## 5. What survives

The strong surviving insight is methodological: for `μ₁…μ₄`, when a descriptive
register saturates, look for a testable new freedom—but demand recovery tests and
a kill criterion before calling the transition strong emergence. For `μ₀`, look
instead for an operationally testable first distinction without inventing a
lower positive freedom to saturate. The sphere can remain a useful selected
visualization without becoming its own cause.
