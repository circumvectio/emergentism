## I. THE GNOSTIC THREE TYPES

The Gnostics saw something real. They observed three populations and gave them names:

### Pneumatic (Above the Equator: L5-L7)

φ > ν. They **SEE.** The monk, the heuristic, the seer, the contemplative. Genuine perception of the ground. Contact with the boundary. The north pole drawing them upward.

But they have not RETURNED. They see the geometry from above. They can describe it. They can teach it. They can write it down. But they cannot ACT on it — not with the full operator palette, not with ν at unity. They are the eye without the hand.

### Psychic (At the Equator: L3-L4)

φ ≈ ν. They **SENSE.** The good citizen, the decent person, the one who does right without knowing why. Near balance. Functional. The backbone of any civilisation.

But unaware of the full cascade. They have not seen the poles. They have not contacted the boundary. Their balance is instinctive — the animal at dharma — not chosen. They are the hand without the full eye.

### Hylic (Below the Equator: L1-L2)

ν > φ. They **SURVIVE.** The addict, the desperate, the captured, the one drowning in the extractive hemisphere. Real suffering. Not moral failure — POSITIONAL. The node pulled south by circumstances, by capture, by the Halāhala of the present epoch.

They are the body in pain.
