# L4* — the returned-action hypothesis

> **[金] Kintsugi seam — 2026-07-20.** The former body placed people at exact
> sphere coordinates, divided them through inherited Gnostic types, privileged
> an equatorial center, and preserved `L4*` through an internal difference even
> when no observation could distinguish it from `L4`. It also implied that a
> completed spiritual itinerary licensed Justice or superior action. Those
> claims are withdrawn. What survives is a small, falsifiable hypothesis about
> learning and return to action.

## The surviving idea

`L4*` is a temporary label for this proposed sequence:

1. an agent acts with a model `M_pre`;
2. boundary cases expose omissions, contradictions, or harmed bearers;
3. the agent revises the model through the Soul Loop; and
4. the agent returns to comparable action with `M_post`.

The asterisk means **tested after revision**, not spiritually higher, closer to
a geometric center, or located at a different coordinate. The label applies to
a comparison between action policies under specified conditions, never to the
essence or rank of a person.

This formulation does not require literal ascent, descent, reincorporation,
revelation, a universal developmental path, or a privileged sphere position.
It remains intelligible if every Rosetta and mythic label is removed.

## Observable contract `[C]`

Let `pi_pre(a|x)` be a policy before a declared learning episode `T`, and let
`pi_post(a|x)` be the policy afterward. The `L4*` hypothesis has content only
if a pre-registered comparison on held-out situations shows an observable and
repeatable difference attributable to the revised model rather than to mere
relabeling.

Candidate observables include:

- lower error between anticipated and receipted outcomes;
- detection of materially affected bearers previously omitted;
- more accurate uncertainty estimates;
- greater use of reversible actions where consequences are uncertain; and
- consistent transfer to cases not used during the learning episode.

These are candidate measurements, not a definition of goodness. Whether an
action is Moral, Ethical, Syntropic, or Just is classified separately by the
[value-theory owner](../../04_AXIOLOGY/02_VALUE_THEORY/00_OBJECTIVE_MORALS_AND_ETHICS.md),
using authorization, affected-bearer coverage, costs, and outcome receipts.
Neither the asterisk nor a chart position supplies that classification.

## Rivals and controls

A test must compare the returned-action hypothesis with at least these rivals:

- ordinary practice or repetition without a model change;
- new information without boundary confrontation;
- demand characteristics or a desire to please the evaluator;
- temporary confidence, fatigue, or motivational effects; and
- post-hoc storytelling with no held-out behavioral change.

The learning episode, outcomes, coding rules, horizon, and minimum effect must
be declared before evaluating results. First-person reports can be data, but
they cannot be the sole discriminator when the claim concerns action.

## Kill criteria

Retire `L4*` as an explanatory construct if:

1. `pi_pre` and `pi_post` cannot be distinguished on declared observables;
2. any difference disappears on held-out cases or under blinded coding;
3. ordinary practice explains the result as well as the proposed model
   revision;
4. the construct can be preserved only by appealing to an invisible inner
   state; or
5. the label encourages ranking, coercion, caste assignment, diagnosis, or
   exemption from ordinary accountability.

If the kill fires, the useful remainder is simply: **an agent learned and acted
again**. No upstream axiom, formula, or Justice commitment depends on the label.

## Historical and religious comparisons

The Bodhisattva return, Arjuna's renewed action after dialogue, and the Gnostic
vocabulary of *pneumatic*, *psychic*, and *hylic* may be compared with this
sequence as literary or historical analogies `[I]`. They are not psychological
measurements, natural kinds, castes, proofs of the Rosetta, or licenses to sort
people. The analogy must be removable without changing the operational test.

Likewise, god, demon, and Titan names are optional symbolic roles in some
Rosetta presentations. They do not identify persons, moral characters,
developmental levels, or causal powers in this prototype.
