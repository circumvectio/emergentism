reading of Nietzsche with empirical prehistory, a forced three-substrate
architecture, unlimited-network claims, and operational instructions. Those

## What survives `[C]`

The tightrope may be used as a removable literary correspondence: finite agents
can face tension between representational coherence (`Φ`) and embodied means
(`V`). Under an independently evidenced conjunctive coupling, severe loss of
either factor can contract the histories an agent can model, coordinate, and
reach. A present model of a desired or feared future may reweight a present
choice; the future does not physically signal backward.

This correspondence does **not** establish:

- that prehistoric populations occupied an Emergentist equator;
- that agriculture caused a universal one-directional fall;
- that balance is optimal outside the declared coupling and Justice conditions;
- that any biological, digital, mechanical, or other substrate trio is forced;
- that a mesh can scale without limit or without hierarchy;
- that an AI system is a sovereign, charioteer, or source of authority; or
- that Emergentism supplies a deployment or release plan.

Remove this page and the operational calculus remains unchanged.
