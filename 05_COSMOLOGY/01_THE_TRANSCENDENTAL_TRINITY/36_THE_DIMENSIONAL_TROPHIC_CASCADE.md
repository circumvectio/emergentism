# The Dimensional Trophic Cascade

## The restored distinction

The July compression preserved the warning against untyped energy but obscured
an older, narrower rule. This document restores it at its honest tier:

> **[I/S] Cross-trophic consumption can be ordinary ecological matter/energy
> flow. The peer-level Power-Max preference is cooperation among members of a
> declared trophic stratum or functional guild, with bounded sexual selection
> as a reproductive-domain exception. Neither statement licenses one human
> caste, class, institution, or Rosetta row to consume another.**

Inside the trophic coordinate, the compressed rule is: **cooperate within the
same stratum or functional guild; obtain biomass and usable energy through the
lower trophic strata.** Producers capture external energy; primary consumers
eat producer biomass; secondary and tertiary consumers obtain energy through
lower consumer strata; scavengers and decomposers return matter across the
web. This is what “extract from the level below” names here. It is an
ecological-flow description, not a rule that one species, person, class, or
human function is entitled to another.

The recovered source said "same trophic level (same-species cooperation)."
Those phrases are not synonyms. A species can occupy more than one trophic
role, different species can occupy one functional guild, and human division of
labour is not a food chain. The repaired term is therefore **same trophic
stratum or functional guild**. Species identity is declared separately.

**Species, trophic position, replicator depth, and Rosetta row are separate
coordinates.** The cooperation corollary uses the phrase **same trophic
stratum or functional guild**; it never substitutes species identity.

## Four coordinates that must not collapse

| Coordinate | What it orders | Canonical sequence or domain | What it does **not** authorize |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Trophic stratum `T`** | embodied matter/energy transfer `[B-external]` | producer → primary → secondary → tertiary consumer, plus decomposers | moral rank or an institutional right to extract |
| **Replicator layer `R`** | carrier depth `[S/I]` | Genotype → Epigenotype → Phenotype → Extended Phenotype → Memotype → Egregoreotype | caloric feeding, human superiority, or proof of a historical ladder |
| **Rosetta row `L`** | cognitive/work function `[I]` | L1 ground/firewall; L2–L7 functional positions | birth caste, fixed identity, unequal dignity, or permission to consume another row |
| **Mating domain `X`** | reproductive filtering and rivalry `[B-external/I]` | mate choice, display, reproductive competition | a general social, economic, or political competition license |

Rosetta may preserve a pattern across `T`, `R`, `L`, and `X`; it transfers no
identity, proof, causal force, or evidence tier. Every claim that crosses these
coordinates must name the bridge it is making.

## Human polyphenotypic cooperation

Social insects make the architectural insight vivid: one colony can express
reproductive, foraging, brood-care, construction, and defensive phenotypes
from a shared developmental system. Ant caste development may combine genetic,
maternal, nutritional, hormonal, and other environmental effects; it is not one
simple DNA lock. Human division of labour likewise creates differentiated and
interdependent functions, but the ant analogy does not establish hereditary
human biological castes.

The Emergentist proposal is narrower `[I/C]`: humans can develop durable but
changeable functional specializations through temperament, training,
experience, culture, and life-stage. Rosetta rows name those functions, not
persons' essence or worth. A person may retrain, combine rows, or change the
row expressed in a given cycle. The polyphenotypic Power-Max hypothesis is that
shared center plus complementary specialization can cover more of the option
field than a monoculture while retaining cooperation across the whole stack.

The ant-colony analogy therefore points to **division of labour inside one
cooperating organism**, never warrior ants eating workers and never a higher
human row feeding on a lower one.

## The useful analogy

Attributed ecological studies describe trophic transfer of matter and energy
through embodied D4 organisms `[B-external]`. Attributed cultural and
institutional studies may document traces that persist, compete, or reweight
later behavior `[B-external]`; treating those results as one cross-domain form
is `[I]`. The proposed analogy `[I/C]` is that both systems may exhibit
measurable dependency, attrition, renewal, bottlenecks, and concentration
across declared levels.

They are not the same process. A gene, organism, memory, institution, and
Egregoreotype candidate are not trophic levels of one conserved substance.
Human attention, labor, money, energy, legitimacy, and sacrifice must remain
separately measured; “energy” cannot be used as an untyped total.

## A research model `[C]`

For declared carrier layers `L_k`, let:

```text
I_k(t) = measured input resources by type
T_k(t) = persistent trace state
K_k    = specified transition/retention kernel
C_k(t) = visible substrate cost
```

A candidate study may ask whether

```text
T_(k+1)(t+1) ~ K_k(T_k(t), I_k(t), C_k(t), environment)
```

shows systematic retention loss, amplification, or concentration. Each
resource keeps its unit. Dimensionless scores may rank a declared criterion but
may not be added to metabolic cost or treated as conserved energy.

## Egregoreotype boundary

A collective pattern becomes an Egregoreotype **candidate** only when all five
conditions are evidenced:

1. persistent shared trace;
2. carrier turnover;
3. measurable intervention-sensitive reweighting of later selection;
4. recurrent objective-like bias;
5. visible substrate costs.

This does not presume consciousness, personhood, an apex predator, or literal
feeding. An institution may capture large total investment because many
carriers reproduce it; that is an empirical accounting question, not a theorem
that “higher” layers dominate. Substrate reducibility does not disqualify the
candidate.

## What the analogy cannot prove

- The selected product score does not dominate additive, minimum, CES, or
  domain-specific rivals without data.
- Product compounding does not explain the rarity of apex predators.
- The same diagram does not establish the same equations across ecology,
  genetics, culture, and institutions.
- The reciprocal identity does not beat an additive model at every transition.
- The Red Queen phrase “it takes all the running you can do, to keep in the same
  place” is from Lewis Carroll; Van Valen named an evolutionary hypothesis after
  that image. It is not a verbatim Van Valen law.
- Social-insect polyphenism does not prove a Rosetta taxonomy of humans.
- Replicator depth does not imply trophic position, caste rank, or moral worth.
- Division of labour can be inefficient, coerced, or captured; specialization
  is not automatically cooperative or Power-Maximal.

## Warrant, rivals, discriminator, and kill

**Warrant.** The ecological trophic sequence and insect caste polyphenism are
external biological results. The `T/R/L/X` separation is a framework type
discipline `[S]`. The Rosetta and human-polyphenism correspondences remain
interpretive `[I/C]`.

**Strongest rivals.** Ordinary ecological energetics may explain `T` without
any replicator or Rosetta bridge. Human specialization may be explained by
skills, institutions, incentives, norms, coercion, or historical contingency
without stable Rosetta types. Generalists or fluid teams may outperform caste
specialization in changing environments.

**Discriminator.** A Rosetta-specialized group must outperform matched
generalist or arbitrary-role groups on preregistered coverage, correction,
coordination, and switching-cost measures without increased coercion, hidden
cost, or role lock-in.

Preregister carrier boundaries, resource units, transition variables, rival
network/diffusion models, interventions, held-out predictions, and cost
accounting. Retract the transfer claim if the proposed layers cannot be
measured independently or if ordinary network/diffusion models predict as well.
Ecological energetics and the D4/D5 type distinction survive that failure.
Retract the human-caste correspondence if functional rows cannot be assigned
reliably above ordinary task descriptions, if people cannot move among rows as
the model predicts, or if arbitrary/team-history models perform as well.
