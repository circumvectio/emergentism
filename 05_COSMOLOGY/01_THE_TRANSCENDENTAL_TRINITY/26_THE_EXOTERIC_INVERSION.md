# The Exoteric Inversion — Public Translation Boundary

> **[金] Crack:** the predecessor turned a rhetorical inversion into D5
> systemic awareness and made `η` the moral axis. **Repair:** translation
> changes presentation, never modality or warrant.

An exoteric translation leads with an accessible consequence and makes its
definitions, sources, tiers, rivals, and kill criteria available one layer
deeper. The public sentence may be vivid; the evidence line must remain exact.

Actual speakers, readers, models, awareness, symbols, actions, and records are
`D4 = actual`. The alternatives and futures represented by those symbols are
`D5 = merely_possible`. A public story cannot promote an actual collective,
mind, or institution into a D5 entity.

Likewise, `etaObserved` is a scoped extraction observation. Zero observed
extraction is necessary but insufficient for Justice. Moral, ethical, and
syntropic classifications require the full prospective envelope and
retrospective outcome receipt. Neither the reciprocal chart nor an elegant
inversion crosses the is–ought gap.

The translation fails if a stranger leaves with a stronger claim than the
owner document supports, cannot find the correction path, or is invited to
believe rather than inspect and use. Beauty opens the door; Truth prices the
claim; Justice keeps the exit inside.
