# Bit to Qubit: Historical Correspondence Boundary

The former essay identified a classical bit with D0, a qubit with D1, a Bloch
sphere with the Burrisphere, measurement with dimensional descent, and
entanglement with shared Emergentist coherence. Those identifications are
withdrawn.

The complete pre-repair text remains recoverable from Git blob
`da9e6935c51a69551274bcdbe50eebc88c73f1df`.

## What is established

- A classical bit has two discrete values.
- A pure qubit state modulo global phase can be represented on the Bloch sphere.
- `S²`, the Riemann sphere, and the Bloch sphere are mathematically related
  surfaces with different distinguished structures and physical meanings.
- Entanglement is not identity of two qubits and does not enable faster-than-light
  signaling.

## Emergentist boundary

The Burrisphere is a selected reciprocal chart. Sharing `S²` does not transfer
the Bloch sphere's physics, establish D0→D1, or make the equator universally
optimal for quantum computation. Quantum measurement is not stereographic
projection, μ, or the Soul Loop's commitment step.

*The same surface can carry different structures; topology alone transfers no
physics.*
