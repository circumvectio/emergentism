# Tat Tvam Asi — bounded correspondence

“Tat tvam asi” belongs to the Chāndogya Upaniṣad. Its traditions of translation
and interpretation are not reducible to an Emergentist equation. The Burri
Sphere, `φν=1`, D4/D5, and the Titan vocabulary do not occur in the source and
do not prove its metaphysics.

Emergentism may nevertheless use the phrase as a contemplative comparison
`[I]`: a finite knower is not exhausted by any one object appearing in its
current model. The pointer can encourage a person to inspect the relation among
model, modeled content, embodiment, and the larger field of consequence. It is
not an empirical identity between self and universe, a proof of Brahman or
Ātman, or a location of consciousness in a sphere.
