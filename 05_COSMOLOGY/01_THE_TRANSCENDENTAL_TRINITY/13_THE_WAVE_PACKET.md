## 1. Physics stays physics

A quantum wave packet is a state represented in a Hilbert space and evolved by
the applicable quantum dynamics. If normalized, its Born probabilities satisfy

```text
𝔓ψ(A) = ∫_A |ψ(s)|² ds
o ~ 𝔓ψ
```

One samples an outcome from the probability measure, never from its scalar
normalization. Gaussian envelopes, phase evolution, Fourier duality, and
uncertainty relations retain their standard meanings and units. None is the
identity `φν=1`, the balance score `B`, a moral law, or proof of a D-register.

## 3. Interpretation fence

Everett's relative-state formulation has no fundamental collapse.
Copenhagen-family actualization language is interpretation-specific. Neither
interpretation adds a literal spatial or spacetime dimension, and they cannot be
stacked as D5 possibility above D4 collapse.

`μ₃` labels the selected state-to-record interface but is not a measurement
operator or collapse mechanism; commitment selects an action and is not quantum
measurement. Anticipated futures influence present action only
through actual model-bearing agents, not through physical backward signaling.

## 4. No consciousness identity

The facts that complex amplitudes rotate, wave packets localize, or projections
relate representations do not show that consciousness is a ray, a sphere, an
equals sign, a quantum state, or a fifth dimension. Such images may remain
poetic Rosetta projections `[I/C]` only when they carry an explicit nonidentity
fence and no evidentiary weight.

Packet spreading or decoherence also supplies no evidence for survival of
personal identity, return to a pure wave, entropy-awareness convergence, or a
literal `D6=D0` cycle.
