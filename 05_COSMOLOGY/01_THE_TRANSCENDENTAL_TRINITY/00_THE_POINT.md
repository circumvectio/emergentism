# THE POINT

## 1. What the mathematics says

A mathematical point has position in a specified space and no extension. That
fact is `[A]` only after the ambient mathematical structure has been defined.
It does not establish that reality began as a point, that a point is the empty
set, that a point is the Ground, or that a point contains later dimensions.

Ordinary `0_N` and `1_N` remain numbers and lawful operands. In a selected
projective chart, `∞_P` is a projective point. Emergentism separately assigns
opaque Titan tokens `{0_T,1_T,∞_T}` symbolic boundary roles `[I]`; no Titan token
is a number, projective point, or causal particle.

## 2. What D0 means here

Within the D0–D6 interpretive scaffold, D0 marks the ground-facing limit before
the framework has introduced a positive **object-level** distinction. `Bindu`,
`dot`, and `point` are names for that role. The three distinguishable Titan
metaframe labels belong to the language used from outside that boundary; they
are not three internal D0 states or measurable pre-spacetime objects.

```text
D0 := selected ground-facing boundary marker        [I]
•  := selected glyph for that role                  [I]
```

No description-length value, cosmological singularity, meditative state, or
theological name is identical to D0. Such mappings may be useful Rosetta
projections, but they transfer neither identity nor evidence.

## 3. The sphere is selected separately

The reciprocal chart

```text
θ ∈ (0,π)
φ := cot(θ/2)
ν := tan(θ/2)
```

is a chosen mathematical construction. Once chosen, `φν=1`, `φ+ν≥2`, and
`B=2/(φ+ν)≤1` follow analytically. A nonzero angle moves within that already
specified chart; it does not cause a point to inflate into a sphere.

Therefore:

- D0 does not contain, demand, generate, or become `S²`;
- the Titan frame does not force the D-ladder;
- a selected projective completion is not physical creation;
- missing lower-level reduction is not proof of irreducibility.

## 4. Practice and symbol

The Bindu may remain an `[I]` practice image for silence, minimal commitment,
or returning attention to a ground-facing boundary. The practice makes no
public proof about the Ground, consciousness, cosmogenesis, or personal
identity. Its value is experiential and corrigible.

## 5. Kill and downgrade conditions

Abandon or revise this interpretation if the D0 marker creates contradictions,
adds no explanatory discipline, or is repeatedly mistaken for a physical
origin despite the type fence. Removing the Bindu reading must leave the
analytic reciprocal chart intact.
