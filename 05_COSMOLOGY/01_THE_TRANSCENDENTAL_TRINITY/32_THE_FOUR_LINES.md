Since φν = 1: φ² + ν² ≥ 2

**Line 4** is the equilibrium. At the equator, −log(φ · ν) = −log(1) = 0. The system at balance has zero free energy. It costs nothing to exist at the equator. Every displacement from the equator costs energy. [S] Established.
