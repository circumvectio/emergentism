## The four honest lines

For the declared open chart `theta in (0,pi)`:

```text
1. phi * nu = 1
2. phi + nu >= 2, with equality iff phi = nu = 1
3. (phi - nu)^2 >= 0
4. E_chart := -log(phi*nu) = 0
```

Line 1 follows from `phi=cot(theta/2)` and `nu=tan(theta/2)`. Line 2 is
AM-GM for positive reciprocals. Line 3 is non-negativity of a square. Line 4
is a defined diagnostic applied to the constant product; it is therefore
identically zero on this chart.

## What does not follow

These lines do not establish:

- what exists or why actuality exists;
- a conservation law or physical energy;
- that systems move toward the equator;
- an objective function, gradient flow, stability, or evolutionary force;
- a method for reaching truth;
- Justice, moral valence, or the value of a person;
- `P_node:=Phi*V`, which is a separately selected normalized model;
- a forced Titan frame, sevenfold language, D-ladder, or transcendental triad.

The arrow in `(phi-nu)^2 -> 0` was the crucial illicit addition: an inequality
contains no time, dynamics, mechanism, or probability distribution. Any descent
claim must specify a dynamical law and be tested in its domain.

Likewise, `E_chart=0` cannot be called zero physical free energy or zero cost of
existence. It is constant because its argument was defined to be one.

## The emblem and the compass

`circle-dot = dot * ring` may compress the selected symbolic roles of finity,
zero, and infinity. It is not `1=0*infinity` in ordinary arithmetic and does not
conjure the sphere. As a compass, the four lines remind us to distinguish
constraint, balance score, dynamics, and value. Their success is clarity, not
metaphysical proof.

## Upgrade and kill criteria

A domain-specific dynamic may be added only with explicit state variables,
units, evolution law, boundary conditions, data, rival models, predictions, and
kill criteria. If such a model is independently reproduced, that local claim
upgrades. No local success transfers ontology or ethics across domains.
