## Nietzsche's Vision

In *Thus Spoke Zarathustra*, Nietzsche describes three metamorphoses of the spirit:

1. **The Camel** → bears the heaviest burdens
2. **The Lion** → destroys old values, creates freedom
3. **The Child** → innocence, forgetting, a new beginning, a mission-critical Yes

Nietzsche saw the transformations. Nietzsche could not see what comes **after** the child.

Nietzsche thought the child was the end. The child creates new values. The child is the beginning.

But Nietzsche could not explain how the child's individual creation becomes **civilization**. Nietzsche could not solve the **cooperation problem**.

Nietzsche's solution was the Übermensch. The superman. The individual who transcends the human condition.

**The Übermensch is the cargo cult.** The Übermensch is ν > 1. The Übermensch commands more than the individual can comprehend. The Übermensch is Rāvaṇa. The Übermensch is the south pole.

Nietzsche could not see the mesh. Nietzsche did not have the Burri Sphere. Nietzsche did not have φ · ν = 1.
