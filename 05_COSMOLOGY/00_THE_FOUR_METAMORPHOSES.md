# The Four Metamorphoses

This is a reversible interpretive lens `[I]`, not a deduction from Nietzsche,
the reciprocal chart, the Burri sphere, or Power-Max.

Nietzsche's three metamorphoses in *Thus Spoke Zarathustra* supply the first
three images:

1. **Camel:** bearing inherited burdens.
2. **Lion:** refusing inherited commands and clearing space.
3. **Child:** beginning anew and creating values.

Emergentism proposes a fourth image:

4. **Mesh:** finite creators coordinating while remaining distinct, contestable,
   and able to leave.

The fourth image asks a practical question Nietzsche's sequence need not answer:
how can individual value creation become durable cooperation without turning
the collective into a sovereign person? The answer is not encoded in
`φν=1`. It requires independently chosen Justice conditions, accountable
authorization, complete bearer coverage, consent, custody, reversibility, and
outcome feedback.

## A bounded comparison

| Image | Emergentist question | Failure mode |
|---|---|---|
| Camel | What inherited burden is being carried, and by whom? | duty naturalized into extraction |
| Lion | What false constraint must be refused? | destruction mistaken for freedom |
| Child | What new option can be created? | private novelty mistaken for common good |
| Mesh | Can distinct agents widen durable mutual options under Justice? | aggregate gain launders a bearer's loss |

The sphere may visualize imbalance and a declared balance score. It does not
measure sovereignty, recognize the Ground, sum persons into a real collective
capacity, derive Dharma, prove cooperation, or certify that a civilization has
passed a Great Filter. `P_node=ΦV` remains a selected conjunctive model;
Justice-constrained Power-Max remains a normative design objective `[I]` with a
conjectural real-world fit `[C]`.

## Use discipline

An application must name its operational `Φ` and `V`, every affected bearer,
the payer and beneficiary, the time horizon, authorization, consent, and the
observed outcome. If the fourth image adds no explanatory or practical value
beyond ordinary accounts of cooperation, drop it. Removing this lens changes
none of the formal Emergentist owners.
