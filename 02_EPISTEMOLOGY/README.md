# 02 Epistemology — How a pattern earns a claim

Epistemology owns **how to know**. It receives direct disclosure, analogy,
testimony, inference, models, and measurements, then asks what each channel can
honestly support. Pattern may orient attention; it does not certify itself.

## The admission rule

```text
notice a pattern
trace its source
name the strongest rival
define the scope and projection risk
assign a justified tier
state a prediction and kill criterion
hand the candidate to method
```

The Rosetta may translate a candidate across domains but cannot transfer proof.
A repeated source is not independent confirmation. A named author, role, or
institution is not intrinsically calibrated.

## Model, action, and outcome

Epistemology keeps three gaps visible:

- cognitive gap: territory versus model;
- execution gap: intention versus performed action;
- outcome gap: expected versus receipted consequence.

Represented futures can influence present selection through present models.
That is distinct from a physical future event causing the present. The physical
light cone remains bounded by spacetime and `c`.

## Boundary

authority to this lane. Ordinary repository work follows the user's scope,
available permissions, provenance, reversibility, and tests. Authorization of
