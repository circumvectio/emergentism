# The brain is not the Burri Sphere

The former document treated bilateral anatomy, the corpus callosum, the default
mode network, contemplative states, operators, and sphere coordinates as one
structural isomorphism. Neuroscience does not establish that mapping. It is
withdrawn as an evidentiary or ontological claim.

Brains are embodied D4 systems. Their actual models, memories, dreams, reports,
and selections are D4 events; merely possible content represented by those
events is D5. Hemispheres, networks, and frequency bands are distributed and
task-dependent, not `φ`, `ν`, Titans, moral ranks, or Rosetta levels.

The sphere may still serve as a removable visual prompt `[I]` for questions
about differentiation, integration, perspective, and counterfactual modeling.
Any proposed correspondence must outperform serious simpler neural and
behavioral baselines on preregistered held-out tests. A failed comparison is
removed without changing the Emergentist calculus.
