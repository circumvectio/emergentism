## 4 · The seven exhaust the game — typed exactly

Define the game alphabet as:

```text
G7 = M4 ⊔ F3
M4 = {give-Φ, give-V, take-Φ, take-V}
F3 = {create-frame, dissolve-frame, preserve-frame}
```

`M4` is the elementary unilateral-transfer alphabet for a two-factor self/other model. `F3` supplies the three boundary readings—Brahmā, Śiva, Viṣṇu—read as create, dissolve, preserve. Frames contextualize; they do not sign, govern, or execute consequences.

A **G7-coded play** is a finite typed word, sequence, or mixture whose primitive symbols belong to `G7`. Neutral, reciprocal, multilateral, temporally extended, and mixed acts may be represented as compositions only when the representation preserves their materially relevant effects. Therefore every G7-coded play is composed of the seven by definition.

This is the exact survivor:

> **The seven exhaust the Emergentist game language.** `[S]` by construction.

It is not the dead claim that algebra universally forces seven, that every real action admits lossless G7 encoding, or that cross-tradition recurrence independently proves the census. Receipt 130 remains controlling: **the grammar is real; the census is selected.**
