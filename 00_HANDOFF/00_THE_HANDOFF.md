# The Handoff

A handoff records what another worker should inspect next. It may carry status,
commands, questions, and evidence pointers. It cannot settle a worldview claim,
promote a tier, or override an active owner.

1. Verify branch, worktree, and current source owners.
2. Treat packet assertions as leads until independently checked.
3. Preserve concurrent work and historical custody.
4. Record the outcome in the appropriate receipt or owner surface.
