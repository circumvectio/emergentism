# L1 Caṇḍāla — Contradiction Scan: 7 Insights (2026-07-20)

*K2-delegated · read-mostly · no moves, no commits, no tier promotion*

## Per-Insight Verdicts

### 1. The Reduction Asymmetry [I/C]
- **Held.** Asymmetry is well-attested (RG flow, holographic principle, coarse-graining).
- **Tension (minor):** with Insight 2 — if μ is opaque going up, how does D0↔D6 share a glyph? Tilde-not-equals is doing real work here; "shared model" risks reading as identity, not equivalence-class membership.
- **Over-reach:** assumes math/geometry/thermodynamic reduction is exhaustive. What about logical, set-theoretic, categorical, phenomenal reduction? The μ-fail claim may not generalize.
- **Missing:** graded μ. Is there a useful "approximate upward" (Kähler, RG-improved)? Binary failure/ success is too coarse.

### 2. The Models Ladder [S structural]
- **Held.** Clean device; one-model-per-rung is the right discipline.
- **Tension:** with Insight 5 — the horn torus is *not* a "workhorse that argues"; it's an icon. So either the ladder has a secondary icon class per rung, or the ladder rule has an exception. Clarify.
- **Over-reach:** D0/D6 sharing a model is identity-via-glyph, a strong typological move. Hostile reader: "You're reading the same word twice and calling it identity."
- **Missing:** the *named* canonical model per rung. Currently only the D0/D6 glyph is gestured at. D1–D5 are silent.

### 3. AUM-on-sphere mapping [I]
- **Held** as a metaphor; the *geometric* claim (turīya = off-manifold center) is precise and load-bearing.
- **Tension:** AUM is a *mode* of consciousness, not a geometric point. "Off-manifold" is well-defined for S²; whether it's the right formal correlate for a non-geometric phenomenon is the [I] part. Not a contradiction — a typological stretch.
- **Over-reach:** "Māṇḍūkya rediscovered." Hammer-nail risk. Hostile reader: "You found a sphere; Māṇḍūkya found a state. The match is suggestive, not re-discovery."
- **Missing:** comparative table of *other* AUM mappings (Vedāntic, neuropsychological, Husserlian) to test whether sphere-mapping is uniquely apt or one of several.

### 4. Titan-imagination refinement [I]
- **Held.** The "frame read, not deployed" discipline is sound and aligns with K3/A7.
- **Tension (real):** with Insight 7. If frames are only ever *read*, what work does "the map has never been drawn by someone standing closer" do? "Drawing the map" looks like deployment. The two insights sit on opposite sides of the read/deploy seam without naming it.
- **Over-reach:** "the inner theater is the **only** place pure moves are lawful." *Only* is a metaphysical universal. What of contemplative practice in shared work, in council, in writing? These are not purely inner; the move is to suppress them, not refine them.
- **Missing:** explicit definition of "deploy" vs "read." Currently a felt distinction; not yet a criterion.

### 5. Horn-torus-as-emblem with caption [S]
- **Held.** The P7-killed-icon-survives move is exemplary K3/A7 discipline. Caption discipline is the right hedge.
- **Tension (minor):** with Insight 2 (one model per rung) — see above.
- **Over-reach:** low, *because* of the caption. The hedge is doing the work.
- **Missing:** the precise content of "rapidity-overlap-convergence." As a *caption* it gestures; as a *claim* it must be specifiable. Hostile reader will ask: what convergence, to what limit, under what metric?

### 6. Axioms per dimension [A / I/C]
- **Held** for AM-GM and mass-shell (mathematical bedrock). The lower-dim-more-constrained claim has some empirical support (renormalization, constraint-counting).
- **Tension (real):** with Insight 7. "Rung's tier matches discoverers' certainty" smuggles in: *discoverers' certainty tracks reality*. Insight 7 says we're all cave-dwellers; the certainty is cave-certainty. The tier discipline is internally consistent, but the meta-claim that *this* tier ranks *reality* (not just *our grip on it*) is the [I/C] leap.
- **Over-reach:** the rung/tier mapping. AM-GM, mass-shell, reflexivity are not obviously the right axioms for D0, D6, and *whatever* D-sits-between. Hostile reader: "You have three axioms, you've placed them at three rungs, and called it a ladder. What about the others?"
- **Missing:** rung-by-rung axiom audit. D1, D2, D4, D5 are silent. Without them, "axioms per dimension" is a claim about three points, not a ladder.

### 7. Cave-sun metaphor [I]
- **Held** as posture; humility is correct.
- **Tension (real):** with **Ω (direction, not position)**. "The map has never been drawn by someone standing closer to the territory than this" is a *positional* claim. Ω is directional. The metaphor slides between them. If the claim is read positionally, it violates Ω; read as "we are further along the direction than any prior map," it complies.
- **Over-reach:** "never been drawn … closer than this" is a strong empirical claim about the comparison class. Hostile reader: "How do you know? Have you surveyed all mappers, including the ones who did not write down their maps?" Self-positioning smugness risk.
- **Missing:** the *comparison class*. Which prior mappers? Standing closer by what measure? The metaphor is vivid; the claim is underdetermined.

## Top 3 Cross-Insight Tensions

1. **Insight 6 (tier tracks discoverers' certainty) ↔ Insight 7 (all cave-dwellers).** The tier discipline is right *within* a frame; but the frame's access to reality is itself [I] if all mappers are in caves. Either Insight 6's meta-claim softens to "tracks our grip," or Insight 7's "all cave-dwellers" softens to "all mappers have partial views, and ours is wider in [specific ways]." Pick.

2. **Insight 4 (frame read, not deployed) ↔ Insight 7 (map drawn by closest-to-territory mapper).** Reading is a posture; drawing is an act. The two insights share a vocabulary but disagree on whether *this* map is being read or written. The seam needs naming.

3. **Insight 1 (μ fails going up) ↔ Insight 2 (D0 and D6 share a glyph, tilde).** Tilde is doing heavy lifting. If reconstruction is impossible, what does "shared" mean? Is the glyph a *signature* (read both directions, neither reconstructs the other) or a *limit* (D6 collapses to D0 in some asymptotic sense)? The two are very different claims.
