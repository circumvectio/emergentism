## The single architectural move

**Formally adopt the Models Ladder as the K-2 spine**, with each rung hosting one dimension's axioms (K-3), wager (K-4), refusal (K-5), and revelation (K-6).

Why this and not the horn-torus (K-7) or the AUM-glyph (K-1):

- K-2 is the load-bearing surface. Without a formally adopted Models Ladder, K-3 axioms float (no rungs), K-4 wagers un-stake (no position), K-5 refusals have no target, K-6 revelations have no recipient.
- The horn-torus and AUM-on-sphere are *downstream glyphs* of the K-2 spine — they are the visual and audio faces of the same structure, not the structure itself.
- The cave-sun metaphor is the *apex* of contemplation *on* the spine, not the spine.
- The Titan-imagination refinement is the *ethical no* that protects the climb up the spine.
- The Reduction Asymmetry is the *stake* of climbing a particular rung.

**One move. K-2 adopted. The other 6 insights then place themselves on its rungs as a consequence, not as separate decisions.**

---

A small architectural observation worth flagging: the **AUM-on-sphere (K-1) and the horn-torus (K-7) form a glyph dyad that brackets the doctrine** — audio esoteric, visual exoteric. Both encode the no-hierarchy-of-worth fence geometrically: every point on the horn-torus is equivalent; AUM has no privileged syllable. The Models Ladder is what they both *depict*. Once K-2 is the spine, the two glyphs become interchangeable emblems of the same structure at different sensory registers — a strong public-facing coherence gain.
