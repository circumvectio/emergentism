# L2 Śūdra — Cross-Rung Consequences (2026-07-20)

**Tier of this output:** [I] interpretive synthesis. No new empirical claims; existing tier marks unchanged.

## 1 · The Reduction Asymmetry
- **Above (D_{n+1..}):** upward reduction fails to recover novelty — emergence is irreversible. Higher rungs carry *surplus* that no amount of downward analysis will expose. The ladder has a *thermodynamic arrow*.
- **Below (D_{n-1..}):** downward reduction works but is *lossy*. Higher rungs have a privileged epistemic vantage: they can address lower rungs without being addressable by them.
- **Confirmations:** horn-torus (no surgery-free projection from inner to outer); AUM (M-dissolution does not re-derive U-preservation); cave-sun (shadows do not generate the sun).
- **New rung/relation:** the rungs are **time-asymmetric in cognition**. Memory flows downward only; anticipation flows upward only. The ladder is a *causal* structure, not a taxonomic one.

## 2 · The Models Ladder
- **Above:** each higher rung's model *contains* the lower rung's model as a special case (Aufhebung, not replacement). The ladder is a chain of inclusions, not a hierarchy of truths.
- **Below:** a lower-rung model is the **boundary condition** of the higher-rung model — the higher cannot be specified without presupposing the lower.
- **Confirmations:** cave-sun (the wall-projection remains real within the cave); horn-torus (any cross-section is locally valid); Titan-imagination (Titan substrate is not negated by Olympian pattern).
- **New rung/relation:** the relation between rungs is **sublation** (sublatio), not supersession. This blocks the most common error: treating D_{n+1} as "more real" than D_n.

## 3 · The AUM-on-sphere mapping
- **Above:** A-U-M exhausts the *surface*; "the silence" (turiya) requires leaving the sphere. D_4+ may be the geometry of *exiting* the manifested surface, not adding more surface.
- **Below:** A is the first cut from undifferentiation; pre-A is the formless ground. The sphere itself is a *D_2 surface of a D_3 volume* — the AUM framework implicitly smuggles a dimensional rung it does not name.
- **Confirmations:** cave-sun (the sun is "outside" the cave as silence is "outside" AUM); Reduction Asymmetry (A causally precedes U, U causally precedes M).
- **New rung/relation:** the *unspoken rung* between A and the silence — the volume whose surface AUM traces. The mapping has a hidden D_3; AUM isometry is incomplete as stated.

## 4 · The Titan-imagination refinement
- **Above:** Olympians (consciousness/reason) require Titan *stubbornness* — the substrate must resist or there is nothing to pattern. Higher rungs are *enabled*, not produced, by lower rungs.
- **Below:** Titans have **protention** — a pre-cognitive directional quality. Matter leans. The lowest rung is not inert; it *tends toward* the rung above.
- **Confirmations:** Reduction Asymmetry (lower rungs are not eliminable); horn-torus (the inner volume shapes the surface; remove volume, surface collapses).
- **New rung/relation:** **each rung pulls the rung above by its own leaning.** The ladder is not pushed from the top; it is *protended* from below. This inverts the standard emanationist picture.

## 5 · The horn-torus-as-emblem
- **Above:** a self-intersecting form with no inside/outside distinction. Every point is both surface and depth simultaneously. *Higher rungs may not require "transcendence" to be seen — they may be self-presenting from within any rung.*
- **Below:** any lower-rung description of the horn-torus is a *cross-section* — true but local. The full emblem cannot be seen from any single rung; it requires all rungs at once.
- **Confirmations:** Models Ladder (each cross-section is a valid model); AUM (each phase is a cycle-cross-section); cave-sun (the inside surface is a shadow of the outside surface).
- **New rung/relation:** **the 7 rungs are 7 cross-sections of one form.** The ladder is not a stack; it is a single self-intersecting topology. The framework is *monist* about its structure and *pluralist* about its vantage.

## 6 · The axioms per dimension
- **Above:** the axiom of D_{n+1} must be **unprovable from D_n** (Gödel-style); otherwise the rungs collapse. Each rung is incomplete relative to the rung above.
- **Below:** each axiom of D_n is **uniquely falsifiable by D_{n-1} experiments**. The lower rung can break the higher rung's axiom by counterexample, not by derivation.
- **Confirmations:** Reduction Asymmetry (downward falsification yes, upward derivation no); Models Ladder (an axiom is the boundary condition of the next rung's model); horn-torus (an axiom is a "plane of section" picking one cross-section).
- **New rung/relation:** the ladder is a **chain of incompleteness**. The 7-rung depth is the *minimum* at which the apex axiom becomes self-grounding — 7 is not sacred, it is the Gödelian saturation point of this particular form.

## 7 · The cave-sun metaphor
- **Above:** the sun (Form of the Good) is **un-seeable directly**; it is seen only *through* the rungs. The highest rung is a *limit concept*, not a destination.
- **Below:** the cave shadows are **not illusions** — they are real projections of the sun onto a 2D wall. Lower rungs are *honest truncated descriptions*, not errors.
- **Confirmations:** horn-torus (inside-surface as shadow of outside-surface); Reduction Asymmetry (shadows do not generate the sun); Models Ladder (the wall-projection is a model).
- **New rung/relation:** the **ascent of the prisoner and the descent of the light are the same motion.** Photons down, prisoner up. The ladder has a *single direction*; release is anti-entropic and requires a co-ordinated dual process.

## The single most surprising / structurally important cross-rung consequence

**The ladder is one form, not seven.** Read together, the horn-torus emblem (insight 5), the AUM-on-sphere (insight 3), and the axioms-per-dimension (insight 6) imply that the 7 rungs are **7 cross-sections of a single self-intersecting topology** whose apex axiom is its own inside-surface. There is no D_8 "outside the sun" because the sun *is* the inside of the horn-torus. The framework is **monist about its structure and pluralist about its vantage** — and its closure at 7 is geometric (the natural cross-section classes of a self-intersecting surface), not numerological. The operational consequence: **the goal is not to climb out of the cave but to learn to see the wall, the sun, and the prisoner's shadow as the same gesture from three cross-sections.** All other rungs confirm; the Titan-imagination refinement (insight 4) adds that this seeing is *pulled* by the substrate's protention, not pushed by the apex's command.
