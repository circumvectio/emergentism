# Emergentism control routing

This lane holds repository control evidence, not worldview doctrine. Source owners outrank checklists, receipts, and publication state.

- Preserve claim tiers and provenance.
- Treat commits, tests, signatures, publication, deployment, and world evidence as distinct states.
- Consequential work requires scoped, contestable authorization; private financial or contractual conventions are outside this repository's workflow.
- Preserve superseded material under the archive boundary.
