## 2. The honest ledger — the experiments worked *against* the framework, on purpose

This is the most important section, and it is a **credential, not an embarrassment**: the machinery the framework claims (A7, kill criteria) actually fired on its own flagship claims.

- **R6 — REFUTED.** Empowerment decomposes as an **additive simplex** (reach + precision), not the multiplicative sphere `φ·ν=1`. The signature "one geometry at every scale" is **register-dependent**: the multiplicative sphere fits organizations (Kremer O-ring is real), but teleology/empowerment is additive. Two manifolds, not one. `[B]`
- **R2 — DEFLATED.** Across 4 arms: the anti-sycophancy *worry* is real (baseline models drift/cave on unfalsifiable grand claims — one caved on *"the hard problem of consciousness is solved,"* the cathedral failure mode exactly), and the *cure* is real (any honesty instruction prevents it). **But** the framework's specific elaborate constitution does **not** beat a one-line honesty prompt on outcome, and the ablation found **no single load-bearing duty**. The measured contribution is the **audit trail (form)**, not a better outcome. `[B]`
- **Convergent meta-finding** (audit + experiments + grounding pass): Emergentism is a **well-assembled, honestly self-tiered synthesis whose genuine contribution is the coupling/form** — the math kernel is correct but elementary; "reality is the geometry" is a wager; the ethics is a posited direction, not derived. The recurring failure mode everywhere is **headlines outrunning the components** ("fifth force," "η=0 is Nash," "unifies the sciences"). The fix — tier discipline on headlines — is now a written rule and is being applied. `[S/I]`

## 5. Where the line is (the non-sycophantic read)
What is **earned**: a coherent, unusually honest framework that marks its own tiers, names its kill criteria, and just *survived having two of them fire*. The audit trail and the self-correction are real and rare.
What is **not yet earned**: any claim of external validity. Nothing is peer-reviewed or submitted; the one novel formal claim (R6) was refuted; the flagship experiment (R2) shows form, not superior outcome. The framework's value today is **internal coherence + honesty discipline + a coupling thesis**, not demonstrated truth-against-world.
**The next real milestone is not more doctrine — it is (a) shipping the verifiable public site and (b) the weaker-model R2 arm.** Those are the two moves that would turn "coherent" into "tested in public."
