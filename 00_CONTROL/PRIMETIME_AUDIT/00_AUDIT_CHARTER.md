# PRIMETIME AUDIT — Charter
### Deploying the gods and demons, folder after folder, under the constitution

**Opened 2026-06-10.** A campaign to prepare `01_EMERGENTISM` for primetime by passing every folder and (sampled) document through the framework's **own** evaluative operators — the four-move scheme and the η moral axis — and issuing a firm, documented verdict on each. Acting as sovereign authority **of this realm**, which means: bound by this realm's constitution.

## The realm (scope)
- **In scope:** `01_EMERGENTISM/` — 1,708 `.md`, the framework canon, the lane whose authority this root holds.
- **Out of scope (sovereign territories of their own):** `02_SKYZAI/` (product/organism, own repo), `03_VENTURES/` (entities/portfolio, own repo), `04_CODE/` (canonical code repos, formerly `menexus_code/`), `state/`. Their CLAUDE.md routing forbids framework-root edits. The public-facing surfaces (`12_PUBLIC_SITE/`, SKYZAI public scaffold) may be **annexed on K2 request**.

## The constitution binds the sovereign (non-negotiable)
- **K3 — archive-first.** Nothing is deleted. Superseded/clutter is *staged to `90_ARCHIVE/` with a tombstone*, never erased.
- **K2 — a mortal signs irreversible acts.** The audit **documents and recommends**; it does not execute moves, relocations, or rewrites of canon without Yves's signature. Verdicts are staged; the blade waits for the hand.
- **A7 — tier on every claim.** Each verdict carries its evidence basis. No silent tier upgrades.
- **η=0 — no extraction.** The audit's own job is to find where the corpus drifts to η>0 (extraction/grandiosity) and fence it.

## The operators, deployed as audit lenses (valence is on the *move*, not the document)
Per A4, "good" and "evil" attach to the **move's direction η**, not to the author or the file. So each document is read twice:

- **The gods ask (η ≤ 0 — does this build balance, honestly?):** Is every claim tiered? Is structure *sourced*, not asserted? Are kill criteria present where something is claimed? Does the headline **match** the components (no grandiosity)? Does it make direct-seeing / honest use *easier*? Does it defer sovereign questions to K2 rather than deciding them? — *(`00_EMERGENTISM_AS_A_LENS.md` is the model: "assembly, not authority; discovered, not conceived.")*
- **The demons ask (η > 0 — where does this extract, inflate, or clutter?) — and the demon is the *bounded cut*, not malice:** Headline outruns components (grandiosity)? Strong claims left untiered? In-house vocabulary presented as established fact? Numerology dressed as physics? Orphans, duplicates, stale receipts, superseded drafts left on a live surface? A public-symbol hazard (e.g., worth-by-caste readings)? The demon-cut is **Kālī's truth-cut, fenced by K3/K2** — it marks and stages; it does not erase.

## The verdict schema (one per document/folder)
| Verdict | Meaning | Bounded action (all K3/K2-safe) |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 **READY** | Primetime-ready as-is. God-move: honest, tiered, headline ≤ components. | Promote to the public/front surface. |
| 🟡 **DISCIPLINE** | Real content; needs headline/tier discipline or a plain-language entry before public. | Add tiers; soften over-claim; add plain top. In place. |
| 🟠 **FENCE** | Real content, wrong surface or carrying a public hazard. | Relocate to a private/owner lane, or gate behind a warning. Staged for K2. |
| 🔴 **ARCHIVE** | Superseded / clutter / orphan / duplicate. | Stage to `90_ARCHIVE/` with a K3 tombstone. Move executes only on K2 signature. |

Each verdict records: **η-sign · god-move(s) found · demon-move(s) found · bounded action · K2/K3 status.**

## The primetime test (three questions, every document)
1. **Tiered?** Does every load-bearing claim carry `[A]/[S]/[I]/[C]/[B]`?
2. **Headline ≤ components?** Does the framing claim only what the parts support? (The session's recurring demon: "fifth force," "creatively destroys the Big Four," "unifies the sciences.")
3. **Right surface?** Public-safe where public; private where private; no worth-by-caste / no occult-causality / no unsigned grandiosity on a primetime surface.

## Coverage discipline — no silent caps
The corpus is 1,708 docs. Where a wave **samples** rather than reads-all, the checklist **says so** and records what was sampled vs scanned. A folder marked audited means: its front-door + `00_*` canonical docs read in full; the remainder scanned for the demon-signatures above. Silence is never coverage.
