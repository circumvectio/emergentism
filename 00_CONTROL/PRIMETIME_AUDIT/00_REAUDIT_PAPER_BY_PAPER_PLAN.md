# Re-audit — Paper by Paper

## 1. Purpose

Re-audit `01_EMERGENTISM/` paper by paper — **full read, not sample** —
against the **Settled Canon Registry** (`00_META/00_SETTLED_CANON_REGISTRY.md`).
Where the prior PRIMETIME_AUDIT sampled (front-door + `00_*` canonical docs
read in full; remainder scanned for demon-signatures), this re-audit
reads every paper, line by line, against the 19 settled questions +
the audit charter's three primetime tests (tiered? headline ≤ components?
right surface?).

The re-audit closes the gap the Charter flagged:

> *"Where a wave samples rather than reads-all, the checklist says so and
> records what was sampled vs scanned. Silence is never coverage."*

The deliverable is **a verdict on every paper**: 🟢 READY / 🟡 DISCIPLINE /
🟠 FENCE / 🔴 ARCHIVE — each carrying η-sign, god-moves found, demon-moves
found, bounded action, K2/K3 status.

## 2. Method (defer to the audit discipline)

The re-audit is **not** a re-litigation. It is a fidelity check. The
method is:

1. **Read the Registry first** (`00_META/00_SETTLED_CANON_REGISTRY.md`).
   The 19 settled questions and their "Do NOT flag" / "Still flag if"
   columns are the audit lens. A flag against a settled item *is itself
   the defect* (per the Registry's own rule).
2. **For each paper:** read in full. For each load-bearing claim, check:
   - **Tier mark present** (`[A]/[S]/[I]/[C]/[B]`)?
   - **Headline ≤ components** (no grandiosity: "fifth force," "η=0 is
     Nash," "unifies the sciences" — the recurring demon)?
   - **Right surface** (public-safe where public; private where private;
     no worth-by-caste / no occult-causality on a primetime surface)?
   - **Registry compliance** (does it assert any of the 19 settled
     questions incorrectly? does it use the canonical shorthand, or
     does it re-derive what was already settled)?
   - **Kill criteria** (if the paper claims something empirical, is the
     kill criterion present and recent?).
3. **Issue the verdict** (🟢/🟡/🟠/🔴) per the Charter's schema. Each
   verdict records: η-sign, god-moves found, demon-moves found, bounded
   action, K2/K3 status.
4. **Stage the bounded action; do not execute.** K2/K3 binds: the audit
   documents and recommends; only Yves executes the moves, relocations,
   or rewrites. Verdicts are staged; the blade waits for the hand.

## 3. Corpus enumeration (what counts as a "paper")

Per the Charter: 1,708 `.md` files in scope. Of these:
- **Top-level `00_*` papers** (canonical): ~80-100 docs
- **Subdirectory papers** (e.g. `05_COSMOLOGY/00_THE_BURRISPHERE.md`,
  `01_TELEOLOGY/00_NEOTENY_THE_HARDWARE_UPGRADE.md`): ~500-700 docs
- **Audit/receipt/banner files** (e.g. `90_ARCHIVE/`, `00_META/00_*`):
  ~200 docs
- **Auxiliary** (READMEs, AGENTS.md, sub-specs, RECEIPTs, _PLANS):
  ~600-900 docs

The re-audit focuses on **papers** (doctrinal claims, semantic content),
not on auxiliary files. The scope refinement:

| Tier | What | Count est. | Audit depth |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A** | Top-level `00_*` canonical papers | ~80 | Full read, full verdict |
| **B** | Subdirectory `00_*` papers (the 7 -ologies + support) | ~500 | Full read, full verdict |
| **C** | Subdirectory non-`00_*` (derivations, experiments, sub-papers) | ~300 | Full read, sampled verdict |
| **D** | Audit/receipt/banner (00_META, 90_ARCHIVE, RECEIPTs) | ~200 | Sampled — already audit-discipline |
| **E** | Auxiliary (READMEs, AGENTS.md, _PLANS) | ~600 | Skip — auxiliary, not doctrinal |

The re-audit covers Tiers A + B + C. Tiers D and E are out of scope (D
is the audit discipline itself; E is auxiliary, not claim-bearing).

**Total re-audit scope:** ~880 papers at full read. At ~5 minutes per
paper, ~73 hours of audit time.

## 6. The non-negotiables (the load-bearing constraints)

Per the audit discipline, these cannot be relaxed:

- **K3 — archive-first.** Superseded/clutter is staged to `90_ARCHIVE/`
  with a tombstone. Never erased. The re-audit may recommend archive
  moves; only K2 signs the move.
- **K2 — mortal signs.** The audit documents and recommends. It does
  not execute. Verdicts are staged. The blade waits.
- **A7 — tier on every claim.** Each verdict carries its evidence
  basis. No silent tier upgrades.
- **η=0 — no extraction.** The re-audit itself is η=0 work: it finds
  where the corpus drifts to η>0 and fences it. The audit's own
  posture is `η ≤ 0`.
- **Registry supremacy.** A flag against a settled item is *itself the
  defect*. The 19 settled questions stand; lagging docs are repaired
  *to* the ruling, never re-opened.
