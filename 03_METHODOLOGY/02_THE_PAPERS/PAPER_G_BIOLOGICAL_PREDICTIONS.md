# Biological Predictions — Kintsugi Successor

> **[金] Crack:** the earlier paper treated the reciprocal chart, a universal
> hyperbolic compensation law, dimensional shutdown, and near-death staging as
> if they already generated biological predictions. It also typed actual
> awareness and reports as D5. **Repair:** retain only protocols that can
> distinguish the Emergentist proposal from ordinary biological models.

## Type boundary

- Actual organisms, measurements, model tokens, awareness episodes, verbal
  reports, interventions, actions, and records are `D4 = actual`.
- Represented alternatives and counterfactual contents are
  `D5 = merely_possible`.
- `D4→μ₄→D5` is a candidate crossing about counterfactual capacity. It is not a
  medical stage, a shutdown sequence, a quantum collapse, or a claim that
  consciousness leaves the organism.

## What qualifies as a biological prediction

A candidate result must preregister all of the following:

1. an operational variable and unit;
2. an intervention and comparator;
3. a sampling and analysis protocol;
4. a prediction that differs from at least one named rival;
5. a kill criterion and an error model;
6. a recovery or ablation showing what the proposed higher-level variable adds.

The chart identity `φν=1` is analytic under the chosen coordinates. It does not
predict biological conservation. Likewise, the normalized product
`P=ΦV` is Emergentism's selected conjunctive model, not a universal biological
law. A biological study may test explicitly declared proxies and alternative
aggregators, but it must not rename a coordinate identity as data.

## Live research program `[C]`

The most defensible discriminator is counterfactual intervention:

> Holding relevant actual conditions as constant as practicable, intervene on
> an organism's represented future and test whether the distribution of its
> present actions changes beyond reactive and associative comparators.

Positive evidence would support the usefulness of an option-field variable in
that protocol. It would not prove strong emergence, a fivefold ontology,
physical retrocausality, or quantum measurement. A complete lower-register
model that predicts the same discrimination without the proposed variable
reclassifies the case as weak emergence.
