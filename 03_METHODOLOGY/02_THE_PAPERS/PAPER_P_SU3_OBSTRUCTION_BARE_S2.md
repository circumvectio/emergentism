# PAPER P: THE PROPOSED SU(3) OBSTRUCTION ON BARE S²

## Retraction of a false bridge and the boundary that survives

> **[金] Kintsugi seam.** The former paper claimed that an `SU(3)` gauge theory
> cannot be defined over `S²`, that eight generators require an eight-dimensional
> base, and that a Veronese embedding into `CP²` supplies a QCD bridge. Those
> conclusions do not follow. This stable path now records the failed theorem
> and the narrower mathematics that survives.

## 1. What is true `[A]`

- `S² ≅ CP¹` is a real two-dimensional, complex one-dimensional manifold.
- `SU(3)` is an eight-real-dimensional Lie group with fundamental
  representation on `C³`.
- A gauge group's dimension is an **internal-fiber** fact; it need not equal
  the dimension of the base manifold.
- Principal `SU(3)` bundles over `S²` are topologically classified by
  `π₁(SU(3))`, which is trivial. Thus the principal bundle is topologically
  trivial, but it may still carry nonzero gauge connections and curvature.
- The Veronese map embeds `CP¹` into `CP²`. That mathematical embedding does
  not identify `CP²` with QCD spacetime or derive the strong interaction.
- The Hopf fibration `S³→S²` is a nontrivial principal `U(1)` bundle. Its
  existence does not by itself derive electromagnetism from the Burri sphere.

Therefore the paper's advertised `SU(3)` obstruction was false. One can study
`SU(3)` gauge fields over a two-dimensional base; what bare `S²` does **not**
provide is a derivation of four-dimensional QCD, its dynamics, matter content,
couplings, confinement, or empirical predictions.

## 2. The valid negative result `[A/I]`

The reciprocal chart and sphere topology alone underdetermine a physical gauge
theory. To specify one requires, at minimum, a base spacetime, principal bundle,
gauge group, connection, curvature, action, representations, matter fields,
boundary conditions, and empirical interpretation.

No assignment of physical forces to D-registers follows from `S²`. The active
D4/D5 grammar instead types actuality and merely possible content; neither is
an internal gauge dimension.

## 3. Consequence for Emergentism

`S²` may remain a selected geometric chart and Rosetta image. It cannot be a
load-bearing unification of particle physics unless a separate model produces
novel quantitative discriminators and survives independent replication.
Adding `CP²` or a Veronese embedding is not such a discriminator.

The durable discovery here is methodological: a beautiful correspondence must
not be promoted into a physical bridge by dimension-counting alone.

## 4. Tests for any future bridge

A renewed gauge-theory proposal must state:

1. the base manifold and bundle;
2. the connection, action, and represented matter fields;
3. which structures are derived rather than chosen;
4. a quantitative result not copied from established theory;
5. a rival model and preregistered kill criterion;
6. an independently reproducible empirical discriminator.

Until then, the quantum/force correspondence is removable `[C]` and the
operational Emergentist calculus must remain unchanged when it is deleted.
