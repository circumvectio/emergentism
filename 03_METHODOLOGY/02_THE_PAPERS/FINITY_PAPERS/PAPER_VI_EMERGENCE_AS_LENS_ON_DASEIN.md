# Kintsugi tombstone

The former draft at this path made strong/weak emergence a bidirectional
ontological loop, treated missing reduction as irreducibility, and let a chosen
boundary algebra carry a world-level answer to why anything exists. Those
claims are not active Emergentist canon.

What survives is narrower: emergence can be used as an interpretive lens on
finite disclosure `[I]`; the reciprocal chart remains elementary mathematics
`[A]`; and any proposed dimensional crossing remains a separately testable
`[C]` claim. `currently_unreduced` never means irreducible. D0–D6 is selected,
not forced. The emblem is not arithmetic or ontology.

`4154ebe`; no nonexistent archive path is claimed.
