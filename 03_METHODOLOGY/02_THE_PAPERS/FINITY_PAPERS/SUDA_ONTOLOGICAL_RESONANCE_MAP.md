## Non-negotiable distinctions

- Ordinary `0` and `1` are numbers and operands. `∞` is a projective point only
  in a named extension. Titan roles do not define operations.
- Field division by zero remains undefined. Projective/meromorphic conventions
  and totalized algebras change the structure explicitly. `0/0` is not assigned
  a universal generative value.
- A Möbius strip, sphere, ladder, and causal feedback graph are different
  topologies. Visual resonance is not isomorphism.
- A control rule can be evaluated for stability without becoming an ethic.
  Justice is a declared bearer-complete construction, not a consequence of
  inversion, product, purity, or `etaObserved`.
