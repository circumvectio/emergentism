# A Product Constraint and a Declared Structural Ethic

> **[金] Crack:** the predecessor carefully admitted an interpretive premise,
> but still made the product, equator, and an overloaded `η` carry moral
> conclusions. **Repair:** mathematics supplies a model of joint necessity;
> Emergentism supplies a declared value postulate; receipts test consequences.

## Analytic result `[A]`

For `θ∈(0,π)`, with `φ=cot(θ/2)` and `ν=tan(θ/2)`:

\[
\phi\nu=1,\qquad \phi+\nu\ge2,
\qquad B=\sin\theta\le1.
\]

Equality for the latter two holds at `φ=ν=1`. Approaching either chart boundary
makes one reciprocal coordinate tend to zero and the other tend to infinity.
These are facts about the selected chart and score. They establish no empirical
conservation law, pathology, virtue, person-ranking, or ethic.

## Selected conjunctive model `[I/C]`

Emergentism declares a normalized conjunctive family `C(Φ,V)` with
`C(0,V)=C(Φ,0)=0`, and selects `C×(Φ,V)=ΦV` as its canonical compression. The
product models the intuition that counterfactual reach and embodied means are
jointly necessary. Other conjunctive aggregators can rank interior points
differently; the product is not forced.

## Declared ethic `[I]`

The framework chooses Beauty, Truth, and Justice as the good. Justice requires
complete bearer accounting, accountable authorization, consent, custody,
truthful cost, payer and beneficiary visibility, reversibility where possible,
exit, contest, and outcome receipts. `etaObserved=0` is necessary but
insufficient. Aggregate gain may not compensate for destroying a bearer.

The directional convention is:

```text
morals:   individual → whole
ethics:   whole → individual
syntropy: both durable potentials rise under Justice
```

“Goodness is the bond” is an interpretive compression of that prior commitment,
not a consequence of `φν=1`. Masculine/feminine, Śiva/Śakti, yin/yang,
hemispheric, and other dyads remain optional Rosetta analogies. They confer no
proof and may never rank persons or groups.

**Kill criterion:** if the product adds no predictive value over alternatives,
replace it. If the Justice construction hides a harmed bearer or resists an
adverse outcome receipt, the moral classification fails.
