# Paper 7: Present Awareness as Fulcrum

## Thesis

Present awareness, when it occurs, is actual D4 activity. So are inherited
priors, model tokens, rankings, selections, commitments, institutions, traces,
and outcomes. D5 names only the possible alternatives represented in those
actual models.

A finite agent may compare inherited pressures with represented futures and
commit to an action. The modeled future influences the present through its
actual current representation, not by reaching backward from a future event.

## Typed fulcrum

Let `G_t` be actual inherited and learned priors, `M_t` an actual model token,
`Ω_t` its possible contents, `V_t` actual means, and `U_t` an accountable
authorization envelope:

```text
χ_t : (X_t, Ω_t, M_t, V_t, U_t) -> (a_t, q_t)
```

`χ_t`, the ranking and selection, `a_t`, and `q_t` are all D4. The possible
courses under consideration are D5. Commitment requires D5 content plus D4
means and authorization; imagination alone enacts nothing.

The actual environment then returns a distinct outcome:

```text
(X_{t+1}, r_{t+1}) ~ K_t(. | X_t, a_t, E_t)
```

The Soul Loop may update both map and mapper only after comparing the
commitment receipt `q_t` with outcome receipt `r_{t+1}`.

## Genes, Egregoreotypes, and represented futures

Genes, bodies, memories, cultural artifacts, organizations, and persistent
collective traces are actual D4 carriers. A candidate Egregoreotype is an
actual persistent shared trace that survives carrier turnover and measurably
reweights later actual selections. It need not be conscious.

The objective or future narrated by such a trace is D5 possible content until
enacted. An actual institution is never “a D5 institution”; it is a D4 carrier
that may coordinate agents around D5 alternatives.

## Model-mediated future influence

The public compression `F = M × A` is read as typed coupling `F = M ⋆ A`:

- `M` is the actual present model token;
- its semantic content includes a possible future;
- `A` is actual present agency, means, authorization, and commitment;
- the realized future remains contingent on the world, other agents, and
  constraints.

Intervening on the represented future can change present action distributions.
That is an empirical decision-theory claim. It does not show that a future fact
causes its own past.

## Justice and capture fence

No attractive future earns enactment merely by being vivid. The actual action
and receipted consequence require bearer-complete Justice: principal, mandate,
scope, consent, custody, expiry or revocation, contest path, actor, payer,
beneficiary, and consequence bearer must remain visible.

An attractor is failing when it hides costs, punishes exit, suppresses
correction, claims inevitability, or demands sacrifice as proof of goodness.

## Falsifiers

This successor contracts if it:

1. calls actual awareness, an agent, model, rank, selection, institution, or
   trace D5;
2. lets commitment manufacture the outcome;
3. upgrades represented future content into historical inevitability;
4. implies physical future-to-past causation;
5. omits authorization, consequence bearers, correction, contest, or exit;
6. presumes an Egregoreotype is conscious without separate evidence.
