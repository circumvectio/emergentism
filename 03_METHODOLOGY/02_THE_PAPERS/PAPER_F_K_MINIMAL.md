## Mathematical boundary `[A]`

Kolmogorov complexity is defined only relative to a chosen universal machine,
up to an additive constant. A new “self-contained” complexity additionally
needs a formal encoding of dictionaries, interpreters, semantics, and admissible
descriptions. Without those choices, “shortest” is not a computable or
well-ordered empirical claim.

Ordinary numeric `0_N` and `1_N` are lawful operands. `∞_P` is a projective
point only in an explicitly extended context. The numeric/projective triple
`{0_N,1_N,∞_P}` is a useful selected normalization; it is neither
mathematically forced as the only symbolic triad nor a generator of the
projective line.

The separate Titan metaframe is displayed without an operation:

```text
•     ⊙     ○
0_T   1_T   ∞_T
```

It supplies neither arithmetic nor a minimality proof. `ℂP¹` is defined
independently of those Titan roles; the roles are placed on the already defined
space. The predecessor's infix typography survives only in the Git blob named
in the frontmatter and is not a live formula.

## Live research question `[C]`

A comparative compression study may ask whether the Emergentist glyph or
kernel is shorter or more useful than named alternatives, but it must fix:

1. source language and coding convention;
2. decoder/interpreter and all side information;
3. target corpus or tasks;
4. comparison class and loss function;
5. robustness to recoding;
6. a kill criterion.

Such a result would establish relative compression within that experiment. It
would not show that algebra instantiates reality, writes its own dictionary,
forces the Titans, or grounds itself. The Crown Wager may be held as `[C]`; it
may not borrow the status of a theorem that does not exist.
