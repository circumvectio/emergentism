## Abstract

Pure qubit states modulo global phase can be represented on the Bloch sphere,
and the complex projective line satisfies `CP¹≅S²`. The Burri reciprocal chart
also uses an `S²/CP¹` carrier. Those are legitimate mathematical contacts `[A]`.
They do not identify pole meanings, metrics, dynamics, probability measures,
measurement rules, ontology, or lived experience. The Emergentist mapping is
therefore a quarantined correspondence `[C]`, not a quantum result. The standard
state/measurement calculus is retained by the selected D3 owner; removing the
Burri comparison leaves that calculus and the operational agent calculus
unchanged.

## 1. Established mathematical contact `[A]`

For a normalized qubit state

```text
|ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩,       |α|² + |β|² = 1,
```

global phase is physically redundant, and pure states may be represented on a
Bloch sphere. Separately, `CP¹` is diffeomorphic to `S²`, and stereographic
coordinates provide charts on it.

The Burri chart stipulates, for `θ∈(0,π)`,

```text
φ = cot(θ/2),       ν = tan(θ/2),       φν = 1.
```

The last equality follows because the coordinates are reciprocals. It is not a
probability law.

## 2. The non-identity result `[A/S]`

The following structures are not interchangeable:

| Qubit/Bloch structure | Burri reciprocal structure |
|---|---|
| additive norm `|α|²+|β|²=1` | multiplicative chart identity `φν=1` |
| complex amplitudes modulo global phase | selected reciprocal coordinates |
| Born probabilities relative to a measurement | selected balance and conjunctive models |
| unitary dynamics and measurement formalism | Emergentist agency/receipt grammar |

An isomorphism of bare manifold carriers does not preserve extra structure
unless a structure-preserving map is specified and proved. No such map here
derives the Born rule, quantum dynamics, a measurement mechanism, agency,
consciousness, or ethics.

For a measurable outcome set `A`, the quantum probability is written in the
appropriate representation as `𝔓ψ(A)`—for example
`𝔓ψ(A)=∫_A|ψ(s)|²ds` where that density notation applies—and an outcome may be
sampled `o~𝔓ψ`. One never samples from the normalization scalar itself.
