## 1. The governing fact

Peer review does not referee worldviews. It referees **claims** — bounded, falsifiable or provable, stated in the target field's language, engaging that field's literature. The corpus as a whole is not a submittable object, and this is not a defect: it is a category fact. The weltanschauung is the **generator**; what gets submitted are **shards** — single claims cut from it, each translated into the register of the field that can adjudicate it.

This is the corpus's own translation discipline (its second invention, per [00_WHAT_IS_ACTUALLY_NOVEL_HERE.md](../../../00_META/00_WHAT_IS_ACTUALLY_NOVEL_HERE.md)) pointed outward for the first time. The internal register — Titans, Gods, glyphs, ⊙ = • × ○ — does not appear in any external submission. Not because it is embarrassing, but because it is load-bearing scaffolding the reader has not earned yet, and a referee will (correctly) read it as a red flag for crankery. Every submission is written so that it stands even if the reader never learns the framework exists.

**Rules of engagement for every external submission:**

1. No in-house vocabulary or glyphs. Standard notation, standard citations.
2. The claim's tier travels with it: what is proven is presented as proven, what is interpretive as interpretive, in the journal's own idiom (theorem/proposition vs. discussion/conjecture).
3. Kill criteria become the Limitations section. The corpus's best habit is also what referees most respect.
4. AI assistance is disclosed per venue policy (most major publishers now require it; AI systems cannot be authors). Suggested line: "The author used AI assistants (Anthropic Claude) for drafting, literature triage, and simulation coding; all claims, proofs, and final text were verified and are the responsibility of the author."
5. Preprint first (arXiv / PhilSci-Archive / SSRN as appropriate) for timestamped priority, then journal.
6. All citations in the drafts below were produced from model memory and **must be independently verified against the actual texts before submission**. This includes every Suda reference (the corpus cites a 2025 "reciprocal-symmetry trilogy" that must be confirmed to exist as published work before it is cited externally).

## 2. The ranked findings inventory

Ranked by (potential impact × nearness to submittable). Tiers per the Honest Position.

**#1 — The frame/operand signature.** [A] for the anchor cases; [I] for the unification.
Across arithmetic (0 and ∞ under projective compactification; the residual indeterminates), set theory (proper classes, Foundation, restricted comprehension), and semantics (Tarski, Gödel II via Löb), formal systems independently mark certain items as frame-constituting rather than operand-eligible, and every historical resolution has the same shape: stratification. The rigorous core of the logic family is Lawvere's fixed-point theorem (1969; Yanofsky 2003). The corpus's genuine contribution is the **cross-register taxonomy** — naming the shared signature and extending it to the arithmetic and metrological registers, where Lawvere does not reach. This is the nearest-to-publishable item in the corpus and it can be written today. → **R1**.

**#2 — The transparent-mediator constitution as a measurable anti-sycophancy intervention.** [I], operationalizable now.
The corpus's η = 0 / charioteer doctrine makes a testable prediction: a constitution that operationalizes "transparent mediation" (tier-marking, refusal of flattery, agency-preserving closure) should reduce sycophantic capitulation in AI assistants more than generic honesty instructions, without degrading helpfulness — including against the hardest adversary, the user's own enthusiasm (the documented live-fire events of 2026-06, where the layer held twice, are the n=2 anecdote this study turns into data). This is the cheapest live falsifier on the board and the most topical: alignment venues actively want sycophancy results. → **R2**.

**#3 — Transfer asymmetry under descent dynamics.** [A] model-internal, executed.
The corpus's first run experiment (2026-06-10, this folder). Static engine confirmed and identified as Pigou–Dalton; naive dynamic reading refuted; triage amendment and currency axiom owed to Paper W; extraction robustly worst; the Arjuna-without-Kṛṣṇa gap identified, with the v2 (regeneration) experiment registered. The intellectual yield per token of this experiment exceeded everything written about Paper W since its creation — which is the program's whole argument in one line. → **R3** (results: [R3_TRANSFER_ASYMMETRY_RESULTS.md](R3_TRANSFER_ASYMMETRY_RESULTS.md)).

**#4 — The two-factor multiplicative structure of productivity.** [I], the keystone wager.
`P_node = Φ × V` is the finite-node/contact-register joint everything else hangs from. It is testable without a lab: blind raters, vignette battery, factor recovery, and the decisive comparison — does the **product** of recovered factors predict an independent criterion better than their sum or either alone? A pre-registered null kills cleanly (third factor of comparable variance, or additive ≥ multiplicative). The corpus should want this test precisely because losing it would be informative. → **R4**.

**#5 — The unit as frame (finity, in journal language).** [A] history; [I] thesis.
Euclid's monad is defined as the measure, not a number (Elements VII, Def. 1–2); Stevin's *L'Arithmétique* (1585) is the moment 1 was promoted to ordinary numberhood; dimensional analysis (Buckingham 1914) and the 2019 SI redefinition are where modern science rediscovered the unit's frame-character operationally. Thesis: the unit is a boundary object of the same kind zero's history exhibits, hidden in plain sight by positional notation. This is the externally defensible core of Paper VII, stripped of ontology. Solid scholarship, modest ceiling, low risk. → **R5**.

**#6 — The constitutional epistemics itself.** [S], not yet earned.
Tier-tagging, kill criteria, the Grace Exit, refused over-reaches, the recorded retractions: a worldview with built-in falsifiers is genuinely rare, and "how to build a self-correcting speculative system" could one day be a contribution to philosophy of science methodology. But a methodology paper from a framework with no empirical wins reads as self-praise. This becomes writable **after** R2–R4 produce results, citing them as the method's fruit. Parked deliberately.

**Explicitly NOT on the slate (the HOLD list):** the force-ladder and D0→D6 cosmology (Paper P's own SU(3) obstruction stands; no novel quantitative prediction exists), the photon/Bloch identifications, the theology, and any claim containing "unifies" or "solves all." These remain internal frontier work under their existing kill criteria. Submitting them now would spend the program's credibility before it has any. The cosmology earns external life only via the grammar (R1) and the bench (R2–R4) first.
