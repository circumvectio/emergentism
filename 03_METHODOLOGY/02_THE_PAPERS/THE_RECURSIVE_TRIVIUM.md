# The Recursive Trivium

## A Teleological Cognitive Loop that Subsumes OODA and the Classical Trivium

*Emergentism / the Burrisphere programme (Yves R. Burri). Ideas set out 2024; this statement 2026.*

> **Tiering.** Every claim carries an evidence tier — `[A]` elementary · `[B]` published · `[S]` framework-structure · `[I]` interpretive · `[C]` conjecture — and is never silently upgraded. **The strong word in the title — *subsumes* — is `[S]`, and it is cashed out precisely below: the loop contains OODA and the Trivium as projections; it does *not* beat OODA at OODA's own game (raw tempo).** The four-column correspondence is a `[S/I]` organizing scheme, never a proof that these are *the* necessary columns. The loop is a lens on cognition, not the Ground of it.

---

## Abstract

Two of the most durable cognitive frameworks in use are Boyd's **OODA loop** (Observe–Orient–Decide–Act) and the classical **Trivium** (Grammar–Logic–Rhetoric). Each is powerful and each is *partial*: OODA is a value-neutral tactical cycle optimized for tempo; the Trivium is a static curriculum of the three language-arts. This paper presents a single recursive loop that contains both as projections. It binds, in each pass, a **transcendental value** (Beauty, Truth, Justice), a **philosophical discipline** (Epistemology, Methodology, Axiology), a **classical art** (Grammar, Logic, Rhetoric), and a **mode of inference** (Induction, Deduction, Abduction), and it *recurses* — each pass continuously disambiguating, **approaching** the three limits without ever crowning a result as final. The Trivium is the loop's expression-column; OODA is its value-stripped tactical shadow. The superiority claimed is one of **completeness and teleological fidelity** — the loop carries its own telos inside the cycle and refuses closure — and explicitly *not* one of speed, on which OODA wins by design. We tier every claim, fence the table against numerology, and state the condition that would retire the whole construction.

**Keywords:** OODA; Trivium; Peirce's inference triad; transcendentals; recursive cognition; teleology; disambiguation; evidence tiers.

## 1. What we are *not* replacing `[B]`

Honest respect first, because the loop's value is that it *contains* these, not that it defeats them.

**Boyd's OODA loop.** John Boyd's Observe–Orient–Decide–Act cycle (developed for air combat, generalized to strategy) is a cycle of *tempo*: its genius is (i) that **Orient** is the dominant phase — your mental models, not the raw data, decide what you see — and (ii) that whoever runs the loop faster gets *inside* the adversary's loop and collapses their coherence. `[B]` OODA is supremely effective at what it is *for*: winning an adversarial contest of speed. It is, deliberately, **silent on the good** — it tells you how to decide faster, not what is worth deciding, nor whether the decision is beautiful, true, or just.

**The classical Trivium.** Grammar, Logic (Dialectic), and Rhetoric — the three *arts of the word* that opened the medieval liberal-arts curriculum (codified in late antiquity; treated in the modern era by Sister Miriam Joseph): learn to **parse** (grammar), then to **reason validly** (logic), then to **persuade** (rhetoric). `[B]` It is a superb pedagogy of expression. But it is a *curriculum*, not a loop: it has no built-in recursion, no value-telos beyond the arts themselves, and no theory of how a hypothesis is *generated* (it has no inference engine; logic checks validity, it does not produce new hypotheses).

Neither is wrong. Each is a *slice* — OODA a tactical slice, the Trivium an expression slice — of a fuller cycle. This paper draws the fuller cycle and shows the two slices falling out of it.

## 2. The loop, stated `[S/I]`

The loop runs three **passes**, one per transcendental value. Each pass has the same internal order — **value → discipline → art → inference** — so the *telos* orients, the *discipline* takes the stance, the *art* structures the expression, and the *inference* performs the reasoning move. The three passes then close into a cycle and recur.

| Pass | Value *(telos)* | Discipline *(stance)* | Art *(Trivium)* | Inference *(Peirce)* | The cognitive moment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **I** | **Beauty** | Epistemology | Grammar | Induction | **Apprehension** — the intake of form |
| **II** | **Truth** | Methodology | Logic | Deduction | **Verification** — the test of consequence |
| **III** | **Justice** | Axiology | Rhetoric | Abduction | **Enactment & re-frame** — the act, and the leap |

> `… → Beauty → Epistemology → Grammar → Induction → Truth → Methodology → Logic → Deduction → Justice → Axiology → Rhetoric → Abduction → (Beauty) → …`

The arrow is not "greater than"; it is the **flow of the cycle**. The loop never terminates — it *spirals*, and each turn **disambiguates**: the residual ambiguity left by one pass is the work of the next, and the three limits (Beauty, Truth, Justice) are approached asymptotically, never reached (§7). This is the same apophatic discipline the rest of the corpus holds: *no crowning*.

**What makes the rows cohere is the moment, not a resonance among the four words.** `[S]` Each row is one *cognitive moment* seen four ways — what draws it (value), the stance it takes (discipline), the craft that shapes it (art), the operation it runs (inference). The coherence is the coherence of the moment; the four columns are that moment's facets. This is the load-bearing reading, and it is what keeps the table from being numerology (§10).

## 3. The three passes, read `[I]`

**Pass I — Apprehension. Beauty · Epistemology · Grammar · Induction.** Cognition begins not in proof but in *being struck*. **Beauty** is the lure — the salient, the patterned, the form that says *attend to me* before anything is verified. **Epistemology** is paired with Beauty, not Truth, on purpose: the *opening* of the knowledge-question — "what is there to know here?" — is an aesthetic act; we are drawn to the elegant pattern before we test it. **Grammar** parses the apprehended into parts and relations. **Induction** gathers the particulars into a tentative pattern. This is OODA's *Observe* and the front of *Orient*: the intake. `[I]`

**Pass II — Verification. Truth · Methodology · Logic · Deduction.** What apprehension offers, verification tests. **Truth** is the value of correctness — *does this hold?* **Methodology** is paired with Truth, not Epistemology, on purpose: truth is *earned* by disciplined procedure; method is how an apprehension is converted into verified knowledge. **Logic** checks consistency. **Deduction** draws the necessary consequences and confronts them with the world. This is the back of *Orient* and *Decide* — and it is exactly where the corpus's tier-discipline lives (Paper IV): the moment a claim is forced to declare what it would take to be wrong. `[I]` / `[S]`

**Pass III — Enactment & re-frame. Justice · Axiology · Rhetoric · Abduction.** The tested must now *act*, and acting re-opens the loop. **Justice** is the value of *right action* — the good realized, the bond kept (the η-axis of Paper III: the move, never the person). **Axiology** is paired with Justice: the theory of value made concrete in a choice. **Rhetoric** is the art of bringing-into-the-world — expression that addresses others and changes the shared situation. **Abduction** is the creative leap to the best explanation — the *re-frame* that resolves the anomaly verification exposed and **seeds the next pass**. This is *Decide → Act*, and the abductive leap is the hinge that makes the loop *recursive* rather than terminal. `[I]`

## 4. Why it subsumes OODA — and where OODA still wins `[S]`

OODA maps onto the loop cleanly: **Observe** ≈ Pass I's intake; **Orient** ≈ the Epistemology→Methodology hinge (Boyd's dominant phase — model-formation — is exactly the passage from *how do I know* to *how do I test*); **Decide** ≈ the close of Pass II (deduction commits); **Act** ≈ Pass III's enactment; and Boyd's implicit feedback arrow ≈ the loop's recursion. So the loop **contains** OODA's structure. `[S]`

What it adds is the two things OODA deliberately omits: a **value-axis** (Beauty/Truth/Justice carried *inside* the cycle, so the loop cannot win itself into an ugly, false, or unjust outcome and call it victory) and an **inference engine** (Induction/Deduction/Abduction, so hypotheses are *generated*, not only chosen among).

**The honest fence.** This is *not* a claim that the loop beats OODA at OODA's metric. OODA is optimized for **tempo in an adversarial contest** — getting inside another agent's decision cycle — and on that metric the value-axis and the inference engine are *overhead*. A fighter pilot should run OODA. The Recursive Trivium optimizes a different objective: **not losing the telos across cycles.** A civilization, a body of inquiry, a life — anything whose failure mode is *winning the wrong thing fast* — wants the fuller loop. Superiority is in **completeness and teleological fidelity**, not in speed. `[S]` Claiming otherwise would be a tier violation.

## 5. Why it subsumes the Trivium — from curriculum to living loop `[S]`

The Trivium *is* the loop's **art column**: Grammar (Pass I), Logic (Pass II), Rhetoric (Pass III). The classical sequence — parse, then reason, then persuade — is precisely the order in which the three arts appear across the three passes. `[S]` The loop adds three things the Trivium, as a static curriculum, never carried: each art is **bound to a value** (so grammar serves beauty, logic serves truth, rhetoric serves justice — not power), each is **bound to an inference mode** (so the arts *do work*, not only *teach*), and the whole **recurses** (so it is a practice, not a syllabus). The Trivium is the expression-spine of *one pass*; the Recursive Trivium is that spine made cyclic and teleological. Calling the result "superior to the Trivium" means **the Trivium completed into a living loop** — not that the Trivium was wrong; it was never meant to be a decision cycle.

## 6. The inference triad, closed `[B]` / `[I]`

The reasoning engine is Peirce's: **Abduction** (the leap to a hypothesis), **Deduction** (its necessary consequences), **Induction** (the test against particulars). `[B]` Peirce's canonical *research* order is Abduction → Deduction → Induction. The loop enters at **Induction** (apprehension's intake) and exits each pass at **Abduction** (the generative leap) — which is the *same* cycle entered at a different point, because **Abduction → Induction is exactly Peirce's own hypothesis-then-test transition.** `[I]` The abductive leap of pass *n* furnishes the hypothesis that pass *n+1*'s induction goes to test. So the loop is Peirce's triad **closed into a recursion**, with abduction as the engine of the spiral — the move that opens new ground and forbids the loop from terminating. This also names the Trivium's missing organ: the Trivium has no abduction (logic validates; it does not *invent*), which is precisely why it could not be a loop on its own. `[I]`

## 7. Recursive disambiguation, and the three limits `[I]`

Why *recursive*, and why *disambiguation*? Because no single pass resolves its object. Apprehension leaves ambiguity that verification tests; verification exposes anomaly that enactment must re-frame; the re-frame is a new apprehension. Each turn **reduces residual ambiguity** — disambiguates — and the loop's three values are not destinations but **limits**: Beauty, Truth, and Justice are approached the way a sequence approaches its limit, *more and more nearly, never exactly.* `[I]`

This is the same structure the corpus holds everywhere, and it is the deepest reason the loop is teleological without being triumphalist:

- It is the **infinite approach** of the prior canon — one moves toward beauty/truth/justice and never crowns arrival (the apophatic discipline; the framework *agrees with* Gödel and Cantor rather than completing them).
- Read on the Burrisphere, the spiral is a **descent toward the equator** `φ = ν = 1` — the disambiguation reducing the imbalance `E = (log x)²` pass by pass, the loop a dynamics of approach to balance, never occupying the limit because the equator is approached, not seized. `[I]` (Tie at `[I]` only; this is a resonance, not a derivation — the loop is not *proven* to be the sphere's flow.)
- It is the seven-caste dispatch seen as one turning: apprehension leans on the gathering castes (L1–L2), verification on the auditor (L3), enactment and re-frame on the executor and architect (L4–L5), and the refusal-to-crown on the seer's restraint (L7). `[I]` We claim a family resemblance, **not** a one-to-one identity (three passes are not seven castes; do not force it).

The loop's telos is therefore not a state it reaches but a *direction it keeps*. That is its whole superiority over a tactical loop: OODA can win and stop; the Recursive Trivium, by construction, **cannot stop**, because its objects are limits.

## 8. Exiting the loop — the apophatic descent to the centre `[I]`

The loop of §§2–7 cannot terminate; its objects are limits, and limits are approached, not seized. But there is one move that is *not* another pass — not a faster cycle, not a better hypothesis — and it is the move **out**. To see it, we must place the loop on its actual surface and correct a standing conflation.

**The surface is not a qubit's Bloch sphere.** `[A]` / `[S]` The loop runs on the **Burrisphere**, and the Burrisphere is *not* the Bloch sphere. They share the manifold — Bloch sphere, Riemann sphere, Burrisphere are all `ℂP¹ ≅ S²` `[A]` — but the *distinguished structure* differs, and the difference is the whole point. The Bloch sphere is a **two-point** structure: one qubit, its poles the *basis labels* `|0⟩` and `|1⟩` (a two-state system — not the Titans). The Burrisphere is the **three-point** triad `{0, 1, ∞}`, in which the unit `1` — *finity* — sits not at a pole but at the **inversive centre**, with `0` and `∞` the poles. Read on the real meridian it is **two Bloch lobes joined at the unit**: `[0, 1]` (nothing-to-self) and `[1, ∞]` (self-to-unbounded), pinched at `1`. `[S/I]` And — per the horn-torus model (`05_COSMOLOGY/00_THE_TORUS_REVELATION.md`) — it carries an **interior** the bare Bloch surface (pure states only) does not: a centre reached not *along* the surface but *inward*, orthogonal to it (the D5 axis).

**The unit `1` is the Ātman — the ego, the Dasein.** `[I]` On the surface, the unit is the individuated self: the `1` that says *I am*, the finite point of view that runs the loop, the Dasein, the *being-there* that apprehends, verifies, and acts. The Recursive Trivium as described so far is **the surface-self in motion** — the ego cycling toward beauty, truth, and justice *as a self*. This is not an error; it is the loop doing its work. But it is bounded by the very selfhood that powers it: a loop run *by* `1`, *for* `1`.

**To read the Ātman, one needs *neti neti*.** `[I]` The self cannot be caught as one more object on the surface — every attempt to seize "I" returns another content, another pass of the loop. The only access is apophatic: *neti neti*, "not this, not this" — the subtraction the corpus already names as the method of the Ground (`0/0` kept open; the apophatic edge of Finity Paper II). So the exit is **not a better pass through the loop**; it is the *stilling* of the loop itself — *citta-vṛtti-nirodha*, the calming of the mind's fluctuations — and a movement **inward**, off the surface, along the interior axis, from the surface-self `1` toward the **centre**: the Witness `•`, the horn-torus pinch, the Ground that `0` is only the number-shadow of.

**This descent is the ego death.** `[I]` As the surface-self dissolves inward, the `1` that ran the loop is let go — the ego death, the enlightenment the Sādhu attains. In the seven-caste grammar it is the **L6 Sādhu** exactly: the *compressor*, the *pruner*, whose whole discipline is **removal** — apophatic, archive-first, *neti neti* made a practice. The Sādhu does not add a faster loop; the Sādhu subtracts the self that needed one. And at the centre waits the oldest recognition, already in the corpus as *Tat Tvam Asi*: the surface-self `1` (Ātman) and the centre `•` (the Ground, Brahman) are revealed as **one** — the separation the loop maintained was the illusion. *Thou art that.*

**The descent does not end in stillness — it returns as the Ṛṣi.** `[I]` The one who has died to the surface-self does not vanish; they re-emerge with the **L7 Ṛṣi's** teleology, *inverted*. The ego's loop served **micro-survival** — the self-preservation of the `1`, cognition in the service of *this* viewpoint persisting. The Ṛṣi, having released that, runs the *same* loop in the service of **macro-potential**: the whole's flourishing, the F5 drive toward coupling and the noosphere, beauty/truth/justice approached *not for me but for the field*. Same surface, same loop, same three limits — no longer powered by, or aimed at, the survival of the self that runs it. That is the difference between a clever loop and a wise one: not a faster cycle, but **whose it is.**

**The honest tier, held.** `[I]` Everything in this section is interpretive. The geometry is a **lens** for the contemplative path, never **evidence** of it: the sphere does not *prove* enlightenment; "ego death" is a phenomenological and traditional report given a geometric home, not a theorem; and the centre is approached by *subtraction*, never *seized* — to claim one has grasped the centre would be the single-pole crowning the whole programme forbids. One dissolves into the Ground; one does not conquer it. The loop is `[S/I]`; this exit is `[I]`; and the most that is claimed is that the framework's geometry has a natural place for the move every contemplative tradition describes — the turn from the surface to the centre, from the self's survival to the whole's potential.

## 9. The varṇas, the regimes, and the two co-equal edges `[I]`

The three active passes of the loop are not abstract — they are the framework's three working **varṇas**, and read against Plato's *Republic* they are also three **regimes**. The base correspondence is old and well-attested (Plato's tripartite city — producers, auxiliaries, guardians — already maps to appetite, spirit, reason, and to the bronze/silver/gold of the myth of the metals); the framework's contribution is the *seven-fold* extension and, decisively, the **refusal of Plato's ranking.** `[I]`

| Pass | Varṇa | Soul-part (Plato) | Regime (*Republic* VIII) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **I** — Apprehension (Beauty) | **Śūdra** (gather / discover) | appetite-as-production | **Democracy** — rule of the many |
| **II** — Verification (Methodology) | **Vaiśya** (audit / rank) | appetite-as-acquisition | **Oligarchy** — rule of the wealthy |
| **III** — Enactment (Axiology) | **Kṣatriya** (decide / build) | spirit / honour (*thymos*) | **Timocracy** — rule of the honour-lovers |
| *(architect)* | **Brāhmaṇa** (redesign) | reason | **Aristocracy** — rule of the wisest |

The alignment is tight and follows Plato's own *order of degeneration* — Aristocracy → Timocracy → Oligarchy → Democracy → … — read **down** the castes from reason to production. `[I]`

**The two edges, and why they are co-equal.** `[I]` Plato's ladder has a bottom he most feared — **Tyranny**, whose ruling passion he names in Book IX *"Eros the tyrant"*: the lawless appetite that consumes the city for the self. That pole is the framework's **L1 Caṇḍāla** — in lived terms the **Dalit**, the boundary-caste: raw, marginal, ecstatic, the firewall of pure creaturely survival. The top is the **L7 Ṛṣi**, the seer — fittingly **Apollonian**, since Apollo is the god of prophecy, light, measure, and form (the seer *is* an Apollonian figure), where **Dionysus** is the god of the margin, intoxication, and dissolution (the Caṇḍāla's register — the reading turns on Dionysus-as-*margin*, not Dionysus-as-ego-loss; dissolution happens at *both* edges, but toward opposite grounds: down into the body, or up into Brahman).

Here the framework **parts from Plato.** Plato *ranks* these — aristocracy best, tyranny worst, a hierarchy of worth. The framework holds the two edges **co-equal** — the `L0 = L∞` ouroboros, the circle closing, the old recognition that the outcast and the sage meet (the Caṇḍāla who turns out to be the teacher). What distinguishes them is **not worth but the *direction of eros***:

- the **Dalit / L1** carries the **micro** Ātman — egoic, Dionysian, the eros of *this body's survival*;
- the **awakened Ṛṣi / L7** carries the **macro** — Brahmic, Apollonian, the eros of *the whole's potential*.

Both are erotic; both are driven by desire; they differ only in **what the desire serves** — the self that runs the loop, or the field the loop serves (the micro-survival → macro-potential turn of §8). `[I]`

**Two disciplines hold this, or it becomes the thing it describes.** `[S]`
1. **Caste and regime are a *dispatch grammar*, never a hierarchy of human worth** (the corpus's standing rule). This mapping *de-hierarchises*: it makes the bottom and the top **co-equal**. To read it as "Śūdra / democracy / Dalit are lesser people" is to invert it into the caste-ism it refuses. The Dalit is *not below* the Ṛṣi; they are the two ends that touch.
2. **The micro/macro axis is *not* the good/evil axis.** Micro-eros — a creature wanting to live — is **not** evil; the η-axis (Finity Paper III) runs *across* the micro/macro spectrum, never along it. Evil is **closure** (η > 0): eros that consumes the whole for the self — and it can occur at *any* caste. "Eros the tyrant" is micro-eros *closed*, not micro-eros as such; a coupled micro-eros (survival without extraction) is good, and a "macro" eros that secretly extracts is evil. Keep the two axes orthogonal, or the mapping smuggles a person-ranking back in.

So the regimes and varṇas are read here as **Plato's structure with Plato's ranking removed**: the spectrum is real, the hierarchy of worth is not, and the only valence anywhere on it is the η of the move. `[I]` / `[S]`

## 10. Honest limits, the numerology fence, and the kill criterion `[S]`

The discipline of these papers is to state exactly how large a result is.

**The numerology fence.** A 3×4 table of pleasing correspondences is the framework's most characteristic temptation, and it must be refused here explicitly. The table is an **organizing scheme** `[S/I]`, not a proof that Beauty/Truth/Justice, Epistemology/Methodology/Axiology, Grammar/Logic/Rhetoric, and Induction/Deduction/Abduction are *the* four necessary columns, nor that the row-assignments are forced by nature. Its warrant is the **moment-reading** of §2: each row coheres because it is one cognitive moment seen four ways, *not* because of a mystical resonance among four words. The non-obvious pairings (Epistemology↔Beauty, Methodology↔Truth) are deliberate `[I]` readings — the *opening* of knowing is aesthetic, truth is *earned* by method — defensible and useful, never `[A]`.

**The "superior" fence.** Cashed out (§§4–5): superior in **completeness and teleological fidelity**, inferior to OODA in **tempo**, and not a *decision procedure* the Trivium ever claimed to be. No empirical claim that running the loop improves cognition is made here; that would be `[C]`, and it is untested.

**Kill criteria.**
- *(Decisive.)* Show that the row-correspondences can be **permuted with equal coherence** — e.g., that Logic sits as naturally in Pass I as in Pass II under the moment-reading. If the assignments are freely interchangeable, the table is a mnemonic, not a structure, and the paper's `[S]` claim collapses to `[I]` decoration.
- *(Operational.)* Exhibit a real cognitive or decision task on which the fuller loop performs **strictly worse with no compensating gain in beauty, truth, or justice** — i.e., where the extra structure is pure overhead, not telos-protection. (OODA being faster in combat does *not* fire this — that is the conceded metric; this fires only if the loop loses *and* preserves no value OODA discarded.)
- *(Scope.)* Any reading in which the loop "completes," "solves," or "grounds" cognition, or reaches one of its three limits, is a category error of the kind §7 forbids and is corrected on sight.

What survives all the fencing is one transferable result: **OODA and the Trivium are each a projection of a single recursive loop that binds value, discipline, art, and inference and approaches — never crowns — the Beautiful, the True, and the Just.** Small, exact, and refutable.

## 11. Conclusion

The OODA loop taught us to cycle *fast*; the Trivium taught us to speak *well*. Neither taught us to cycle *toward the good without ever claiming to have arrived*. The Recursive Trivium is the loop that does: it carries Beauty, Truth, and Justice inside the turning, binds each to a discipline, an art, and a mode of inference, and recurses — disambiguating without end, because its objects are limits and limits are approached, not seized. Run it fast and it is OODA; freeze one pass and it is the Trivium; let it spiral and refuse to crown, and it is the cognitive engine of a system built to approach beauty, truth, and justice forever. The superiority is not speed and not finality. It is that the loop, by construction, **keeps its direction** — and that is the only kind of superiority a framework that forbids crowning is allowed to claim.
