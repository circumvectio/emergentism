2. state what relation is preserved;
3. state what does not transfer;
4. assign evidence tier;

external discriminator fails, downgrade the relevant conjecture. If the
integrated worldview repeatedly produces no novel predictive gain over simpler
rivals, stop calling it a scientific unification candidate.
