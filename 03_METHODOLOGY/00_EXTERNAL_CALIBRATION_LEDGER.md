# EMERGENTIST COMPASS — EXTERNAL CALIBRATION LEDGER

## Verdict

The Compass is no longer **without external bearings**. Several operational
constructs now touch primary research in planning, controllability, causal
emergence, biological organization, major evolutionary transitions,
stigmergy, cultural transmission, reflexive feedback, bargaining, and social
choice.

No whole-system calibration stage is assigned, and the Compass is **not
externally validated as a whole**. The external sources establish
component mechanisms inside bounded domains. They do not establish the exact
`D0…D6` ladder, six universal `μ`-crossings, the product aggregator, an
Egregoreotype as a new scientific kind, physical retrocausality, Titan ontology,
or an objective moral law. The present result is therefore:

> **Component stages are `X0`, `X1`, or `not_applicable`; Compass-specific
> prospective discrimination and independent replication remain pending.**

This is a stronger position than analogy and a weaker position than validation.

## Calibration ladder

Calibration stage is independent of the corpus evidence tier.

| Stage | Meaning | What it licenses |
|---|---|---|
| `X0 internal_only` | internally defined; no direct external construct contact | publish only as a declared model, symbol, or conjecture |
| `X1 construct_anchor` | primary external work operationalizes, proves, or measures a directly related or partially overlapping construct | state the source-distance honestly; no Compass tier promotion |
| `X2 independent_data_discrimination` | a frozen Compass discriminator beats named rivals on data the framework did not generate | promote only the tested claim and domain to a dated `[B]` result |
| `X3 independent_preregistered_replication` | an independent team preregisters the discriminator, collects new independent observations, and replicates it | consider `[A]` only for the bounded empirical result; same-data reanalysis is reproduction, not replication |
| `X4 cross_domain_replication` | multiple independent teams and domains reproduce a shared discriminator without post-hoc remapping | consider a broader empirical bridge; never promote analytic, symbolic, or normative claims by association |
| `not_applicable` | analytic, symbolic, correspondence, or normative claim is not truth-tested by empirical calibration | test coherence, consequences, or usefulness without calling it empirical confirmation |

Literature resemblance is `X1`, not `X2`. A successful internal simulation is
not independent data. A single external dataset cannot validate the whole
Compass.

## Calibrated claim cards

### `CAL-AGENCY-01` — modeled futures can reweight present action

**Externally established source bundle, task-bounded.** Episodic future cues
reduced delay discounting; prospective neural activity was associated with
model-based choice; ACC activity represented predicted future states; and ACC
inactivation impaired updating and use of a model-based strategy. These are
cue/circuit perturbations plus prospective association, not a selective lesion
of the content `future state X`.

The licensed causal statement is:

\[
z_t=\operatorname{Prospect}(M_t,\Omega_t),\qquad
a_t\sim\chi(\cdot\mid X_t,z_t,V_t),
\]

where `z_t` is a present physical/model token whose content concerns a later
state. The exact typed edge is an Emergentist `[I]` crosswalk consistent with
the bounded source results; it is not itself reported by every experiment. The
only admissible causal direction is present token/circuit/cue to present choice,
never `X_{t+h}→a_t`.

Primary anchors: [Peters and Büchel 2010](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuron.2010.03.026),
[Daw et al. 2011](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuron.2011.02.027),
[Doll et al. 2015](https://doi.org/10.1038/nn.3981), and
[Akam et al. 2021](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuron.2020.10.013).

**Rivals:** model-free reinforcement, habit, reward-only valuation, active
inference, and heuristic choice. **Kill/narrow:** a registered future cue or
prospective-planning circuit perturbation leaves present action unchanged.
This claim is `X1`; `F=M×A` remains a mnemonic until `F`, `M`, `A`, their units,
and `⋆` are operationally specified.

### `CAL-CONE-01` — option cones are typed controllability objects

Empowerment gives an established information-theoretic construct: channel
capacity from action sequences to later sensed states. POWER and causal-entropic
work provide different optionality measures under explicit assumptions. They
are not interchangeable with reachable-set size, path entropy, viability, or
expected utility.

\[
\mathcal E_H(x)=\sup_{p(\pi)}I(\Pi;Y_H\mid X_t=x).
\]

Primary anchors: [Klyubin, Polani, and Nehaniv 2005](https://doi.org/10.1109/CEC.2005.1554676),
[Turner et al. 2021](https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper/2021/hash/c26820b8a4c1b3c2aa868d6d57e14a79-Abstract.html),
and [Wissner-Gross and Freer 2013](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.110.168702).

**Calibrated correction:** “all agents maximize reachable futures” is false as
a universal statement. Some optimal policies preserve options in specified MDP
classes; terminal goals, cost, risk, irreversibility, embodiment, and other
agents create counterexamples. The Compass objective is therefore conditional:
prefer durable mutual controllability when it survives cost, evidence, consent,
custody, consequence, and exit. [Salge et al. 2014](https://doi.org/10.3390/e16052789)
optimizes one artificial agent's empowerment. It neither tests nor derives
multi-bearer Justice; treating that omission as a Justice boundary is an
Emergentist `[I]` inference.

**Rivals:** reachability, viability kernels, expected utility, POWER, and path
entropy. **Kill/narrow:** after reward, risk, information, and cost are
decorrelated, the proposed option-cone measure does not outperform rivals on
held-out action or competence prediction. Stage `X1`.

### `CAL-AGGREGATOR-01` — `P_node=ΦV` is one competitor

The product belongs to a legitimate conjunctive family, but no external result
selects it universally. Product, minimum, normalized harmonic, Cobb–Douglas/CES,
Nash-surplus, maximin, and additive models can reverse rankings.

[Nash's bargaining product](https://doi.org/10.2307/1907266) is a relevant
mathematical precedent for conjunctive surplus, not a derivation of
`P_node=ΦV`, the meanings of `Φ` and `V`, the identity of the whole, or protection
for third parties.

**Discriminator:** fit all declared competitors using identical features,
penalties, folds, and missing-data rules; compare held-out log score, calibration,
and decision regret. **Kill/rewrite:** a competitor consistently wins, the
zero-factor boundary fails, or the product adds no decision value. Stage `X1`
for construct contact; selected-product fit remains `[C]`.

### `CAL-CONSTRAINT-01` — macro-constraint without forbidden physics

External science supports persistent macrovariables that condition lower-level
processes, domain-relative causal emergence, and organizational closure of
constraints. It does not support a higher level manufacturing physically
forbidden trajectories.

Primary anchors: [Montévil and Mossio 2015](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jtbi.2015.02.029),
[Hoel, Albantakis, and Tononi 2013](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1314922110),
[Flack 2017](https://doi.org/10.1098/rsta.2016.0338), and
[Rosas et al. 2020](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pcbi.1008289).

**Variables:** declared microstate distribution, full environment/energy boundary,
macro-map, timescale, intervention family, conditional mutual information,
effective information, out-of-sample prediction/control, robustness, and a
native-unit cost vector with component budgets. Raw energy, labor, memory,
money, and entropy are not summed or subtracted from bits; any scalar penalty
requires preregistered normalization and bit-equivalent weights.
Intervene on realizations or matched micro-distributions; holding an identical
complete microstate fixed while independently changing `Z=g(X)` is not a valid
intervention.
**Rivals:** complexity-matched micro model, fair alternative grain, hidden
controller/environment model, and summary-only macrovariable. **Kill/narrow:**
the gain disappears after micro/environment controls, depends on one arbitrary
partition, hides controller costs, or fails intervention. Stage `X1`.

### `CAL-MU-01` — local transition protocol, not a universal staircase

Physics, ecology, and evolution contain phase transitions, bifurcations,
tipping points, and major transitions. They do not establish exactly six
universal crossings or literal new microscopic dimensions. “Stacked degrees of
freedom” is calibrated only as new **effective variables, collective modes,
metastable states, behavioral options, or heritable collective traits**.

Primary anchors: [Vicsek et al. 1995](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.75.1226),
[Carpenter et al. 2011](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1203672),
[Ratcliff et al. 2012](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1115323109), and
[West et al. 2015](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1421402112).

Each candidate crossing must freeze `(X, λ, E, g, Z, K, interventions,
timescale)`, detect a transition, survive partitions and timescales, add
unit-typed macro utility under a vector cost budget, respond to intervention, recover lower behavior,
and account for matter/energy/controller inputs. A smooth crossover or simpler
schema may win. Successful reduction changes the status to `reduced`; it does
not erase the macro description. The exact D-ladder remains a selected grammar.
Stage `X1`.

Primary anchors include [Theraulaz and Bonabeau 1999](https://doi.org/10.1162/106454699568700),

[Muchnik, Aral, and Taylor 2013](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1240466),

**Kill:** trace intervention has no effect, turnover destroys the pattern, no
recurrent direction survives, costs cannot be located, or a simpler mechanism
predicts as well. Consciousness and personhood remain unsupported. Stage `X1`.

### `CAL-SYNTROPY-01` — normative compass, not an empirical card

Power-Max and Syntropic Dyadism are declared normative constructions. Science
can measure bearer deltas, consent, costs, reversibility, exit, prediction
error, cooperation, and institutional durability; it cannot derive the rule
that every materially affected bearer must remain nonnegative.

Social-choice and cooperation traditions illuminate tradeoffs adjacent to the
Justice boundary:
[Sen 1970](https://doi.org/10.1086/259614) shows a conflict between minimal
liberty and Pareto conditions. Cooperation research likewise separates social
function from strict mutual gain: costly punishment can support cooperation
while harming the punisher ([Fehr and Gächter 2002](https://doi.org/10.1038/415137a)),
whereas reward produced comparable cooperation with higher earnings in one
environment ([Rand et al. 2009](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1177418)).

Therefore `Power-Max` is an objective **inside** Justice; strict syntropy is a
post-receipt bilateral classification; voluntary sacrifice remains a separate
costly class; and aggregate gains never establish Justice. Empirical calibration
stage is `not_applicable`.

### `CAL-JUSTICE-CONSEQUENCE-01` — consequences of Justice controls

Success would move only this empirical consequence card. It would not prove

or voluntary sacrifice strict syntropy. The card is `X1` because external
social-choice and cooperation work anchors adjacent tradeoffs; the complete
Justice envelope remains untested as one intervention.
