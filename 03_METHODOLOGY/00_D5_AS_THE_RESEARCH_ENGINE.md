# D5 as a research content register

The legacy filename remains stable, but its former claim that institutions,
measurement, strategy, or research activity “live at D5” is retired.

In the canonical typing:

- **D4 is actuality:** actual researchers, models, observations, institutions,
  resources, selections, commitments, interventions, outcomes, and records.
- **D5 is possible content:** counterfactual alternatives and modeled future
  histories that a D4 system can represent and rank.

A research program is therefore a D4 practice that uses D5 content. It becomes
empirical only when an actual protocol commits actual means, the environment
returns an outcome independently of the selector, and the result updates a
public record.

## The defensible bridge

Game theory, behavioral science, mechanism design, law, coordination research,
and AI can test whether a specified intervention changes actual behavior or
preserves actual options. Those disciplines do not become dimensions, and a
D5 label does not confer consciousness, normativity, sovereignty, or authority.

The useful public question is:

> Under a preregistered model, comparator, boundary, horizon, and kill
> criterion, does this intervention produce a discriminating D4 receipt?

That is a research program `[D]`, not evidence that the selected D0–D6 scaffold
is a necessary decomposition of nature.
