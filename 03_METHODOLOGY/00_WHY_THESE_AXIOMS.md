# Why these axioms

> **[金] Crack.** The historical O1–O5 argument claimed that a compact,
> orientable, simply connected complex substrate uniquely forced `S²`, then
> treated the chosen premises as if they were independently necessary. It also
> transferred quantum formalism into ontology. That was conditional selection
> presented as discovery.

The current Emergentist axioms are chosen because they form a compact,
correction-governed grammar for constrained emergence, typed actuality and
possibility, reflexive action, and bearer-complete justice. They are not the only
coherent axiom set and do not force a unique manifold, sevenfold ontology,
physical theory, or ethic.

`S²` remains a useful selected chart because it compactly represents reciprocal
coordinates and two distinct boundary limits. Its analytic facts are real; its
ontological use is interpretive. Alternative geometries remain legitimate rival
models and must win or lose on explicit compression, prediction, and intervention
tests rather than elegance alone.
