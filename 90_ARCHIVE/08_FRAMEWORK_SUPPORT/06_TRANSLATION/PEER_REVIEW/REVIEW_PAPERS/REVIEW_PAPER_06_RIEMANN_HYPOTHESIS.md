# REVIEW GUIDE: Paper 6 — Riemann Equatorial Balance

## Specific Review Questions
1. Is the observation that Re(s) = 1/2 is the "equator" of the critical strip mathematically meaningful or merely a verbal analogy?
2. Does the framework's claim that primes "balance at the equator" have any predictive content?
3. Is there any known connection between the Burri Sphere (S² with φ·ν=1) and the Riemann zeta function?
4. Could the ζ(s) zeros at Re(s)=1/2 be derived from any property of S²?

## Definitions to Scrutinize
- "Equatorial balance" applied to ζ(s) — is this a definition or a metaphor?

## Kill Criteria
