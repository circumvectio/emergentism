# CONJECTURES AND PROOFS — INDEX

## One Review Surface for What Is Claimed as Proof and What Remains Conjecture

**Status:** Active  
**Date:** 2026-04-20  
**Purpose:** Give peer reviewers one place to start when they need the framework's proof-facing and conjecture-facing materials separated but adjacent.

---

## How To Use This Index

1. Find the surface type.
2. Read the evidence-tier note.
3. Open the actual packet or guide from its home folder.
4. Review the packet on its own terms.

This index does **not** relocate the files. It routes to them.

**Archive compatibility note:** older rows may still show `[E]` from the preserved peer-review packet vocabulary. In live surfaces, split older `[E]` into `[A]` for external facts or `[B]` for repository/runtime receipts before reuse.
