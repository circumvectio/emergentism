## The Practice

The sitting practice is the foundation. Everything else is elaboration.

**Twenty to sixty minutes a day. Every day.**

1. Sit comfortably. Spine upright.
2. Breathe naturally. Do not control the breath.
3. Listen to whatever is present.
4. Look inward — at the space in which thoughts appear, not at the thoughts.
5. Recognize what is there when the narrator quiets. The ground. φ = 1.
6. Return. Open your eyes. Come back. The return is the point.

The practice is free. It requires no teacher, no app, no institution, no subscription. It requires only time and the willingness to sit still long enough to see what is already present.
