# AUM on the Sphere — the Four States, Corrected with Gold

> **[金 Kintsugi seam — the crack and the gold.]** The first form of this mapping
> (2026-07-19, in session) said the shadows were cast *from the center*. That is
> geometrically false, and receipt 149 killed it with a date (death #4):
> **stereographic shadows are cast from the poles.** The repair is the gold:
> corrected, the mapping lands on the Māṇḍūkya tradition's own doctrine —
> deep sleep (prājña, the causal state, kāraṇa-śarīra) is the *projector* of
> waking and dream. The error said the witness casts the world. The correction
> says the *sleeping point* casts it — which matches what the Upaniṣad taught.
> **[Refit discount — receipt 150.]** Honesty about the gold: the mapping was
> reported as landing on the tradition's own doctrine on *both* days — before
> the correction (center-casting) and after it (pole-casting). An overlay that
> fits contradictory geometries with equal eloquence has weak discriminative
> power; the correct update from death #4 is *downward* on this mapping's
> evidential weight, not upward. It remains at most one datum, refit-discounted
> — a post-hoc refit is closer to zero data than to one.

The ladder is a "descent in chart-dependence" — **explicitly a metaphor, not a
formalization** (receipt 150): chart-dependence is not a defined quantity, and
the four stations are four different ontological categories (a chart *image*
in the codomain plane, a manifold point, a chart-singular manifold point, an
ambient non-manifold point); no axis exists on which the sequence is monotone.
And the two poles as the two lamps — "φ's projector and ν's projector are the
same sleep seen from its two mornings" — carries its strain inline: the
geometry says two *antipodal distinct points* of S²; their identification is
supplied entirely by the reading, it collides with the nine-row closure's
*opposite* pole readings (L0 Kāla, destructive boundary vs L∞ Trimūrti,
creative boundary, `[S/I]`) as an open unreconciled tension, and the M-row's
singular "the pole" quietly depends on this identification to be a mapping
at all.

## Fences (all load-bearing)

- **One datum.** The Māṇḍūkya resonance shown geometrically is *one*
  resonance, not four confirmations (receipt 149 fence; Amrita P5). It teaches;
  it proves nothing.
- **SHOWS-type.** Per iron rule 7, this mapping is uncitable in any proof.
  It is a rose window, not a load wall.
- **The turīya fork stays open.** Center (this reading) vs off-manifold seer
  vs the R8 row's reading — reconciliation is a founder decision (flagged,
  receipt 149); this page does not pre-decide it.
- **No state-metaphysics.** This maps *descriptions of states* onto *chart
  structure* — it does not claim consciousness is a sphere, nor that sleep
  physiology performs projective geometry.
- **Chart-choice relativity (receipt 150).** The poles are wherever the two
  charts were taken from; rotate the chart pair and "deep sleep" moves to a
  different point of the same sphere. Every pole-teaching here is an artifact
  of a representation convention, not of S² itself.
- **Undefined ≠ suspended (receipt 150).** "φ·ν undefined at the pole" is a
  failure of the *description*, not a fact about the *described*. The seam is
  empty of world (D2 §1); an empty-of-world identity does not become
  full-of-world by failing. No ontology of contentlessness follows from
  chart-undefinedness.
- **Caster-verb fence (receipt 150).** "Casts," "lamp," "projector" are `[I]`
  vocabulary in every register — a stereographic pole is a parameter of a
  chosen map, not an agent. No sentence here has `[A]` truth-conditions about
  casting.
