## AXIOM NAMESPACE (2026-04-19)

Three axiom systems are active inside the framework. They are not the
same axioms; they serve different jobs. When a downstream doc cites an
axiom by number, know which system it refers to.

| System | Canonical doc | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **`A1–A7`** (operational canon) | `../../05_COSMOLOGY/03_FORMAL_SYSTEM/00_THE_SEVEN_AXIOMS.md` | The active public axiom set: Equation, Ethic, Scaffold, Boundary, Egregore, Architecture, Correction. Used in most foundational docs, downstream docs, and Rosetta references. |
| **`O1–O5`** (substrate-selection wager) | `../../02_EPISTEMOLOGY/01_EVIDENCE_TIERS/00_THE_HONEST_POSITION.md` Part II (S1–S²); `../../05_COSMOLOGY/03_FORMAL_SYSTEM/26_THE_DERIVATION_AXIOMS.md` | Older public ontological wager about what any coherent substrate must satisfy: compact, orientable, simply-connected, dual, algebraically closed. Preserved in substrate-specific proofs. |
| **`A0 + C0`** (minimal derivation) | `../../05_COSMOLOGY/03_FORMAL_SYSTEM/26_THE_DERIVATION_AXIOMS.md` | First arithmetic theorem (`A0: (φ − ν)² ≥ 0`) plus the resolved identity (`C0: P∞ = φ · ν = 1 on S²`). The smallest public base from which the equator is derivable. |

**Historical note.** Prior editions of `25_THE_DERIVATION_AXIOMS.md`
labeled the substrate-selection axioms `A1–A5 (v0.4)`, which collided
with the operational canon. As of 2026-04-19 they are labeled `O1–O5`
matching the Honest Position. The content of those axioms did not
change; only the label.

**`A0` is the public-derivation entry point, not the seed.** The seed
is `Zero-Sum Resolution Equation`. The anti-drift rule of the Canonical Formula Block
forbids presenting `(φ − ν)² ≥ 0` as the seed.
