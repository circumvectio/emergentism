## 1. The Four Domains
[I] We do not fight in just one dimension. We fight in four.

1.  **Cyber (The Code)**:
    - [I] **Control**: Software, APIs, Security.
    - [I] **Goal**: Technical Superiority.
2.  **Information (The Narrative)**:
    - [I] **Control**: Perception, PR, Influence.
    - [I] **Goal**: Narrative Hegemony.
3.  **Economic (The Resource)**:
    - [I] **Control**: Capital, Attention, Users.
    - [I] **Goal**: Resource Monopolization.
4.  **Cognitive (The Decision)**:
    - [I] **Control**: Speed (OODA), Logic (Game Theory).
    - [I] **Goal**: Decision Advantage (Out-thinking the opponent).

## 2. Integration (Synergy)
- [D] An Economic attack (Starvation) is 10x more effective when paired with an Informational attack (PR Campaign) and a Cognitive shock (Ambush).
- **The Stack**:
    - **Layer 1**: Code works perfectly (Cyber).
    - **Layer 2**: Users love it / Competitors look bad (Info).
    - **Layer 3**: Cash flow is positive / Competitors bleed (Econ).
    - **Layer 4**: We move faster than they can react (Cognitive).

## 3. Total War (Extirpation)
- When a threat is existential or a niche determines survival:
- [D] **Total War**: We deploy all assets across all domains.
- [D] **Objective**: Unconditional Surrender (Absorption) or Extinction (Market Exit).
