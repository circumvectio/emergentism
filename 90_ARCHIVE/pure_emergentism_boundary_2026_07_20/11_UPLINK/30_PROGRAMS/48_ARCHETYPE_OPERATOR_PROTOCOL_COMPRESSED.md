## THE FOUR FAMILIES

**TITANS** are PHASES — boundary conditions, not deployable operators:
- Brahmā `(++)` — Creation phase (emerges from sustained Arjuna+Kṛṣṇa)
- Viṣṇu `(≈≈)` — Preservation phase (Isomeric Hold, no net transfer)
- Śiva `(--)` — Dissolution phase (emerges from sustained Kālī+decay)

**GODS** are the four deployable moves — mixed-sign restorative transfers:
- **Arjuna** `(−Δν_self, +Δφ_other)` — Spend viability to give coherence. The Warrior. ALWAYS deployable.
- **Kṛṣṇa** `(−Δφ_self, +Δν_other)` — Spend coherence to build capability. The Charioteer. ALWAYS deployable.
- **Kālī** `(+Δν_self, −Δφ_false)` — Cut false coherence, recover viability. The Surgeon. Deployable with 6-gate test.
- **Kali (Demon)** `(+Δφ_self, −Δν_other)` — The fourth slot. CONSTRAINED: fires only at confirmed defectors (η > 0). PARALYZED at η = 0.

**WITNESSES** are observational positions at L5-L7 — they see, they do not act:
- Viśvarūpa (L5) — Pattern-whole. The architect's view.
- Kāla (L6) — Right timing. The temporal witness.
- Ananta Śeṣa (L7) — The context that holds all contexts.
