## Summary

Skeptic B verdict: the pilot supports only a narrow behavioral claim: in this small ultimatum-game dataset, a fairness/coherence proxy improves prediction over material payoff alone. It does **not** prove the full Extraction Law, the biological gate story, the Brahmā convergence claim, or the wider `Sigma-delta-P` metaphysics.

- Cut "one extractive round re-inflates Phi-spend far faster than it entered" until tested.
