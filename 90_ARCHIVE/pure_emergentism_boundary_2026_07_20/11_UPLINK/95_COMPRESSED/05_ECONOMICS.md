# The Economics

**Evidence tier:** [I]
*Organism document. Interpretive operational content. Bounded by current system state.*

**Rosetta boundary:** [I/D] This compressed economics map preserves target design and merchant-first sequencing. It does not [B] prove token launch, protocol liveness, or commercial traction without receipts.


## The stack

The financial architecture still reads as four layers:

- settlement
- credit
- equity
- applications

But the **merchant-first wedge** matters more than the elegant stack.

The first practical economic lane is:

- Business Account
- acceptance modes
- OFN receipts

Not the full target-state SoResFi machine all at once.

## The token split

In target design:

- `ZAI` = capital / governance
- `SKY` = currency / energy

That separation is constitutionally important.

But some token-economic claims remain **target-state design** rather than
live operational truth. Reconciliation surfaces outrank inherited
economic hype.

One name, one instrument still governs here:

- retired or overloaded economic terms stay retired
- protocol design is not runtime truth
- merchant proof outranks beautiful capital-stack language

## OFN

OFN is the standards layer for transferable, exit-compatible assets.

What matters most:

- right to resell
- royalty persistence
- exit clarity
- protocol-level `eta = 0`

## LP-100 / PRISM / Distillation

These remain part of the real design grammar, but not every inherited
mechanism should be narrated as fully live.

The compressed practical law is:

- product claims should follow merchant proof first
- deeper capital-engine claims should follow reconciliation and receipts

## One sentence to carry

The economic architecture is real as doctrine and partial
implementation, but commercial truth still begins with merchant proof,
not with the most beautiful token diagram.
