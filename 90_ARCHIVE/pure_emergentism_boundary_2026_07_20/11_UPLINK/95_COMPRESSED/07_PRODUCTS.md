The internal HTS substrate can be replicated. The graph of senior
decision-makers who trust Agentz as their tokenization-centre guide cannot.
[D] **Agentz Academy** is the vehicle proposed for building that graph while generating
revenue — seven products from Executive Briefings and
invitation-only Agentz Circle through to Executive Academy,
Founders' Retreat, a certification program, Intelligence Brief
subscription, and Board Advisory retainers.

[D] Because the delivery vehicles are themselves planned revenue products, **marketing is modeled as a profit centre**. Phase 1 allocation assumptions remain planning figures until governed receipts exist.

Builder-side funnel runs via a strategic partnership with **[yaru.ai](https://yaru.ai)** (Web3 Ecosystem-as-a-Service) — non-exclusive, no capital commitment, LOI is a T1 milestone.

Current PWA/spec mirror: [`00_AGENTZ_ACADEMY_v0_2.md`](../../../02_SKYZAI/01_NOOSPHERE/02_INFRASTRUCTURE/Cascade/08_AGENTZ_CLOUD_PWA/02_ACADEMY_ROUTES/00_AGENTZ_ACADEMY_v0_2.md). OFN is the standards/product bridge for transferable, exit-compatible
assets. It is real as a standards lane and partial build surface, but it
is **not yet a fully live end-to-end protocol lane**.

## Nexus

Nexus is the private-DAC / sovereign-identity product idea:

- one natural person
- one sovereign root
- one K2 gateway

It is central conceptually and architecturally, but the public product
surface is still narrower than the doctrine.
