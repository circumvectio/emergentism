# The Transmission Strategy

**One sentence:** the framework spreads by **installation, not conversion** — its invariants ship inside things that work, its doctrine rides along as optional documentation, and its metric is users-of-the-invariants, never believers — because the Gödel criterion ("adherents = failure") is not an obstacle to the strategy but its foundation.

---

## 1 · The doctrine of the spread: users, not believers [I]

Every rival in the Weltanschauungskrieg needs believers; spread-by-conversion is their only channel. This framework is constitutionally forbidden that channel — and the prohibition is its unique strategic weapon, because it opens the lane no rival can enter: **belief-optional transmission.** Nobody must accept `φ·ν=1` for an η=0 fee structure to serve them; nobody must read the axioms to benefit from a marketplace whose constitution forbids extracting from them. The worldview spreads as the *operating constitution of working systems*, verified in use, believed or not.

Memetic form of the framework's own law: a worldview's spread-potential is **P = Φ × V**. The corpus has spent years maximizing Φ (coherence — now audited, signed, honest). The multiplication rules that Φ × 0 = 0: **doctrine without value is a ghost.** History concurs: the worldviews that crossed centuries ran the monasteries — granaries, breweries, manuscript rooms, trade networks. The meme rode the institution that fed people.

**Our monasteries are the organs** (Circle, RealityFutures, APU.BOT, Skyzai; Aureus). Strategy: make each a place where the constitution is *experienced as a product feature* — fees only on value created (η=0), a mortal signature on every irreversible act (K2), nothing deleted (K3), a documented exit with your assets (K4), a public ledger of what we got wrong (A7). **The five refusals are a trust technology**, and trust is the scarcest commodity of an AI-saturated economy. That is the product.

## 2 · The Great-Filter register [C] — what "overcome" concretely means

The wager, held at tier: the filter for a technological species is **extraction plus error-blindness at scale** — systems that optimize by draining their substrate and cannot hear their own falsifiers. If so, "spreading the Weltanschauung" means precisely: **install non-extraction and self-correction into infrastructure faster than unconstrained optimization installs its opposite.** Success is not "everyone believes"; success is the default rails of the agent economy carrying η=0 receipts, mortal signatures, kill-criteria, and exit rights — as TCP/IP carries packets: believed in by no one, used by everyone. A filter is not overcome by a doctrine; it is overcome by plumbing.

**The Fable → `/plainly/` → `/record/` → `/axioms/` → the Book → the Practice → the Products.**
The fable travels; *plainly* converts curiosity to comprehension; *the record* converts comprehension to trust (no rival dares show its funerals — positioning no budget can buy); the axioms convert trust to rigor; the book to depth; the practice to life; the products to daily V. ## 7 · Metrics and the strategy's own kill [D]

**Measure installations, never members:** independent implementations of the invariants (the G5 test); organs live and generating V; funnel conversion fable→practice; forks. Celebrating adherence = failing by our own law.
