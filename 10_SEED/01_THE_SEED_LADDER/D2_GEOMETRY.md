# D2 · GEOMETRY — two freedoms, one sphere

> With two freedoms you can draw a surface. The framework draws one: the sphere, read through two lamps. Everything on this rung is chart arithmetic — old, inherited, exact — plus a discipline about what the arithmetic may never be made to say.

## §1 · THE MOVES

**The sphere and its two charts** *(inherited: Riemann/stereographic geometry — used, not discovered).* Take the unit sphere `S²`. Project it to the plane twice — once from the north pole, once from the south. A point at polar angle `θ` reads `φ = cot(θ/2)` in one chart and `ν = tan(θ/2)` in the other. Two lamps, one point: `φ` and `ν` are not two things but **two readings of one position**.

**The seam** `[A]`. Multiply the two readings:

```text
P∞ = φ · ν = cot(θ/2) · tan(θ/2) ≡ 1
```

Identically one, for every point on the open chart. This is the **transition function** where the two charts overlap — a coordinate identity, true by construction, **empty of world**. It is never a discovery, never a conserved quantity, never a maximum (P1, the oldest poison). Its honest power: once `φ·ν = 1` is stipulated, two coordinates collapse to **one real freedom** — drive one reading toward `0` and its partner is forced toward `∞`. The ring closes; the runaway is exact.

**The score** `[A]` — derived by chord product *(inherited: circle geometry + AM-GM — used, not discovered).* Draw the two chords from the point to the two poles: lengths `2sin(θ/2)` and `2cos(θ/2)`. Their product is `4·sin(θ/2)·cos(θ/2) = 2·sinθ`. Normalize:

```text
B = sinθ = 2/(φ + ν) ≤ 1,   with equality iff φ = ν = 1 (AM-GM)
```

`B` is a genuine **variable**: it moves with `θ`, and it peaks exactly at the **equator**, the one point where the two readings agree at the self-dual unit. The imbalance price `γ = (φ+ν)/2` rises monotonically toward either pole. This equatorial maximum is pure arithmetic — the one piece of the balance story that survived every audit untouched.

**The iron separation** (keel-108, CC-CORE-1): the **seam is never the score is never the node.** `P∞ ≡ 1` is a constant (chart identity); `B ≤ 1` is a variable (max at the equator); `P_node = Φ×V` is a *finite-node model* whose product interior is `[C]` — only its AND-class boundary (lose either factor entirely, lose everything) is settled. The seam stays 1 even as `ν → 0`; it therefore never witnesses the zero-factor catastrophe, and citing it as the ethics warrant is the convicted laundering move. Three objects, three tiers, no silent bridge.

## §2 · THE DISSOLUTIONS

**One-and-many.** How can the world be one and things be many? On this rung the paradox is a **chart error**: oneness lives at the seam (`φ·ν ≡ 1` — every point carries the same closure), manyness lives on the surface (each point has its own `θ`, its own `B`, its own pair of readings). "One" and "many" were answers to *different questions* — frame versus content — mistaken for rival answers to the same question. **Cure: register separation.** The unity is tautological and therefore says nothing about the plurality (Wittgenstein 6.1, per the Open Canon Covenant); the plurality is where all the information is. No mysticism required; none licensed either.

**Plato, repaired — with the debt shown.** Plato was partly right: some forms are stable, recurring, more-than-conventional. The repair reads **Forms as degrees of freedom** — robust patterns across constrained freedoms, not perfect objects in a separate realm. What exists is what a typed freedom permits; stability is earned by constraint, not granted by heaven. The **plenitude filter** is the `[C]` wager attached: from typed plenitude, emergence *filters* what persists — coherence buys admission to the trial, never existence by fiat. The wager carries a named **μ-debt**: whether a new freedom's aperture (`μ`) opens under stated conditions is an open causal hypothesis, not a theorem, and the filter is W1-class — held, priced, killable. **Cure: demote the realm to a constraint-structure, and pay for the demotion in the open.** Two-world metaphysics dies; pattern-realism survives at tier.

## §3 · THE OLOGY

**Ontology — the Soul-Loop asks: *what is there?* This rung answers: *typed existence.*** To exist is not one flat property. A thing exists **as** something — as a position on a chart, as a reading in a register, as a pattern across freedoms — and the type travels with the claim. `φ` exists as a coordinate; `B` exists as a derived score; the equator exists as an arithmetic optimum; the sphere itself exists as a chosen lens on the multiplicative face of number, not the only geometry (the additive line keeps its own rights). Asking "does X exist?" without stating the register is the D2 form of the one-and-many mistake.

So the rung's ontology in one breath: **being is position-under-constraint, read through declared charts, with the seam as frame and the surface as content.** What is *necessary* here is thin — the chart arithmetic, `[A]`, empty of world. What is *actual* is thick and stays conjectural — which freedoms are instantiated, which patterns persist, whether das All realizes the algebra at all (W0, the crown wager, never the proof). The stranger takes one thing from this page: the same point has two readings whose product is always one and whose balance is at most one — and the whole discipline of the framework is refusing to confuse those two sentences.
