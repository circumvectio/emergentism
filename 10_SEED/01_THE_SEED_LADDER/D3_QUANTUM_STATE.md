# D3 · THE QUANTUM PROBABILITY-STATE

> D3 has nothing to do with change as its defining property. It is the selected
> register for the state of a declared, individuated quantum system: the object
> that generates outcome probabilities relative to a declared measurement. The
> physics is inherited `[A/B]`; calling it D3 is the Emergentist crosswalk `[I]`.

## §0 · THE MODEL — density operator plus measurement context

```text
system boundary S declared
ρ_S=ρ_S†,  ρ_S⪰0,  Trρ_S=1
M={E_k},  E_k⪰0,  Σ_kE_k=I
p(k|ρ,M)=Tr(ρE_k)
```

“Individual” means the system boundary is named; `S` may be a particle, field
mode, composite, or subsystem. It does not imply a classical point-particle,
separability, or purity. `ρ` is not one freestanding classical probability
distribution. The pair
`(ρ,M)` generates a distribution. This matters because two states can give the
same probabilities for one measurement and different probabilities for
another.

For a qubit:

```text
ρ = 1/2(I+r·σ),       ||r||≤1
p(±|ρ,n) = (1±r·n)/2
Tr(ρ²) = (1+||r||²)/2
```

Pure states lie on the Bloch sphere (`||r||=1`); mixed states fill the Bloch
ball (`||r||<1`). The reciprocal Burri chart is not the Born rule and is not
used by this model.

## §1 · THE MOVES

1. **Prepare a state assignment.** An actual laboratory preparation is D4;
   `ρ` is its D3 quantum description.
2. **Declare the measurement.** A basis, POVM, or quantum instrument must be
   named. No context, no unique outcome distribution.
3. **Compute the probabilities.** `p(k|ρ,M)=Tr(ρE_k)` and `Σ_kp_k=1`.
4. **Keep prediction and receipt apart.** A sampled demonstration on the public
   page is classical Monte Carlo from the displayed distribution. It is not a
   quantum experiment and proves no collapse.
5. **Let the world answer.** An actual apparatus interaction and recorded
   outcome are D4. Repeated receipts can be compared with D3 probabilities.

Unitary evolution `ρ↦UρU†` and noisy channels `ρ↦𝓔(ρ)` act on D3 states, but
transformation is a relation between states—not the definition of the rung.

For a nonrelativistic particle, D2 defines position and momentum operators; D3
supplies their state-conditioned distributions; D4 supplies one actual
apparatus interaction, momentum transfer, clock correlation, and record. Thus
D3 lacks a jointly sharp classical `(x,p)` point or receipted worldline, not a
momentum distribution. An isolated D3 state has zero **realized** change. The
uncertainty relation follows from noncommutativity, not from the register names.

The time insight can be stated without breaking the physics. D3 may describe a
stationary or constraint-satisfying quantum state. D4 is where relational clock
readings, realized change, entropy-production history, and causal records
appear. Momentum as the generator of spatial translations and the state
functional `S(ρ)=-Tr(ρ logρ)` are already available before that D4 history;
actual momentum transfer and `ΔS/Δτ` are not. The claim that D4 time always
emerges from D3 this way is the falsifiable **Burri time-seam conjecture** `[C]`,
not a solved theorem.

## §2 · THE DISSOLUTION

**"A quantum state is just our uncertainty about a hidden value."** One fixed
measurement distribution cannot carry the full state. The pure state
`|+⟩⟨+|` and the maximally mixed state `I/2` both give `(1/2,1/2)` for a
Z measurement, but an X measurement distinguishes them: `|+⟩` gives the `+`
outcome with probability one while `I/2` remains `(1/2,1/2)`.

This establishes a formal discriminator `[A]`, not one unique metaphysics.
Whether `ρ` is ontic, epistemic, relational, or otherwise interpreted remains
open. Everett, Copenhagen-family, Bohmian, objective-collapse, and other
approaches cannot be stacked as dimensions.

## §3 · THE OLOGY

The epistemic lesson is precise: **probability is conditional on a declared
question.** The same state can answer different measurement questions with
different distributions. The ontological lesson is weaker: quantum theory
constrains what predictions work, but its successful formalism does not by
itself settle what the state ultimately is.

This is the D3 Kintsugi seam:

```text
D2 supplies the configured mathematical carrier.
D3 supplies the probability-bearing quantum state.
D4 supplies the actual interaction, outcome, and record.
D5 supplies agent-represented counterfactual meaning.
```

## μ₂ · FROM CONFIGURATION TO QUANTUM STATE

`μ₂:D2→D3` names the selected lift from Hilbert-space/functions/operators to a
normalized positive state functional. In the qubit example the construction is
mathematically reduced; it does not prove strong emergence. Lower-register
recovery forgets `ρ` and retains the D2 arena. Dephasing in a declared basis,

```text
Δ_B(ρ)=Σ_i Π_iρΠ_i,
```

yields a diagonal state identifiable with a classical probability vector in
that basis; it is a D3 channel/discriminator, not the D2 recovery map.

## μ₃ · FROM DISTRIBUTION TO RECEIPT

A Born distribution is not a detector click. `μ₃:D3→D4` names that typed
boundary: state plus actual apparatus yields an outcome record according to the
declared quantum instrument. The stochastic instrument-to-record interface is
operationally reduced `[A/I]`; any claim about fundamental ontic actualization
or universally emergent time remains `[C]`. This is not a claim that
consciousness causes collapse or that D4 is Copenhagen.

**Kills:** abandon the D3 ordinal if it adds no explanatory discipline;

discriminator; reject any page that maps quantum branches to D5 agent futures,
