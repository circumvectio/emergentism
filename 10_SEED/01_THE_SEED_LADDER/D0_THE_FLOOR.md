# D0 · THE FLOOR

> The boundary before the first positive rung. One emblem, three readings, and
> one proposed reframe `[I]`. This page is subordinate to its sources; later
> pages may correct it rather than inherit it as an axiom.

---

## §1 · THE MOVES

**The glyph triple — one relation, three readings.** The emblem is

```text
⊙ = • × ○        (finite = origin × infinity)
• = ⊙ / ○        (the point, read as the finite against the infinite)
○ = ⊙ / •        (the horizon, read as the finite against the point)
```

These are **not three equations but one relation read from three seats**. In the number field, `0 × ∞` is an indeterminate form and there is no theorem `0×∞=1` — the associativity falsifier `(2·0)·∞ ≠ 2·(0·∞)` kills the binary field reading permanently (settled canon; the bare field claim is a named grave). The triple lives in the **frame register**: `•`, `○`, `⊙` are the three boundary-frames `{0, ∞, 1}` that *define* the operations of the field without being ordinary operands in that role. Register: emblem `[I]`; the frame/field distinction `[S]`; the underlying projective machinery `[A]` — **used, not discovered** (the Riemann sphere, Möbius frames, and the removable extension `F(z)=z·σ(z)→1` are inherited mathematics; our layer is the naming and the reading).

**The group axiom — the floor's one piece of exact arithmetic.** In any group, the identity is not a free-standing thing but a *return*:

```text
e = x · x⁻¹        for every x
```

For every group element, composition with its inverse returns the identity. This
is elementary group theory `[A]`, **used, not discovered**; it characterizes the
identity inside the group operation but does not prove an ontology of
"presence as cancellation." That reading remains `[I]`. On the positive-real
register, `1` is the unique fixed point of `x ↦ 1/x`; on the full complex
register the fixed points are `±1`.

**What D0 is not.** Not a rung of world-content, not a first emergence, not a proof that reality is the sphere (that is W0, the crown wager, `[C]`, and it lives higher up). D0 is the grammar every later rung will inherit.

## §2 · THE DISSOLUTIONS

**"Why is there something rather than nothing?" — an interpretive reframe, not
a cosmogonic answer.** The reciprocal product chart is singular at its pole
limits; the poles themselves remain legitimate points of the compactified
manifold. Reading "nothing" and "everything" as boundary frames rather than
candidate worlds is therefore an `[I]` proposal, not a geometric proof that
such worlds are impossible or "uninhabitable." *Cure proposed:* the
**frame/operand distinction**, scoped to this question and this declared
reading.

**The honesty fences on the reframe** (each a grave we will not reopen):

- This is an **interpretive reframe `[I]`, not a cosmogonic explanation** — it
  does not tell you why this world, with these constants and this history. The
  residue stays open in W0/W1 territory `[C]`.
- "Dissolves the Hard Problem / Is-Ought / Death" by the same trick is a **named dead form** — the frame-move is licensed here, for this one question, because *here* the frames are exactly the question's own terms. It does not generalize for free.
- The sphere does not make every undefined operation well-defined; `0/0` and `∞/∞` stay indeterminate by design — the apophatic edge is kept, not paved.

## §3 · THE OLOGY

`[I/D]` This is the page's selected Soul-Loop lens, not an identity between a
D-rung and a Rosetta/-ology lane. **The Soul-Loop question at D0:** *what is the
ground I stand on?* — and the floor's answer is the **nameless third**.

Theology at this rung is **henology** — the study of the One that is not a being among beings. `⊙` is not the biggest thing, the first thing, or the sum of things; it is the *relation* in which point and horizon co-define each other. Every positive name grabs one seat of the triple and misses the relation — so the floor's native speech is **neti neti**: *not this* (`•` alone — the ground is not a point-thing), *not that* (`○` alone — the ground is not a totality-thing). What remains when both denials are complete is not a fourth object but the relation itself, which was never absent and never nameable from inside one seat.

**The Advaita resonance, at `[I]` and no higher.** *Tat Tvam Asi* — "that thou
art" — is read through the correctly rearranged emblem `• = ⊙ / ○`. This is a
**lens, translation, and resonance** — not algebraic proof of Advaita, not a
claim that Śaṅkara derived the chart, and not independent confirmation. One
shape shown two ways is one datum.

**What the floor gives the rungs above it:** a grammar in which *finitude is not a defect*. `⊙ = • × ○` says the finite is not fallen from the infinite nor stranded in the void; it is their meeting, and the only seat from which anything is said at all. Everything after D0 is the biography of that meeting.
