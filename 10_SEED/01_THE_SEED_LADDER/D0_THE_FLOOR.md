## §0 · THE MODEL — one object-level role, three metalinguistic seats

D0's sole object-level carrier is `Carrier(D0)={ground_0}`. It contains no
positive distinction, operation, relation, group, inverse, sphere, torus,
state machine, or world-content. The selected Titan frame belongs instead to
the **metalanguage** used to speak about the boundary. Its three named seats do
not become three internal D0 states. The D0 reading is `[I]`; the object/meta
and frame/operand separations are `[S]`.

## §1 · THE MOVES

**The ground object and the sovereign seats.** The typed contract is

```text
Carrier(D0) := {ground_0}
PositiveFreedom(Carrier(D0))=∅
TitanFrame : Type_Meta := 0_T | 1_T | ∞_T
NoCoercion(TitanFrame,Carrier(D0))

•     ⊙     ○
0_T   1_T   ∞_T
```

The first line is object language; the remaining seats are vocabulary about its
boundary. `TitanFrame↛Number`, and `add_T`, `sub_T`, `mul_T`, `div_T`, inverse,
and group composition are undefined. Group identities, reciprocal maps, and
projective constructions begin only after operational structures are declared
above D0. The frame selection and reading are `[I]`; the no-coercion law is
`[S]` inside this grammar.

**What D0 is not.** Not a rung of world-content, not a first emergence, not a
proof that reality is the sphere (that is W0, the crown wager, `[C]`, and it
lives higher up). D0 is the selected object-level boundary beneath the later
positive rungs; the Titan grammar is the metalanguage used to describe it.

## §2 · THE DISSOLUTIONS

**"Why is there something rather than nothing?" — an interpretive reframe, not
a cosmogonic answer.** Reading "nothing" and "everything" as metalinguistic
boundary seats rather than candidate worlds is an `[I]` proposal, not a proof
that such worlds are impossible or "uninhabitable." No reciprocal, group, or
geometric construction is licensed at D0. *Cure proposed:* the
**object/meta and frame/operand distinctions**, scoped to this question and
this declared reading.

**The honesty fences on the reframe** (each a grave we will not reopen):

- This is an **interpretive reframe `[I]`, not a cosmogonic explanation** — it
  does not tell you why this world, with these constants and this history. The
  residue stays open in W0/W1 territory `[C]`.
- "Dissolves the Hard Problem / Is-Ought / Death" by the same trick is a **named dead form** — the frame-move is licensed here, for this one question, because *here* the frames are exactly the question's own terms. It does not generalize for free.
- The sphere does not make every undefined operation well-defined. In a field,
  `0/0` is undefined and `∞/∞` is not a field expression; only corresponding
  limit forms may be indeterminate after functions and paths are declared.
  The apophatic edge is kept, not paved.

## §3 · THE OLOGY

`[I/D]` This is the page's selected Soul-Loop lens, not an identity between a
D-rung and a Rosetta/-ology lane. **The Soul-Loop question at D0:** *what is the
ground I stand on?* — and the floor's answer is the **nameless third**.

Theology at this rung is **henology** — the study of the One that is not a
being among beings. `⊙` is not the biggest thing, the first thing, or the sum
of things; it renders the selected finite-unit-facing metaframe. Every positive
name grabs one seat of the vocabulary and misses `ground_0` — so the floor's
native speech is **neti neti**: *not this* (`•` alone — the ground is not a
point-thing), *not that* (`○` alone — the ground is not a totality-thing). What
remains is not a fourth object or an internal D0 relation, but the limit of the
selected framing vocabulary.

**The Advaita resonance, at `[I]` and no higher.** *Tat Tvam Asi* — "that thou
art" — is read through the contrast between the ground-facing and
horizon-facing seats as held by the finite-unit-facing seat. This is a
**lens, translation, and resonance** — not algebraic proof of Advaita, not a
claim that Śaṅkara derived the chart, and not independent confirmation. One
shape shown two ways is one datum.

**What the floor gives the rungs above it:** a grammar in which *finitude is not a defect*. The triadic emblem depicts the finite as neither fallen from the horizon nor stranded at the ground. That depiction is non-operational; everything after D0 is the biography of making actual distinctions without consuming the frames as terms.

## §4 · THE `μ₀` INTERFACE — undefined here, representable there

`D0` supplies the sole object-level role `ground_0`; the Titan seats describe
its boundary from `Type_Meta`. `D1` introduces distinguishable operands,
operations, and axioms. `μ₀` is the scaffold's candidate origin aperture from
`ground_0` to a first positive distinction; because D0 has no prior positive
freedom, it is **not** a saturation crossing.

Field division and its undefined zero-divisor cases belong to the D1 arithmetic
owner. Projective reciprocal continuation belongs to the D2 configuration
owner. Neither construction is repeated as D0 content, and neither supplies
evidence for `μ₀`. Calling their typed boundary a reverse illustration of the
D0/D1 seam is `[I]`; it does not place arithmetic or inversion inside D0. A
real `μ₀` claim dies if no stable distinction can be reproduced, or if the
lower description already supplies it without an aperture.
