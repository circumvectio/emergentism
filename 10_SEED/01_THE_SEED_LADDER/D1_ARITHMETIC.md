## §2 · THE DISSOLUTIONS — this rung's paradox, and its exact cure

**Division by zero.** The old paradox: `N/0` "has no answer," yet reciprocal
values grow without bound near zero. The cure is a **domain and category
correction, scoped honestly**:

- In a field, `N/0` for `N≠0` is undefined because `0·y=N` has no field
  solution. For `N>0`, the real one-sided limits are
  `lim[x→0+]N/x=+∞` and `lim[x→0−]N/x=−∞`; their disagreement is why no
  two-sided extended-real limit supplies the missing quotient. The signs reverse
  for `N<0`.
- On the projective line, the **associated map** `f_N(z)=N/z` extends with
  `f_N(0)=∞` and `f_N(∞)=0` `[A]`. Adding `∞` makes the pole representable;
  it does not turn the field expression `N/0` into a field value.
- In field arithmetic, `0/0` is undefined and `∞/∞` is not a field expression
  because infinity is not a field element. Only corresponding **limit forms**
  may be indeterminate once functions and approach paths are declared. The
  sphere does not make *every* undefined operation well-defined; local
  dissolutions are justified case by case.
- The frame/operand distinction is an explicit **type split**. `0_T`,`1_T`,`∞_T`
  inhabit opaque `TitanFrame` and admit no arithmetic; ordinary numeric `0_N`
  and `1_N` inhabit number structures and remain lawful operands. The dead form
  was the claim that ordinary identities can never be operands—not the sovereign
  Titan type law.
- Consequently the triadic glyph is a **non-operational emblem**. It does not
  define Titan multiplication or division, while ordinary field arithmetic
  retains its own independently typed laws.

The sovereign roles are therefore shown without an infix operator:

```text
•      ⊙      ○
0_T    1_T    ∞_T
```

This is a **typed boundary crossing**, not evidence for ontological `μ₀`:
division already presupposes D1 arithmetic. The example shows why changing a
domain can preserve a representation after an operation reaches its boundary.
It does not show that D0 generated D1, and it adds no second independent
coordinate to the number line.

**Infinity and the D1→D2 boundary.** The real projective line `ℝP¹` remains
one-dimensional; adding its point at infinity is a compactification, not a new
degree of freedom. A primary formal D1→D2 lift is instead `X↦(X,R)`, where the
bare carrier gains an explicitly typed relation `R⊆X^k`. Cantor's theorem states
that no map `X→𝒫(X)` is surjective. Separately, the unrestricted Russell
expression `{x | x∉x}` is not admitted as a set in standard foundations. These
are inherited formal boundary facts `[A]`, not proof that an ontological `μ₁`
has fired.

**What this rung refuses to claim.** No conserved discovery hides in `φ·ν = 1`
(it is `ν := 1/φ` restated — grave 5); no trinity is forced (the sovereign
Titan meta-frame is a selected naming frame, not a numeric subset or closure —
grave 9); no ethics falls out of the seam
(grave 21). The paradox dissolved here is arithmetic's own, and only arithmetic's.
