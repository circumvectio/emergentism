# The Seed

Emergentism is a compass for asking how constrained possibilities become stable
actualities, how agents act among modeled futures, how consequences return, and how
finite beings may widen durable mutual possibility without confusing a map for the
territory.

## The ladder

| Register | Compressed reading | Modality |
|---|---|---|
| D0 | ground-limit | actual boundary frame |
| D1 | distinction | actual |
| D2 | configuration | actual |
| D3 | probability-bearing quantum state; measurement-relative distributions | actual state assignment |
| D4 | causal actuality, embodied means, action, and record | actual |
| D5 | counterfactual possibility, modeled futures, represented rankings, and selection candidates | possible |
| D6 | apophatic recognition/exit; no added positive freedom | actual recognition |

Exactly five candidate positive crossings, `μ₀…μ₄`, open D1 through D5. Each is a
testable emergence claim, not a proof of irreducibility. `b₆:D5↝D6` marks exit or
recognition; `r₆:D6↝D0` is an interpretive, non-identity return. Neither is `μ`.

## The finite-agent loop

An agent holds a fallible D4 model of D5 possibilities, selects through a D4 event under D4 means, commits an action, receives
the world's outcome, and updates both map and selector. Anticipated futures affect the
present through their model tokens; no physical signal from the future is required.

`F = M⋆A` is the public compression. The environment, other agents, and constraints
still co-determine what becomes actual.

## The compass

Before consequential action, ask:

1. What is actual, and what is only modeled?
2. Which freedom or constraint is changing?
3. What evidence tier supports the claim, and what would kill it?
4. Who authorized the consequence, who acts, who bears it, and how can it be contested?
5. Does the action durably widen both the individual's and the coupled whole's option
   field under truth, non-extraction, consent, custody, reversibility, and Grace Exit?

The worldview's strongest honesty rule is simple: analytic identities do not become
empirical laws, correspondences do not transfer proof, and beauty does not rescue a
broken derivation.
