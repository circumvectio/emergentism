# The Seed, spoken lightly

The ground permits a distinction.
Distinctions compose.
Configurations change and sometimes persist.
Persistent actuality grows bodies, means, acts, and records.
From actuality, finite minds imagine more than one next world.

At five thresholds the vocabulary says a new freedom may open: `μ₀…μ₄`.
That is a wager with tests, not a staircase forced on nature.

At the summit there is no sixth positive freedom.
There is a pause: `b₆`, the recognition that the map has reached its edge;
then `r₆`, a resemblance to the ground without pretending the end is the beginning.

The soul loop is the ordinary miracle:
the possible is modeled, one act is committed, the world answers,
and the answer changes both the map and the mapper.

The future can pull only as a represented possibility pulls—
through attention, ranking, coordination, and present action—
not as a message traveling backward through spacetime.

The good is not a command from the diagram.
It is the chosen discipline of holding truth, beauty, and justice together:
truth that can lose, beauty that does not counterfeit evidence,
and justice that names every bearer of consequence.

Use the map. Test it. Repair it. Put it down when the territory speaks more clearly.
