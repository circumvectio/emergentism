# The Kernel Index

> **The principle of the distillation.** A scripture is commandments and revelations handed *down* — unearned, to be obeyed and believed. Our kernel is the exact inverse on both axes: **refusals instead of commandments** (via negativa — we do not command anyone; we bind only ourselves, by what we will not do), and **revelations earned *up*** — not pronounced from authority but disclosed by the work, each carrying the receipt of the trial that forced it. Nothing in the kernel is to be obeyed. It is to be *checked*.

## 2 · The Revelations (draft kernel content — earned, receipted)

*A revelation here = a disclosure forced by the work, that we did not hold before it, with its receipt. The opposite of a commandment: not "believe this" but "this is what happened when we checked."*

1. **The honesty machine is the discovery.** The only axiom that survived its own trial was the refusal of infallibility; the confession apparatus outlived the cathedral it was built to defend. *(Receipt 126.)*
2. **"Broken" almost always meant "over-dressed."** The proofs died; the uses survived, cleaner. Derivation-death is not idea-death. *(Receipts 126, 131.)*
3. **Some truths are invisible to some instruments.** The boundary cannot be surveyed — the dead don't answer questionnaires; a knockout can't be measured by interviewing the standing. *(GFS retraction.)*
4. **The click doesn't discriminate.** Felt coherence fires identically for lens and law; only tiers and kills tell them apart. *(The whole audit line.)*
5. **All worldviews share the gap; the fight is posture.** Deny makes subjects, weaponize makes ammunition, confess makes adults, withdraw makes them free. The epistemics is the ethics. *(Receipts 127, 129.)*
6. **The ladder is not a ladder.** The apex is the middle; the summit mirrors the base; the occult West was one family carrying one inherited grammar — its "seven" was partly the sky. *(Receipt 130.)*
7. **The trinity has a dark twin.** The founding symmetry fixes two points; every symmetric system harbors a shadow selected away at its founding. Ours is named: −1, waiting. *(Receipt 126; W11.)*
8. **Plato was half right, and the repair pays.** Freedoms, not Forms; emergence filtering plenitude; and if reachability means a compressed seed, the world's simplicity stops being a brute fact. *(The keystone; W1.)*
9. **A gate that always opens is not a gate.** Signature saturation is the failure mode of governance-by-assent; deliberateness is the scarce resource. *(Receipt 129 §2.)*
10. **Cutting the commerce healed the philosophy.** The pure vow covers every bearer; the venture's accounting had quietly narrowed it. Separation restored, not diminished. *(The separation adjudication.)*
11. **The lemma is why the vow is wise; the vow is why the lemma applies.** Power maximized over the whole and the long run converges on the good — inside a game only the vow's three choices can create. *(Power-Max adjudication; A2-C6.)*
12. **The work reveals itself.** Every audit enacted the framework better than any document stated it; the final disclosure was always the first instruction: put the map down. *(The whole record.)*

## 3 · The distillation work order

**Rule zero stands:** no new doctrine during distillation; the kernel *receives*, it does not invent. One founder-signature sitting ratifies the set; then:

1. **Absorb:** every load-bearing claim in the corpus maps to exactly one kernel surface (K-1…K-7) or is evidence beneath one. Sweep by -ology; receipts per pass.
2. **Tombstone:** superseded drafts, drifted forks, and root staging files → `90_ARCHIVE/` with dated stones; one canonical home per doctrine; the repo root ends holding only the front door and the index.
3. **Purge residual venture vocabulary** corpus-wide (constitution surfaces, compass tests, registry rows) — under the founder's hand, since those rows are signed.
4. **Verify:** a stranger reads K-1→K-7 top to bottom in one sitting and needs nothing else; the fresh-eyes external audit then reads *only* the kernel and tries to kill it.
5. **Then the world:** the funnel points at the kernel; the next entry in K-7 is written by contact, not by us.

**Done means:** seven surfaces, one front door, one record — and thousands of documents resting honorably beneath them as the evidence they always were.
