# The Kernel Index

> **The principle of the distillation.** A scripture is commandments and revelations handed *down* — unearned, to be obeyed and believed. Our kernel is the exact inverse on both axes: **refusals instead of commandments** (via negativa — we do not command anyone; we bind only ourselves, by what we will not do), and **revelations earned *up*** — not pronounced from authority but disclosed by the work, each carrying the receipt of the trial that forced it. Nothing in the kernel is to be obeyed. It is to be *checked*.

## 2 · The Revelations (draft kernel content — earned, receipted)

*A revelation here = a disclosure forced by the work, that we did not hold before it, with its receipt. The opposite of a commandment: not "believe this" but "this is what happened when we checked."*

1. **The honesty machine is the discovery.** `[B/I]` Receipt 126 records 7 settled, 66 broken-as-stated, and 34 synthetic-gap claims; A7 was the correction axiom explicitly tested reflexively and it survived. The confession apparatus outlived many proof-forms it was built to test. Calling it the framework's central native contribution is an interpretation, not a historical-priority theorem.
2. **“Broken” often meant “over-dressed.”** `[B/I]` Receipt 131 shows that derivations died while many uses survived as scoped structure, tool, posit, vow, lens, or wager. *Derivation-death is not idea-death*—but the ledger, not “almost always,” decides each case, and the named dead forms stay dead.
3. **Some truths are invisible to some instruments.** `[S/I]` The GFS retraction showed that a survey of standing respondents could not test a zero-factor knockout boundary: the dead do not answer questionnaires, and a knockout is not measured by interviewing the standing. This is an instrument-boundary finding, not permission to protect every absent witness from testing.
4. **The click does not discriminate.** `[I]` Felt coherence can accompany both lens and law. Tiers, preserved kills, and world contact—not felt fit alone—separate them. “Fires identically” remains phenomenological compression, not a measured identity.
5. **Worldviews with synthetic cores can be compared by posture.** `[I/C]` Deny / weaponize / confess / withdraw is Emergentism's chosen taxonomy. The proposed ethical reading—deny makes subjects, weaponize makes ammunition, confess restores agency, withdraw preserves freedom—remains an interpretation whose completeness and comparative advantage are open.
6. **The ladder is not merely a ladder.** `[B/I]` Receipt 130 preserves centre+mirror grammar and the scoped four-slot mover structure while rejecting a universally forced seven. The staged reading places the apex at the middle and the summit in a base-reflection; the planetary genealogy travels with the Western seven. *Shape real; census selected; universal lost.*
7. **The trinity has a dark twin.** `[A/C]` Inversion fixes both `+1` and `−1`; the honest foursome is `{−1,0,1,∞}`. The functional “shadow selected away at founding” is W11 `[C]`, not an algebraic law of every symmetric system.
8. **Plato was partly right, and the repair has debts.** `[I/C]` Stable forms are read as robust patterns across constrained degrees of freedom; emergence filters typed plenitude. If operational reachability means construction from a compressed seed, world-simplicity may become less brute—but this is W1, not an earned theorem.
9. **A gate that always opens is not a gate.** `[B/I]` Receipt 129 §2 exposed competing claimed signatures; receipts 135–136 repeated the failure in provisional promotion form. Signature saturation is the failure mode of governance-by-assent: deliberateness, not assent frequency, is the scarce resource.
10. **Cutting the commerce healed the philosophy.** `[B/I]` The separation adjudication removed Skyzai and venture accounting as philosophical warrant and restored η=0 to the staged all-bearer vow. Separation clarified the claim; it did not prove the vow's external adequacy.
11. **The lemma is why the vow may be wise; the vow is why the lemma may apply.** `[I/C]` Power-Max does not derive the vow. A chosen non-extraction vow may define an admissible whole-boundary, long-horizon game in which potential is optimized. Whether that strategy converges on the good or outperforms alternatives is W6, not theorem.
12. **The work reveals itself—and then must stop talking to itself.** `[I/S]` The record repeatedly enacted public correction, preservation of graves, and exit more clearly than any completeness claim could. The next disclosure must come from contact. If the kernel makes itself compulsory, erases its losses, or turns exit decorative, this revelation dies. *Put the map down.*

## 3 · The distillation work order

**Rule zero stands:** no new doctrine during distillation; the kernel *receives*, it does not invent. One founder-signature sitting ratifies the set; then:

1. **Absorb:** every load-bearing claim in the corpus maps to exactly one kernel surface (K-1…K-7) or is evidence beneath one. Sweep by -ology; receipts per pass.
2. **Tombstone:** superseded drafts, drifted forks, and root staging files → `90_ARCHIVE/` with dated stones; one canonical home per doctrine; the repo root ends holding only the front door and the index.
3. **Purge residual venture vocabulary** corpus-wide (constitution surfaces, compass tests, registry rows) — under the founder's hand, since those rows are signed.
4. **Verify:** a stranger reads K-1→K-7 top to bottom in one sitting and needs nothing else; the fresh-eyes external audit then reads *only* the kernel and tries to kill it.
5. **Then the world:** the funnel points at the kernel; the next entry in K-7 is written by contact, not by us.

**Done means:** seven surfaces, one front door, one record — and thousands of documents resting honorably beneath them as the evidence they always were.
