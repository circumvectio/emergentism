# The Known Unknowns Program

## A dimension-first research constitution

The internal scaffold is assembled. The open work is contact: definitions that
still need operationalization, components that need discriminating tests, and
whole-system wagers that need independent criticism and replication.

This program routes that work. It does not create doctrine, unfreeze parked
claims, or turn a complete question packet into evidence.

## 1. Frontier states

| State | Meaning | Required treatment |
|---|---|---|
| **internally complete** | one semantic owner, stable type, explicit tier, and no live contradiction | maintain with tests; do not redescribe it as empirically proven |
| **packet-complete** | question, rival, discriminator, kill, and survivor are specified | execute or seek external review; do not add more prose merely to feel progress |
| **evidence-open** | the specified contact has not occurred or does not yet discriminate the local claim | preserve `open`; publish nulls and adverse results |
| **deferred** | a prerequisite definition, instrument, dataset, or native-theory bridge is absent | build the prerequisite or leave the claim alone |
| **frozen** | deliberately parked because its test is presently impossible, unsafe, or dominated by a cheaper question | do not reopen without a new enabling condition and dated reason |

The D0–D6 owner contracts are internally complete. The eleven `GP` sockets are
packet-complete and evidence-open. No contradiction exists between those two
sentences.

## 2. The active research lanes

### Lane A — formal choice and measurement

**Question:** which parts of the grammar are definitions, which are useful
models, and which survive contact?

| Socket | Work now | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| `GP-01` `mu_1` | choose a declared system with identical element inventories and intervenable relation structure | held-out comparison of element-only and relation-aware models |
| `GP-03` product fit | freeze domain, outcome, normalization, and rival aggregators before fitting | cross-validated ranking and calibration report including nulls |
| `GP-04` foresight/means complementarity | measure option-model quality, actual resource cost, and remaining usable means independently | candidate budget-surface comparison; no chart-to-node inference |

**Stop rule:** if the measurement choices already encode the desired result,
the study is circular and must not run as confirmation.

### Lane B — state, history, and possible histories

**Question:** what is gained at the D3/D4 and D4/D5 seams after the strongest
lower-register account is supplied?

| Socket | Work now | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| `GP-02` `mu_4` | intervene on represented future contents/weights while controlling present state and means | causal estimate against model-based and model-free D4 baselines |
| `GP-07` future influence | separate content, salience, demand, reward framing, memory, and expectancy | preregistered future-content manipulation with negative controls |
| `GP-08` block plurality | identify an interpretation-sensitive observation, if one exists | either a discriminator or a written non-identifiability result |
| `GP-09` cone/bundle | define history entropy independently of Minkowski cross-section and model resolution | test whether the metric adds anything beyond causal volume and search budget |

**Stop rule:** represented possibilities are not promoted to physically
co-existing worlds merely because a complete-history model contains them.

### Lane C — crossings and forces

**Question:** does the fivefold map carry a non-permutable mechanism, or only a
beautiful census?

| Socket | Work now | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| `GP-05` five-crossing census | define one independent novelty and recovery test per seam | crossing-by-crossing evidence matrix; no joint score until rows work separately |
| `GP-10` force/freedom affinities | map native objects and dynamics for each row, then test all row permutations | five modular verdicts plus one global non-permutability verdict |

Required order:

1. native-theory object map;
2. accepted-domain recovery;
3. permutation challenge;
4. held-out discriminator;
5. independent replication;
6. joint unification claim only if every earlier gate survives.

**Stop rule:** equal list length, symmetry, or numeric resemblance is not a
mechanism.

### Lane D — agency, collectives, and consequences

**Question:** do the agency and collective models add intervention-sensitive
value without smuggling in consciousness or ethics?

| Socket | Work now | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| `GP-06` Egregoreotype | identify a trace-bearing system where carriers can turn over and the trace can be removed or scrambled | five-marker pass/fail report with payer and substrate costs visible |
| `GP-11` integrated adequacy | compare the complete grammar with serious plural-domain rivals on one novel frozen prediction | external red-team report, replicated discriminator, and published null route |

The Justice envelope governs how studies treat participants and consequence
bearers. It does not count as evidence that the Egregoreotype or integrated
worldview is true.

## 2A. Scaffold-first frontier-model assembly strategy

Emergentism's development strategy is not to ask one author or one model to
declare the completed picture. It is to build a stable, typed scaffold and let
frontier AI models propose missing pieces **one bounded piece at a time**.

The scaffold owns only:

- registers, interfaces, and type distinctions;
- source ownership and evidence tiers;
- open sockets, rivals, discriminators, and kill criteria;
- provenance, correction, and exit;
- the rule that native sciences keep their own warrant.

Frontier models may search, compare, formalize, translate, simulate, attack,
and propose candidate connections. They do not supply empirical warrant merely
by agreeing, sounding certain, or producing an elegant fit. Model consensus is
a search signal, never replication. A model may assemble evidence; it may not
manufacture its own evidence or promote its own candidate.

Every proposed puzzle piece must arrive as a packet:

```text
nativeSourceAndDomain
nativeDefinitions
candidateSocket
claimAndTier
formalMap
assumptions
strongestRivals
nonCircularDiscriminator
killCriterion
survivorIfKilled
dataCodeAndCitationCustody
independentReviewRoute
```

The assembly loop is sequential:

1. recover the strongest native account;
2. state it without Emergentist vocabulary;
3. propose a typed placement in the scaffold;
4. run formal, source, and category-error attacks;
5. compare alternative placements and the no-placement rival;
6. freeze one discriminator before outcome contact;
7. test or simulate at the warranted evidence class;
8. seek independent human and machine criticism;
9. replicate where the claim is empirical;
10. promote only the surviving local claim, or archive it visibly if killed.

The picture is therefore allowed to emerge asymptotically. The scaffold is not
proof that every socket has a real occupant, that the selected sockets exhaust
reality, or that a final theory must result. If repeated pieces add no
incremental prediction, compression, or consequence value beyond their native
sciences, the honest endpoint is a useful worldview and research index—not a
theory of everything.

## 3. Cheapest-honest execution order

Work proceeds in this order unless a new enabling condition changes the cost:

1. **GP-03 aggregator comparison** — formal and existing-data friendly.
2. **GP-04 resource-surface measurement design** — clarifies the user's
   foresight/means saturation insight without assuming it.
3. **GP-07 controlled future-content effect** — component contact already exists;
   the next task is mechanism separation.
4. **GP-01 relation intervention** — tests the cleanest candidate aperture.
5. **GP-06 Egregoreotype trace intervention** — composite but operational.
6. **GP-02 distinct counterfactual capacity** — only after fair D4 baselines.
7. **GP-09 history-bundle metric** — only after independent definition.
8. **GP-05 crossing census** — row-by-row, never as one score.
9. **GP-10 force map** — native theory before numerology.
10. **GP-08 ontology-sensitive plurality test** — defer if non-identifiable.
11. **GP-11 integrated worldview test** — last, because it inherits every earlier
    measurement error.

## 4. Deferred and frozen boundaries

### Deferred

- a full force unification or new Lagrangian before any W7 row has a mechanism;
- a unique quantum interpretation, Born-rule derivation, or quantum-gravity
  solution from the D-address system;
- a consciousness ontology inferred from D4/D5 typing;
- historical priority or perennial-universality claims without comparative
  scholarship;
- large longitudinal institutional claims without a stable measurement and
  consequence protocol.

### Frozen

- literal `D6=D0`;
- Titan arithmetic or coercion into ordinary numbers;
- division by zero inside an unchanged field;
- Everett/Copenhagen as extra dimensional layers;
- physical retrocausality presented as necessary for the Soul Loop;
- force/freedom identity by equal list length;
- ethics derived from the reciprocal chart;
- new expensive data collection while the same discriminator can be tested with
  existing data or simulation.

Frozen dead forms are not “questions we have not answered yet.” They are
prohibited routes whose failure is already recorded. A genuinely new mechanism
must enter as a new claim, not by quietly thawing the old wording.

## 5. What counts as progress

Progress is one of:

- an operational definition survives construct-validity attack;
- a serious rival is strengthened and still loses a held-out comparison;
- a null or adverse result closes a wager cleanly;
- an open socket receives independent component contact without tier inflation;
- a deferred item obtains its prerequisite;
- a whole-system claim is narrowed because one modular leg failed; or
- another investigator can reproduce the protocol without corpus lore.

More pages, more correspondences, and more internal agreement do not count by
themselves.

## 6. What counts as failure

The frontier program is failing if:

- open sockets are renamed instead of tested;
- the desired result is encoded into `Phi`, `V`, saturation, or the impact boundary;
- native sciences are summarized inaccurately to make the ladder fit;
- null results disappear while analogies accumulate;
- an external neighbor is described as calibration of the integrated worldview;
- the framework asks for believers, suppresses exit, or makes correction costly;
- applications, products, ventures, or governance schemes become premises of
  the pure worldview; or
- a theory-of-everything claim appears before a novel replicated discriminator.
