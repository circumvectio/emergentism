# THE K-NAMESPACE LEDGER

The owner asked for a complete list of the K-rules and axioms before revising
them. Six independent sweeps returned **385 catalogued rules** and **100
collision reports**.

**The headline is not that the names are untidy.**

> **`K2` carries twenty-four distinct meanings. `K3` carries seventeen — and two
> of them are canonical rulings that point in opposite directions. `K*` carries
> twenty-one, including three unrelated mathematical quantities fused under one
> letter.**

The letter `K` is not a namespace in this corpus. It is **six namespaces wearing
one letter**, plus a person, plus a process, plus a verb.

## 4 · Three findings that are not about naming

**4.1 · The extraction law wears two different numbers.**
η = 0 is **refusal #1** in the constitution — and **`K6`** in the DAC namespace.
Meanwhile six files assert **`K0` = η=0**, which `03A_CONSTITUTIONAL_INVARIANTS_CANON`
explicitly forbids. The corpus's most load-bearing law has three identifiers and
one of them is prohibited.

**4.2 · `K*` fuses three unrelated quantities.**
The extraction coefficient, selection complexity, and standard Kolmogorov
complexity are not the same object, do not share units, and cannot be
substituted. `00_KSTAR_DISAMBIGUATION.md` exists precisely to separate them —
and the fusion persists in `11_UPLINK/00_CORE/03_HOLOBIONT_SELF_PORTRAIT.md:43`,
which asserts `A7 = K* = 0` and identifies K* with **the Planck constant** *and*
"the largest tolerable concentration of authority" in one sentence.

**4.3 · K2 is deactivated at runtime — and this is the receipt-156 question.**
Three incompatible scopes are live:
- **universal** — every consequential act carries a mortal signature;
- **private-DAV-only** — public DAVs have *no* K2 signer; PRISM substitutes;
- **off** — `k2_required=false`, "no K2 bootstrap or founder fallback."

Receipt 156 flagged a routing edit replacing K2-staging with *"no private
person's financial or contractual signature is an AI-work gate."* **That edit is
consistent with a scope the runtime already adopted.** The membrane question is
therefore not about one sentence in one file — it is about which of three live
scopes is canonical. **FOUNDER-GATED.**

## 6 · Open questions the sweeps could not settle

1. **Do `K0` and `K1` exist as refusals?** No. The refusal series has no 0 or 1;
   `K0`/`K1` belong to N4 and N5 only. The refusals are **not** a 0-indexed
   series with missing entries — they were never designed as a series at all.
2. **What is the DAC `K3`?** The DAC namespace has **no slot for "no silent
   erasure."** Archive-first has no DAC representation.
3. **Which K-5 is the constitution?** The active file is
   `00_META/00_THE_FIVE_PLUS_ONE_CONSTITUTION.md`, but a staged **unsigned**
   `00_META/00_K5_THE_REFUSALS.md` exists that a forwarding stub points to.
4. **`K4` "leave with everything"** is a **retracted** slogan still live in
   `05_COSMOLOGY/00_WHOLE/03A_CONSTITUTIONAL_INVARIANTS_CANON.md:30` and on the
   public site in four incompatible wordings.
