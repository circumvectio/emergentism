# Emergentism Internal Completion Register

This register answers one question: **is each part internally typed, routed to
one owner, and priced at its warranted tier?** It does not measure empirical
truth, popularity, publication, or metaphysical completeness.

## State vocabulary

Only four states are used:

- **complete** — one owner and a contradiction-free internal contract exist;
- **conflicted** — two active owners or incompatible types remain;
- **open** — the internal contract exists, but the named evidence or world test
  has not been supplied;
- **projection-only** — useful reader or disciplinary crosswalk, never source
  authority.

A complete owner may contain open conjectures. “Complete” therefore means
internally coherent and correctly tiered, not externally confirmed.

## Pure-authority boundary

runtime organism, legal entity, token, or private financial/contractual
signing convention. Such material is application or provenance and belongs
outside active source authority. Consequential action is typed through the
`AuthorizationEnvelope` and separate commitment/outcome receipts.
