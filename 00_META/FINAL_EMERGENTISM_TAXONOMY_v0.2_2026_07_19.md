# Final Emergentism Taxonomy v0.2

> **Status:** final **candidate list**, not final authority. It is `[D]` as an assembly. Existing signed receipts retain precedence. Adoption, if desired, is a separate correction-governed act.

This list corrects four classification errors in the earlier partitions:

1. standard mathematics is a **resource**, not an Emergentist axiom or discovery;
2. a worldview axiom is a declared foundational commitment, not automatically an `[A]` theorem;
3. degrees of freedom are not Platonic replacement entities, and forms are not identical to DoF;
4. quantum measurement is not solved by saying consistency selects one outcome.

No count below is sacred. The partition is functional, not numerological.

## 8. Quantum measurement disposition

### Established boundary

- Interference supports a quantum-amplitude description, not by itself the ontic actuality of every classical outcome.
- Measurement interactions can entangle, amplify, decohere, and record.
- Standard quantum mechanics supplies probabilities; interpretations disagree about collapse and actuality.

### Emergentist survivor

> **A recorded outcome can be treated as a stable emergent macro-record.** `[I/C]`

### Unsolved remainder

> **Why this outcome alone is actual—if only one is actual—and why Born weights govern outcomes is not solved by logical coherence or consistency.**

All allowed outcomes can be coherent and consistent alternatives. In the quantum sense, measurement often destroys or disperses phase coherence rather than selecting “the coherent one.” Therefore the proposed measurement insight enters as **C4/C5**, not as an axiom or discovery.
