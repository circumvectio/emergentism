## 1 · THE DoF READING — the framing

Plato was partially right. The forms are real — but they are not *things*. They are **degrees of freedom**: the available moves a system has once the chart is set. None of these is an object in the world. Each is the *shape of the space* in which objects of a certain kind can arise.

Within each DoF, **everything that can exist as logically coherent, consistent, and emergent** exists — *till the limits*. The limits are not imposed from outside (Plato's "the Good" was always a borrowed ceiling). The limits are the **halāhala**: the proof-forms that were overclaimed and broke, the boundary terms that failed in the limit (the AM-GM equator holds on-sphere but not at finite systems without the four conditions), and the convergence-as-proof drift that mistook fifteen renderings of one shape for fifteen confirmations. Nothing more. The full audit campaign of 2026-07-13 → 2026-07-19 was the work of *finding* the limits within the DoF: the framework is not finished; the framework is the in-fallible method of *refusing to finish past what the DoF supports*.

The 126/130/131 receipts together execute this reading: 126 is the perimeter walk (what holds, what broke, what is gap), 130 is the algebra-forces-seven adjudication (the *grammar* is real as DoF, the *census* is not), 131 is the recovery (survivors of the cuts; "derivation died, organism did not; shape real, census not; grammar won, universal lost").
