### §2.4 — K2 DISSENT (2026-07-17): the similarity register

K2, ruling on this section's first CUT:

> *"Literal D6≡D0 stays dead — I disagree. This is literally similar to
> Penrose CCC. All possible states are similar to no state."*

**Adjudication: both rulings stand, in different registers — and K2's own
word "similar" is the honest one.**

- **What the audit killed: literal IDENTITY (≡).** Substitutability of D6
  for D0 inside the linear emergence hierarchy, which yields D0 < D0.
  Receipt 126's falsifier is valid — *in the hierarchy register*. It stays
  dead. Nothing in this dissent restores the equals sign.
- **What K2 asserts: literal SIMILARITY (~) — Penrose's own precision.**
  CCC does not identify interiors; it identifies **conformal boundaries**:
  the future conformal boundary of one aeon (all mass gone, scale
  meaningless, only conformally invariant content left) *is* the boundary
  of the next Big Bang. At the limit, "all possible states" and "no state"
  are both scale-free — conformally indistinguishable. That is [A]-grade
  compactification geometry ([C] as cosmology), and the doctrine's own
  model supplies it natively: **z ↦ 1/z is a Möbius automorphism of S²
  exchanging 0 ↔ ∞ [A]** — the poles are *symmetry-equivalent* points of
  the model. Similar, not identical: the map moves each pole to the other;
  it does not collapse them.
- **Why no contradiction:** similarity is not substitutable into a strict
  order. D6 ~ D0 plus D0 < D1 < … < D6 does **not** yield D0 < D0. The
  contradiction required ≡. The audit's falsifier kills identity in the
  hierarchy register and says nothing about similarity in the boundary
  register.
- **Candidate restatement (staged for K2 ruling):** *"D6 ~ D0 literally at
  the conformal boundary — CCC-similarity, [A] as compactification
  geometry, [C] as physics. D6 ≢ D0 in the emergence hierarchy (126). The
  aeon closes by similarity, not by identity."* The corpus's slogans
  bearing the "=" sign are the register collision; the repair is the "~"
  discipline, not deletion. This coexists with the apophatic primary:
  similarity is the geometric fact ([A], boundary), neti-neti is the
  epistemic discipline ([I], approach) — different limbs, no conflict.
- **Falsifier:** exhibit a doctrinal derivation that must substitute D6
  for D0 inside the strict order (dies with 126), or show the
  compactification fails to make the boundary states conformally
  equivalent (kills the similarity claim).
- **Consequence for §2.1:** all CUTs were identity-form ("same point",
  "falls out", "IS the arithmetic") — they stand. The dissent adds the
  positive [A] register the corpus should use *instead*: the "~" of the
  conformal boundary and the z↦1/z automorphism, which §2.2's surviving
  claims already partially carry.
