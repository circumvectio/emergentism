## 6 · Mandatory dissent (L6 Sādhu turn)

Before K2 signs, the L6 surfaces three things:

1. **"Are the dated cycle artifacts *really* not at root for a reason?"** The standard's §4 says "do not move a file because it feels nearby — move it only when its authority owner changes." The 2026-07-19 cycle artifacts have a *cycle* owner (`2026_07_19`), not a *doctrine* owner. They belong in `00_HANDOFF/2026_07_19_*`/, not at root. The move is *with* the standard, not against it.

2. **"What about `00_WELTANSCHAUUNG_KERNEL_v0.1` vs v0.2?"** v0.1 is superseded by v0.2 (which lives at `06_ONTOLOGY/00_WELTANSCHAUUNG_KERNEL_v0.2_EMERGENTISM_ONLY.md` per K-2 row). K3 says move + tombstone; v0.1 goes to `90_ARCHIVE/old_front_doors/`. Dissent preserved in D5: a future reader may still want v0.1 — the tombstone + archive makes that possible without keeping the file at root.

3. **"Does the standard's per-pillar-`00_META`-forbidden rule (§10.2) conflict with putting files in root `00_META/`?"** No — the rule forbids *pillar-internal* `00_META/`, not the root one. The root `01_EMERGENTISM/00_META/` is the governance spine, and these files belong there.

The L6 verdict: **UPHOLD** the move list as staged, with the 3 dissents above preserved in D5.
