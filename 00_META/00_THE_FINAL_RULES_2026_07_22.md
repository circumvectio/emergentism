# THE FINAL RULES

**What this is.** The consolidated rule set for Emergentism and Skyzai, built
only on text that was transcribed verbatim from source and then re-verified by
a second reader who opened every cited file. Ten transcription errors — one an
outright fabricated quotation — were caught by that gate and are not in this
document. Three adversarial attacks on the consolidation draft were run; their
repairs are applied and noted.

**What it is not.** It resolves no genuine contradiction. Where two live
sources disagree, the item is **founder-gated** (§6) and both readings are
shown. There are twelve such items, and they include the most consequential
questions in the corpus.

---

## 1 · The naming law (the one new rule this document adds)

The attacks converged on one finding: the draft's two-layer scheme (plain names
*plus* eleven voluntary register tags) rebuilt the exact mechanism that already
failed once — a voluntary tagging convention was proposed at
`K2_PRISM_BOUNDARY_2026_05_16.md:160` and did not take. The corpus's one
*successful* repair is the live constitution, which **deleted the letters
entirely**: `00_META/00_THE_FIVE_PLUS_ONE_CONSTITUTION.md` contains zero
occurrences of η, K2, K3, K4, A7, or K\*. Verified.

So the law is one layer, mechanical, with a surface rule:

> **N-1 · One rule, one plain-language name.** The canonical identity of every
> rule is an English phrase statable to a stranger in one sentence.
>
> **N-2 · Letters are history, not names.** Every legacy token (K2, K3, L4, η,
> K\*, …) lives in the read-only alias index (§5) and resolves to exactly one
> rule. No new prose may mint a bare letter-number as a rule name.
>
> **N-3 · The surface rule.** Public pages, marketing copy, and agent route
> cards use plain names only. Internal spec and code may use a bracketed
> register tag (`[DAC-K]6`) where brevity matters — the tag, never the bare
> letter.
>
> **N-4 · Enforcement is a check, not a request.** A predeploy / pre-commit
> lint rejects bare K/L/A-letter rule references in new text. Voluntary
> conventions have already failed here once; a rule without a check is a hope.
