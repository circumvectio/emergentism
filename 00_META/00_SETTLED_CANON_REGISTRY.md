## Standing discipline

  requires matching observation/test and custody; `[I]→[S]` requires an explicit
  structural argument; draft→adopted remains a lifecycle change, not evidence.
- A failed derivation changes proof status; it does not automatically erase a
  useful model. Conversely, beauty does not restore a broken proof.
- The seam is never the score: the reciprocal chart and finite-node objective
  are distinct types.
- Every live claim names its evidence tier, rival or alternative, prediction,
  and kill criterion in proportion to its strength.
- Historical receipts, quotations, compatibility paths, and explicit
  tombstones may preserve retired wording. They are not current owners.
- No belief, membership, institutional adoption, model agreement, or AI output
  counts as evidence that a worldview claim is true.
