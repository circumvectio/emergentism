## 3. The five apertures as research packets

### GP-MU0 — origin, not saturation

- **Source:** one opaque object-level role.
- **Candidate freedom:** stable positive distinction and orientation.
- **Recovery:** forget magnitude, operations, and orientation; quotient to one role.
- **Rival:** D1 is simply stipulated metalanguage with no emergence content.
- **Discriminator:** a declared physical or computational system produces a
  reproducible distinction unavailable in the frozen lower description.
- **Kill:** the lower model already contains the distinction or Titan labels are
  confused with internal D0 states.

### GP-MU1 — elements to configured relations

- **Source:** a carrier with D1 distinctions.
- **Candidate freedom:** relation/configuration variables with their own
  invariants and interventions.
- **Recovery:** forget the relation and retain the relata.
- **Rival:** ordinary definitional expansion or feature engineering.
- **Discriminator:** configurations with identical element inventories but
  controlled relation changes yield held-out outcome differences.
- **Kill:** the relation adds no prediction or the claim depends only on
  adjoining projective infinity.

### GP-MU2 — configuration to state assignment

- **Source:** configuration space and declared observable structure.
- **Candidate freedom:** probability-bearing state assignments and incompatible
  measurement contexts.
- **Recovery:** restrict the state assignment to the lower configuration data.
- **Rival:** a formally reducible representation lift with no ontic novelty.
- **Discriminator:** any stronger emergence claim must identify an independently
  observed variable not exhausted by the full lower formal description.
- **Kill:** formal equivalence/reduction accounts for the claimed novelty.

### GP-MU3 — probability state to actual history

- **Source:** D3 state, dynamics, preparation, clock relation, and instrument.
- **Candidate freedom:** one ordered, receipted run.
- **Recovery:** marginalize or forget the record and recover the state-level
  probability predictions.
- **Rival:** time and record order are externally supplied or reconstructed,
  rather than emergent.
- **Discriminator:** a non-circular internal-clock and record model predicts an
  ordering statistic unavailable to a matched timeless description.
- **Kill:** unordered state support is called time, scalar normalization is
  sampled, or measurement interpretation is smuggled in as a dimension.

### GP-MU4 — actual history to represented alternatives

- **Source:** an actual D4 model token embedded in one actual history.
- **Candidate freedom:** compare and rank a bounded weighted set of alternative
  D4-shaped histories.
- **Recovery:** suppress the option model and recover reactive D4 behavior.
- **Rival:** ordinary memory, model-free learning, or a sufficiently rich D4
  controller predicts the same behavior without a distinct option-field variable.
- **Discriminator:** intervene on represented future contents or weights while
  holding current means and actual conditions fixed; observe a preregistered
  shift in present action distributions.
- **Kill:** no shift beyond the strongest fair D4 baseline.

## 6. The interlock—what the assembled picture actually says

The strongest whole that the current pieces license is:

1. **Difference is prior to calculation in the selected telling.** D1 arithmetic
   requires distinguishable carriers, but the telling is not a physical genesis
   theorem.
2. **Relations add explanatory variables without violating their relata.** D2
   configuration is often reducible in construction while indispensable in
   description.
3. **Quantum states assign probabilities; records are events.** D3 and D4 are a
   disciplined state/record separation, not a solution to quantum measurement.
4. **Possible futures act only through actual models.** D5 content influences a
   present decision through a D4 token and actual computation, not backward
   physical signaling.
5. **Action requires both a map and means in the selected node model.** Their
   product is one testable AND-class instance; the tradeoff surface is separate.
6. **Higher organization constrains admissible trajectories.** It need not add a
   forbidden micro-event to change which allowed histories persist.
7. **Collectives can acquire trace-mediated causal stability.** Calling one an
   Egregoreotype requires the full intervention-sensitive composite, not awe.
8. **Ethics is chosen constraint, not geometry's command.** The Justice envelope
   forbids aggregate laundering and keeps authorization, payer, beneficiary,
   reversibility, and exit visible.
9. **The map stays alive by losing.** A failed wager is removed without collapsing
   the address system or erasing its grave.

This is a candidate translation grammar across sciences. It is not yet a
unification theory because no common dynamics, constants, or novel replicated
discriminator has been derived.
