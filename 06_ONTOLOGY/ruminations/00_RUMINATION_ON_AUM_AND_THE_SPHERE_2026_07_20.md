# AUM on the Sphere — the Four States, Corrected with Gold

> **[金 Kintsugi seam — the crack and the gold.]** The first form of this mapping
> (2026-07-19, in session) said the shadows were cast *from the center*. That is
> geometrically false, and receipt 149 killed it with a date (death #4):
> **stereographic shadows are cast from the poles.** The repair is the gold:
> corrected, the mapping lands on the Māṇḍūkya tradition's own doctrine —
> deep sleep (prājña, the causal state, kāraṇa-śarīra) is the *projector* of
> waking and dream. The error said the witness casts the world. The correction
> says the *sleeping point* casts it — which is what the Upaniṣad taught all
> along. The crack, filled, carries more truth than the unbroken glaze.

## The corrected mapping `[I]`

| State | Position on the Burri sphere | What the geometry says unbidden |
|---|---|---|
| **A — waking** (jāgrat) | **The projection on the plane** — the shadow | Chart-dependent by construction: always *from* somewhere, never the thing. Two lamps, two shadows — waking is perspectival because projection is. |
| **U — dream** (svapna) | **The point on the sphere** | On the manifold, prior to any chart: content unanchored to a single viewpoint — vivid, real-feeling, unprojected. |
| **M — deep sleep** (suṣupti / prājña) | **The pole — the projection point itself** | Three teachings at once: (1) the pole is where the chart is *undefined* — no shadow of its own, consciousness without content; (2) the pole is **off the seam** — φ·ν = 1 fails only there: no subject–object product, the coupling grammar suspended; (3) **the waking shadow is cast *from* this point** — deep sleep as the causal state, the projector of the waking world. The lamp that illuminates every mapping cannot map itself. |
| **Turīya** — the fourth | **The center — the interior, off the surface entirely** | In no state, equidistant from every point, crossed by every diameter, fixed under every symmetry of the sphere. It casts nothing and receives nothing; it *grounds* the sphere without appearing on it. The signed anchor says it already: **"the seer is not on the sphere."** |

The ladder is a monotone descent in chart-dependence: waking (chart-bound) →
dream (on-manifold, chartless) → deep sleep (chart-*casting*, chart-breaking)
→ turīya (off-manifold). And the two poles are the two lamps: the one causal
depth, appearing as the caster of either chart — φ's projector and ν's
projector are the same sleep seen from its two mornings.

the world and cannot see itself; and the witness is the center every diameter
must cross — in no state, missing nothing, never once on the surface.**
