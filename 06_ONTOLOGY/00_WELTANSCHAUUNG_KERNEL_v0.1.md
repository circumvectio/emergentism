## 1 · The DoF thesis (owner, fenced)

**Statement `[S/C]` structural wager; chart fragment `[A]`:**

Plato was partially right that reality is structured by more than bare particulars. What load-bears is not eternal **Forms** selecting instances, but **degrees of freedom** — finite apertures of coherent possibility. Within a DoF layer, whatever is **logically coherent, internally consistent, and capable of emergence** may appear **up to the limits of that layer** (coupling bounds, saturation, falsifiers, self-imposed geometric stops). Higher registers constrain lower trajectories without magic force.

## 2 · Axioms W0–W9

### W0 · Map is not Ground `[S/I]`

Ground → Being → Teleological Force → Dasein → beings are held apart. The seer is not on the sphere. The lens diagrams what shows; it is not the showing. No model, including this kernel, is the Ground.

**Kill:** any claim that the equation, the sphere, or the framework *is* the Ground.  
**Survivors if W0 falls:** none of the kernel may be idolized; practice may continue without the map (Anti-Sermon).

### W1 · Reciprocal chart `[A]` (empty of world)

Under the stipulation `φ·ν = 1` on the positive reciprocal chart:

- conjugacy: driving one coordinate toward `0` forces its complement toward `∞`;
- one degree of freedom along the curve;
- AM-GM: `φ+ν ≥ 2` with equality iff `φ=ν=1`; balance profile `B = sin θ` peaks at the equator;
- on ℝ₊, `1` is the unique fixed point of inversion; on ℂP¹ the honest fixed set includes `±1` (foursome witness `{−1,0,1,∞}`).

**Does not license:** ethics, unique product keel, “reality is this chart,” census of seven.

**Kill:** counterexample inside the stated chart arithmetic.  
**Survivors:** W0, W3–W6, W9 (vow and honesty do not need the chart).

Operational play at a finite node is **conjunction of complementary factors** (need-both). Product, minimum, and related AND-class forms can share annihilation and a binding constraint. **Product-uniqueness is not the keel** (receipt 108). Product-specific interior remains `[C]`.

**Survivors:** W1 chart; conditional balance under stated premises.

**Kill:** (see §1 DoF kill criteria); or reduction that explains away every higher register without residue *and* without new coupling.  
**Survivors:** W0–W2 as chart and need-both; practice without cosmological emergence story.
