# DR-0…DR-6 — Dimensional Register Axioms

This document fills the selected D0–D6 registers without turning them into
physical dimensions, substances, stages of personal worth, or a forced census
of nature. The [Degrees-of-Freedom Ontology](02_THE_DEGREES_OF_FREEDOM_ONTOLOGY.md)
remains the sole ontology owner; this is its typed register contract.

## 1. Three orders that must not collapse

```text
DependencyPriority ≠ Actuality ≠ PsychologicalSalience
```

- **Dependency priority** asks which admissibility rules and carriers a
  realization presupposes.
- **Actuality** asks what causally occurred or is presently instantiated.
- **Psychological salience** asks what is vivid, accessible, or important to an
  agent now.

A higher-register realization cannot violate the admissibility rules it
depends on. Within this selected scaffold, lower rules are therefore
dependency-prior. This is `[S]` inside the grammar and `[I/C]` as a universal
reading of nature. Priority does not imply greater vividness, moral worth,
causal sufficiency, or complete reduction.

Use cumulative predicates and roles:

```text
RegisterRole :=
  ground_boundary | positive_structure | probability_bearing_state | actual_event
  | possible_content | exit_boundary

TokenModality := actual | merely_possible
```

An actual D4 carrier realizes D1 distinguishability and D2 configuration and
may receive a D3 quantum-state assignment when that physical description is
applicable. D3 supplies probabilities relative to measurement contexts; it is
not itself a recorded outcome. D5 is not another actual substance; it types
merely possible content represented by an actual carrier. D0 and D6 are
boundary roles; their inscriptions and acts of recognition are D4 tokens.

## 2. The seven register axioms

### DR-0 · Ground boundary

```text
PositiveFreedom(D0)=∅
role(D0)=ground_boundary
μ₀:D0↝D1 has triggerType=origin_aperture
```

D0 marks the boundary before positive articulation. It is `[S]` by selected
definition and `[I]` as a Ground reading. Collapse every D1 distinction into
one undifferentiated description to recover the boundary abstraction.

`TitanFrame={0_T,1_T,∞_T}` is the metalinguistic vocabulary used to describe
ground-, unit-, and horizon-facing seats. Its three written constructors are
not three internal D0 states. D0's object-level role is a singleton opacity;
`μ₀` opens the first positive object-level distinction, not the first mark in
the language describing the scaffold.

**Kill/revision:** reclassify any independently measurable positive capacity;
remove D0 if it adds no discipline. **Does not claim:** literal nothingness,
vacuum, mathematical zero, pre-Big-Bang physics, a causal entity, or a proved
Ground.

### DR-1 · Distinction

```text
PositiveModel(X,O) -> |X / ~O| ≥ 2
```

A positive model requires operationally distinguishable states. Labels that
are gauge- or observation-equivalent are not distinct freedoms. Logic,
identifiability, and information-bearing symbols are established neighbors
`[A]`; the universal aperture remains `[I/C]`.

The selected arithmetic projection is signed and positive-first. The oriented
unit-magnitude pair `{+1_N,-1_N}` opens two rays. A singleton `U={★}` represents
arithmetic `1_N`, and nonempty finite cardinalities form `ℕ⁺={1_N,2_N,…}` by
repeated disjoint union. The punctured signed set
`ℤ_•=ℕ⁺⊎(-ℕ⁺)=ℤ\{0_N}` is not an additive group:
`(+1_N)+(-1_N)=0_N∉ℤ_•`. Standard `ℤ=ℤ_•∪{0_N}`, and the ambient
fields `ℚ,ℝ` retain zero; only their multiplicative sectors exclude it. This
does not identify arithmetic `1_N` with sovereign Titan `1_T`, and it does not
make one a unique first set.

**Recovery:** quotient all observational equivalence classes into one to
recover D0. **Kill/revision:** merge a proposed distinction if no allowed
observation or intervention separates its states. **Does not claim:** a binary
universe, matter, space, consciousness, or genesis from arithmetic.

### DR-2 · Configuration

```text
Cfg=(X^I/Γ, R, C)
```

A configuration is a constrained arrangement of distinctions and relations.
The same elements under different relations may form different configurations.
State spaces, graphs, symmetry quotients, and constraint sets are established
neighbors; the ordinal rung is an Emergentist selection `[I/C]`.

The primary reduced neighbor is `X→(X,R)` with `R⊆X^k`; forgetting `R`
recovers the D1 carrier. Adjoining `∞_P` to the real line gives the
one-dimensional projective line
`ℝP¹`; it does not itself open D2. Rigorous D2 neighbors instead include a
function graph `Γ_f⊆X×Y` and, for a prior set `A`, the bounded membership
relation `Mem_A⊆A×℘(A)`. Russell's unrestricted `{x|x∉x}` does not form a
set in ZF-style foundations; it exposes a comprehension/self-application
boundary. Cantor's no-surjection theorem for `X→℘(X)` is a formal neighbor,
not a proof that nature crossed `μ₁`.

**Recovery:** forget relations and arrangement to recover D1 relata.
**Discriminator:** test whether relational variables distinguish systems with
the same inventory. **Does not claim:** time, two-dimensional space, chemical
bonding by ordinal necessity, `S²`, a universal set, or a Platonic Form.

### DR-3 · Quantum probability-state

For a complex Hilbert space `ℋ`,

```text
ρ=ρ†,  ρ⪰0,  Trρ=1
M={E_k},  E_k⪰0,  Σ_kE_k=I
p(k|ρ,M)=Tr(ρE_k)
```

D3 is the selected probability-bearing state of a declared, individuated
quantum system. The density operator `ρ` is not one classical probability
distribution: it generates an
outcome distribution only relative to the declared POVM `M`. For a qubit,
`ρ=(I+r·σ)/2`, with pure states on `||r||=1` and mixed states in the interior
`||r||<1`. These are standard quantum-information facts `[A/B]`; assigning
them to ordinal D3 is an Emergentist crosswalk `[I]`.

A unitary or quantum channel may transform D3 states, but change is not the
definition of the register. Mathematical flows and transition functions are
D2 configurations; actual physical interaction, preparation, outcome, and
record are D4.

For a nonrelativistic particle, D2 defines the position and momentum operators
on a suitable common domain; D3 supplies their state-conditioned distributions;
D4 supplies an actual apparatus interaction, clocked momentum transfer, and
record. This separation does not derive the uncertainty relation or solve
quantum gravity. It permits a narrower `[C]` time-seam hypothesis: stationary
or constraint-satisfying D3 states may yield experienced time only through D4
clock correlations and records. Momentum distributions and `S(ρ)` remain D3;
realized transfer and entropy-production histories are D4.

The stronger film-from-frames form remains `[C]`: “all D3 at the limit” must
include more than an exhaustive state set. It requires phase-sensitive
transition structure, compatible contexts, record conditions, and an internal
clock relation that orders conditional states. An unordered infinity of states
does not determine adjacency, direction, duration, or actuality. If external
`t` already orders the states, the construction reconstructs motion but does
not derive time.

**Lower-register recovery:** erase the state assignment with
`ForgetState(ℋ,Obs,ρ)=(ℋ,Obs)` and retain the D2 arena, operators, contexts, and
relations. In a declared basis `B`, dephasing
`Δ_B(ρ)=Σ_iΠ_iρΠ_i` yields a basis-relative classical probability vector, but
that is a D3 channel/discriminator rather than the D2 recovery map.
**Discriminator:** `|+⟩⟨+|` and `I/2` have
the same Z-basis distribution but different X-basis distributions, showing
that one fixed classical distribution is insufficient. **Kill/revision:**
remove any strong-emergence claim if the discriminator is fully recovered by
the D2 mathematical model; reassign the ordinal if it adds no explanatory
discipline. **Does not claim:** a third spatial dimension, a three-coordinate
proof of D3, a classical hidden distribution, life, consciousness, collapse,
or a preferred quantum interpretation.

### DR-4 · Causal actuality and record

```text
(X_(t+1), r_(t+1)) ~ K_t(. | X_t, a_t, E_t)
```

D4 contains actual carriers and events: preparations, physical models,
memories, rankings, selections, acts, measurement interactions, outcomes, and
records, including the order of a realized history. Event, record, state
assignment, and truth are distinct. Relativistic
causal order, physical information cost, laboratory outcome statistics, and
action/outcome gaps provide established component neighbors; assigning the
D3-to-D4 state/record interface to `μ₃` remains `[I/C]`.

**Recovery:** remove the concrete run, apparatus, outcome token, provenance,
and receipt, retaining only the D3 probability assignment and instrument
model. **Kill/revision:** contract the proposed crossing where record variables
add no operational discrimination. **Does not claim:** fundamental collapse,
one uniquely actual global branch, `3+1` spacetime, consciousness, presentism,
authorization as physical necessity, truth of every record, or physical
retrocausality.

### DR-5 · Counterfactual content

```text
CausallyRelevant(p:D5)
  -> exists m:D4_actual such that represents(m,p)
```

D5 types merely possible alternatives represented by actual D4 tokens. Actual
modeling, comparison, ranking, and selection remain D4 events. Future-cue
experiments, decision theory, reflexivity, and control reachability provide
external neighbors; the universal option-cone and `μ₄` claims remain `[C]`.

At the conjectural block-to-ensemble limit, an actual D4 token co-represents
the complete support of alternative D4-shaped histories admitted by a declared
model, context, horizon, and tolerance. These are D5 “probable timeline”
contents. Co-representation is not physical co-existence, and “all” never means
every logically describable universe. An agent's reachable option cone is only
a subset of this modeled support.

**Recovery:** suppress counterfactual representation and test whether reactive
D4 behavior returns. **Kill/revision:** contract D5 in a target system if
represented-alternative interventions add no held-out effect beyond fair D4
baselines. **Does not claim:** a fifth physical dimension, actual possible
worlds, future-to-past signaling, guaranteed enactment, consciousness, or
morality.

### DR-6 · Exit and nonclosure

```text
PositiveFreedom(D6)=∅
b₆:D5↝D6
r₆:D6↝D0
```

D6 is the selected exit boundary. `b₆` and `r₆` are interpretive, noncausal,
non-μ edges. An actual act of withdrawal, recognition, or inscription is D4;
D6 names the role assigned to it. Fallibilism, formal limits, and apophatic
traditions are comparisons `[I]`, not scientific calibration of D6.

**Kill/revision:** extend or renumber the scaffold if a reproducible positive
capacity beyond D5 earns a useful register. **Does not claim:** `D6≡D0`, cosmic
recurrence, fractal identity, enlightenment, survival after death, or proof
that nature has no further freedom.

## 3. The μ contract

There are five aperture labels and only four saturation candidates:

```text
μ₀ : origin_aperture
μ₁…μ₄ : saturation_candidate
```

`μ₀` owes a distinction discriminator and recovery test. Each of `μ₁…μ₄` owes
a system boundary, saturation statistic and threshold, new-freedom
discriminator, lower-register recovery, prediction, evidence tier, and kill.
No current missing reduction proves strong emergence. A successful reduction
reclassifies one crossing as weak/reduced without destroying the scaffold.

## 4. The remembered-sunset test

A remembered Bali sunset from 2012 may be psychologically vivid today without
being ontologically or nomologically basic:

```text
m_t : MemoryToken(D4, actual)
h_τ : HistoricalClaim
represents(m_t,h_τ)

if veridical:
  e_τ : HistoricalEvent(D4, actual)
  refersTo(h_τ,e_τ)

counterfactual variants:
  p'_τ : D5, merely_possible

Salience_t(m_t,agent) : nonnegative real
```

The present memory token depends on D1 distinguishability, D2
neural/configurational organization, and D4 actual instantiation. A physical
model may additionally assign its substrate a D3 quantum state, but this
example neither requires nor measures that assignment. Its imagined variants
are D5 content. Salience changes none of those types and does not prove the
historical claim.

> The remembered sunset can be the first thing I know without being the first
> thing it depends on.

This example does not solve phenomenal consciousness or prove a complete
reduction of memory.

## 5. General dependency contract

Write `D_n ≺_N D_(n+1)` only for a declared system where:

1. every higher realization has a lower carrier;
2. `support(K_n^C)⊆support(K_n)`;
3. ablating the higher constraint removes its discriminator while lower
   dynamics remain; and
4. `Recover_n` reproduces the lower description within declared tolerance.

Psychological salience supplies no bridge:

```text
Salience_t(x)>Salience_t(y)
  does not entail x is dependency-prior to y
```
