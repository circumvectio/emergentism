# Ontology across dimensions

> **[金] Kintsugi seam.** The former synthesis protected an apophatic upper
> boundary but also switched the meanings of D4/D5, made the end identical to
> the beginning, and declared a universal two-way emergence law from missing
> reductions. This edition keeps the dimensional lens while removing those
> proofs by assertion. The earlier body remains recoverable at the Git blob
> above.

## 1. Scope

`D0…D6` is a selected structural and interpretive scaffold. It is not a theorem
derived from `S²`, a necessary decomposition of nature, or a claim about seven
spacetime dimensions. A register earns use only by improving description,
prediction, intervention, or audit in a named system.

The scaffold approaches reality as **constraint-structured freedom**. A degree
of freedom is a typed capacity for independent variation after constraints and
redundancies are declared. A stable form is not a free-standing Form or the
freedom itself; it may be an invariant, equivalence class, order parameter,
attractor, or robust organization across freedoms.

## 3. Five candidate crossings

The scaffold names exactly five candidate positive-freedom crossings:

```text
μ₀:D0→D1
μ₁:D1→D2
μ₂:D2→D3
μ₃:D3→D4
μ₄:D4→D5
```

This count is selected `[I]`, not forced by geometry or established as nature's
complete census. Each crossing owes:

1. a system boundary and saturated lower register;
2. a measured saturation statistic and threshold;
3. a newly discriminable effective freedom;
4. recovery of lower-register behavior;
5. a reduction status;
6. an intervention-sensitive prediction;
7. a kill criterion.

```text
reductionStatus ∈ {reduced, currently_unreduced, candidate_strong}
```

Absence of a reducing law yields `currently_unreduced`, not irreducibility.
Affirmative evidence is required for `candidate_strong`. A successful reduction
changes classification without erasing a useful macro-description.

## 4. Dasein at the D4/D5 threshold `[I]`

In this lens, Dasein is a finite situated bearer of disclosure and action. It
does not stand outside the world and is not identical with Being or Ground.

- D4 names its actual body, access, memory, model tokens, reasoning events,
  commitments, actions, and receipts.
- D5 names the alternatives those actual tokens can represent, compare, and
  aim toward.

Finite commitment uses a selected D5 candidate together with D4 means and
accountable authorization to attempt a D4 action. The environment, other
agents, constraints, and chance separately determine the outcome. This is not
the crossing operator and does not create history by choosing it.

Anticipated futures can influence present action because present embodied
models represent them. The causal carrier remains D4 and every physical arrow
points forward in time.

## 5. Generation and constraint

Two explanatory directions may be tested without declaring a universal law:

- **construction:** interactions among lower-level variables produce a
  macro-pattern;
- **constraint:** an instantiated macro-pattern reweights or restricts
  lower-law-admissible trajectories.

For a lower transition kernel `K_X` and a higher constraint `C`:

```text
support(K_X^C) ⊆ support(K_X)
```

A higher-level description earns causal or explanatory standing only when an
intervention on its constraint changes lower-level distributions beyond rival
summaries and controls. It never manufactures a lower-law-forbidden trajectory.

## 6. Closure without identity

D6 adds no positive freedom. The selected topology uses:

```text
b₆:D5↝D6       r₆:D6↝D0
```

Both are non-μ interpretive edges. D6 and D0 may be compared as boundary-role
markers under named invariants; they are not numerically, logically,
phenomenologically, or physically identical. The return is a practice of
non-finality, not a cosmological recurrence.

## 7. Reciprocal chart and finite-node model

On the stipulated chart, for `θ∈(0,π)`,

```text
φ=cot(θ/2),  ν=tan(θ/2),  φν=1,  φ+ν≥2.
```

These are analytic facts `[A]` inside the chart. They do not prove the
dimensional scaffold, ontology, emergence, Dasein, or ethics. The Titan frame
`{0,1,∞}` is selected symbolism; ordinary 0 and 1 remain numbers, and infinity
is a projective point in the named extended context.

The node model `P_node=ΦV` is the selected normalized product member of a wider
monotone conjunctive family. Its transfer to a real system is conjectural and
must compete with minimum, harmonic, Cobb–Douglas, and other suitable models.

## 8. Value is not derived by the ladder

No register, crossing, chart, or balance extremum proves morality. Emergentism
chooses non-extraction and tests action through a bearer-complete Justice
envelope. Strict Syntropic Dyadism requires both the individual's and the
whole's durable potential to rise; aggregate gain cannot launder destruction
of one side. Voluntary sacrifice is recorded as a distinct costly class.

## 9. D3 quantum state and the interpretation fence

The standard density-operator/Born interface supplies D3's selected formal
model: `ρ⪰0`, `Trρ=1`, and `p(k|ρ,M)=Tr(ρE_k)`. The formalism is inherited
`[A/B]`; its D3 placement is `[I]`. D4 supplies actual interactions, outcome
tokens, and records. D5 is agent-represented counterfactual content and is not
quantum branch space.

Interpretations remain removable. Everett has no fundamental collapse;
Copenhagen-family actualization is interpretation-specific; neither is an
added spacetime dimension. `μ₃` is an interface label, not a demonstrated
collapse mechanism, and commitment is not quantum measurement.
