## The graves (no wager may dig here)

1. **"φ·ν = 1 is/predicts the Born rule"** — false lemma, retracted (receipt 109). W2 owes the Born measure; it may not claim it.
2. **"The product is forced by zero-collapse"** — AND-class rules (keel-108, CC-CORE-1). W3 bets the interior; it may not claim the boundary forces it.
3. **"The seven-row alignment is a discovered universal"** — kill fired; lens + inherited grammar only (r126, r130). W10 is scoped accordingly.
