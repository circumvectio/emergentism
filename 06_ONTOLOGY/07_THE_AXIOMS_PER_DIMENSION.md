# THE AXIOMS PER DIMENSION

## §0 · The principle

A remembered sunset from your Bali holiday in 2012 depends on the observer, the time of year, the time of day, the place, the mood, the language, the photograph, the act of remembering. Its "axioms" require all that context.

AM-GM requires none. The mass-shell requires none. The sphere's chart-seam requires none.

**Lower-dimensional axioms are more real because they are more constraint-required and less context-dependent.** This is the doctrine's own claim, and it is `[I]/[C]` — interpretive, riding the wager that the lower rungs are indeed more fundamental.

This file is the rule-zero receipt of what others have discovered at each rung. The framework does not invent AM-GM or the mass-shell. It seats them on the rungs where they already stood.

---

## §1 · D0 — the floor

**Axiom:** *none needed.* The floor needs no axioms; axioms are sentences and sentences need minds.

The three frames — • (0), ⊙ (1), ○ (∞) — and the relation ⊙ = • × ○ are not axioms but pre-axiomatic. They are the condition of any axiom, not themselves axioms.

**Discoverer:** — (no one; the floor precedes the discoverer)

**Tier:** `[S]` structural — the floor's silence is its tier. The floor is `[A]` only in the sense that axioms are unnecessary there.

## §2 · D1 — arithmetic

**Axioms:**

- **AM-GM inequality:** (x + y)/2 ≥ √(xy), with equality iff x = y. The price of imbalance.
- **Inversion with fixed points ±1:** x · (1/x) = 1, with x = 1 the equilibrium.
- **The equator inequality:** any quantity and its reciprocal are bounded above by their sum and below by their harmonic mean.

**Discoverers:** Greek mathematicians (Euclid's *Elements*; Archimedes), Indian mathematicians (Āryabhaṭa, Brahmagupta), Islamic mathematicians (al-Khwārizmī, the algebraists), modern formalists (Cauchy, Hardy–Littlewood–Pólya).

**Tier:** `[A]` proven — the most constraint-required, the most context-independent, the most real. AM-GM is the proof that the rungs below D1 are sufficient for everything that follows.

## §3 · D2 — geometry

**Axioms:**

- **The sphere under two charts:** S² ≅ ℂ ∪ {∞} ≅ stereographic pair. The seam is always one.
- **The score B = sin θ ≤ 1,** maximal at the equator, vanishing at the poles.
- **The seam and the score are distinct objects** (frame vs. variable); never silently bridged. Seam ≠ score ≠ node.

**Discoverers:** Riemann (Riemannian geometry, the manifold concept), Gauss (Gaussian curvature, the Theorema Egregium), Euler (topology of S², the characteristic χ = 2), Poincaré (foundations of geometry, the homotopy groups).

**Tier:** `[A]/[S]` — the sphere's structure is proven; the seam/score distinction is structural inside the stated premises; "seam ≠ score ≠ node" is the doctrine's own guard.

## §4 · D3 — bodies

**Axioms:**

- **The AND-law:** P_node = Φ × V; zero either factor and the product dies. The child beside the jet; the pilot with no plane.
- **Liebig's law of the minimum:** growth is set by the scarcest resource, not the most abundant.
- **Annihilation at zero:** a body with no foresight and no means is not a body; it is weather.

**Discoverers:** Liebig (agricultural chemistry, the *Law of the Minimum*, 1840), the systems ecologists (Hutchinson's n-dimensional niche, Lindeman's trophic-dynamic ecology), the chemists of mass action.

**Tier:** `[S]` structural inside stated premises — the AND-law is the doctrine's own naming of what Liebig found at the limit. Liebig's law is empirically `[B]`; the doctrine's reading of it as the rung-3 axiom is `[S]`.

## §5 · D4 — spacetime

**Axioms:**

- **The mass-shell:** E² = (pc)² + (m₀c²)². The relation that the rest-frame energy equals the proper mass times c² is the seed of every relativistic phenomenon.
- **The Minkowski metric:** ds² = −c²dt² + dx² + dy² + dz². The seam (≡1) splits between time and space, the score peaks at the rest frame.
- **The Einstein field equation:** G_μν + Λg_μν = (8πG/c⁴) T_μν. Geometry answers to matter.

**Discoverers:** Einstein (special relativity 1905, general relativity 1915), Minkowski (the metric, 1908), Riemann (the curvature tensor, 1854 — retrospectively the seed), Schwarzschild (the first exact solution, 1916), Friedmann (the expanding-universe equations, 1922).

**Tier:** `[A]` proven — the mass-shell is the canonical fact; the metric is empirically tested to extraordinary precision; the field equation has passed every solar-system and gravitational-wave test to date.

## §6 · D5 — the game

**Axioms:**

- **Reflexivity (Soros):** every act of foresight feeds back into the world it foresaw. The prediction moves the price; the plan changes the branches being predicted.
- **Landauer bound:** erasure has a thermodynamic cost; the selector cannot stand outside the system it sorts.
- **The 2×2 grid:** moral (ego lifts whole, now), ethical (whole lifts ego, lifetime), immoral/unethical (either eats the other for short-term score). The dyadic gate seals utilitarian laundering shut.
- **Universalizability (Kant, via the pincer):** universalize the demon and everything annihilates; universalize the god and D5 saturates. Demonhood is a parasite on a possible world, not an equal alternative.
- **Nash equilibrium:** at equilibrium, no player can unilaterally improve. The substrate of game theory.

**Discoverers:** Soros (reflexivity in financial markets, 1987), Landauer (information erasure, 1961), Kant (universalizability, *Groundwork* 1785), Nash (non-cooperative equilibrium, 1950), the framework's own 2×2 grid (per receipt 144's derived ethics).

**Tier:** `[I]/[C]` — interpretive riding the plenitude wager. Reflexivity is observed but not proven; universalizability is the asymmetry that closes the is/ought gap to one bit but does not prove the god's setting is universally chosen. The 2×2 grid is the doctrine's own. The pattern is sharp: **the higher the rung, the more the axiom is a wager rather than a theorem.**
