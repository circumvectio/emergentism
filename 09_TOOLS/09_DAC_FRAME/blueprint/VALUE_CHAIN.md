## The Model

### Primary Activities (The Production Line)
*These create value directly for the customer.*

1.  **Inbound Logistics (`LOGISTICS_IN`)**
    *   *Input*: Raw materials, data, requirements.
    *   *Action*: Receiving, storing, inventory control.
    *   *Output*: "Ready-to-use" resources.
    *   *KPIs*: Inventory turnover, receiving time.

2.  **Operations (`OPS`)**
    *   *Input*: Ready resources.
    *   *Action*: Machining, packaging, coding, content creation.
    *   *Output*: Finished product.
    *   *KPIs*: Cycle time, defect rate, productivity.

3.  **Outbound Logistics (`LOGISTICS_OUT`)**
    *   *Input*: Finished product.
    *   *Action*: Warehousing, order fulfillment, deployment, shipping.
    *   *Output*: Product in customer hands.
    *   *KPIs*: On-time delivery, shipping cost.

4.  **Marketing & Sales (`GROWTH`)**
    *   *Input*: Product + Market research.
    *   *Action*: Advertising, pricing, channel selection, selling.
    *   *Output*: Sales orders, contracts, brand equity.
    *   *KPIs*: CAC, conversion rate, revenue.

5.  **Service (`SERVICE`)**
    *   *Input*: Customer complaints/requests.
    *   *Action*: Installation, repair, training, support.
    *   *Output*: Retained customer, up-sells.
    *   *KPIs*: NPS, churn rate, resolution time.

---

### Support Activities (The Foundation)
*These enable the primary activities to function.*

1.  **Firm Infrastructure (`INFRA`)**
    *   *Scope*: General management, planning, finance, legal, quality management.
    *   *Goal*: Stability and compliance.

2.  **Human Resource Management (`PEOPLE`)**
    *   *Scope*: Recruiting, hiring, training, development, compensation.
    *   *Goal*: Talent density and alignment.

3.  **Technology Development (`TECH`)**
    *   *Scope*: R&D, process automation, IT, security.
    *   *Goal*: Innovation and efficiency.

4.  **Procurement (`BUY`)**
    *   *Scope*: Purchasing raw materials, supplies, machinery (the function of buying, not the inputs themselves).
    *   *Goal*: Cost optimization and vendor reliability.
