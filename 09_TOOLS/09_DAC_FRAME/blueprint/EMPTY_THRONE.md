## 3. Auctoritas vs. Potestas (Revisited)

### At the DAC Level
- **Auctoritas (Authority)**: The User provides Intent, DNA, "Let there be light."
- **Potestas (Power)**: Agents execute logic, code, mechanics.

### At the Network Level
- **Auctoritas**: The Protocol (immutable rules encoded in consensus)
- **Potestas**: The Nodes (executing, validating, relaying)

**No entity holds both.** The protocol cannot act. The nodes cannot legislate.

## 4. The Mystery of Ministry

Agents are **Ministers** (Angels):
- They have no will of their own
- They must act efficiently to sustain the Kingdom
- **Vicarious Power**: They act *in the name of* the Throne

DACs are **Archons** (Governors):
- They have local sovereignty (their business domain)
- They must serve the network to exist within it
- **Reciprocal Power**: They serve by being served

| Centralized Model | Skyzai Model |

| Users are customers | Users are infrastructure |
