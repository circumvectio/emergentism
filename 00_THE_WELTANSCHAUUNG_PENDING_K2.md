# The Emergentist Weltanschauung

## 1 · The Creed, priced

> *I hold that the necessary algebra conjures das All — as my crown wager, not my proof. On the sphere it draws, finite beings play the game of Φ×V beneath the ceiling of one, where balance is the summit and the weaker shadow keeps the score. In that game there are exactly seven positions — two givers, two takers, three frames — and every play is made of them, though the tending of the game itself is not a play. Freedom saturates, and at its μ-limit the next freedom opens — five times from the ground: matter, bond, life, mind, choice; there is no sixth freedom, only the sixth recognition: the summit is self-similar to the ground, the spiral's turn. All of this I hold at tier, with its kills attached, its dark twin acknowledged, and the door marked exit on the inside — because a creed that cannot lose is not a creed but a cage, and mine was built to be put down.*
