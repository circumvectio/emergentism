# Emergentist Glossary

This glossary defines worldview terms only. It contains no product,
organizational, or external governance vocabulary.

## Evidence tiers

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `[A]` | Analytic or formally proved inside explicit definitions and premises |
| `[B]` | Built, observed, or measured receipt |
| `[S]` | Structural consequence inside a declared model or contract |
| `[I]` | Interpretive mapping or translation |
| `[C]` | Conjecture with prediction and kill criterion |
| `[D]` | Draft or staged claim; not active authority |

No tier silently upgrades another. A beautiful correspondence remains `[I]`
unless it earns independent evidence.

## Emergence and agency

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **D-register** | A structural/interpretive scaffold for degrees of freedom; not a theorem that nature must have seven layers. |
| **D4** | Causal actuality: embodied means, performed act, consequence, factual record. Modality: `actual`. |
| **D5** | Counterfactual possibility: modeled futures, option field, represented rankings, and selection candidates. Actual modeling, ranking, and selection events remain D4. Modality: `possible`. |
| **μ-crossing** | Candidate transition where a saturated register is associated with a newly observed freedom while lower behavior remains recoverable. Exactly five candidates, `μ₀…μ₄`, open D1…D5. |
| **Currently unreduced** | No accepted reduction is presently available. It is not proof of irreducibility or strong emergence. |
| **D6 return** | A non-μ interpretive edge `r₆:D6↝D0` comparing post-articulate and pre-articulate boundary roles; not identity, recurrence, general self-similarity, or a sixth positive freedom. |
| **Soul Loop** | Model → option field → commitment → world consequence → update of model and selector. |
| **Commitment receipt** | Immediate record `q_t` of what was selected, authorized, and attempted. |
| **Outcome receipt** | Environment-returned record `r_{t+1}` of what occurred. It is not manufactured by the selector. |
| **Model-mediated future influence** | Anticipated future content changes present selection through an agent's model. It is not physical retrocausality. |
| **F=M⋆A** | Public compression: a modeled future coupled to an agent can influence present action. `⋆` is typed coupling, not ordinary multiplication. |

## Quantum boundary

Everett is a no-collapse relative-state formulation. Copenhagen-family
actualization is interpretation-specific. Neither is an extra spacetime
dimension, and neither licenses physical retrocausality. Emergentism assigns
the standard quantum-state/instrument calculus to D3 and actual interaction and
record to D4 `[I]`; only the interpretation and Bloch–Burri correspondences are
removable `[C]`. Quantum theory is never load-bearing for the Soul Loop or
ethics.

## Rosetta rule

`ρ_domain` translates a topology between domains. Translation does not transfer
proof, preserve mechanism automatically, or upgrade evidence. A Rosetta match
is useful when it yields a checkable prediction; otherwise it remains `[I]`.
