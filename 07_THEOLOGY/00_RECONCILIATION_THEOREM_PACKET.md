## Theorem Candidate 1 -- Canonical Multiplicative Kernel

**Statement**
Within the source-foundation register of the corpus, the load-bearing sequence is:

```text
Zero-Sum Resolution Equation
P∞ = φ · ν = 1 on S²
(φ − ν)² ≥ 0
φ + ν ≥ 2
```

The operational claim is that `Zero-Sum Resolution Equation` is the canonical seed and `P∞ = φ · ν = 1 on S²` is its canonical geometric resolution. The later lines are consequences in that register, not prior axioms.

**Register**
Canonical operational / source-foundation register.

**Assumptions**
- [00_CANONICAL_FORMULA_BLOCK.md](../05_COSMOLOGY/00_CANONICAL_FORMULA_BLOCK.md) is authoritative for compression order.
- [00_THE_TRANSCENDENTAL_TRINITY.md](../05_COSMOLOGY/01_THE_TRANSCENDENTAL_TRINITY/README.md) and [00_THE_LAGRANGIAN_SPHERE.md](../05_COSMOLOGY/00_THE_LAGRANGIAN_SPHERE.md) are authoritative for the source-foundation mapping.
- `S²` here means the framework's Lagrangian-sphere substrate, not an already-proved identity with all of external physics.

**Dependencies**
- [00_CANONICAL_FORMULA_BLOCK.md](../05_COSMOLOGY/00_CANONICAL_FORMULA_BLOCK.md)
- [00_THE_TRANSCENDENTAL_TRINITY.md](../05_COSMOLOGY/01_THE_TRANSCENDENTAL_TRINITY/README.md)
- [00_THE_LAGRANGIAN_SPHERE.md](../05_COSMOLOGY/00_THE_LAGRANGIAN_SPHERE.md)

**Proof Obligation**
- Show that the source corpus consistently preserves the order `seed -> sphere resolution -> arithmetic consequence -> teleological inequality`.
- Show that no later derivation note is required to make the canonical block internally coherent.

**Disproof Condition**
- A source-authoritative foundation document reverses the order and makes the inequality or the exponential companion prior to the multiplicative seed.
- The canonical source surfaces disagree about whether `P∞ = φ · ν = 1 on S²` is the geometric resolution of the seed.

**Forbidden Overread**

