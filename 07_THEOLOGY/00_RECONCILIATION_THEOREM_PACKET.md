## Result 1 — reciprocal chart identities `[A]`

For `θ∈(0,π)`, define

\[
\phi=\cot(\theta/2),\qquad \nu=\tan(\theta/2).
\]

Then

\[
\phi\nu=1,\qquad
\phi+\nu=\frac{2}{\sin\theta}\ge2.
\]

Equality in the inequality holds exactly at `θ=π/2`. With
`u=\log\phi`, `φ+ν=2\cosh u`.

**Licensed:** these are exact analytic relations in the selected chart.

**Not licensed:** an empirical conservation law, a necessary ontology, a moral
law, a Born rule, or the arithmetic claim `1=0×∞`.

**Kill:** an algebraic counterexample inside the stated domain.
