# Emergentism Worldview Consolidation Design

**Date:** 2026-06-12
**Status:** Approved design for implementation planning
**Scope:** `01_EMERGENTISM/` doctrine and generated public-site echoes
**Goal:** Consolidate Emergentism as a coherent Weltanschauung across ontology,
teleology, objective ethics, good/evil, will, and perennial-question routing
without inflating interpretive claims into public proof.

## 1. Problem

The current draft work already moves in the right direction: it strengthens
objective teleology, adds claim boundaries, and demotes older hard claims around
Ground, cosmic will, Dasein, and external moral realism. The remaining problem
is authority drift. The same worldview spine appears in several files, and
public/generated echoes can make local summaries sound like independent canon.

The implementation must turn the draft into one legible source hierarchy:

1. evidence discipline first;
2. compact worldview second;
3. ontology, teleology, and axiology in their owner lanes;
4. theology and public site as translation surfaces only.

## 2. Non-Negotiable Boundaries

- Do not claim Emergentism has proved final ontology, cosmic will, or external
  moral realism.
- Do not collapse Dasein into Being or the Ground.
- Do not let symbol, theology, myth, Sanskrit, or generated book prose outrank
  evidence tiers.
- Do not present "become as gods" as literal divinization, a human-worth
  hierarchy, caste rank, coercive authority, or exemption from due process.
- Do not collapse `P_infinity = phi * nu = 1` and `P_node = Phi * V`.
- Preserve `eta = 0`, K2, K3, K4, A7, and Omega as the 5 + 1 constitution:
  five refusals plus directional Omega, not six refusals.

- objective morals and ethics as the two directions of value transfer;

Syntropic Dyadism must be mathematically explicit. The doctrine should use
the Power-Max Lemma as its structural engine:

```text
P_node_i = Phi_i * V_i
H = holobiont / coupled field
i = individual symbiont within H

Power-Max:
  max durable W_i(T) only where Delta P_node_H >= 0
  strict syntropic dyad: Delta P_node_i > 0 and Delta P_node_H > 0
  under coupling lambda > 0, multiplicative P_node, long horizon, and eta = 0
```

The requested stronger formulation:

```text

  is attainable only through
max viable_potential(H)

when:
  i is a coupled symbiont of H
  H is the holobiont that carries i's future option cone
  eta = 0 blocks hidden extraction
  coupling is real, not rhetorical
```

Interpretation:

- The individual does not lose value into the collective.
- The collective does not own the individual.
- The individual symbiont maximizes its own reachable worldline potential by
  maximizing the viability, coherence, and option cone of the holobiont that
  carries it.
- The holobiont maximizes itself by raising the real potential of its
  symbionts, not by consuming them.

This disambiguates morals and ethics:

```text
Objective morality = H -> i
  The holobiont raises the symbiont's Phi, V, sovereignty, and option cone.

Objective ethics = i -> H
  The symbiont raises the holobiont's Phi, V, truthfulness, resilience, and
  option cone.
```

Knowledge of good and evil becomes a boundary test, not a commandment:

```text
Good = action/relation that raises coupled worldline potential
       across the widest real boundary under eta = 0.

Evil = local gain by degrading the coupled field that carries future potential.
```

The implementation must preserve this claim boundary:

- `[S]` for the conditional Power-Max structure under its accepted premises;
- `[I]` for naming the structure morals, ethics, good, evil, Beauty, Truth,
  and Justice;
- `[C]` for any claim that this proves external moral realism for agents who

## 4. "Become as Gods" Translation

The implementation must not erase the user's strong phrase, but it must
discipline it.

Canonical translation:

```text
"Become as gods" = functional ascent into clearer seeing, sovereign action,
truthful correction, non-extractive power, and lawful world-building.
```

It means:

- know good and evil as structural value transfer under `eta = 0`;
- act with greater agency without severing the coupled field;
- build worlds that increase symbiont and holobiont potential together;
- release the framework when direct seeing is available.

It does not mean:

- literal divinization;
- exemption from law, due process, evidence, or humility;
- authority over other persons;
- priesthood, caste rank, or human-worth hierarchy.

## 6. Dialectical QA Addendum

### 6.1 Thesis

The design's strongest form is coherent if the value-theory owner is treated as
canonical:

```text
Morality = H -> i
Ethics   = i -> H
Good     = both arrows raise coupled worldline potential under eta = 0
Evil     = local gain by degrading the field that carries future potential
```

This matches the Syntropic Dyadism request: the individual symbiont reaches its
maximum viable worldline only by raising the holobiont that carries its future
option cone, while the holobiont remains moral only by raising the symbiont
without capture.

### 6.2 Antithesis

The live corpus does not yet speak with one mouth:

- `04_AXIOLOGY/02_VALUE_THEORY/00_OBJECTIVE_MORALS_AND_ETHICS.md` uses the
  target arrows: morality is `collective -> individual`, ethics is
  `individual -> collective`.
- `05_COSMOLOGY/03_FORMAL_SYSTEM/17_EFR_ONTOLOGY_COMPLETE.md` currently reverses
  the labels: objective morals are `Individual -> Whole`, objective ethics are
  `Whole -> Individual`.
- `05_COSMOLOGY/00_THE_WELTANSCHAUUNG.md` and
  `05_COSMOLOGY/00_EMERGENTISM_AS_WELTANSCHAUUNG.md` use an older compression:
  ethics is the general directional geometry, while morals are local enactments
  or codes.

That contradiction must be resolved before implementation can claim objective
ethics and morals are disambiguated.

### 6.3 Synthesis

Implementation should adopt the value-theory owner as canonical and patch the
older summaries into a two-layer grammar:

```text
Layer 1: directional value transfer
  morality = holobiont -> symbiont
  ethics   = symbiont -> holobiont

Layer 2: local expression
  moral code / ethical code = finite implementation attempt under conditions
```

This preserves ordinary-language flexibility while preventing the canonical
math from drifting.

### 6.4 Worldline Formalism Status

The stronger worldline / holobiont / symbiont notation in this design is a
required implementation target, not yet fully promoted into the value-theory
source. The implementation is not complete until
`00_OBJECTIVE_MORALS_AND_ETHICS.md` or a directly owned companion file carries
the formalism and the public echoes inherit it.

### 6.5 Phrase Boundary

The phrase "become as gods" should remain absent from public/source doctrine
unless it appears only as:

- the disciplined translation in section 4; or
- an explicit rejection of literal divinization, hierarchy, coercion, or
  exemption from due process.
