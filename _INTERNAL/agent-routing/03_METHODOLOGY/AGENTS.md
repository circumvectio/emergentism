## What This Means

**Methodology is how we act on what we know** — not what IS (Ontology), not what we
know (Epistemology), not what we value (Axiology). It is the second of the *three operative Gods*:
Kṛṣṇa, the charioteer. The faculty of process — derivation, formal proofs, testing
protocols, deductive rigor. Kṛṣṇa's transfer (−Φ_self, +V_other) is the epistemic
signature: spend your own coherence to build the other's capability. The charioteer
steers the horses; the driver chooses the destination.

Artha (productive prosperity) is the virtue: capability built in the system, not
hoarded by the methodologist.
