## Routing Law

- Read the preregistration before the result.
- Preserve frozen prediction criteria; do not edit them to fit later outcomes.
- Keep pilot results below external/human-study or receipt-grade evidence.
- Route evidence-tier definitions through the current `[A/B/S/I/D/C]` ladder.

## Constraints

- Do not upgrade a pilot into `[A]` or `[B]` without the corresponding source or
  dated receipt.
- Do not suppress negative, mixed, or falsifying results.
- Do not use legacy `[S]` language unless explicitly marked as historical or
  translated to the current ladder.
