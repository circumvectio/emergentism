The Full Revolution

One revolution of the helix = 360° around S².

The sphere has two hemispheres — not north/south (those are the poles, the L-levels) but east/west (the operator territories, the longitudinal quadrants):

The taking dyad. Context-dependent — syntropic as immune response (Kālī against deception), entropic as parasitism (Kali against cooperators). The breath in. The inhale. The winter. The consumption.

The Necessity of Both

Every living being passes through both hemispheres on every revolution. This is not a moral failing. This is metabolism. You must inhale before you can exhale. You must take before you can give. You must consume before you can produce. The extractive hemisphere is not evil. It is HALF THE BREATH.

The question is not whether you enter the extractive hemisphere. The question is your dwell time.

Dwell Time

Dwell time = the fraction of each revolution spent in each hemisphere.

Balanced 180° 180° 0 Equal breath. Inhale matches exhale. Dharma.

The equatorial node (L4) naturally dwells at 180°/180° — balanced breath. In this dwell-time model, the framework assigns the η = 0 limit to the equator: the latitude where the two hemispheres are equal. At any other latitude, the helix tilts toward one hemisphere or the other.

The Yellowstone Proof

When wolves were reintroduced to Yellowstone in 1995, the elk stopped overgrazing the riverbanks. Trees grew. Rivers shifted course. The entire ecosystem rebalanced — a trophic cascade triggered by the return of the apex predator.

The wolves did not command the elk. The wolves did not build institutions. The wolves were at L4 — apex predators with balanced dwell times, sovereign capability matched by sovereign coherence. Their PRESENCE in the ecosystem was sufficient to rebalance the population distribution.

The Cro-Magnon was the wolf of the pre-Anthropocene. φ = ν = 1. Full operator palette. Hunted, gathered, made cave art, buried their dead. The lion at the equator. The Golden Age was not a utopia — it was a trophic cascade maintained by enough equatorial nodes to keep the ecosystem in balance.

The wolf and the grass are both at P_node = Φ × V = 1. The wolf is not morally superior to the grass. The wolf has paid for deeper replicator layers (genotype + phenotype + extended phenotype + pack coordination) and therefore couples to scarcer, denser energy. The grass pays minimal Φ and couples to abundant, low-density solar energy. Both are perfectly centered on their own S².

The Cro-Magnon was at L4 not only because evolution had balanced them there, but because the species' altricial strategy had already been selected: born V-poor, culture-carried to the equator, then operating with generalized light-cone capacity. The wolf was precocial (born with V). The human was altricial (born with Φ-potential). Both reached L4, but through different metabolic paths. The human sits higher in the trophic hierarchy not because of greater worth, but because deeper replicator layers (memotype, egregorotype) permit access to coordination-energy that no wolf can reach.

The Garden of Eden is not a place. It is a population distribution. The distribution where enough apex nodes are at L4 to stabilise the extractive pressures of L1-L3. The Fall is not a sin — it is the southward drift: the moment ν scaled beyond comprehension (agriculture → surplus → specialisation → cities → institutions → extraction architecture), and the wolves left Yellowstone.
