ABSTRACT

Corollary C4 (Replicator Stack) identifies five replicator levels: Genotype → Phenotype → Extended Phenotype → Memotype → Egregorotype. This paper shows the stack is not a single ladder but two multiplicative chains that converge at the Extended Phenotype (D4), producing the framework's core equation at the replicator level:

V = Genotype × Phenotype × Environment (material stack: D2-3 × D3 × D4) Φ = Memotype × Egregorotype × Environment (informational stack: D5 × D5-limit institutional patterning × D4) P_node = Φ × V (the product of both stacks)

The Extended Phenotype — technology, tools, architecture, infrastructure — is the equatorial product where the two stacks meet. It is ⊙: finity itself, made of matter (V-side) and shaped by mind (Φ-side). The fire at the equator. The × operator instantiated in D4.

This decomposition generates a testable prediction for the Global Flourishing Study: V-items and Φ-items should form two distinct factors whose product predicts ektropy better than their sum.

I. THE TWO STACKS

1.1 The V-Stack (Material Replicators)

The viability side of P traces through biological replicators operating in the material domain:

V = G × Ph × E_material

  G  = Genotype    (D2-3)  — genetic endowment, biological blueprint
  Ph = Phenotype   (D3)    — expressed organism, body, health, capability
  E  = Environment (D4)    — material conditions, resources, geography

Each factor is multiplicative. Superior genes in a sick body produce low V. A healthy body in a resource-desert produces low V. Near-zero in any factor collapses the product — the Zero-Factor Catastrophe operating at the biological level.

V is what you ARE and what you HAVE. Body, health, skills, resources, material circumstances. The substrate that acts on D4.

1.2 The Φ-Stack (Informational Replicators)

The coherence side of P traces through informational replicators operating in the meaning domain:

Φ = M × Eg × E_informational

  M  = Memotype      (D5)  — personal meaning structures, beliefs, values, narrative
  Eg = Egregorotype  (D5-limit)  — collective standing waves, culture, institutions, traditions
  E  = Environment   (D4)  — informational conditions, access, freedom of thought

Each factor is multiplicative. Profound personal insight inside a captured egregore produces low Φ. A liberating culture imposed on a fragmented self produces low Φ. Near-zero in any factor collapses the product.

Φ is what you UNDERSTAND and what HOLDS you. Meaning, coherence, integration, the cultural field that shapes and is shaped by mind. The pattern that selects in D5.

1.3 Environment Appears in Both

Environment (D4) is the shared substrate — it appears in both stacks because D4 is where material and informational replicators coexist. A prison collapses both V (material constraint) and Φ (informational constraint). A library raises both (V: accessible resource; Φ: accessible meaning). This is why D4 institutions are so powerful — they multiply into both sides of P simultaneously.

II. THE EXTENDED PHENOTYPE IS THE FIRE

2.1 Where the Stacks Meet

The Extended Phenotype (D4) — tools, technology, architecture, infrastructure, cities — is not on either side. It is the product:

Extended Phenotype = V-stack × Φ-stack instantiated in D4
                   = material capability shaped by informational coherence
                   = ⊙ (finity: the product of • and ○)

A bridge is steel (V) shaped by engineering knowledge (Φ). A book is paper (V) shaped by meaning (Φ). A computer is silicon (V) shaped by mathematics (Φ). Technology is Zero-Sum Resolution Equation at the replicator level.

2.2 Fire is Not Metaphor

The × in Zero-Sum Resolution Equation has been identified as light — the electromagnetic field, D2 interaction (MF-280v2, A3). Technology literally runs on electromagnetic interaction: electrons in circuits, photons in fiber optics, electromagnetic fields in motors. The "fire" of technology is D2 (EM) mediating between V-substrate and Φ-pattern.

This is why Prometheus's gift is fire. Why Agni carries the sacrifice. Why the Vedic equation is Agni = the carrier between realms. The extended phenotype is the fire that carries capability (below) to coherence (above) and pattern (above) to matter (below).

2.3 The Equatorial Position

On S²: the V-stack lives in the southern hemisphere (V > Φ, approaching ○). The Φ-stack lives in the northern hemisphere (Φ > V, approaching •). The Extended Phenotype lives at the equator — |z| = 1, where Φ ≈ V, where P is maximised.

This is why L4 (the apex) is also the level of integrated agency — the agent who can wield technology (extended phenotype) effectively is the one whose V-stack and Φ-stack are in balance. The warrior with the bow. The craftsman with the tool. The scientist with the instrument.
