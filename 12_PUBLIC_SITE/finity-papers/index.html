Why the programme exists

The number system wears two faces:

  • Additive — centred on 0, ordered, the natural metric for translation and order.
  • Multiplicative — centred on 1, compactifiable to the Riemann sphere, the natural metric for scale, growth, and ratio.

Standard mathematics teaches the additive face and treats the multiplicative face as derivative. The Finity Papers programme makes precise a simple consequence: 1 is the unique positive fixed point of the reciprocal I(x) = 1/x, and on the Riemann sphere S² ≅ CP¹ with half-angle coordinates φ = cot(θ/2), ν = tan(θ/2) (so φ · ν = 1), the multiplicative centre x = 1 is the same locus as the equatorial maximum of the sphere's balance functional B = sin θ. The two charts are conjugate by the logarithm.

The framework names this centre finity — a third reification co-equal with 0 and ∞. The naming is [I] interpretive; the geometry is [A].

The five papers

Paper I — The Two Faces of Number

Tiering: [A] the elementary identities; [S] the two-chart framing; [I] the naming of 1 as finity.

Formalises the claim that the number system carries two complementary charts — an additive chart centred on 0 and a multiplicative chart centred on 1 — and shows they are conjugate by the logarithm. Proves the exact bridge between the sphere's balance functional B = sin θ and the multiplicative energy E = (log x)²: B = sech √E. The reciprocal is exactly the colatitude reflection θ ↦ π − θ on S².

Kill criterion: A published counterexample to B = sech √E, or a proof that the additive/multiplicative charts are not conjugate by log.

Paper II — Division By Zero As Category Correction

Tiering: [A] for the wheel-theoretic / wheel-fraction formalisation; [S] for the "category correction" reading; [I] for the framing as a moral-axiom anchor.

Argues that N/0 = ∞ is well-defined on the Riemann sphere and that the standard "indeterminate" reading is a category error of the additive chart exported into the multiplicative chart. The boundary "indeterminate" belongs to 0/0, not N/0. In log coordinates, log ∞ − log 0 is well-defined as +∞ (when direction is named).

Kill criterion: A consistent formalisation of N/0 as indeterminate across all charts, or a demonstration that the wheel-theoretic reading fails in the operational consequences.

Paper III — A Product Constraint As A Structural Ethic

Tiering: [S] for the product-form reading; [C] for any claim that this is more than a moral-axis diagnostic.

Reads the framework's η = 0 fence as the operational guard around the product constraint. The manifold claim is P∞ = φ · ν = 1; the lived-system application is P_node = Φ × V. Any system that drives either lived factor toward zero drives the relation toward collapse or runaway. The ethic "preserve the reciprocal relation" is the structural reading of "do not let either factor collapse."

Kill criterion: A demonstration that zero-on-either-side does not require the other to diverge, or a moral framework that survives such a divergence.

Paper IV — Φ × V In Lived Systems (Product Constraint Applied)

Tiering: [S] for the model-space/lived-space distinction; [A] for the empirical-coherence and empirical-viability definitions; [I] for the framework reading of flourishing as P_node = Φ × V.

Distinguishes P∞ (model-space, conserved on the sphere) from P_node = Φ × V (lived-space, varies for organisms/institutions/DACs/strategies). The product form is severe because zero on either side threatens the whole — and zero is empirically reachable in lived systems (a body can fail; an institution can lose coherence; a community can lose viability). The discipline is to keep both alive.

Kill criterion: A demonstration that the product form misdescribes flourishing, or that the model-space/lived-space distinction fails to hold empirically.

Paper V — A Steelman Of The Burrisphere Programme

Tiering: [I] for the steelmanning reading; [S] for the framework internal structure being steelmanned.

Argues the strongest case FOR the Burrisphere programme, in the form a sophisticated critic would give: that the {0, 1, ∞} architecture is a deep structural feature of multiplicative reality, not interpretive metaphor. Identifies the latitude assignment (specific colatitude values for L1–L7) as the I-tier design choice constrained by S-tier geometry, and the equivocation "feminine = extractive" as the most dangerous failure mode.

Kill criterion: A demonstration that the steelman is internally incoherent, or that the strongest-case reading is not what the framework actually delivers.

The SUDA convergence

The Finity Papers owe a structural debt to Minoru Suda (2025, Parts I–III), whose Fractional Structure and Möbius Transformation independently derived the reciprocal-symmetry apparatus: the three equivalent charts (multiplicative x, additive s = log x, bounded-hinge u = (x−1)/(x+1)), the inversion-invariant energy E = (log x)², and the twist index τ = sign(log x). The framework's contribution is the naming of 1 as finity, the coupling of the multiplicative chart to the additive chart, and the bridge to a balance functional on S² — not new theorems, but new reading.

Convergence vs precedence. The framework's underlying ideas ({0, 1, ∞} as boundary-frames, the coinage "finity," the emblem 1 = 0 × ∞) were set out by Yves R. Burri in 2024. Suda's 2025 work is read as an independent convergence — corroboration, not a precedence claim in either direction. [I] epistemic heuristic, not a result.
