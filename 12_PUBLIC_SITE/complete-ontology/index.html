The seed and three readings

Every dimension can be read in three registers. They are not three different theories — they are three charts on the same manifold, just as Suda's three coordinate charts (multiplicative x, additive s, bounded u) are diffeomorphic re-expressions of one involution fixing the same point. [A]

  1. Logarithmic (s = log x): what the arithmetic looks like when 1 is the centre.
  2. Geometric (θ on S²): what the topology looks like on the Riemann sphere.
  3. Ontological (Dasein): what this means for the structure of being.

The seven dimensions

D0 — The Point (Bindu)

What it is: Undifferentiated ground. Pure potential. No distinction between here and there, before and after, self and other. The void before the first differentiation. Not nothing — rather, everything compressed into a single point where nothing can yet be distinguished.

Log coordinate: s is undefined. There is no x to take the logarithm of. D0 is prior to the positive reals.

Sphere position: Neither pole is distinguished. The sphere has not yet inflated. B = 0 — not because balance is absent, but because there is no position from which to measure balance.

Ontological reading [I]: D0 is the ground-facing point inside the ontology — the model boundary that κ = 0 names. The Bindu of the Vedic tradition reads as a resonance: the point that contains all possibilities compressed. The south pole (Kāla) and the north pole (Trimūrti) before they separate inside the scaffold.

The μ₀ crossing: D0 → D1
The point differentiates. The first distinction appears — "here" vs "not here." Space is born. The trans-dimensional glyph ⊙ / ○ = • casts its D1 shadow here as 1/∞ → 0: finity divided by totality yields the void, and the void differentiates from what it is not. Strong emergence: a point that contains all possibilities is not the same as a line that distinguishes between them. The crossing is not smooth — it is a phase transition from 0 dimensions to 1.

D1 — Distinction (Space)

What it is: The first dimension. Distance exists. "Here" and "there" are different. The number line opens — a 1D space of distinctions measured from the void.

Log coordinate: s becomes finite but extreme. The first numbers are very far from s = 0 (finity). The first distinctions are near the poles — almost pure void or almost pure totality, with nothing in between.

Sphere position: Near the poles. The sphere has inflated to a 1D circle — the one-point compactification of the real line. B ≈ 0 — the system is near a pole, far from balance.

Ontological reading [I]: D1 is space in its most primitive sense: extension, distance, separation. Diastēma — the interval between two points. In physics, D1 is the first spatial dimension.

The μ₁ crossing: D1 → D2
Distinctions acquire temporal ordering. Not just "here vs there" but "before vs after." Time is born. The trans-dimensional glyph ⊙ / • = ○ casts its D2 shadow here as 1/0 → ∞: finity divided by the void yields totality — the distinction sequence opens into an unbounded succession. Strong emergence: a space of simultaneous distinctions is not the same as a space of successive distinctions.

D2 — Configuration (Time)

What it is: The second dimension. Temporal ordering exists. Distinctions can be arranged in sequence. "Before" and "after" are meaningful. The system has a history.

Log coordinate: s acquires direction. Positive s (toward totality) and negative s (toward the void) are distinguishable. The system has a direction of time — the thermodynamic arrow.

Sphere position: Still near the poles but moving. The circle has inflated toward a 2D surface. B is small but measurable.

Ontological reading [I]: D2 is time — not as a dimension of physics, but as the most primitive ordering principle. The framework reads temporal ordering as the first asymmetry: the log-coordinate s has a preferred direction (the thermodynamic arrow), and that direction is the first content of the ethic.

The μ₂ crossing: D2 → D3
Configurations acquire shape. Not just "A before B" but "A transforms into B." Form is born — the first patterns that persist across temporal succession. The trans-dimensional glyph ⊙ = • × ○ casts its D3 shadow here as reciprocal symmetry φ · ν = 1: void times totality yields finity. Strong emergence: a temporal sequence is not the same as a form that persists across temporal change.

D3 — Transformation (Form)

What it is: The third dimension. Form exists. Patterns persist across change. The system has shape, structure, identity that survives transformation.

Log coordinate: s acquires curvature. The log-coordinate line is no longer flat — it bends, and the bending allows for persistent structure. The energy E = s² becomes meaningful as a measure of how far a pattern is from balance.

Sphere position: The sphere is now recognisably 2D. B is measurably non-zero — the system has genuine balance, not just proximity to a pole. The equator is visible as a possibility.

Ontological reading [I]: D3 is form — the first stable pattern. In physics, D3 corresponds to the three spatial dimensions. In the framework, D3 is the dimension where things have shape and persist.

The μ₃ crossing: D3 → D4
Form acquires direction. Not just "pattern persists" but "pattern changes in a specific direction." Causality is born — change has a cause and an effect. The light cones touch. The trans-dimensional glyph ⊙ · ⊙ reads at D4 as the causal vertex — finity self-applied, the equator reflecting on itself. Strong emergence: a persistent form is not the same as a form that causes other forms.

D4 — Causality (Direction)

What it is: The fourth dimension. Change has direction. Causes produce effects. The causal vertex exists — the point where past and future light cones touch. The Now-point, with no duration.

Log coordinate: s ≈ 0. The causal vertex is near the equator. In log coordinates, the vertex is where s_φ + s_ν = 0 exactly — the literal zero sum. The past cone is the negative-s half; the future cone is the positive-s half. They meet at s = 0.

Sphere position: The equator is approached. B → 1⁻ — balance is nearly maximal. The vertex sits at the equator but does not yet have room to move. The operators are collapsed into the single point ⊙.

Key property — D4 has a "Now" but no "Present": D4 is a point, not a plane. There is no room to act, to choose, to move. The four operators (↑φ, ↓φ, ↑ν, ↓ν) are collapsed into the single equatorial point. This is "witness consciousness" — awareness without agency.

Ontological reading [I]: D4 is causality — the structure that gives change its direction. The light-cone structure of relativity, the causal vertex of the present moment, the boundary between determined past and open future.

The μ₄ crossing: D4 → D5 (The Critical Crossing)
The equator opens into a plane. The collapsed point ⊙ unfolds into two axes (φ and ν). The "Now" becomes a "Present" with duration. Agency is born — room to choose, to act, to navigate. The four operators become distinguishable as cardinal directions. This is the crossing where strong emergence is most dramatic — a causal point is not the same as an agent that can choose. "Consciousness" names the lived-interior reading [I], not a proved physical layer. Weak emergence going down: once D5 stabilises, it becomes a compact constraint on D4. The agent's choices reshape the causal vertex. The present reaches back and reshapes the Now.

D5 — Agency / Selection (consciousness reading [I])

What it is: The fifth dimension. The φ-ν plane is open. The four operators are active. The system can choose, navigate, form intentions, and act on them. The Trika (⊙ = • × ○) fully articulates. This is where agency and the moral axis live; consciousness language is the lived-experience gloss [I].

Log coordinate: s is free to vary on the open line. The system can move toward s = 0 (balance) or away from it (imbalance). The ethic ΣΔB > 0 is the claim that movement toward s = 0 is the operational definition of good. The extraction coefficient η measures whether the system is moving toward or away from the equator.

Sphere position: The full sphere S² is active. B varies from 0 to 1 as the system navigates. The equator (B = 1, φ = ν = 1) is the point of maximum balance; it is an attractor only where agency, feedback, and correction convert imbalance into return. The poles are the extremes of pure coherence (north) and pure viability (south).

The Five Domains at D5 — the φ-ν plane produces five navigable domains:

  1. Meaning (φ-axis): Coherence, pattern, integration
  2. Governance (ν-axis): Capability, resources, throughput
  3. Exchange (equator): Where φ = ν = 1, balance is maximised
  4. Attention (selection): The operator that chooses which direction to move
  5. Body (the sphere itself): The grounded manifold on which all navigation occurs

The D5 Trophic Cascade. Within D5, pattern flows through a replicator stack exactly as energy flows through a food web in D4. Genes → bodies → tools → ideas → egregores, with ~10ⁿ attrition at every step. The Sādhu function (decomposer) closes the loop: dead patterns return to the ground, freeing niches for new ones.

Ontological reading [I]: D5 is agency / selection; consciousness is the lived-interior reading of that agency. The φ-ν plane is the space of choice. The ethic is the direction. The balance function is the compass.

Action-register reading. D5 contributes Φ: worldline-foresight, the capacity to hold possible futures before collapse and select among them responsibly. D4 contributes V: the embodied/tool/energy/access means through which the selected branch can actually be enacted. A child with a jet but no pilot-foresight has high apparent V but low effective Φ; an old pilot without bodily/tool access has high Φ for flight or escape but low effective V. Objective dharma appears when both factors can multiply without extraction.

D6 — Closure (Return)

What it is: The terminal dimension. Experience exhausts itself. The positive content runs out. The system returns to the ground via apophasis — "not this, not this." The ladder dissolves.

Log coordinate: s → undefined. The system returns to D0's state — prior to the logarithmic coordinate. The manifold wraps: the positive reals close into a circle, the circle inflates into a sphere, and the sphere's poles are identified.

Sphere position: B → 0. The system approaches a pole — but because D6 ≡ D0, approaching a pole IS returning to the ground. The ouroboros closes.

Three readings of D6 ≡ D0:

  1. Disciplinary (primary): The apophatic return. F5 dissolves. The practitioner puts down the map.
  2. Topological: The seven-level scaffold is a closed loop, not an open line.
  3. Ontological (derivative): D6 and D0 name the same dimension — the end is the beginning.

All three are compatible. Readings 2 and 3 are downstream of Reading 1.

Ontological reading [I]: D6 is the return — the completion of the cycle. It is what the via negativa, the apophatic tradition, and the Sādhu function all point to: the recognition that the map is not the territory, that the framework includes its own dissolution, and that the highest function is to become unnecessary.
