Kill criteria · fired 2026-07-13

The Rosetta must be allowed to weaken — and it has.

If the seven-row alignment is shown to be forced, or if new strong domains break the alignment rather than refine it, the Rosetta weakens or falls. A public canon has to preserve that failure mode.

A formal audit found the criterion has, in fact, fired: traditions outside the polarity-encoding family (Mesoamerican, Buddhist, Daoist) do not fit the seven-row grid, the count "7" is conceded a design choice, and narrowing the claim to "polarity-encoding traditions" is the falsification, not survival. The table is honest as a cross-domain lens; it is not a discovered universal. Scoped accordingly, pending K2.

What the algebra does rescue (adjudication 2026-07-13). The objection that the seven are algebraically forced was tested and half-upheld. The mirror-ladder grammar is a genuine algebraic object — the inversion z↦1/z forces a fixed centre and mirror pairs; the sign square forces four moves — and owes nothing to any tradition; "curated relatives" undersells it. But the algebra forces an odd ladder (3/5/7/9…) and four moves, not seven: "seven" is a 4 + 3 stitch of movers to boundary-frames, and by the corpus's own order-of-inference the operators were abduced from the rows — so they cannot be independent evidence for them. The shape is real; the census of seven and the convergence-as-proof are not.

Named confound · new 2026-07-13

The cleanest column's "seven" is the seven planets.

The Mithraic grades — this table's strongest-fitting initiatory column — are the seven classical planets (Corax→Pater = Mercury→Saturn). Their "seven" is the naked-eye sky, the same septenary that gives seven days and seven metals, diffused across one Hellenistic-Mediterranean-Vedic family. So the witness that agrees best has its number over-determined by astronomy, not discovered from the balance-ladder — and the traditions that don't fit are exactly the ones outside that family. Independence and fit run opposite: the fingerprint of shared descent, not independent discovery. Named here per A7 — a fired confound the corpus had not surfaced.
