The first frame

The long human recognition.

Before the geometry, before the sphere, the perennial traditions had already recognized what Emergentism tries to formalize. I resonance, not A identity. The framework reads as a *thin perennialism* — a grammar that recovers what the traditions have always said, without claiming to be them.

The convergence is real but not original

The framework's load-bearing ideas — the {0,1,∞} Titans as boundary-frames, the coinage of finity, the emblem ⊙ = • × ○ — were arrived at independently in 2024. Minoru Suda's 2025 PhilArchive trilogy on fractional structure and Möbius transformation later converged on the same multiplicative face with formal rigour. Citing Suda is for the formalisation; priority on the ideas remains the framework's 2024 statement. This is the framework's own three-paths-to-the-summit epistemology: a later independent convergence is evidence the structure is real, not a precedence claim against it.

What the framework does NOT claim

The framework does not claim to have discovered the perennial recognition. It claims to have rediscovered a particular grammar for it, and to be working in convergent resonance with the traditions, not in priority. Reading the framework as "the perennial tradition" would be a kind of capture; reading it as a particular I reading that resonates with several I readings is the honest position.

The perennial frame: convergence without capture. Resonance without identity.
