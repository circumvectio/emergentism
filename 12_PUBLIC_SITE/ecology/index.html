The non-collapse rule

Four questions.
Four coordinates.

01 · vertical ecology

Trophic stratum

Where does matter and energy flow? Producers capture external energy; primary, secondary and tertiary consumers obtain biomass and usable energy through lower strata; scavengers and decomposers return matter across the web. Trophic position is not dignity or moral rank.

02 · persistence

Replicator layer

What persists and reproduces—code, regulation, body, effects, ideas, or collective traces? Replicator depth is not a licence to consume a shallower human layer.

03 · human function

Rosetta row

Which complementary function is operating? People may specialize, train, combine, or change functions. Rows are cooperating organs of equal worth, never hereditary classes.

04 · reproduction

Mating domain

Where is sexual selection operating? Mate choice and competition are bounded to a declared reproductive domain. They do not authorize economic, political, or social predation.

The ant analogy stops at architecture.

Ant societies show differentiated functions within one colony. They do not establish genetic equivalence with human roles, hereditary assignment, compulsory specialization, or unequal human worth.

Three registers · no bare η

Balance, action,
and domain.

η_ratio = 1 · balance inside a declared trophic model
η_move = 0 · constitutional fence for a human or institutional action
η_domain · bearers + payer + beneficiary + baseline + horizon
+ consent + reversibility + regenerative capacity

The relational fence refuses hidden, decoupled, or ground-negating arrangements. It does not pretend that organisms live without metabolism. A trophic flow can be coupled while a human arrangement remains unjust; the registers answer different questions.

Power-Max · selected corollary [I/C]

Cooperate across functions.
Compete only where typed.

Seek cooperation within the same trophic stratum or functional guild and among complementary human functions. This may widen durable options through specialization and trust. It is not a theorem: defection, free riding, dependency, bottlenecks, and capture are serious rivals.

PEERS

Cooperation is the default strategy.

Compare it honestly against the strongest rival over the same horizon, resources, and bearer set.

MATING

Competition is bounded.

Sexual selection supplies a reproductive-domain exception, never a general social or economic rule.

EXIT

Differentiation remains changeable.

A human role must permit training, combination, movement, contest, and exit. Birth cannot settle worth.

Kundalini → Tantra → Vajrayana

Turn rivalry into
disciplined power?

The Kundalini/serpent correspondence is symbolic `[I]`. Tantra as redirection of sexual drive into attention, restraint, cooperation, and durable power is a historical/practice claim plus testable hypothesis `[I/C]`. Vajrayana's poison-to-path framing is an interpretive practice analogy `[I/C]`. No special endocrine, chakra, or physiological power effect is established here.

What would distinguish it?

Pre-registered longitudinal comparison with matched controls and ordinary attentional training, using behavioral outcomes, visible costs, adverse events, and independent custody. A null result or stronger simpler rival defeats the domain claim.
