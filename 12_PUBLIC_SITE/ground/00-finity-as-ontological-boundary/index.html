Why This Document Exists

L5 already teaches the discovery of finity as a positive Trinity surface:

0  -> absence
∞  -> totality
1  -> finite presence / relation

L6 must read the same recognition differently.

At L5, finity is a model that can be taught. At L6, finity is the boundary exposed when every model of the finite is subtracted.

So this note stabilizes the sentence:

The discovery of finity is as profound as zero and infinity, but it is not a possession of the Ground. It is the recognition that presence is relational and finite disclosure is where Being becomes sayable. [I/S]

The Register Boundary

Keep three registers distinct:

Register What may be said Tier
Mathematical background The Riemann sphere compactifies the complex plane by adding a point at infinity; 0, finite values, and ∞ can be handled on one extended surface. [A] when stated conventionally
Framework structure Zero-Sum Resolution Equation and φ · ν = 1 on S² read finity as reciprocal closure inside the model. [S] inside the formalism
Ontological reading Presence is not brute fact but disclosed relation; the finite witness is where Being becomes sayable. [I/S]

Do not present the framework's 0 × ∞ = 1 compression as ordinary field arithmetic. It is the model's symbolic / coordinate grammar for closure, not a license to flatten mathematical registers.

Create: Finite Presence Is Relation

The L6 creation move is not making a new object. It is allowing a missing dimension to appear.

The missing dimension is finity:

absence alone      -> no disclosure
totality alone     -> no boundary
finite relation    -> disclosure can occur

Finity says that presence is not merely a third item beside zero and infinity. It is the relational site where absence and totality can become sayable without either one being possessed.

This is why the L6 core state names Dasein as the finite witness-agent:

Ground -> Being -> Teleological Force -> Dasein -> beings

Dasein is not Being itself. Dasein is the finite ⊙ in whom Being is encountered, questioned, and enacted.

Stabilize: The Dimensions of Finity

At L6, "ontology is the dimensions" means the Sadhu asks what dimensions are revealed by finitude itself.

Finity reveals:

  • Boundary: a finite thing can appear because it is not everything.
  • Disclosure: a finite witness can encounter Being because it is situated.
  • Mortality: a finite site cannot claim infinite possession.
  • Measure: finite presence can be compared, weighed, balanced, and tested.
  • Relation: the finite is not isolated substance; it stands through relation.
  • Responsibility: because the witness is finite, action must be signed, tiered, and corrigible.
  • Release: because finity is not the Ground, the framework can be put down.

This is the grand L6 move: finity is not less profound than zero or infinity. It is the condition under which zero and infinity can be encountered without becoming idols.

Destroy: False Finity

The Sādhu operation destroys false readings:

  1. Finity as human supremacy. The finite witness is not a rank above other beings. It is the site of disclosure at a scale, not a human-worth hierarchy.
  2. Finity as mathematical overclaim. The framework's closure grammar is not ordinary arithmetic proof that 0 × ∞ = 1 in every register.
  3. Finity as final doctrine. If the sentence "finity is the answer" becomes an idol, L6 subtracts it.
  4. Finity as positive D6 science. D6 remains apophatic closure, not one more public register.
  5. Finity as escape from embodiment. Finitude includes mortality, body, dependency, and limit. It is not abstract purity.

The clean destruction rule:

If finity is possessed, it has been reified.
If it is reified, it has fallen below L6.

The L6 Compression

Zero names absence.
Infinity names totality.
Finity names disclosed relation.
Being is closure.
Dasein is finite disclosure.
The Ground remains prior.

Or shorter:

Finity is the recognition that presence is relational, finite, and non-possessive. It lets Being become sayable without letting the saying become the Ground.
