2. Why Core State Here Is Axiomatic by Design

Positive core state says: "Reality is X." Axiomatic core state says: "Reality is not exhausted by any X."

The framework uses positive structures — φ · ν = 1 on S², the four-line canonical block, the Three-Stage Process, the Rosetta — but it does not identify any of these with the ground. The structures are maps. The ground is what maps are made of and what maps cannot capture.

The design logic:

  1. Any framework that claims positive possession of the ground has already mistaken the map for the territory.
  2. Any framework that refuses all structure has collapsed into nihilism — which is also a positive claim (the claim that nothing can be said).
  3. The middle path: use structures honestly, tag their evidence tier, and build in the self-dissolving exit.

This is why D6 is axiomatic closure, not a higher positive register. D5 is the summit of what can be said. D6 is the recognition that saying does not exhaust the said. The public science stack closes at D5. D6 is Falsification Protocol.

5. The Practice: Direct Access, No Levels Required

The sitting practice (00_THE_SITTING_PRACTICE.md) is the framework's most important document for one reason:

The ground is accessible without the framework.

The framework calls this φ = 1, the equator, the ground perceived from the inside. But those are after-images — names given after the fact to what does not need names.

6. Decentralized Authority Model: The Framework Succeeds by Becoming Unnecessary

Decentralized Authority Model (packet 139) is the doctrine that the highest product of this framework is its own dissolution.

The name is deliberate:

  • Brahmanism: the tendency to turn insight into institution, practice into gatekeeping, liberation into law.
  • Decentralized Authority Model: the active refusal of that tendency. The prefix "A-" is the Sanskrit privative — non-, un-, without.

Decentralized Authority Model is not anti-structure. It is the discipline that lets structure be useful without becoming a cage. The framework builds lattices — Rosetta, Three-Stage Process, VMOSK-A, P-scores — and then insists, at every lattice joint, that the lattice is not the ground.

Three signatures of Decentralized Authority Model in the framework:

  1. Self-dissolving exit. The terminal instruction: if you can access φ directly, put this down. The framework that requires its own perpetuation has failed.
  2. K4 / Grace Exit. Every participant can leave with what is theirs — their legitimate property and data. The organism that traps its members has become a cage.
  3. η = 0. Zero extraction. Not merely a moral rule — the enforced non-extraction profile the framework uses to keep the equatorial game stable. An organism that extracts from its participants is drifting toward the south-pole action reading, but real systems require coupling, monitoring, due process, exit, and enforcement for that profile to hold.

The framework's measure of success is not adoption. The framework's measure of success is whether people who encounter it eventually no longer need it.

Tier: [I] — Decentralized Authority Model as a normative stance is interpretive. [S] — η = 0 and K4 are structurally built into the formalism.
