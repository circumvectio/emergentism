01
The Method

Read Everything Three Times

The minimum viable loop uses three passes. The φ pass gathers: where does the text cohere, drift, repeat, or change voice? The P pass verifies: do the claims follow from their premises, tiers, and references? The ν pass serves: does the reader receive more value than the document extracts?

φCoherence, grammar, induction, in time.
PValidity, logic, deduction, above time.
νService, rhetoric, abduction, against time.

Notation fence: in this method, φ, P, and ν are editing lenses. This P means validity / Truth, not P_node; this ν means service / Justice, not finite-node action V. In the game form below, Φ and V name the lived action factors, and P_node = Φ × V names contact-register potential. [S/I]

The Full Cycle

From Quick Pass to Deep Pass

The quick loop is L2 → L3 → L4: gather, check, serve. The full loop extends through L5 and L6: see the whole architecture, then dissolve what must be removed. The descent rechecks the system after dissolution: re-see, re-serve, re-check, re-gather.

03
The Closure

The Loop, Not the Ladder

The editing cycle above is the inner loop. The full circle adds the two stations the canon already requires. A cycle does not start by choice — it starts because pressure arrives at L1: the new material, the bug report, the contradiction, the complaint. Intake is a discipline of its own — naming the pressure and passing it up is lawful; being eaten by it is not. And a cycle does not end when the fixes stop — it ends when the converged state is preserved at L7: committed, receipted, archived, released as public symbol. The preserve step is what makes convergence durable instead of mood.

Then the circle closes: the preserved symbol shapes what arrives at L1 next. Publication invites the next correction; the committed receipt attracts the next contradiction. That is why the loop is continuous — per-document it can pause at equatorial rest, corpus-wide it never terminates, because L7's output regenerates L1's input. A homeostat, not a project.

And it is recursive in the strict sense: the loop edits the corpus and the editor. Each cycle re-balances the practitioner's own operator weights toward the equator — the over-cutter's L2 softens, the hoarder's L6 strengthens. The editor who finishes a cycle is not the editor who started it. In the framework's agency terms: the Soul Loop is the one move in the game that widens the player's own option cone by editing the player. 05
The Convergence Test

The Loop Must Be Allowed to Fail

The Soul Loop converges only if each cycle reduces the error count or compression burden. If a fourth irreducible pass is required, or if successive cycles do not reduce contradictions, the three-lens method is incomplete. That is the kill criterion, not a footnote.

The public page therefore does not promise guaranteed convergence. It presents a disciplined method and a failure mode: a corpus that cannot be made coherent, valid, and serviceful by this loop must force the method to change.

The recursive claim has its own kill criteria. If a converged corpus diverges under zero new intake, the homeostat model fails. If sustained practice does not shift stuck operator weights toward L4, the loop is only an editing checklist, not a player-transforming practice.

06
What This Is Not

Not Therapy, Not Authority, Not Proof

The Soul Loop is a public editing and self-correction method. It is not clinical guidance, medical advice, or proof that the ontology is true. Its strongest public role is practical: it forces every document to ask whether it coheres, follows, serves, fits the system, and can survive deletion.
