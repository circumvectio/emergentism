Compute envelope

Every run has a hard edge.

A contribution is valid only when purpose, cost, tools, data, publication, expiry and revocation are explicit before execution.

AUTHORITY

Who and why

Principal, declared purpose, question identifier, permitted providers and attribution.

  • No donor truth authority
  • No hidden secondary purpose
  • No application outside scope
COST

What may be spent

Maximum currency spend, tokens, runs, rate, models and expiry.

  • Hard server-side limits
  • Visible cost receipt
  • Automatic stop at exhaustion
CUSTODY

What may be seen

Allowed sources, data classification, tools, retention and deletion route.

  • No secrets in prompts or logs
  • No arbitrary public tool execution
  • No undisclosed training use
CONSEQUENCE

What must be published

Prompt and model provenance, outputs, disagreements, failures, costs and review status.

  • Nulls remain visible
  • Sponsor cannot suppress results
  • Revocation stops future runs
