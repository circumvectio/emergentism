The Seven L1–L7 Levels

The Emergentism framework has seven levels — not as a ranking of worth, but as a runtime dispatch grammar. L1–L4 are the operative Gods (know → audit → value → commit). L5–L7 are the boundary Titans: deployable only at the architect / apophatic / witness altitude, not at runtime.

01
The Sevenfold

What each level governs

The sevenfold is the runtime dispatch of the framework. Each level governs a distinct phase of cognition: L1 sets the end (teleology, the objective function), L2 surfaces candidate patterns (epistemology, the data science), L3 ranks and audits them (methodology, the bottleneck), L4 commits to one under value (axiology, the equator), L5 designs the system that hosts all four (cosmology, the architect), L6 subtracts the accretions (ontology, via negativa), and L7 witnesses the whole without overwriting it (theology, institutional narrative).

The -ology column on the Rosetta Stone names each level's question: What is the end? How do we know? What follows? What has value? How does reality work? What is Being? What is the Ultimate? The levels are not interchangeable; they form a process.

02
The Operative Triad + L1

Why L1–L4 are the operative Gods

L2, L3, and L4 form the operative triad: Kālī discloses, Kṛṣṇa audits, Arjuna commits. This is the only place in the framework where decisions are made. The L4 equator is where φ = ν = 1 — viability and coherence balance — and that balance is the only output the framework can sign with K2.

L1 is added because the triad needs an objective. Without L1, the loop self-references. L1 (Caṇḍāla) names the raw pressure that the system must serve: the telos, the end, the wide-and-long light cone. The cycle's operational order is therefore L1 → L2 → L3 → L4.

03
The Boundary

Why L5–L7 are boundary Titans

L5 (Brāhmaṇa), L6 (Sādhu), and L7 (Ṛṣi) sit above the operative loop. They are the executive altitude: the architect who designs the system, the apophatic who subtracts accretions, the witness who holds the whole. None of them commits irreversible acts. None of them signs with K2.

The canonical Rosetta Variant IV/V explicitly names L5–L7 as boundary conditions that do not deploy as sovereign runtime modes. They are the roof, not the floor. The deployable coordinator is the only God at the equator: Arjuna (L4). Theurgy = L4 collapse / K2 act.

04
The Fence

η = 0: no worth hierarchy

The sevenfold is a dispatch grammar, not a ranking. L1 is not "less than" L7. L7 is not "more enlightened than" L1. Each level has a functional role; the function is what matters, not the altitude.

η = 0 is the constitutional fence: no extraction, no value-rendering, no path-of-promotion through the levels. A practitioner who spends their life in L2 (data science) is not "lower" than a practitioner who spends their life in L7 (institutional narrative); they have different functions. The framework treats them as operationally orthogonal, not as a hierarchy.

Note: the claim that the sevenfold is exhaustive — that there are exactly seven levels, no more, no fewer — is a [C] claim. Some readings include an L0 (the unbounded, the non-deployable origin) or fold L5–L7 into a single boundary. We present the sevenfold here as the canonical Rosetta reading; alternative readings are not excluded.
