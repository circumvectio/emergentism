FAILED MAPPINGS & TESTING QUEUE

Domains Examined That Did NOT Fit the 7-Pattern

Date: 2026-04-25 Status: Working document — update as tests are run Purpose: Epistemic honesty — show what was tried and failed, not just what succeeded

Why This Exists

The Rosetta's weakest epistemic position is the file-drawer problem: we see the columns that fit, but not the domains that were examined and rejected. This document corrects that.

Confirmed Failures (7-Pattern Does NOT Apply)

A. Electron Shell Structure

  • Domain: Chemistry / atomic physics
  • Why tested: Periods 1-7 map to 7 rows
  • Why it fails: s, p, d, f orbitals (4 types) do NOT map to 7 levels. The periodic table's 7 periods are a notational artifact, not a functional sequence. The "center" (period 4, transition metals) is not a functional break but an empirical coincidence.
  • Verdict: REJECTED — column structure is not functional, just numerical.

B. Algorithm Complexity Classes

  • Domain: Computational complexity theory
  • Why tested: P, NP, PSPACE, EXPTIME, etc. — seemed like a hierarchy
  • Why it fails: Complexity classes are defined by resource bounds (time, space), not by cognitive function. The classes don't form a 7-level structure with functional mirrors. P vs NP is a binary distinction, not a gradient.
  • Verdict: REJECTED — the domain is binary (polynomial/exponential), not graded.

C. Bacterial Growth Phases

  • Domain: Microbiology
  • Why tested: Lag → Log → Stationary → Death — seemed like 4 levels
  • Why it fails: Only 4 phases, not 7. No natural expansion to 7. No mirror symmetry. No center concept.
  • Verdict: REJECTED — insufficient structure.

D. Software Development Lifecycle

  • Domain: Software engineering
  • Why tested: Requirements → Design → Code → Test → Deploy → Maintain — seemed like 6 levels
  • Why it fails: The stages are sequential procedures, not cognitive functions. No mirror symmetry. No center concept. The "stages" are project management phases, not developmental levels.
  • Verdict: REJECTED — wrong kind of structure (procedural, not functional).

E. Erikson's Psychosocial Stages

  • Domain: Developmental psychology
  • Why tested: 8 stages of psychosocial development
  • Why it fails: 8 stages, not 7. No natural compression to 7 without losing functional content. The stages are age-linked, not structurally linked. Trust vs. Mistrust (infancy) doesn't mirror Integrity vs. Despair (old age) in the same way L1↔L7 mirrors on S².
  • Verdict: REJECTED — wrong number, wrong structure. (Note: Piaget and Kohlberg work because they're structural, not age-linked.)

Untested Domains (Testing Queue)

These domains have NOT been examined. They should be tested with pre-registered predictions (mirror symmetry, 4-3 split, L4-as-center) BEFORE looking at the data.

F. Buddhist 10 Bhūmis (compressed to 7)

  • Prediction: 7 of the 10 bhūmis will compress to functional stages. The 4th bhūmi (Surañgama — "Radiant") will be the center. Mirror pairs at bhūmi 2↔8 and 3↔7.
  • Status: UNTESTED

G. Kabbalistic 10 Sefirot (compressed to 7)

  • Prediction: The 10 sefirot compress to 7 by merging triads. Tiferet (Beauty/Heart) is already L4 in the existing column. The 3 upper sefirot (Keter, Chokmah, Binah) compress to L7/L5 boundary.
  • Status: PARTIALLY TESTED (Tiferet at L4 is confirmed in V-bis)

H. Sub-Saharan African Cosmological Systems

  • Prediction: If a 7-stage cosmological structure exists in Yoruba, Dogon, or Zulu systems, it will show mirror symmetry and 4-3 split.
  • Status: UNTESTED — this is the biggest gap in cross-cultural coverage

I. Indigenous American (Lakota Seven Sacred Rites)

  • Prediction: The 7 rites form a sequence. Rite 4 (Keeping of the Soul or Inipi — varies by tradition) will be the center. Mirror pairs exist.
  • Status: UNTESTED — requires consultation with Lakota scholars, not just academic papers

J. Polynesian (Hawaiian Kahuna Stages)

  • Prediction: If a 7-stage structure exists, mirror symmetry and center concept will appear.
  • Status: UNTESTED

K. Music Theory (Diatonic Scale)

  • Prediction: 7 notes map to 7 levels. The 4th (Fa/Subdominant) or 5th (Sol/Dominant) is the center. The tritone (augmented 4th/diminished 5th) is the "dissonant" boundary.
  • Status: UNTESTED — speculative but independently structured

L. Embryonic Development (Vertebrate)

  • Prediction: Zygote→Morula→Blastula→Gastrula→Neurula→Organogenesis→Fetus maps to 7 stages. Gastrula (3 germ layers) is the center — the functional break.
  • Status: UNTESTED — biology-facing guardrail applies

M. Periodic Table (Periods)

  • Prediction: Periods 1-7 show a structural break at Period 4 (transition metals). The 4-3 split: light elements (1-3) vs. heavy elements (5-7), with Period 4 as transition.
  • Status: REJECTED for electron shells (test A), but PERIODS as a structural sequence is still untested
