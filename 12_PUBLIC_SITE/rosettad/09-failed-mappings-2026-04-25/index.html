0. Why Failed Mappings Matter

The strongest move an empirical framework can make is to report its own negative results.

If the Rosetta only includes domains that fit, it is a file-drawer exercise — the pattern-matching equivalent of only publishing successful trials. If the Rosetta includes domains that were examined and failed, it becomes a research instrument.

This document is the immune system. It is Kālī cutting false coherence to recover viability.

1. Daoist 內丹 (Internal Alchemy) — FAILED 7-Stage Test

Attribute Finding
Tradition Daoist internal alchemy (nèidān)
Canonical stage count 3 stages (refining essence→qi→spirit→void) or 5 stages (Quanzhen school)
7-stage versions Exist in some later syncretic lineages but are interpretive elaborations, not canonical
Why it failed The foundational texts articulate 3 or 5 stages, not 7. The 7-stage versions are secondary.
What DOES map The operator grammar (jīng→qì→shén→xū→Dao as coherence-viability gradient) holds regardless of stage count.
Verdict 7-stage claim FAILS. Operator-grammar claim HOLDS.
Source basis Quanzhen lineage texts; scholarly consensus (Kohn, Komjathy)

2. Buddhist 10 Bhūmis — FAILED Clean 7-Collapse Test

Attribute Finding
Tradition Mahāyāna bodhisattva path (Daśabhūmika Sūtra)
Canonical stage count 10 bhūmis
7-collapse attempts Multiple non-unique mappings proposed (equator at bhūmi 4 vs. bhūmi 7)
Why it failed The 10→7 collapse is interpretively ambiguous. The tradition itself emphasizes the bhūmi-7 pivot (effort→spontaneity), which does not align with the framework's L4 equator.
What DOES map The operator gradient (progressive refinement of śīla, dhyāna, prajñā) is clear. The stage count is not 7.
Verdict 7-stage claim FAILS. Operator-grammar claim HOLDS. Equator placement AMBIGUOUS.
Source basis Daśabhūmika Sūtra; Buddhist scholarly consensus

3. Sub-Saharan African Traditions — FAILED 7-Stage Test

Tradition Stage Count Finding
Yoruba Ifá 16 odu (basic), 256 odu meji (compound) Fundamental structure is 16, not 7.
Dogon Awa society ~60 grades (Griaule); disputed (van Beek) Either far richer than 7-grade or structurally disputed.
Bantu system architecture (Kagame) 4-fold (muntu/kintu/hantu/kuntu) 4-fold base, not 7.
!Kung/San healing Functional grades (n/om holders) Not a graded ascent; functional, not developmental.

Why they failed Sub-Saharan traditions exhibit different structural logics: 4-fold, 16-fold, functional, or disputed. None present a consistent 7-grade initiatory ascent.
What DOES map Some lineages show coherence-viability gradients (Bantu personhood development).
Verdict 7-stage claim FAILS across surveyed Sub-Saharan traditions.
Source basis Griaule 1948; van Beek 1991; Kagame; Ellis 1829

5. Confucian Self-Cultivation — FAILED 7-Stage Test

Attribute Finding
Tradition Confucian self-cultivation (Daxue 大學 — The Great Learning)
Canonical stage count 8 steps (eight items: investigating things, extending knowledge, sincerity, rectifying the heart, cultivating the person, regulating the family, governing the state, pacifying the world)
Why it failed The Daxue explicitly articulates 8 stages, not 7. Compression to 7 requires merging two stages, which is interpretive.
What DOES map The progression from personal virtue to social governance follows the L2→L4 causal chain. The stage count does not match.
Verdict 7-stage claim FAILS. Causal-chain logic PARTIALLY HOLDS.
Source basis Daxue (Book of Rites, ch. 42)

6. Yoruba Ifá Divination — FAILED 7-Stage Test

Attribute Finding
Tradition Yoruba Ifá divination system (Nigeria, Benin, diaspora)
Canonical stage count 16 principal odu; 256 odu meji (compound); then odu-kindreds
Why it failed The base structure is 16 (4²), not 7. The system is combinatorial, not linear-graded.
What DOES map Nothing in the Rosetta framework maps cleanly to a 16-fold combinatorial structure. The operator grammar (4 Gods + 3 Titans = 7) has no natural relationship to 16 odu.
Verdict 7-stage claim FAILS. Operator-grammar claim DOES NOT MAP. This is a genuine structural mismatch.
Source basis Bascom 1969; Abimbola 1976

7. Chemistry (Periodic Table) — FAILED Functional Mapping Test

Attribute Finding
Domain Chemistry — periodic table of elements
Structural feature 7 periods (rows) in the standard periodic table
Why it failed While there are 7 periods, the functional content of chemical periods does not map to the operator geometry. Period 4 contains transition metals (high chemical complexity) — a possible L4 analog — but Period 1 (H, He) is not a "boundary condition" in any meaningful sense parallel to L1/Kali.
What DOES map Period 4 as "center" is suggestive (transition metals are the most chemically versatile). But the rest of the mapping is forced.
Verdict 7-stage claim PARTIALLY HOLDS numerically but FUNCTIONALLY FAILS. The number 7 appears, but the rows are not functional mirrors.
Source basis IUPAC periodic table; standard chemistry

8. Biology (Embryonic Development) — FAILED 7-Stage Test

Attribute Finding
Domain Embryonic development (animal)
Canonical stages Zygote → Cleavage → Morula → Blastula → Gastrula → Neurula → Organogenesis → Fetus
Stage count 8 stages, not 7
Why it failed The standard sequence has 8 stages. Compression to 7 requires merging cleavage+morula or neurula+organogenesis, which is biologically arbitrary.
What DOES map The progression from simple to complex is real, but the stage boundaries are biological, not geometric. There is no "equator" stage where balance peaks.
Verdict 7-stage claim FAILS. Biological development does not map to operator geometry.
Source basis Standard developmental biology (Gilbert, Developmental Biology)

10. What This Means for the Framework

The honest claim

The 7-stage initiatory structure converges in Indo-European and Mediterranean traditions. Other traditions express coherence-viability gradients through different counts (3, 5, 8, 10, 16, varying) or non-graded functional structures. The operator grammar (4 mixed-sign Gods + 3 same-sign Executive boundary frames) is structurally exhaustive and appears across all surveyed traditions, but the specific 7-stage articulation is empirically Indo-European/Mediterranean, not universal.

Framework status

Operator grammar exhaustive HOLDS — no counter-example found
φ·ν = 1 on S² generates pattern [C] unchanged — not directly tested by this survey
Cross-cultural validity WEIRD bias confirmed — non-IE traditions show non-7 structures
Rosetta Stone falls? NO — the framework survives by honest scope-narrowing

The kill criterion is NOT triggered

The Rosetta's kill criterion (from 04_DEEP_RUMINATION_2026_04_25.md):

  1. A domain requires a fundamentally different number of levels — PARTIALLY OBSERVED. Multiple domains have non-7 counts. But these are not "fundamentally different" in a way that breaks the operator grammar. They are different resolutions of the same surface.
  2. The operator grammar proves non-exhaustive — NOT OBSERVED.
  3. The equator is not a global minimum — NOT OBSERVED.

Verdict: The framework survives. It is narrowed, not falsified.
