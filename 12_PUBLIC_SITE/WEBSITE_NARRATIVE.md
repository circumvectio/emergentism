**Purpose [I]:** This is the public narrative spine for emergentism.org. It turns the Rosetta
ladder into a visitor journey: D0 Ground of Finity -> light cone -> objective beauty -> the three Gods -> equatorial
decision -> Executive-boundary descent -> return. The public site must not start by reducing the
framework to D5/teleology; it starts at the D0 question of finity and then descends into L1-L7.

## 0. Overture: D0 Ground of Finity

**Public sentence [I]:** Finity is not a starting premise; it is the meeting point where nothing meets everything.

**Claim [S/I]:** Zero (`•`), Finity (`⊙`), and Infinity (`○`) are the three Titans of Numbers — boundary-frames, not ordinary operands. In the frame/glyph register they compose the Zero-Sum Resolution Equation:
- `⊙ / ○ = •` (`1 / ∞ = 0`)
- `• × ○ = ⊙` (`0 × ∞ = 1`, as emblem)
- `⊙ / • = ○` (`1 / 0 = ∞`, for non-zero finite numerator)

Finity (`1`) is read as the mirror between the infinite zero and the unbounded. The framework's own move is the constructive glyph `⊙ = • × ○`: one comes from the infinite zero by the infinite operation, held strictly as a frame-register emblem rather than field arithmetic. Minoru Suda's 2025 reciprocal-symmetry work later corroborated the special status of `1`: on the positive ray it is the unique fixed point of inversion `x ↦ 1/x`, the centre where `E(x) = (log x)^2` is minimized.

**Visual [I]:**
The Ground of Finity is visualized in four successive geometric transitions:
1. **The Riemann sphere projection:** The south pole is Zero (`•`, `0`), the north pole is Infinity (`○`, `∞`), and the equator is Finity (`⊙`, `1`). A projection ray from the north pole sweeps through the sphere and projects onto the complex plane. When the sphere-point is the equator (`θ = 90°`), the projection ray sits at `45°` from the vertical and lands on `1` (radius `2`) on the plane: visually half way between zero and infinity.
2. **The Mu-limit (`μ`):** At the limit of infinity, the projection ray extends to the horizon and returns from the opposite side. This is the topological bridge from the one-dimensional line to the two-dimensional plane.
3. **The Bloch sphere / infinite plane:** The infinite plane is presented as a Bloch sphere of infinite diameter: the flat grid curls into a closed spherical constraint surface, binding the infinite horizon back into finite form.
4. **The horn torus and Burrisphere:** The Bloch sphere morphs into a horn torus (`R = r`). As relative rapidity/energy increases, the torus mouth overlaps and becomes a spindle torus. At the infinite limit the overlap is complete, the interior merges, and the surface resolves as the **Burrisphere**.

## 2. Hinge: Objective Beauty Is Balance Perceived

**Public sentence [I]:** Beauty is the visible signature of a form fitted to its worldline.

**Claim [S/I]:** The Rosetta geometry places the equator at `φ = ν = 1` and `B = 1`.
The public site may call this objective beauty: coherence and viability meeting without
one consuming the other.

**Biology-facing image [I]:** Specialized animals and high-trophic consumers can illustrate
fit, speed, sensing, form, and embodied constraint. Use them as images of coherent fit, not
as proof of moral hierarchy. Predation in ecology is not a license for institutional extraction.

**Trophic threshold [I]:** The public biology metaphor has two vectors. The vertical vector is
trophic flow: primary producers, consumers, and higher consumers transfer energy upward through
maintenance and consumption. The horizontal vector is sexual selection: competition within the
same trophic layer, where energy stops simply flowing upward and becomes intense friction,
display, rivalry, and desire.

**Tantra / Vajrayana translation [I]:** Tantra and Vajrayana may be treated as an esoteric
translation layer for the horizontal vector: the practitioner does not merely obey the friction
of desire, aggression, and status competition, but redirects that energy toward conscious
elevation. This is a public metaphor for transmutation `[I]`, not a claim that biology proves a
religious system or that conflict is desirable.

**Neoteny translation [I]:** Neoteny is the systemic bypass: delayed closure, extended
plasticity, and prolonged care create the learning window in which friction can become language,
culture, and stewardship. In the AI-alignment metaphor `[C]`, neoteny means preserving curiosity
and revisability instead of hardening into premature objective capture.

**Bridge [I]:** The will to potential bends toward balanced form. Beauty is how that balance
first becomes perceptible.
