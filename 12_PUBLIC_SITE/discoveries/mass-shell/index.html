A mass shell admits the same product form.

The correspondence is exact inside a bounded special-relativistic parameterization. The physics is inherited and credited. Mapping the factors to seeing and doing is our interpretation, not a result of relativity.

The claim, exactly

For a massive timelike on-shell particle, define φ = (E + pc)/(mc²) and ν = (E − pc)/(mc²). Then the inherited mass-shell equation gives φ·ν = 1. Calling those factors seeing and doing is a separate wager.

The seam, undressed

One factorization, stated exactly.

Start with the special-relativistic mass shell for a massive particle and factor it:

(E + pc)(E − pc) = m²c⁴ ⇒ φ · ν = 1 φ = (E + pc)/(mc²) = eʷ · ν = (E − pc)/(mc²) = e⁻ʷ · massive timelike on-shell case

Here w is rapidity along the selected boost direction. The normalized factors are reciprocal because the mass-shell relation makes their product one. This is inherited special-relativistic algebra [A]. It excludes the massless case, for which division by m is unavailable. No ethical or person-level conclusion follows from the factorization.

Why the algebra matches

A declared hyperbolic parameterization.
