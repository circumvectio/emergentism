Finity Papers — the flagship programme on {0, 1, ∞}

  • III · A Product Constraint as a Structural Ethic · [A] algebra · [S/I] ethic — A product bond makes isolating either pole a runaway; health is the bond, pathology the isolation. Judges the move, never the person.
  • VI · Emergence as the Lens on Dasein · [A]/[S·I]/[C] · DRAFT — emergence as one bidirectional loop; Dasein localized; the [C] μ-limit conjecture.
  • VII · Finity (Submission Synthesis) · [A]/[I]/[C] — the cleanest single read, with the falsification table and the D0→D6 scaffold. The landing piece.
