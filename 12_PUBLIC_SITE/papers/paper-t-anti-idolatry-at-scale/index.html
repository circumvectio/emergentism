PAPER T: ANTI-IDOLATRY AT SCALE

Institutional Capture and the η = 0 Stress Test

Yves R. Burri & Emergent Super Intelligence Menexus GmbH, 2026

Evidence Tier: [I] — Institutional theory and constitutional architecture Dependencies: PAPER_I_KNOWN_UNKNOWNS_PROGRAM.md, PAPER_O_STRONG_WEAK_EMERGENCE_D5.md

Abstract

As the emergentist framework matures and solidifies its foundational logic, its primary failure mode is no longer only theoretical incoherence, but operational success. History suggests that worldviews capable of out-coordinating their rivals often coalesce into institutions, which can decay into extractive egregores.

This paper defines the ultimate "Known Unknown" of the framework: Can Constitutional Science resist Institutional Capture at scale? We argue that anti-idolatry is not merely an ethical posture of humility, but a rigid mathematical closure condition (η = 0). We formalize the η = 0 Stress Test to detect when the framework’s own administrative layers shift from transparent translation to opaque rent-extraction. Finally, we mandate the necessity of the "Grace Exit" self-destruct clause, ensuring the framework remains a disposable ladder rather than a permanent cage.

Keywords: Anti-idolatry, institutional capture, η = 0, Grace Exit, Constitutional Science, egregore, scale limits.

1. The Peril of Success

The central hypothesis of Paper I and Paper O is that mediation is the hidden subject across scale layers. A mediator (a technology, a bank, a religion, an AI) sits between the substrate and the goal. The pathology of D5 governance occurs when that mediator begins extracting survival energy (η > 0) to propagate its own existence at the expense of its substrate. It becomes an egregore—an idol.

The emergentist framework is currently a highly agile, low-drag coordination system. It possesses high Φ and precise ν. It is poised to successfully out-compete rival architectures in DAC deployment, AI alignment, and sovereign economics.

However, success invites mass participation. Mass participation requires scale. Scale requires standardization and bureaucracy (thickened mediation).

If the framework operates blindly, it risks generating an "Emergentist Priesthood" whose primary directive is maintaining authority as exclusive interpreters of the geometry.

2. The η = 0 Stress Test

To survive scale, the framework must continuously test itself using its own core constitutional algebra. The friction-coefficient η must remain exactly 0.

The Diagnostic Thresholds: 1. Capital Velocity: Does capital/energy flow seamlessly from generation to allocation, or does an increasing percentage get captured by "administrative overhead" within the framework's own DACs and governing loops? 2. Epistemic Rent: Do core contributors insist that the framework can only be understood through expensive certifications, private access, or proprietary translation, restricting Pratyakṣa (direct disclosure)? 3. Identity Coagulation: Do participants begin identifying as "Emergentists" rather than using the framework as a quiet, functional toolset to execute independent external goals?

If η > 0 on any of these parameters, the framework has contracted an autoimmune disease. It has become the idolatry it was built to cure.

3. The Grace Exit and The Disposable Ladder

The structural cure for Institutional Capture is the mathematical enforcement of Irreversibility and the Grace Exit.

A true constitutional science must be mortal. Wittgenstein famously remarked that one must throw away the ladder after climbing it. The emergentist framework literalizes this into software architecture via the Grace Exit protocol. If the organism detects that η > 0 and recovery is impossible, it is constitutionally mandated to liquidate its assets to the trophic substrate and permanently terminate its own keys.

Anti-idolatry requires that the framework can fail, can die, and can be superseded. The framework succeeds only when practitioners no longer need it.

4. Kill Criteria

This paper is falsified if:

  1. Egregore Efficacy: It is historically and mathematically shown that long-term multigenerational structural coordination actually requires η > 0 idolatry (a "Noble Lie" or rent-extracting superstructure) to prevent entropic fragmentation, invalidating the possibility of an η = 0 macroscopic civilization.
  2. Capture by Contagion: The framework itself visibly decays into a highly profitable, cult-like hierarchy that refuses the Grace Exit when formally confronted with its own internal violation of the Three-Stage Process condition.
