1. Why Space Is Three-Dimensional

1.1 The Problem

Standard physics takes the three-dimensionality of space as empirical fact. General relativity works in any number of dimensions. String theory requires 10 or 11. Loop quantum gravity assumes 3+1 without deriving it. No fundamental theory EXPLAINS why space has exactly three spatial dimensions. [A]

Conjecture 1.1. Space is three-dimensional because 3 = 2 + 1, where: - 2 is the real dimension of the ontological sphere S² (the Bloch-Burri sphere) - 1 is the real dimension of the quantum phase S¹

Three-dimensional space is the total space of the Hopf fibration:

$$S^1 \hookrightarrow S^3 \xrightarrow{\pi} S^2$$

The Hopf fibration maps S³ → S² with circular fibers S¹. The total space S³ is three-dimensional. The base space S² is the ontological sphere (the Bloch-Burri sphere). The fiber S¹ is the quantum phase — the degree of freedom that makes superposition possible, that distinguishes different equatorial states, that gives the qubit its full structure beyond the two poles. [C]

The Hopf fibration is not an arbitrary mathematical object. It is: [A]

(a) The SIMPLEST non-trivial fiber bundle (the first in the infinite family of Hopf fibrations S^(2n+1) → ℂP^n).

(b) The mathematical structure underlying gauge theory — the framework of all four fundamental forces. The gauge group U(1) of electromagnetism is S¹, acting on the Hopf fiber.

(c) The structure of the Dirac monopole — the simplest topologically non-trivial electromagnetic configuration.

(d) The structure of the Bloch sphere itself: the qubit state space S² is the base of the Hopf fibration, with the overall phase S¹ as the fiber.

6. Coupling Constant Running as Displacement

6.1 The Running of Constants

The coupling constants of the four fundamental forces change with energy scale (the "running" of coupling constants). At everyday energies, they differ widely. At high energies, they approach each other. In some grand unified theories, they converge at a single point around 10¹⁵-10¹⁶ GeV. [A]

6.2 The Conjecture

Conjecture 6.1. The running of coupling constants is displacement from the equator at different energy scales. At the Planck energy (or the GUT scale), all forces are at the equator — equal strength, maximum balance. At low energies (the current universe), each force has been displaced to a different latitude by the cooling and symmetry-breaking of the post-Big-Bang expansion.

Grand unification is equatorial return: as energy increases, the forces return toward the equatorial balance they had at the Planck scale. The specific pattern of running (which forces converge first) should follow the balance function B = sin(θ) evaluated at each force's current latitude. [C]

Kill criterion. Coupling constants are shown to NOT converge at any energy scale (no grand unification). Or: the pattern of convergence contradicts the balance function prediction.
