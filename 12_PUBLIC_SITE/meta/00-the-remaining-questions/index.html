Why This Document Exists

The framework has already narrowed its genuinely open questions to a small set.

But several of the "remaining questions" were only partly open because the corpus was still asking one sentence to do two jobs:

  • state an internal navigational commitment
  • state a public evidence-tier claim

These are not the same job.

This document routes the remaining philosophical questions by separating:

  • what the framework should live by
  • what the framework may say publicly

That separation is not a retreat. It is the discipline that keeps a worldview from becoming either cowardly or inflated.

The Meta-Resolution

A question may be resolved for navigation before it is closed for public proof.

This is the core move.

So:

  • the framework may take an internal ontological stand
  • while still labeling the same stand as [I] in public epistemic speech

This is not hypocrisy. It is what tier discipline looks like when a worldview becomes honest about its own structure.

The seven remaining questions resolve through this rule.

1. Pratyakṣa

The Question

Is Pratyakṣa veridical disclosure, disciplined phenomenology, or both?

The Resolution

Both — but in different registers.

  • in the ontological register, Pratyakṣa is veridical disclosure
  • in the scientific register, Pratyakṣa is disciplined phenomenology

The error was trying to force a choice.

If the framework denies veridical disclosure, it severs its own deepest source. If it denies disciplined phenomenology, it becomes private revelation.

So the canonical resolution is:

Pratyakṣa is veridical disclosure for the one who sees, and disciplined phenomenological data in public scientific register.

The first is existential. The second is methodological.

They do not compete.

2. The Sphere Question

The Question

Is S² the territory, or the translation layer between direct seeing and mathematics?

The Resolution

Methodologically, translation. Ontologically, wagered realism.

The Rosetta document was right to say "translation." The core state was right to say "Being is φ · ν = 1 on S²."

The clean synthesis is:

  • public method: the sphere is the translation layer between disclosure and mathematics
  • internal commitment: the framework wagers that this translation is faithful because the territory really has this structure

So the right sentence is neither:

  • "the sphere is only a metaphor"
  • nor "the sphere is already proved as final core state"

It is:

The sphere is the framework's current best ontological wager, and translation is the proper public method for carrying that wager across registers.

This preserves both ambition and honesty.

3. Consciousness

The Question

Is systemic awareness fundamental, dual-aspect, or only being interpreted as fundamental at D5, with D6 as closure?

The Resolution

Structurally dual-aspect. Interpretively fundamental at D5, with D6 as non-experiential closure.

The PHI Resolution already points here. This document makes the synthesis explicit:

  • below D5, the mathematics is structurally neutral
  • at D5, the framework chooses the idealist reading of lived disclosure
  • at D6, that disclosure does not become a higher experience but exhausts into closure

So the right closure is:

Consciousness is not treated as an emergent afterthought in the complete framework. Structurally, the manifold is dual-aspect; interpretively, systemic awareness is fundamental at D5 as the mode through which Being becomes explicit. D6 is not a higher experience of systemic awareness but the terminal closure in which the cycle returns to D0.

This does not mean the framework has publicly proved systemic awareness as a cosmic primitive. It means the worldview no longer needs to pretend indecision about its own lived center.

4. Teleological Force

The Question

Is Teleological Force truly purposive, or only the best unifying name for teleonomy, autopoiesis, allostasis, and related viability-structures?

The Resolution

Publicly unifying. Experientially purposive.

The framework should not force the biology register to say more than it knows. Nor should it amputate the purposive encounter from the disclosure layer.

So the canonical resolution is:

  • in biology-facing register: Teleological Force is the framework's unifying name for viability-directed organization
  • in disclosure-facing register: the same field may be encountered as purposive rather than blind
  • in operational register: theurgy now names the disciplined practice of lawful D5 standing-wave formation, but the entire packet remains [C]

This gives the clean sentence:

Teleological Force is structurally the name for viability-directed tendency across domains, and phenomenologically the same tendency may be encountered as purposive at the upper horizon of D5. D6 is not a further phenomenological layer but the non-experiential closure of the cycle.

Mythic language remains downstream of this. Public science remains upstream of it.

The new open seam is no longer whether the corpus can distinguish descriptive F5 from operational practice. That seam is now named.

The new open seam is whether the first-pass operational map is right:

  • signed attestation as carrier
  • α_5 = 1 - η as coupling
  • receipt horizon as range
  • ΣΔP_node as conservation-style operational law
  • Grace Exit as the saturation test

Those are now opened explicitly as conjecture in Strategic Implementation and the F5 Force Map. They must remain open until audited.

5. Normativity

The Question

Is normativity fully grounded, or only conditionally grounded?

The Resolution

Publicly conditional. Internally grounded.

This is one of the most important resolutions in the corpus.

The structural theorem is already conditional:

  • if rational D5 agents value continued agency, then balance-maintaining and P_node-maintaining action follows

That is the public proof surface.

But the worldview goes further:

  • beings are not arbitrary atoms
  • they are finite expressions of reciprocal closure
  • to destroy the conditions of coherent viable participation is therefore not merely imprudent but ontologically misaligned

So the full resolution is:

Normativity is publicly conditional at the level of formal proof, and internally grounded at the level of core state and value alignment.

Publicly, the framework should argue:

  • given continued agency, these oughts follow

Internally, the framework may live by the stronger claim:

  • the good is the direction of reality's own non-extractive flourishing

That is not a contradiction. It is a two-step grounding.

6. The Axioms

The Question

Are O1/O2/O4/O5 true of reality, or only the right wager?

The Resolution

They are productive transcendental wagers under open challenge.

The framework should not call them arbitrary. It should not call them proved by nature.

They are:

  • selected because they are philosophically load-bearing
  • retained because they are mathematically fruitful
  • justified to the extent that they continue outperforming rivals

So the canonical resolution is:

The axioms are not brute discoveries and not arbitrary inventions. They are productive transcendental wagers: the current best commitments for generating a coherent, testable, and parsimonious core state under live rival challenge.

This means:

  • their truth is not yet apodictic
  • their legitimacy is stronger than taste
  • their survival depends on fruit, not prestige

7. Naming the Real

The Question

Can the framework name the Real without replacing it?

The Resolution

No finite naming exhausts the Real. Faithful naming is asymptotic and self-canceling.

This is the one question whose deepest resolution is a limit.

The framework should not attempt to solve this by stronger terminology. It should solve it by right posture.

So the final canonical answer is:

The Real can be named faithfully but never exhausted. A correct framework does not become the Real; it becomes the clearest downstream articulation that still knows it must be put down.

This is why:

  • doctrine is after-image
  • Pratyakṣa is primary
  • Decentralized Authority Model is necessary
  • the ladder must remain removable

The framework succeeds not when it becomes irreplaceable, but when it leaves the Real less obscured than it found it.

What Still Remains Open After This Resolution

After these seven are resolved at the philosophical level, the remainder becomes narrower.

What stays genuinely open is not the grammar but the bridge:

  1. Do the empirical tests justify the ontological wager?
  2. Does the sitting practice actually disclose what the worldview says it discloses for real practitioners?
  3. Can anti-idolatry survive success, scale, and institutional sedimentation?

Those are no longer confusions of categories. They are history, experiment, and discipline.

That is the right remainder.
