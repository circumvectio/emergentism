“Flourishing is multiplicative — coherence × viability, not their sum.”

April's test only pitched the product against the sum. But the zero-factor law selects a whole AND-class — min (Liebig), CES, geometric mean, product — that a ×-vs-+ test cannot tell apart. What it costs: A1's multiplicative interior now has evidence against it, on the one population instrument the corpus has tested — not merely unproven.
