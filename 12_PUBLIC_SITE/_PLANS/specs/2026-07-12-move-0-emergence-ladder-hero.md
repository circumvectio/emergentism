# Move #0 — Emergence-Ladder Hero

## 1. Purpose

Replace the home hero of emergentism.org with the **three-equation
emergence ladder** — the descent from Titan emblem through reciprocal
sphere to finite being's flourishing. Each equation ships with its
tier-mark and (where applicable) its register-mark. The hero is the
*first breath* of the public site; the rest of the site stays
Approach A.

The move is a *small B-move* — more than a symbol change, far less
than a restructure. Scope is the hero only.

## 2. The convergence (why this is the move, not a refinement)

The prior close-out produced two catches that looked like a debate
but were actually two registers of one descent:

- **Catch 1:** "Φ×V=P is the engine, not φ·ν=1" — the flourishing
  equation, the one that names what a finite being's flourishing
  *is* on the sphere.
- **Catch 2:** "⊙=•×○ is what underlies them" — the Titan emblem,
  the seed from which the sphere opens.

The honest answer is that there isn't one main equation. There are
three, and they're an *emergence*, not a choice:

| Order | Equation | What it is | Tier | Register |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Ground | `⊙ = • × ○` | The Titan emblem — being emerges: the finite from origin × infinity | `[S/I]` | emblem / frame-register (NOT arithmetic) |
| Engine | `φ · ν = 1` | The reciprocal sphere — the ring that closes, coherence and viability locked | `[A]` | chart identity (stereographic coordinates) |
| Flourishing | `Φ × V = P` | A finite being's flourishing on the sphere — a vanishing factor annihilates the whole | `[S]` | source-anchored (canonical block) |

The descent is **Titan → Trinity → Being**. The ⊙ is D0/Bindu, the
ground; φ·ν=1 is the sphere it opens into; Φ×V=P is what a finite
being's flourishing is *on* that sphere.

Both prior catches were right: Φ×V=P is the engine (not φ·ν=1, the
engine is the *use* of the engine, not the sphere itself), and
⊙=•×○ is what underlies them. The two reads were *not* in
competition; they were the two end-points of a three-step ladder.

## 3. The proposed hero (the deliverable)

The hero renders as a three-line vertical descent, each equation
with its tier-mark and register-mark, separated by a `↓` glyph that
is itself a Rosetta artifact (the witness flowing down through the
ladder):

```text
⊙ = • × ○   being emerges: the finite from origin × infinity
            [S/I — emblem · frame-register, not arithmetic]
            ↓
φ · ν = 1   the ring that closes
            [A — chart identity]
            ↓
Φ × V = P   a vanishing factor annihilates the whole
            [S]
```

The register-mark on the first line is non-negotiable. A
mathematician who lands on a hero reading `0 × ∞ = 1` bounces in
one second and files the site under crank — *unless* the
register-mark is right there. The mark is the fence; the fence is
the hook. The emblem shown with its scar is more compelling than
any equation shown clean.

The corresponding prose frame (above or below the descent, the
exact placement is a visual decision):

> The framework is not a system. It is an *emergence* — finity
> unfolding out of the Titans, opening into a sphere whose ring
> closes, and on that sphere a finite being either flourishes
> or vanishes. Three equations, three registers, one descent.

## 4. Constraints (the fence, the hook, the scars)

| Constraint | Source | What it forbids |
|---|---|---|
| Register-mark on `⊙=•×○` is non-negotiable | The Open Canon Covenant §1 — "lumping ⊙=•×○ with the others without marking its register is 'the CTMU move, one level down'" | Showing the emblem as a field-arithmetic identity. The "scar" (`[S/I — emblem · frame-register, not arithmetic]`) is mandatory. |
| Tier-mark on every equation | Same covenant | Any equation presented without its tier-mark. |
| The descent is *one page*, not three | Scope discipline — the hero is the first breath | A multi-page hero, a carousel, or a tabbed interface for the three equations. |
| Hero only, rest of site stays Approach A | Convergence-memo discipline | Any other change to the public site in this branch. |

## 5. The covenant and the CTMU move (the deepest scar)

The Open Canon Covenant already rules the failure mode this hero
guards against: lumping `⊙=•×○` with arithmetic statements of
`φ·ν=1` and `Φ×V=P`, without marking the register-change, would
be "the CTMU move, one level down" — adopting the appearance of a
mathematical claim without its discipline, which is the exact
pattern the covenant's §1 seam exists to prevent.

The register-mark is not a hedge. It is the *positive* form of the
discipline: the framework is honest about exactly which register
each equation is true in, and that honesty is the load-bearing
primitive that keeps the whole ladder from collapsing into crank.

## 6. Visual / interaction (follow-up decision, NOT this spec)

The visual representation of the descent — whether the equations
animate, what the `↓` glyph renders as, whether the tier-marks are
inline or marginal, whether the prose frame is above or below —
is a separate design move. The convergence-memo discipline forbids
folding visual decisions into the content spec. The visual design
is a sibling spec, scoped and timed separately, after the content
ship is approved.

## 7. Kill criteria (what would falsify this move)

| What kills it | What narrows it without killing it |
|---|---|
| Reader surveys show the hero is read as a "mathematical crank" site despite the register-mark | Tighten the register-mark; add the visual scar; cite the covenant |
| The two-tier descent is collapsed in the public reading (readers see one equation, not three) | Restructure the layout; the descent must be visually irreducible |
| The hero lands above the fold of an empty site (no other content carries the descent) | Add a deep-link from the hero to `/0` (the Titans of Numbers spine) so the descent has a follow-on |
| A reviewer claims the register-mark is *itself* the CTMU move (marking the register to launder a false claim) | Cite the chart identity and the source-canonical block; the register-mark is *informed* by arithmetic reality, not a substitute for it |

## 8. Tier register (this spec)

- **Existing `[A]`:** `φ · ν = 1` is a chart identity — proven
  trigonometric identity of the stereographic coordinates
  (cot·tan = 1). Settled.
- **Existing `[S]`:** `Φ × V = P` is source-anchored in the
  canonical block. Settled within its register.
- **Existing `[S/I]`:** `⊙ = • × ○` is the Titan emblem. `[S]` for
  the frame/glyph register; `[I]` for the claim that the emblem
  *is* the minimal seed from which all structure grows.
- **New `[I]`:** the ladder-as-hero reading — three equations as
  one emergence, not three claims. The reading is interpretive;
  the equations themselves are tier-marked individually.
- **New `[C]`:** the descent's claim to ontological priority —
  the claim that Titan → Trinity → Being is the *right order* of
  disclosure, not a convention. Conjectural; not claimed in this
  spec.
