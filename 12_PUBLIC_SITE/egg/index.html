What the egg shows

• The two poles 0 and ∞ sit on the rim; 1 — finity, Suda's Critical-One — sits at the dead center.

• A number x and its reciprocal 1/x are always diametrically opposite through the center. Inversion is the half-twist u → −u — a rotation by π. Only 1 stays fixed.

• The warmth toward the center is E(x)=(log x)² at its minimum: 1 is the calm, the still point the whole reciprocal tension turns around.

The honest fence — corroboration, not proof

Independent convergence, tier [I]. Suda reached this reciprocal geometry by his own road. That it agrees with our finity is corroboration — not proof, and not authority. He did not originate Emergentism or the doctrine of finity; we did not originate his egg. Two people, two vocabularies, one structure — which is exactly the kind of agreement that is worth something because neither borrowed it from the other.

Two registers of one fact. The egg is the generative reading — how 1 hatches from the fusion of 0 and ∞. Finity is the structural reading — how 1 stabilizes the tension between them. Same center, genesis-voice and state-voice.

Un-mixed. This is the Cayley / egg chart (u = (x−1)/(x+1)) — a distinct coordinate from the stereographic sphere and the torus family. Different charts of one related structure; we keep them in separate frames on purpose.
