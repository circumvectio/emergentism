Act IV — The Riemann Sphere: The Truer Model

Step 2 — the Riemann sphere S² ≅ CP¹. Riemann (1857) completed the complex plane by adding a single point at infinity. On the resulting sphere:

  • φ = cot(θ/2) and ν = tan(θ/2) are the dual stereographic coordinates

  • B = sin θ measures equatorial alignment — the balance function
