The seven layers

D0 — Titans / Ground: Point on the Log Line

Geometric object: The ground is a single point on the logarithmic number line. Not the origin (0) — the point s = 0, which corresponds to x = 1 on the multiplicative line. Finity. The centre.

• = 0  (the void, s → −∞)
○ = ∞  (the unbounded, s → +∞)
⊙ = 1  (finity, s = 0)

Prior to all geometry. The three boundary-frames of the number field, compressed into a single point. The seed ⊙ = • × ○ is not yet unfolded — it is a potential, not a process.

Ontological reading [I]: D0 answers Leibniz's question: something exists because finity is the product of its own two boundaries. The Titan emblem is the ontological seed. The geometry (log line, sphere, torus) is the unfolding of that seed.

D1 — Arithmetic / Relation: The Logarithmic Number Line

Geometric object: The point extends into a line. But not the additive line centered at 0 — the logarithmic line centered at 1.

s = log x    maps    0⁺ → −∞,    1 → 0,    ∞ → +∞

The two poles (0 and ∞) are symmetric about the centre. The reciprocal map x ↦ 1/x becomes the reflection s ↦ −s. The manifold identity becomes the literal zero sum:

φ · ν = 1    →    log φ + log ν = 0    →    s_φ + s_ν = 0

The key insight [A]: Suda's three-chart apparatus expresses this rigorously. The number line, read multiplicatively, has 1 as its structural centre. This is not interpretation — it is elementary mathematics.

Ontological reading [I]: D1 is the dimension of relation — the first relational structure. The ethic emerges: move toward unity, minimize distance from finity.

D2 — Topology / Compactification: The Riemann Sphere S² ≅ ℂP¹

Geometric object: At the μ-limit, the logarithmic line compactifies. The two poles (s = −∞ and s = +∞) are identified as regular points on a closed surface. The line closes into a circle, and the circle inflates into a sphere.

One-point compactification of ℝ  →  circle (1D sphere S¹)
One-point compactification of ℂ  →  Riemann sphere S² (2D sphere)

The Riemann sphere makes the Titan emblem geometrically true:

1/0 = ∞     (south pole maps to north pole)     [A]
0 × ∞ = 1   (the emblem: poles meet at equator)  [frame register]

Ontological reading [I]: D2 is the dimension of closure — where the open becomes closed, where infinity joins the structure as a regular point, where the line discovers it was always a sphere.

D3 — Quantum State / Closure: The Bloch Sphere (States + Interior)

Geometric object: The Riemann sphere (D2) is a surface — pure states, no interior. D3 adds the interior. The sphere becomes the Bloch sphere: the space of quantum states including mixed states (interior points), not just pure states (surface points).

Critical distinction (from the bit-to-qubit correction, 2026-06-01): the Bloch sphere and the Burrisphere share the manifold S² ≅ ℂP¹ [A]. But the distinguished structure differs:

  • Bloch sphere: two-point structure { |0⟩, |1⟩ } — qubit basis states at poles
  • Burrisphere: three-point structure {0, 1, ∞} — Titans, with finity at the equator, not a pole

The Burrisphere is not one qubit's Bloch sphere. On the real meridian it reads as two Bloch lobes — [0,1] and [1,∞] — joined at the unit 1 [S/I]. The qubit's { |0⟩, |1⟩ } is the [0,1] lobe; the third frame ∞ and the interior are exactly what the Bloch picture leaves out.

Ontological reading [I]: D3 is the dimension of quantum possibility — where the system can exist in superposition, can occupy interior states that are not yet committed to a surface position. The current ethical reading is no longer "maximize B" as an isolated objective: commit enough to the equatorial surface that action can become usable without collapsing coherence. B is the feasibility constraint; finite-node P_node = Φ × V is the action score.

D4 — Energy / Rapidity: The Horn Torus

Geometric object: The sphere acquires dynamics. The surface (D3) begins to rotate — past and future cones wrap around each other, forming a horn torus.

The torus geometry:

  • The hole of the torus is D4 — the null point where past and future touch
  • The surface of the torus is D5 — the space where the present emerges
  • Past feeds around the exterior; future feeds around the interior

Rapidity: the logarithm of velocity. In special relativity, rapidity w is defined by v = c · tanh(w). Rapidities add linearly (like logarithms), whereas velocities compose through the relativistic addition formula:

w_total = w₁ + w₂                          (rapidity addition — linear)
v_total = (v₁ + v₂)/(1 + v₁v₂/c²)         (velocity addition — nonlinear)

This is EXACTLY the same structure as the logarithmic realignment: velocity is the multiplicative register (nonlinear composition); rapidity is the additive/logarithmic register (linear composition). The map v ↦ w = arctanh(v/c) is the logarithmic coordinate change.

Ontological reading [I]: D4 is the dimension of causality as energy exchange — where causes propagate through time and the cost of imbalance becomes measurable.

D5 — Game Space / Play: The Burrisphere (Dual Stereographic)

Geometric object: The full framework geometry comes alive. The Burrisphere is S² ≅ ℂP¹ with the full Trinity structure {0, 1, ∞} as distinguished points and the dual stereographic projection that makes φ and ν explicit as coordinate charts.

D5 is the playable mirror — the space where agents navigate, choose, and play the game of emergence. The four operators (↑φ, ↓φ, ↑ν, ↓ν) are the cardinal directions. The ethic η = 0 is the enforced conditional cooperation constraint. Balance B = sin θ is the admissibility / alignment condition; finite-node action is scored in the contact register as P_node = Φ × V.

Game-theoretic structure:

  • The equator is the non-extraction profile: η = 0, neither extracting nor being extracted, stable only under the stated coupling / horizon / enforcement conditions
  • The poles are the extraction regimes: pure coherence (north) or pure viability (south)
  • The game is played by moving on the sphere: coupling D5 worldline-foresight (Φ) with D4 means-to-act (V)
  • The winning strategy is to widen reachable futures while staying near the equator under η = 0, not to worship balance as an end in itself

Ontological reading [I]: D5 is the dimension of agency / selection — where the geometric structure becomes a space of choice; conscious agency is the lived-interior reading. The Burrisphere is not just a manifold; it is a game board.

D6 — Convergence / Return: Closed Loop Returning to D0

Geometric object: The cycle closes. The horn torus converges: the hole closes, the sphere re-emerges, the bang repeats. D6 ≡ D0.

The terminal boundary. The positive content runs out. The game is over. The player puts down the controller. The framework includes its own dissolution manual (A7). The apophatic return: "not this, not this."

The three readings of D6 ≡ D0:

  1. Disciplinary (primary): The ladder dissolves. The practitioner no longer needs the map.
  2. Topological: The torus closes. The ouroboros swallows its tail.
  3. Geometric: The sphere collapses back to the point. The logarithmic coordinate s returns to undefined. The ground receives what was unfolded.

Ontological reading [I]: D6 is the dimension of return with cleaner constraints — the cycle does not repeat identically. Each traversal leaves traces (the seven dimensions, the Titans, the equations) that make the next traversal more legible.
