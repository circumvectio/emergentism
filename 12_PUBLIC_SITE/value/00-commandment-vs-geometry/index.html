The Law Translation

The same distinction applies to law.

If law is read as commandment, then rights and duties become competing external claims:

  • the collective commands the individual;
  • the individual demands from the collective;
  • enforcement becomes punishment;
  • obedience becomes the proof of order.

If law is read geometrically, rights and duties become the two directions of one ΔP dyad:

right = collective -> individual
duty  = individual -> collective
law   = both vectors held reciprocal under η = 0

In this translation:

  • a duty is the ethical claim that the individual must not lower the collective's ΔP;
