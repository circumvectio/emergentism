Strong and Weak Emergence Close the Ground

Strong emergence reads upward: lower interactions generate new irreducible structure. Weak emergence reads downward: higher structure constrains, organizes, and makes lower behavior intelligible.

D0 is the reciprocal hinge between those readings. It is not a finished physical theory; it is the ontological scaffold the public spine uses before stepping into D1.
