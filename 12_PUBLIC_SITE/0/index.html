§1 · The Moves

One Emblem, Three Readings

The glyph triple is not three equations but one relation read from three seats. In ordinary field arithmetic, ∞ is not a field element and 0 × ∞ is undefined; there is no field theorem 0 × ∞ = 1, and no comparison of differently parenthesized 0·∞ expressions can serve as an associativity counterexample, because neither side is defined. The bare field claim remains a named grave. The triple lives in the frame register: emblem [I]; the declared frame/field distinction [S]; the underlying projective machinery — the Riemann sphere, Möbius frames, the removable extension — [A], used, not discovered.

The group axiom — the floor's one piece of exact arithmetic. In any group, the identity is not a free-standing thing but a return: e = x · x⁻¹ for every x. This is elementary group theory [A], used, not discovered; it characterizes the identity inside the group operation but does not prove an ontology of "presence as cancellation" — that reading remains [I]. On the positive-real register, 1 is the unique fixed point of x ↦ 1/x; on the full complex register the fixed points are ±1.

What D0 is not: not a rung of world-content, not a first emergence, and not a proof that reality is the sphere — that is W0, the crown wager, [C], and it lives higher up. D0 is the grammar every later rung will inherit.

§2 · The Dissolutions

"Why Something Rather Than Nothing?" — Reframed, Not Answered

The reciprocal product chart is singular at its pole limits; the poles themselves remain legitimate points of the compactified manifold. Reading "nothing" and "everything" as boundary frames rather than candidate worlds is therefore an [I] proposal, not a geometric proof that such worlds are impossible or "uninhabitable." Cure named: the frame/operand distinction, scoped to this question and this declared reading.

An interpretive reframe [I], not a cosmogonic explanation — it does not tell you why this world, with these constants and this history; the residue stays open in W0/W1 territory [C]. "Dissolves the Hard Problem / Is-Ought / Death" by the same trick is a named dead form — the frame-move is licensed here, for this one question, because here the frames are exactly the question's own terms. §3 · The Ology

What Is the Ground? The Nameless Third

[I/D] The Soul-Loop question at D0: what is the ground I stand on? Theology at this rung is henology — the study of the One that is not a being among beings. ⊙ is not the biggest thing, the first thing, or the sum of things; it is the relation in which point and horizon co-define each other. Every positive name grabs one seat of the triple and misses the relation — so the floor's native speech is neti neti: not this (• alone — the ground is not a point-thing), not that (○ alone — the ground is not a totality-thing). What remains when both denials are complete is not a fourth object but the relation itself, which was never absent and never nameable from inside one seat.

The Advaita resonance, at [I] and no higher. Tat Tvam Asi — "that thou art" — is read through the correctly rearranged emblem • = ⊙ / ○. This is a lens, a translation, a resonance — not algebraic proof of Advaita, not a claim that Śaṅkara derived the chart, and not independent confirmation. One shape shown two ways is one datum.

What the floor gives the rungs above it: a grammar in which finitude is not a defect. ⊙ = • × ○ says the finite is not fallen from the infinite nor stranded in the void; it is their meeting, and the only seat from which anything is said at all. Everything after D0 is the biography of that meeting.

Foundations · attribution before interpretation

Axioms and inherited machinery

An axiom is a starting rule inside a named system. A theorem, construction, or Emergentist reading is labelled separately; inherited mathematics is never presented as our discovery.

Group theory · inherited [A]

The four group axioms

  • Closure: composing two group elements returns a group element.
  • Associativity: (a·b)·c = a·(b·c).
  • Identity: an element e satisfies e·a = a·e = a.
  • Inverse: every a has a⁻¹ with a·a⁻¹ = a⁻¹·a = e.

e = a·a⁻¹ follows inside that structure. It does not prove an ontology of “presence as cancellation.”

Complex field + ℂP¹ · inherited [A]

The field boundary and its projective repair

The complex numbers obey the field axioms. Consequently 0 has no multiplicative inverse, ∞ is not a field element, and 0·∞ is undefined. The Riemann sphere ℂP¹ = ℂ ∪ {∞} is a standard projective construction that adds one point at infinity; it is not a new field axiom and does not legalize every pole operation.
