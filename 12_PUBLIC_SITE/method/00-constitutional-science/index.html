CONSTITUTIONAL SCIENCE

Toward a Science of Corrigible Systems Under Power

Status: Canonical strategic note Date: 2026-04-15 Evidence Tier: [I] Meta-strategic routing document Depends on: 00_EXECUTION_GUARDRAILS.md, 00_D5_AS_THE_RESEARCH_ENGINE.md, ../08_FRAMEWORK_SUPPORT/01_GOVERNANCE/00_ANTIFRAGILITY_PROTOCOL.md

Why This Document Exists

The framework may not only be proposing:

  • a better core state
  • or a better ethics

It may also be proposing:

a constitutional science

By that is meant:

  • a science of how systems remain corrigible under recursion, power, abstraction, and scale

The Core Question

How do you build a system that:

  • can act
  • can scale
  • can coordinate
  • can recurse on itself

without collapsing into:

  • extraction
  • lock-in
  • sovereignty loss
  • ideological self-protection

That is the constitutional science question.

The Framework's Answer

The answer is not one mechanism. It is a packet:

  • Three-Stage Process separation
  • K2
  • η = 0
  • Grace Exit
  • Three Gates
  • anti-idolatry

Each prevents one class of failure. Together they create a system that can remain active without becoming total.

Why This Matters Beyond the Organism

If this packet works, it is not only relevant to one DAC.

It could apply to:

  • labs
  • protocols
  • councils
  • cooperatives
  • AI product architectures
  • public institutions

That is why constitutional science may travel farther than the rest of the core state in the near term.

The Shortest Summary

Constitutional science is the study of how recursive systems remain sovereign, non-extractive, corrigible, and exit-safe under real power and scale.

That may be one of the framework's most exportable contributions.
