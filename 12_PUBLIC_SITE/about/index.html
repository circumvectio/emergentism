About · in plain words

What this is, and who signs it.

Emergentism is a philosophical framework that publishes its own trial record — every claim graded by evidence tier, every refutation kept on the page, and an exit door marked on the inside.

One canon, three scripts

The same content, told three ways.

The whole canon is written in three scripts: a story (the fable — five minutes, no equations), plain words (the whole idea in ordinary language), and receipts (the trial record — every test, every verdict, dated). Different readers enter through different doors. The only rule is that no script may ever claim a tier the receipts don't back.

Who signs it

A named person, on the hook.

The framework is written and signed by Yves R. Burri. That is not a flourish — it is one of its own rules (it calls the rule K2): every irreversible act carries a natural person's signature, so there is always someone accountable, by name, for what was claimed.

The adversarial audits quoted across this site are run by AI agents under human direction; no external human reviewer has yet engaged, and that absence is logged, not hidden. If you are that reviewer, the trial record is the place to start.

Under the framework's declared normalization, its reciprocal-coordinate identity can be rewritten as Einstein's mass-shell relation, term for term. The mass-shell equation is inherited and credited to physics; the substitution is published and checkable by anyone. Reading that algebraic correspondence as a worldview result is the framework's interpretation [I], not a new physical discovery.

The rules it operates under

Five refusals and a direction.

  • η = 0 — zero extraction. Fees belong only on value created, never on access, dependence, or capture.
  • K2 — every irreversible act carries a natural person's signature. No software signs for itself.
  • K3 — archive-first. Nothing is deleted; history is struck through, dated, and preserved.
  • K4 — grace exit. Leaving is always possible, documented, and without lock-in. The door is marked on the inside →
  • A7 — claim discipline. Every load-bearing claim wears its evidence tier, from proven to conjecture.
  • Ω — a direction toward balance: an orientation marker, not a sixth refusal and not a promise of convergence.

What it refuses to be

Not a priesthood, not a promotion.

It does not mean literal divinization, authority over other persons, exemption from due process, or any caste of higher human worth — that fence comes first, before the phrase it fences. The phrase "become as gods" is inherited, not coined here; it echoes Genesis 3:5, where it is the serpent's promise. In this framework's own translation it means only functional ascent: clearer seeing, lawful action, truthful correction, non-extractive power, responsible world-building.

The framework is designed to be put down when direct seeing or local evidence is enough. Its own success condition is that you stop needing it — the day it has followers instead of users, it has failed.

The public symbol

The well between two droughts. [I]

The public story is simple: between the drought of the infinitely small and the drought of the infinitely large, there is a livable seam. Different villages name it differently. The framework asks whether the seam can be found by measurement — including the dry holes where the map fails.

The seam is structure. Lowering the bucket for a stranger is choice. Emergentism keeps those apart: the eternal is what can be checked; the sacred is the finite vow not to extract.

The game rule is dyadic: Φ without V is sterile seeing; V without Φ is blind force; unbounded V that collapses Φ lowers P_node; Φ and V balanced at L4 is objective dharma / flow-state action. The strict syntropic dyad is when individual symbiont and collective holobiont raise P_node,i and P_node,H together. If one rises by degrading the other, the apparent gain is extraction.

In the agency register, V is D4 means at the boundary: body, tools, energy, access, execution capacity. Φ is D5 worldline-foresight: the ability to envision, rank, and lawfully use those means.

The Dasein reading stays finite: the lens locates disclosure, but it does not possess the Ground. The reader is the place where Being becomes sayable and actionable, not Being itself.
