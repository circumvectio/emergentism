In the Soul Loop, epistemology is the first check after ontology names the field and the reader as Dasein. It asks what has actually been disclosed, what is only projection, and what evidence tier the claim can honestly wear.

Its output is not a command to act. It is a tiered candidate handed to methodology: direct disclosure, pattern, analogy, or observation made explicit enough to test. In P_node = Φ × V language, epistemology protects Φ from hallucinated foresight and protects V from imaginary means.

Objective Beauty

Evidence Tier: [I] Interpretive Metaphor

In the public translation layer, we identify L2 as the realm of Objective Beauty. Beauty is not a personal opinion; it is the visible signature of a form fitted to its worldline.

When a system achieves balanced coherence and viability, it may display specialized, high-fit form. The observer detects coherent fit through sensory alignment, then hands the claim to evidence tiers; beauty discloses a candidate pattern, but it does not prove the pattern by itself.

Grammar & Induction

Evidence Tier: [S] Structural Math
dP_node = V dΦ + Φ dV

Epistemology represents the First Power of reason: **Induction**.

Through induction, the mind moves from particular observed cases to a general candidate rule. This pattern-extraction requires a structured vocabulary—a **Grammar**—to organize, store, and transfer the observed structures across agents. Without grammar, sensory pattern recognition remains transient and cannot form civilization.
