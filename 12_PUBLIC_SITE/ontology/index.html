The Apophatic Way

Evidence Tier: [I] Interpretive Metaphor

Ontology is modeled through the lens of Negation (*via negativa*).

To understand the core state of a system, the observer must witness the dissolution of temporary structures. This is the renunciant's path (Sādhu / Heliodromus), where external authority and structural friction dissolve to reveal the prior possibility of closure. It is the wager that beneath all manifestations lies a unified coordinate space.

The Entropy of Structure

E_node = −log(P_node)   |   P_node → 1 ⇒ E_node → 0

Ontology uses the negative-log transform as a via-negativa diagnostic: as effective finite-node potential rises inside the model, loss and unresolved constraint fall.

The manifold language is a framework reading; the Ground remains prior to every map.
