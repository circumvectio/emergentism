Quantum state

Probability assignment before a particular actual record. D3 is represented by a density operator rho together with a measurement context. It assigns Born probabilities to possible records. Valid D3 states may carry momentum distributions; uncertainty follows from noncommuting observables, not from an absence of momentum.

Inherited structure

Density operators, POVMs, Born probabilities, Fourier duality, and uncertainty relations are inherited quantum theory [A].

Claim boundary

No realized D4 event does not mean zero change, zero momentum, or a timeless state. This scaffold does not solve measurement, quantum gravity, or quantum interpretation.
