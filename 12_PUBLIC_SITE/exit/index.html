The Guarantee

What K4 means

[S] Structural

Leave with what is yours

Your data, your keys, your relationships, your history — all portable. We do not hold your identity. We do not hold your assets. We do not hold your attention by friction.

[B] Bounded

No extraction, ever

Zero extraction — written η = 0. The organisation does not profit from your lock-in. Revenue comes only from value created, not from exit penalties or switching costs.

[S] Structural

Enforced by code

K4 is not a Terms of Service clause. Wherever possible, the exit path is built into the protocol. Self-custody is the default. Export is one click. No support ticket required.

[I] Interpretive

Grace, not mere permission

Grace Exit means more than "you may leave." It means the system is designed to help you leave well — with continuity, with dignity, with your full history intact.

[S] K4 is one of six constitutional fences (5 + 1): η = 0, K2, K3, K4, A7, Ω. See Five + One Constitution.

The Path

How to exit

1

Export your data

Every product and protocol provides a machine-readable export. No gate. No delay. No "contact support." The export format is documented, standard, and portable.

[B] Export paths are tested in CI on every release.
2

Rotate your keys

Self-custody means you hold the keys. Rotation is your right and your responsibility. We provide the tools; you control the timing.

[A] Cryptographic key ownership is non-negotiable.
3

Close without penalty

No exit fees. No minimum periods. No "your data will be deleted in 30 days" hostage clauses. Closure is immediate and complete.

[S] Penalty-free exit is a K4 compliance gate for every product release.
4

Return any time

The door swings both ways. Your export is your re-import. No loss of history. No re-verification treadmill. Grace is circular.

[I] Re-entry is not guaranteed by all products; portability is.
