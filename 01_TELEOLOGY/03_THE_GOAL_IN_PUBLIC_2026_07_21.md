# The Goal in Public

The canonical [Goal](00_THE_GOAL.md) already declares the vision, mission, and
`Ω` direction. This proposal neither restates nor amends them. It answers one
narrower question: **how should the public site serve that goal?**

## Objective

> Make the Emergentist lens operable by a stranger. On the public spine, every
> load-bearing claim must be directly checkable, linked to its source and kill
> criterion, or explicitly dated as conjectural, unresolved, superseded, or
> dead. Measure success in use and correction, never assent—and never by
> watching the reader.

The site is **a means**, not the only means. A book, paper, lesson, program, or
conversation can carry tiers and kill criteria. The site's special advantage
is that it can place prose, controls, computed consequences, source links, and
correction history in one revisable public surface. Whether that advantage
actually improves understanding or transfer is a testable `[C]` channel
hypothesis, not a constitutional truth.

The strategic defect is therefore not “too many pages” by itself. It is any
failure to distinguish:

- the short public spine from supporting depth;
- a working instrument from an illustration;
- a live claim from a wager or grave;
- local build evidence from public use and outside replication.

## Six falsifiable objectives

### O1 · One-sitting operation

A cold reader can reach a substantive instrument in at most two clicks from
the front door and operate it in under one minute without first accepting the
ontology.

**Failure:** observed cold readers cannot find the control, cannot explain what
changed, or must learn private vocabulary before acting.

### O2 · Checkable, traceable, or explicitly open

Every load-bearing spine claim has one visible disposition:

1. a direct calculation or interactive test;
2. a source, tier, prediction, and kill criterion;
3. an explicit unresolved/wager status; or
4. a dated correction, supersession, or death.

An instrument checks only the formal behavior it implements. It does not
validate the ontology, empirical fit, or interpretation surrounding it.

**Failure:** a claim receives authority from typography, animation, repetition,
traffic, or beauty rather than one of the four dispositions.

### O3 · Method without doctrine

A reader who rejects every Emergentist metaphysical wager can still use the
evidence tiers, type distinctions, kill criteria, receipts, and correction
discipline.

**Failure:** the method works only after assent to the worldview, or criticism
is reclassified as evidence for the worldview.

### O4 · Real exit

The exit remains prominent, unpenalized, and usable from every deep public
page. A reader may take a method, reject a doctrine, and leave without loss.

**Failure:** exit is hidden, moralized, obstructed, or treated as deficient
understanding.

### O5 · Non-extractive access

The public instruments remain accessible without payment, identity capture,
email capture, manipulative engagement loops, or coerced assent. This is a
chosen public-access policy `[I]`, not a theorem derived from the sphere.

**Failure:** access to checking becomes conditional on surrendering money,
identity, identity-level attention data, manipulatively prolonged attention, or
allegiance.

### O6 · Sustainable custody

The public instrument names who maintains it, the bounded time or budget
available, how costs are paid, and who can continue or close it without making
readers the product. This is a custody requirement, not a funding authorization
and not a command that the founder provide indefinite unpaid labour.

**Failure:** continuation depends indefinitely on invisible unpaid labour, has
no named successor or closure path, or funds itself by violating O5.

## Measures

Use neutral identifiers so this public objective cannot be confused with any
private governance vocabulary:

- `M1` — shortest click path from `/` to a substantive control; target
  `≤2`, computed from the release graph;
- `M2` — one-shot observer-timed operation by a consenting cold reader;
  target `<60 s`, with no persistent reader identifier;
- `M3` — fraction of load-bearing spine claims with one O2 disposition;
- `M4` — boundary comprehension in a consented usability session, reported
  only in aggregate or as an explicitly volunteered quotation;
- `M5` — publicly inspectable method transfer by people outside the project,
  including readers who reject the ontology;
- `M6` — dated corrections and deaths; a zero quarter triggers review, not an
  automatic presumption that a claim must be killed. The review and its result
  must themselves be dated.

Instrument count, page count, traffic, dwell time, return visits, conversion,
and agreement are not success measures by themselves. The release ships no
reader telemetry. Privacy-preserving, consented usability studies and voluntary
feedback replace persistent identity-level behavioral tracking.

## Strategy

1. Put the transferable practice first; use a small rack of substantive
   instruments to demonstrate the discipline locally.
2. Keep prose, sources, and graves adjacent to what each instrument actually
   checks.
3. Preserve archive and working material as provenance while keeping it below
   the release boundary.
4. Keep story, plain-language, formal, record, and exit entrances available;
   none is the one compulsory route.
5. Publish corrections as product features, not institutional embarrassment.
6. Broadcast is permitted; capture is not. Reach is never warrant.

## Fences

- A browser calculation is not external calibration or independent replication.
- A round-trip identity is not an independent test of itself.
- Simulator ground truth is not detector output.
- A beautiful visualization may increase assent without understanding; test
  comprehension and boundary recall.
- An instrument rack no one can transfer to a real problem is ornament, not
  mission success.
- A dated death quota would reward performative falsification. Zero deaths
  should trigger a review of test pressure, not manufacture a funeral.
- No accreditation, badge, ranking, certification body, paid interpretation,
  or authorized-interpreter class may be built around the method.
- The site cannot make Emergentism true. It can make particular claims easier
  to inspect, challenge, and correct.

## Kill criteria for this proposal

**Review date: 2027-01-21.** No outside-use data by that date counts against the
adoption hypothesis; it is not evidence that the worldview is false.

Withdraw or rewrite this objective layer if controlled usability work shows
that the interactive site does not improve operation, boundary recall, or
method transfer relative to serious rival media; if the instruments recruit
belief while hiding scope; if readers cannot separate the warrant discipline
from the doctrine; if non-extractive access is abandoned; or if no sustainable,
non-extractive custody line can be named.
