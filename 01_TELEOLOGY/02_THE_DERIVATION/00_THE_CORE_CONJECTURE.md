# The Core Conjecture — Viable Completion

> **[金] Kintsugi seam — a wager restored to wager form.** The earlier nucleus
> mixed chart identities, a forced product, a force↔dimension census, moral
> conclusions, and a world-level will. The useful intuition survives at its
> honest tier: finite agents can model futures, act toward some of them, receive
> consequences, and sometimes widen durable mutual options without extraction.

## In one sentence

> **Emergentism conjectures `[C]` that, wherever finite agency, feedback,
> memory, and real coupling persist, viable completion can be modeled as durable
> option-cone widening inside the Justice envelope; it does not claim that a
> fifth physical force, inevitable progress, or conscious cosmic will has been
> established.**

## The declared model

For a bearer `x`, Emergentism selects the normalized conjunctive instance

\[
P_x=\Phi_xV_x,
\qquad
W_x(T)=\int_0^T P_x(t)\,dt,
\]

where `Φ` is an operationalized measure of modeled foresight/coherence and
`V` an operationalized measure of embodied means/viability. The zero-factor
condition supports an AND-class family; it does not force multiplication.
Product, minimum, harmonic, CES, and other rivals must be compared in every
real domain.

The option cone is not the physical light cone. The physical cone remains
bounded by spacetime and `c`. An option cone is the subset of physically
admissible histories an agent can model, rank, coordinate, and reach.

## The constraint field

The feasible actions are not defined by a legacy status list. They are the
actions that satisfy the active five refusals and their typed accountability:

```text
no extraction
no unaccountable consequence
no silent erasure
no trapped exit
no infallibility
```

A consequential action therefore names principal, mandate, scope, consent,
custody, expiry or revocation, contest path, actor, and consequence bearers.
It also names every payer and beneficiary. No gain to an aggregate may launder
the destruction of an individual, sustaining whole, or exposed third bearer.

Let `\mathcal A_J` be the actions admissible under those conditions. When the
field and objective meet the stated existence conditions, Power-Max may be used
as a design objective:

\[
a^\star\in\operatorname*{arg\,max}_{a\in\mathcal A_J}
\mathbb E_{M_t}[W_i(T)\mid a].
\]

Justice defines the feasible field; optimization only chooses inside it. If the
field is empty, expectations are undefined, the objective is unbounded, or a
maximum is not attained, this expression licenses no selection. One-shot
extraction is the known counterexample to unconditional Power-Max.

## The dyad

For an individual `i` and sustaining whole `H`:

```text
Moral      iff durable H rises, i does not fall, and Justice holds
Ethical    iff durable i rises, H does not fall, and Justice holds
Syntropic  iff durable i and H both rise and Justice holds
```

These are Emergentist definitions `[I]`, not consequences of `φν=1`. The
strict syntropic case cannot be established by an anticipated benefit alone;
it requires outcome receipts. Voluntary sacrifice remains a distinct costly
class and may not be demanded as proof of virtue.

## The Soul Loop and the future

The public compression `F=M×A` is typed as `F_t=M_t⋆A_t`: a present model
containing represented futures couples with present physically admissible means
and can reweight present selection. This is model-mediated future influence,
not a physical signal from the future.

```text
model + option field -> selection + commitment receipt
action + environment -> outcome receipt
both receipts -> update map and selector
```

The selector cannot manufacture its own consequence. A future is realized only
through the world, other agents, constraints, and chance. Cognitive, execution,
and outcome gaps remain separately inspectable.

## F5 at its honest tier

`F5` is retained as an Emergentist name for the **teleological/ordering reading
of viable completion** `[I/C]`. It is not a fifth Standard Model interaction,
not a proven force↔dimension correspondence, and not evidence that the universe
is morally directed. In biological language, teleonomy, autopoiesis,
homeostasis, allostasis, niche construction, and selection remain the first
explanatory vocabularies.

The five candidate `μ`-crossings are a separate structural hypothesis. A
crossing must name saturation evidence, newly observed freedom, recovery of the
lower register, reduction status, prediction, and kill criterion. Missing
reduction is `currently_unreduced`, never proof of irreducibility. `D6` is not
a sixth positive freedom. The optional `r₆` reading compares D6's
post-articulate boundary role with D0's pre-articulate role; it asserts no
identity, recurrence, or general self-similarity.

## Predictions and kill criteria

1. **Necessary-factor intervention.** If `Φ` or `V` is claimed necessary,
   driving it toward zero should sharply reduce the local modeled outcome. If
   substitution routinely prevents collapse, the local AND-class claim fails.
2. **Aggregator comparison.** A rival that predicts and intervenes better than
   `ΦV` displaces the product for that domain.
3. **Represented-future intervention.** Changing represented futures should
   sometimes change present action distributions. Persistent specified nulls
   reject that coupling.
4. **Constraint value.** Comparable Justice-constrained couplings should show a
   durability advantage over extractive couplings across declared horizons.
   Reproducible contrary results defeat the viable-completion wager.
5. **New freedom.** A reproducible, irreducible positive freedom beyond the
   candidate D5 agency register breaks the five-crossing census.
6. **Physical-force discriminator.** No literal F5 claim may rise above `[C]`
   without a novel, quantitative discriminator independently reproduced beyond
   established physics.

## What survives defeat

If the world-level conjecture dies, the reciprocal chart remains mathematics,
the Soul Loop remains causal bookkeeping, the evidence tiers remain a useful
honesty discipline, and each successful local model keeps only its local
receipt. A creed that cannot lose is a cage; this conjecture is built to lose
cleanly.
