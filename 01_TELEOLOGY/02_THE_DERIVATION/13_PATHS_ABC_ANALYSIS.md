## 1. Path C: what the round-sphere spectrum actually says

On the round unit sphere, the scalar Laplacian has eigenvalues and
degeneracies

```text
lambda_l = l(l+1),
dim E_l = 2l+1,
l=0,1,2,...
```

Thus the first eigenspaces have dimensions `1,3,5,7,9,...`; no one of them has
dimension eight. This is a valid scalar-spectrum statement. It does not imply:

- the constant `l=0` scalar is a `U(1)` gauge connection;
- the `l=1` rotation triplet derives chiral `SU(2)_L` weak dynamics;
- the `l=2` scalar-harmonic sector is the spin-2 gravitational field;
- the lack of an eight-dimensional scalar eigenspace is a complete `SU(3)`
  obstruction for every enriched field theory on `S²`.

Equal dimensions are necessary in some proposed identifications but never
sufficient to supply a representation action, bundle, connection, curvature,
matter content, Lagrangian, coupling, anomaly cancellation, or observables.

## 2. Path A: what complex projective space actually says

For the Fubini–Study metric, `CP^n` has real dimension `2n`; its connected
holomorphic isometry group is `PU(n+1)`. Consequently `CP²` has a symmetry Lie
algebra isomorphic to `su(3)`. This is a mathematical resemblance to the color
gauge algebra, not a derivation of QCD.

Choosing `CP² x CP¹` as a six-real-dimensional internal space can be posed as a
Kaluza–Klein research input. Added to four-dimensional spacetime, it gives a
ten-dimensional starting geometry. The choice is not forced by the reciprocal
chart, the Titan metaframes, or the D-register scaffold; a symmetry-group match
does not determine the compactification spectrum, chiral matter, couplings, or
the Standard Model vacuum.

The old substrate checklist also did not uniquely select the projective family.
In particular, “algebraically closed” is a property of a base field, not by
itself a manifold-selection axiom. No current owner maps `D0…D6` to
`CP^0…CP^3`.

## 3. Path B: the long-chain fence

Liouville theory, worldsheet conformal field theory, critical-string dimension
counts, compactification, and four-dimensional gauge physics are distinct
theories joined only by explicit hypotheses. Naming them in sequence does not
establish the first bridge from Emergentism or select a Standard Model
compactification. The former chain remains a historical research prompt, not a
result.
