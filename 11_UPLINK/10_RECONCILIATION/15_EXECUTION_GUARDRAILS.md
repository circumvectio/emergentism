# 15_EXECUTION_GUARDRAILS

**Evidence tier:** [I]
*Organism document. Interpretive operational content. Bounded by current system state.*


This is the compressed operating surface for the organism's constitutional packet.

## The Rule Set

1. `F1` observes.
2. `F2` prices futures.
3. `F3` recommends.
4. `F4` executes.
5. `K2` signs.
6. `eta = 0` means no toll on access or participation.
7. `Grace Exit` means the door must remain real.

## The Core Clarifications

- APU does **not** decide in the sovereign sense. It recommends and prepares a `K2` envelope.
- The human or constitutionally authorized signer decides.
- `eta = 0` does **not** mean everything is free. Fees may exist on demonstrated value creation. Rent may not exist on access.
- `Chief of Staff synthesis` is the active term, not `Chairman synthesis`.

## Public-Safe Language

Use:

- `APU recommendation`
- `K2 envelope`
- `no access fee`
- `alpha-only performance participation`

Avoid:

- `APU decision`
- `everything is free`
- `2/20`
- `Chairman synthesis`

## Why It Matters

These are not style notes.
They are the guardrails that stop the organism from quietly violating its own constitution in language first, then in architecture.

Continuous recursive disambiguation applies here too:

- if a term starts carrying two meanings, clarify it
- if target-state language starts masquerading as current truth, restate time posture
- if a planning note starts sounding like execution, reclassify the surface before anyone builds from it
- if a downstream mirror carries the wrong guardrail, repair the owner first

Language drift is usually where constitutional drift begins.
