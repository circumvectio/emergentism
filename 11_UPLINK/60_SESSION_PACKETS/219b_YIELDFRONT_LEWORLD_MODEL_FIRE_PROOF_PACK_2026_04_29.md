## 5. Ranking Rule

Opportunity ranking is L3 Vaiśya work running on the LeWorldModel substrate, bounded by L7 Rishi Vision-setting and L1 BitNet routing.

Default ranking formula:

```text
opportunity_score =
  0.30 * constitutional_fit_score
+ 0.25 * risk_adjusted_return_score
+ 0.20 * liquidity_score
+ 0.15 * evidence_quality_score
+ 0.10 * niche_alignment_score
```

Hard refusals override the score:

- no receipt -> `REJECT`
- unknown counterparty risk -> `HOLD`
- opaque leverage or missing liquidation model -> `HOLD`
- unaudited contract / unverifiable substrate -> `HOLD`
- extractive fee path or non-API-PAY compute rail -> `REJECT`
- forbidden-category or substrate-harm route -> `REJECT`

The first proof defaults to **HOLD_UNKNOWN**. Nothing graduates to `CANDIDATE` without enough evidence for the watchmen to explain why.
