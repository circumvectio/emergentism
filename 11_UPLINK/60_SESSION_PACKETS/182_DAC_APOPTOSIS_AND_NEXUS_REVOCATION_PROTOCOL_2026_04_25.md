## 0. Axiomatic guard

This packet names **how a DAC dies cleanly**.

It is not:
- a legal liquidation procedure
- a securities, insolvency, or bankruptcy opinion
- an automatic token burn rule
- a Nexus confiscation power
- a SPECTRE authority claim
- a runtime implementation

It is a governance grammar: the minimum states, triggers, receipts, and limits required so a failed or captured DAC can leave the organism without becoming a zombie, parasite, or tumor.

---

## 1. Why this protocol exists

The current architecture specifies genesis:
- `01_FACTORY/` births a DAC
- `09_NEXUS/` licenses identity / A-anchor relation
- `12_COVENANT/` hash-matches η=0 + K2 + Three-Stage Process + K4
- `08_CORTEX/` records memory
- `04_CREDIT/` records debtor-creditor relations
- packet 181 Gate A names L7 as **SEVA**

But birth without death creates pathology. A DAC that no longer serves SEVA must have a clean exit path.

Without apoptosis:
- failed DACs remain visible as if active
- captured DACs can trade on inherited legitimacy
- abandoned wallets can accumulate unresolved claims
- Cortex records can become inaccessible or manipulable
- Nexus licensee status can persist after covenant breach
- members may exit individually while the DAC carcass remains operationally dangerous

Biology solves this with apoptosis: a cell dies without poisoning the body. SKYZAI_COM needs the organizational equivalent.

---

## 2. Core distinction: death is not punishment

**Apoptosis** is clean dissolution.

**Revocation** is removal of Nexus license / organism-recognition.

**Quarantine** is temporary containment while facts are established.

**Liquidation** is legal/financial settlement and requires the relevant jurisdictional process.

**Composting** is reuse of non-identity assets (patterns, templates, code, lessons, anonymized receipts) after identity death.

These must not be collapsed. A DAC can voluntarily enter apoptosis without covenant breach. A DAC can be quarantined without revocation. A DAC can have its Nexus license revoked while legal liquidation remains pending.

## 3. DAC lifecycle states

| State | Meaning | External posture |
|---|---|---|
| `ACTIVE` | DAC is operating under Nexus license and covenant | Listed as active |
| `WATCH` | Warning condition present; new-risk review required | Active with warning marker |
| `CURE` | Notice-and-cure window open | Restricted claims; cure clock visible |
| `QUARANTINE` | Material covenant risk; new obligations frozen | No new factory promotion, credit issuance, or DEX maker listings |
| `REVOKED` | Nexus license removed; organism no longer recognizes the DAC as a licensed cell | Not active; history remains visible |
| `DISSOLVING` | Voluntary or forced wind-down in progress | Settlement / memory / member-exit workflow active |
| `ARCHIVED` | DAC identity closed; Cortex final snapshot preserved | Read-only historical surface |
| `COMPOSTED` | Reusable non-identity material routed back to commons | No identity continuity |

State changes require OFN/Cortex receipts. No silent death. No silent resurrection.

## 4. Trigger classes

### 4.1 Voluntary sunset

The DAC sovereign signs a K2 dissolution act. Members receive notice. New obligations freeze. Existing debtor-creditor relations wind down.

This is clean apoptosis.

### 4.2 Dormancy

The DAC has no sovereign heartbeat, agent heartbeat, Cortex entries, member activity, or wallet activity for a defined dormancy window.

Dormancy does not prove breach. It triggers `WATCH`, then possible `CURE`.

### 4.3 Operational failure

The DAC cannot perform its stated function:
- agents fail repeatedly
- Cortex cannot record receipts
- wallet signers cannot reach quorum
- Three-Stage Process organs collapse into an unauditable process
- POS/DEX/credit flows cannot generate receipts

Operational failure triggers `WATCH` or `QUARANTINE` depending on severity.

### 4.4 Covenant breach

The DAC violates η=0 + K2 + Three-Stage Process + K4:
- extraction without reciprocal creation
- capture of member exit
- delegation of non-delegable K2 powers
- concealed wallet control
- Three-Stage Process collapse
- receipt suppression
- false Nexus/license representation

Covenant breach triggers `CURE` if remediable, `QUARANTINE` if active harm is possible, and `REVOKED` if uncured or severe.

### 4.5 Capture / carcinoma

A DAC becomes structurally hostile to organism-scale ΣΔP:
- local-P maximization at organism cost
- debt traps that defeat K4
- use of Skyzai legitimacy to extract outside the covenant
- coercive member retention
- agent/wallet takeover
- false SEVA posture

Capture triggers immediate `QUARANTINE`, then revocation review.

## 6. Notice-and-cure

Every non-emergency breach should pass through a cure window.

Minimum notice packet:
- DAC id and Nexus license id
- triggering evidence with receipt links
- breached covenant clause(s)
- risk class (`LOW`, `MATERIAL`, `SEVERE`)
- cure actions required
- cure deadline
- interim restrictions
- appeal route

Emergency quarantine is allowed only when continued operation can create irreversible harm: new debt issuance, wallet drain, member captivity, false public legitimacy, or receipt destruction.

Cure is successful only when new receipts demonstrate the breach has stopped and the repair is visible to the affected parties.

---

## 7. Nexus revocation

Nexus revocation is the act of removing organism-recognition.

It does:
- remove the DAC's licensed-child status
- remove active catalogue/promotion status
- mark the Nexus identity as revoked
- freeze claims that the DAC is a current Skyzai-recognized organism cell
- preserve historical receipts and the revocation receipt

It does not:
- seize assets
- erase debts
- destroy private legal rights
- rewrite Cortex history
- transfer equity into parent ZAI
- decide legal liquidation
- make SPECTRE a sovereign

Revocation is a boundary act, not a confiscation act.

## 8. Dissolution sequence

### 8.1 Freeze

On `DISSOLVING`, `QUARANTINE`, or `REVOKED`:
- no new debt-credit obligations
- no new DEX maker listings
- no new POS merchant onboarding
- no new agent mandates
- no new member lockups

Existing obligations remain visible.

### 8.2 Ledger close

The DAC's wallet and Cortex emit a closing ledger:
- cash / SKY balances
- outstanding debts
- outstanding credits
- unfilled DEX orders
- POS settlement batches
- member equity table
- unresolved disputes

This is an accounting surface, not a legal finality claim.

### 8.3 Member exit

K4 remains active through dissolution.

Members must be able to:
- export receipts
- exit mandates
- revoke agent delegations
- receive their wallet/accounting statement
- preserve evidence for legal claims

No dissolution path may make member exit harder than active operation.

### 8.4 Cortex final snapshot

The Cortex final snapshot is read-only and hash-anchored:
- public digest for organism memory
- private/redacted layer for protected data
- unresolved claims explicitly marked unresolved
- revocation/dissolution receipt included

The snapshot is not a cleansing ritual. It records the truth, including failures.

### 8.5 Compost

After identity closure, non-identity assets may return to commons:
- reusable code
- templates
- schemas
- sanitized receipts
- lessons learned
- failure patterns

The dead DAC's name, license, and covenant identity do not transfer. A successor must undergo fresh genesis.

## 9. Equity, debt, and open OQs

Packet 181 leaves OQ-EQUITY-1, OQ-EQUITY-2, and OQ-BOND-5 open.

Therefore this protocol does **not** decide:
- whether child equity is independent, parent-carved, or hybrid
- whether child equity cap is hard or parameterized
- whether credit auto-liquidation has a threshold
- whether local equity extinguishes, transfers, or converts at dissolution

Until those gates are signed, apoptosis only requires:
- freeze new obligations
- disclose current equity/debt table
- preserve receipts
- route disputes to the appropriate legal/contract process
- prevent false active-catalogue claims

No hidden tokenomics should be inferred from this packet.

## 10. SEVA test

A DAC remains organism-recognized only while it can state and evidence its SEVA:

> This DAC serves organism-scale ΣΔP by ________, bounded by η=0 + K2 + Three-Stage Process + K4.

Failure to articulate SEVA is not enough for revocation by itself. But failure to evidence SEVA under audit moves the DAC to `WATCH`.

False SEVA claims under active extraction move the DAC to `QUARANTINE`.
