A new DAC joining the organism inherits full commons on day one: shared compute (Menexus), shared workforce lineage (Polygenetic Agent Tree), shared knowledge (Open-Source), shared supplier relationships (collective procurement). **No cell builds HR, IT, R&D, or procurement in-house — ever.** The organism-whole provides them; attempting to build any of the four in-house would violate the covenant (η=0 requires no proprietary support chokepoint).

This is **constitutionally enforced**, not voluntary. Unlike platform cooperativism where support-commons is an aspiration, here it is an architectural prerequisite. A DAC *cannot* be a DAC and own a proprietary HR/IT/R&D/procurement function.

### 1.3 What this makes the DAC

**The leanest possible firm.** It carries only its primary activities in-house. Everything else is inherited / rented / shared from organism-commons.

Kinship with existing theory:
- **Commons-based peer production** (Benkler) — at constitutional layer, not just production layer

- **Cell biology** (cell carries metabolism + organelles; organism carries circulatory / immune / endocrine / structural systems) — DAC primary = cell-internal; DAC support = organism-systemic

The DAC is the cell; the organism provides the systems that no individual cell can carry.

## 2. The Marketing/Sales split

### 2.1 Why the split is constitutional, not cosmetic

Classical firms fuse them under "Marketing & Sales." That fusion is where most distortion happens — marketing over-promises, sales under-delivers, truth erodes, receipts become PR theatre. The fraud vector is *built into the org chart*.

**Sovereign-named split (2026-04-25, Yves):** *"marketing is separated from sales and marketing is public relations."*

- **Marketing = Truth.** Public truth-surface. Lives in Circle (publication / observation mesh) + Nostr (public identity) + public-OFN (publicly visible receipts) + Emergentism doctrine-layer (organism-scale narrative).
- **Sales = Exchange.** Counterparty-graph (Nexus) + membrane flow (Uplink/Downlink) + settlement rail (API PAY).

### 2.2 Truth-Gates-Marketing (sibling of Truth-Gates-Money)

Marketing-as-PR must pass a truth-gate the same way money does. The seventh-named Kernel Invariant set per packet 181 Gate B includes **Truth-Gates-Money**. This packet proposes the structurally implied sibling: **Truth-Gates-Marketing** (or simply: the truth-gate applies to all public organism speech, of which money-claims are one subset).

Promotional extraction is closed by architecture, not policy. Persuasion-through-distortion has no place to live because Marketing and Sales are separated organs and any divergence is visible in Cortex.

### 2.3 What the split prevents

Any divergence between Marketing's claim and Sales' delivery is **visible in Cortex** → becomes a **Service event** (mistake / dispute / rollback). The fraud vector that fuses persuasion-then-transaction is closed structurally. A DAC cannot quietly market one thing and deliver another; the receipts will dissent.

The four classical firm moats are removed by architecture, not by policy:

- **No "talent acquisition" competition** — HR is polygenetic lineage, not proprietary talent pool
- **No patent chokepoints** — Tech Dev is OSS, not proprietary IP
- **No exclusive compute** — Firm Infrastructure is Menexus, not private servers
- **No bilateral supplier extraction** — Procurement is collective through Circle + Nexus, not solo bargaining

Individual DACs **cannot** compete on proprietary HR/tech/infrastructure/supplier-access; the organism-whole provides all four as shared substrate. Each cell's unique contribution is its **Operations configuration only**. Strategy reduces to: do your particular Operations well; everything else is given.

This is η=0 at the value-chain layer. The proprietary moat-surfaces have been removed. There is no rent-seeking surface to extract through.
