## Executive Summary

This packet contains a comprehensive systems-architecture analysis of the Phoenician civilization as a **decentralized franchise model** that survived and thrived after the Bronze Age collapse. It frames the Phoenician achievement not merely as historical event but as **resilience engineering precedent** for decentralized network architecture.

This maps directly onto Emergentism's anti-capture framework: φ·ν=1 requires distributed coherence, not centralized extraction.
