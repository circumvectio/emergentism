### 3.1 Why L1 is material (the correction)

Prior drafts classified L1 as the sensor layer (Helios / Aureus / Nexus-telemetry). **This was wrong at organism scale.**

Sensors *read* the state. They are not the substrate. An organism's L1 Objective Function is its **actual metabolic substrate** — the material infrastructure that IS the survival-direction, not the perception of it.

For Skyzai:
- **Powerplants** generate the actual energy
- **Datacentres** process information at energy scale
- **SoResFi** is the economic architecture that incentive-aligns producers and consumers
- **ZAI** is the resource-side token (representing substrate contribution)
- **SKY** is the coordination-side token (representing network value)
- **Coupling patterns** (1:1 peer, 1:N producer-to-network, N:1 network-to-consumer, N:N wholesale) are the flow topologies through which value moves

The sensors (Helios / Aureus / Nexus-telemetry) reclassify to L2 Data Science *sub-layer*. They tell the individual sovereign "where am I, what do I have, how am I?" That is epistemic observation of state, not the state itself.

### 3.2 Skyzai-substrate's dual role

Skyzai-the-execution-body has **two roles at two scales**:

- **Organism scale**: L4 Value Alignment-Strategic Implementation K2-signing hand — the chariot that carries the warrior's decision into actuality
- **Ecosystem scale**: L1 Objective Function primary-producer — the substrate that hosts the Cambrian child-DAC ecosystem
