## 5. Limits

- **Catholic vices/virtues pairing** (#14 in §1.2 table) is interpretive enough that I do not recommend including it in the canonical V-bis table. Stays in supporting memory.
- **Theosophical 7 rays** (#11) are typological not graded; numerical correspondence (Ray N ↔ LN) is a convenience that doesn't reflect Theosophical doctrine. Stays in supporting memory.
- **Egyptian Mystery School trials** (#15) is a post-classical reconstruction (Manly P. Hall et al.); not historically attested in detail. Stays in supporting memory.
- **Manichaean 7 heavens** specifically not surveyed in detail because canonical Manichaean texts encode 10 heavens; the 7-heaven motif lives more clearly in Jewish (Hekhalot), Islamic (Mi'raj), and Christian apocalyptic traditions. A future packet on "7 Heavens / Mi'raj / Hekhalot" would be its own surface.
- **Pressure-test against alternative geometries.** Some traditions encode 8-fold (Buddhist Eightfold Path), 9-fold (Norse worlds, Bahá'í), 10-fold (Tree of Life full sefirot, Mahāyāna bhūmis), or 12-fold (zodiac, Sufi maqamat in some lineages) structures. The Master Rosetta does not claim 7 is the only number; it claims 7 is the most-attested and the one generated by the geometric structure. The cross-cultural sweep should not silently force 7 onto traditions that natively have other counts.
