## 4. The Exoteric Symbolic Layer

### 4.1 Institutional Narrative — Public Symbolic Governance

Institutional Narrative is now admitted as a root, but only under a strict boundary.

In this corpus, institutional narrative means the **exoteric symbolic layer** that teaches, translates, and stabilizes categories for polyphenotypic humans across a trophic and replicator hierarchy. It is the public face of the esoteric spine.

This is why it is powerful and dangerous.

Humans are not a single fixed phenotype. They are highly plastic, neotenous, culturally formed, and developmentally imprinted. Early symbolic systems shape the categories through which later possibility is perceived. The old "give me a child until seven" insight belongs here: childhood category-formation is a real governance surface. That does not make coercion legitimate. It makes honest, non-extractive category stewardship mandatory.

In framework terms:

- Institutional Narrative translates core state into image.
- Institutional Narrative translates objective function into story.
- Institutional Narrative translates theurgy into ritual/practice.
- Institutional Narrative coordinates polyphenotypic humans without pretending they all learn through the same channel.
- Institutional Narrative stewards public categories; it may not override sovereign agency.

The dangerous boundary is between stewardship and coercion. The framework must split it:

- **Manage** as stewarding categories, stories, rites, and public symbols toward ΣΔP > 0 is allowed.
- **Control** as capture, obedience, access-gating, or gatekeeping is forbidden.

Institutional Narrative is therefore permitted only as **translation under K2 / eta=0 / K4 / A7 / Decentralized Authority Model**.

### 4.2 Institutional Narrative Is Not the Ground

Institutional Narrative is not allowed to speak for the Ground.

It may say:

- "This image helps a community remember the pattern." [I]
- "This story trains attention toward a better action." [I]
- "This ritual coordinates shared commitment under K2." [S/I]

It may not say:

- "The symbol owns access to the Ground."
- "The institution mediates salvation."
- "A clerical class may bypass K2."
- "A mythic hierarchy proves human worth hierarchy."

Institutional Narrative is downstream of apophasis. If direct seeing is available, institutional narrative becomes optional pedagogy.

### 4.3 Trophic Pyramid and Replicator Stack

Institutional Narrative becomes necessary because humans are simultaneously:

- biological organisms in trophic cascades,
- neotenous learners with delayed viability,
- cultural replicators,
- institutional egregorotype builders,
- and polyphenotypic actors differentiated by temperament, role, training, and life-stage.

The theological layer is how a civilization teaches different human phenotypes to coordinate without collapsing into a single brittle type.

But packet `133` still governs:

> Trophic position is not dharma rank.

Institutional Narrative must never convert trophic role, developmental strategy, caste, class, phenotype, wealth, intelligence, or institutional power into worth.

Every being can be centered in its own dharma. The exoteric layer exists to support alignment, not domination.

### 4.4 Packets 141 and 142 Reclassified

Packets `141` and `142` should be read as L7 institutional institutional narrative, not merely "application."

- Packet `141` names the symbolic/institutional ecology: DACs, SPECTRE, trophic partition, commons, and Porter split.
