## 4. Coherence fixes made in the model

The model is now cleaner in five places:

1. **Name layer:** brand, source path, chapter code, reserved future product, and historical provenance are distinct categories.
2. **Authority layer:** portfolio-minority venture authority is separated from organism-native K2 authority.
3. **Commercial layer:** Agentz is a tokenization operator with Academy / Circle / briefing products as relationship-moat surfaces, not the whole business.
4. **Money membrane:** QNTM (the institutional MPC/ZK-Identity rail) can become a money-side counterparty later without making Agentz a bank project or QNTM (the institutional MPC/ZK-Identity rail) an Agentz chapter.
5. **Evidence layer:** compressed Uplink prose is explicitly subordinate to source documents and runtime-truth boundaries.
