## 4. Specific Corrections To Common Drift

### 4.1 AIA

Correct:

> AIA is the Automated Information Architect: the gardener / porter engine that
> organizes information, proposes structures, and prepares decision surfaces.

Incorrect:

> AIA is the Artificial Immune Architecture.

The organism has immune functions, but AIA is not the whole immune system. AIA
may help surface contradictions; Cortex may remember and witness them; SPECTRE
is the graph-immune design lane for routing and selection once earned.

### 4.2 SPECTRE / BEAM

Correct:

> The BEAM SPECTRE package has a canon-aligned route-energy kernel and closed
> replay contract in controlled prototype conditions.

Incorrect:

> SPECTRE is live as a distributed immune network.

Packet 110 and Runtime Truth authorize prototype-real language. Packet 47 is a
planning specification for the future full immune system.

### 4.3 VMOSK / Cortex

Correct:

> VMOSK-A says what should be true across timescales. Cortex stores the proof,
> recalls context, surfaces drift, and raises review obligations.

Incorrect:

> VMOSK is packet 39 or the cognitive substrate interface.

Packet 39 governs the interface between reasoning warrant and structural
warrant. VMOSK-A is the replicator stack itself.

### 4.4 Skyzai / K2

Correct:

> Skyzai executes after lawful authority and K2-boundary satisfaction.

Incorrect:

> Skyzai or its agents can commit irreversible action because the model judged
> the path correct.

AI remains the charioteer. Mortal signature authority remains the warrior.
