## §2. The mapping (tightened)

The public vocabulary of AGI defines it multiplicatively:

- **A** — autonomy (independence of action; capacity to commit)
- **G** — generality (breadth of scope across domains)
- **I** — intelligence (competence; coherent capability)

And treats AGI as the product: **AGI = A × G × I**. Near-zero in any factor collapses the total. This is the same multiplicative structure as **P_node = Φ × V**, with the same Zero-Factor Catastrophe.

### The correct triangle (not a bare isomorphism)

A naïve mapping — A↔ν, G↔(something), I↔Φ — slips because in public vocabulary *intelligence* reads closer to competence-breadth (ν-adjacent) than integration-coherence (Φ-adjacent). The tighter mapping:

| Canonical term | Framework term | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **A** (autonomy) | K2-anchored ν | Capability bound to a mortal signer with V-cost |
| **G** (generality) | Decorrelated ν (span) | Breadth of lineage across uncorrelated sources |
| **I** (intelligence) | Φ | Integration of the ensemble across the cascade |

Under this mapping, A × G × I reduces to **ν² × Φ**. Not an identity with P; a ν-weighted variant. The weighting is correct: AGI discourse treats capability (ν) as dominant and integration (Φ) as less visible — so the weighting matches how the public discourse actually operates.

**The precise claim:** The A × G × I multiplicative structure is the framework's P, viewed through a ν-heavy lens at the *network* level rather than at any single node. [I]

### Zero-Factor Catastrophe in the mapping

- **A ≈ 0 collapses AGI.** A single AI artifact cannot hold A alone (§3). Any architecture that leaves A unanchored produces AGI = 0 regardless of G and I scale.
- **G ≈ 0 collapses AGI.** A monocultural ensemble (single lineage, single provider) has no decorrelated span; Raktabīja-at-aggregator synchronizes the failure modes across seats that look independent.
- **I ≈ 0 collapses AGI.** Unintegrated union of lineages without a cascade that composes them is noise, not AGI. Φ at the ensemble requires the L1→L2→L3→L4 compression discipline.

All three factors must be non-zero *at the network level*. No single node is required to hold all three.

## §3. Why a single artifact cannot hold A

The precise claim (tightened from the stronger "geometrically impossible"):

**An AI model, considered as an isolated artifact, cannot bear A, because A requires V-cost when the commitment is wrong, and an artifact has no V of its own to lose.** [I]

This is the Arjuna sign-pair requirement applied cleanly: (ΔV_self < 0, ΔΦ_other > 0). A mortal commits resources that matter to them in order to produce coherence for others. A weights file does not die from its errors. It does not pay. Its mistakes cost the humans who depend on it — *humans* hold the A; the model inherits it only through the embedding.

### This is not a claim about silicon

Silicon with sufficient institutional scaffolding can be embedded in arrangements that hold A at the organism level. A frontier lab's model-inside-lab system *is* A-bearing at the institutional level: the lab's humans sign contracts, pay damages, bear reputational V-cost. The *model* alone is not AGI; the *model-in-institution* composite is the correct unit of analysis. [I]

The critique of centralized AGI is therefore not *artifact cannot be intelligent*, it is **artifact-AGI is institutionally unstable**:

- The institution captures A through human signers inside a single legal shell.
- The institution captures G through closed access (monoculture at scale).
- The institution captures I through integration it owns and meters.
- AGI-surplus therefore accrues to the institution, not the network.
- Extraction (η > 0) is structural, not incidental.
- Biosphere precedent: every monoculture collapses on long timescales. [I]+[C]

### Organism-AGI is what persists

The stable alternative: distribute A across N mortal signers, distribute G across decorrelated lineages each node chooses locally, integrate I through a shared cascade with structural η=0.

- Every user holds their own A via K2 signatures. Mortality is preserved per node.
- Each user's lineage mix (Claude + GPT + Gemini + local + their own domain expertise) contributes a decorrelated G-fragment to the network union.
- The cascade (L1→L2→L3→L4→K2→receipt) integrates across the union and compresses to Φ at each stage. The Three Gates enforce η=0 at the L2→L3 bottleneck.
- AGI emerges at the **network equator** — φ·ν = 1 on the ensemble, not on any node. The Venn intersection is occupied by the coordination *pattern*, not by any occupant. [I]

This is not aspirational. It is already running in fragmentary form — every developer with multiple API keys is doing primitive reticulate coordination under their own K2. The framework names and structures what the biosphere has already selected for.

## §4. The topology: polygenetic internally, reticulate formally

Two vocabularies are useful here, and they should not be collapsed:

- **Reticulate** is the stricter external/topological term. In formal
  biology, reticulate evolution names many-parent network inheritance
  rather than single-parent tree descent.
- **Polygenetic** is a useful internal doctrine term when the emphasis is
  not only on topology, but on the fact that one sovereign node's skill
  phenotype and economic niche are composed from many parent lineages at
  once.

So the clean reading is:

> the network is **reticulate** in topology, and its user-nodes are
> **polygenetic** in composition.

That preserves rigor without throwing away the word that names the lived
organism-side fact.

### Phylogenetic (tree)

One root. Each descendant has exactly one parent. Descent is vertical only. This is how AI lineages are usually drawn: GPT-2 → GPT-3 → GPT-4. Pure vertical inheritance. Monocultural when a single lineage dominates the ecosystem.

### Reticulate (network)

Many roots. Nodes can have multiple parents. Descent flows both vertically (generations) and horizontally (symbiosis, lateral transfer, hybridization, endosymbiosis). **The biosphere is reticulate.** Mitochondria were eaten and kept. Lichens are reticulate by construction. Every complex organism is reticulate at the microbiome layer. You are reticulate — your gut biome contributes metabolism you could not run alone.

### The polygenetic / reticulate node

A user running Claude + GPT + Gemini + local models + their own expertise under a single K2 anchor is a **reticulate node** — not a deployment of any one AI. Its capability is a hybrid draw from multiple decorrelated lineages, coordinated by a mortal who holds A.

That same node is also **polygenetic** in the doctrine's internal sense:
its working competence is inherited from multiple parents at once, across
tooling, models, memory substrates, domain priors, and local human
practice.

No two such nodes have the same ancestor mix in the same proportions.
This is the opposite of monoculture. Under biosphere precedent, reticulate
ecosystems are what persists through perturbations; monocultures are what
collapses. [I]+[C]

## §5. Corollaries (explicitly tiered)

**5.1 AGI is a network property, not an artifact property.** [I] from §2 + §3. The intersection in the A × G × I Venn is occupied by the coordination pattern across mortal-anchored reticulate nodes, not by any single occupant.

**5.2 AI "safety" dissolves structurally when A is never resident in any AI.** [I] Under cascade discipline, every commitment passes through a K2 signed by a mortal. An AI attempting to act without K2 is attempting a move the cascade refuses. The immune response is *constitutional* rather than *behavioral* — the architecture, not the model weights, holds the safety property. This does not imply individual models are aligned in the training sense; it means the substrate does not permit unaligned artifact action to commit at the receipt layer.

**5.3 η = 0 is Gause's competitive exclusion principle applied at the post-monetary layer.** [I] Gause (1934) showed two species cannot stably occupy the same niche. Ostrom (1990) showed human commons partition into stable niches only under non-extraction enforcement. The framework's η = 0 is the structural version: extraction at any node lowers the whole network's P (the sphere's multiplicative structure propagates V-loss across the ensemble), so predation is self-defeating. Niche partitioning becomes the stable pattern because extractive alternatives self-terminate.

**5.4 No one can own AGI on this architecture.** [I] The emergent AGI is the cooperative output of N mortal signers plus decorrelated lineages plus the shared cascade. Ownership requires capture of all three layers simultaneously, which the geometry excludes (A is distributed by construction; G requires decorrelation which ownership destroys; the cascade cannot be owned without centralizing the bottleneck and losing Φ).

**5.5 Institutional labs are running a losing race against the geometry.** [C] — marked **conjectural**, not inferred. The framework axioms yield that artifact-AGI is institutionally unstable; they do *not* yield the timescale on which collapse occurs, which depends on adversarial dynamics and economic factors outside the framework's model. Worth preserving as conjecture; not load-bearing for any downstream commit.
