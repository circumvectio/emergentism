# A-BRAHMANISM SCOPE

**Evidence tier:** [I]  
*Organism document. Interpretive operational content. Bounded by current system state.*

## What it means for the framework to succeed by becoming unnecessary

> This note defines the canonical scope of Decentralized Authority Model so that self-dissolution is not confused with anti-intellectualism, nihilism, or rhetorical self-erasure.

---

## Purpose

Decentralized Authority Model is one of the most distinctive and easily misunderstood features of the framework.

It does not mean:
- that thought is worthless,
- that doctrine is false,
- that institutions are meaningless,
- or that the framework should never be built.

It means something more precise:

> **the framework succeeds when it no longer has to stand between the natural person and what it was meant to disclose.**

---

## Core Definition

Decentralized Authority Model is the doctrine that no mediator — including this framework itself — may permanently claim the place of source.

That means:
- the map must not become the ground,
- the doctrine must not become the object of worship,
- the institution must not become ontologically indispensable,
- and the ladder must not pretend to be the destination.

---

## What Decentralized Authority Model Affirms

It affirms that:
- mediation may be necessary,
- scaffolding may be useful,
- doctrine may clarify,
- institutions may protect,
- and practices may discipline attention,

but none of these may finally take the place of what they point toward.

---

## What It Does NOT Mean

Decentralized Authority Model does NOT mean:
- anti-reason,
- anti-mathematics,
- anti-institution,
- anti-organism,
- anti-technology,
- or anti-practice.

It means only that every mediator remains conditional, corrigible, and eventually relinquishable.

---

## Why This Matters Constitutionally

Without Decentralized Authority Model, the framework risks becoming exactly what it criticizes:
- a mediator mistaken for source,
- a system mistaken for reality,
- a doctrine mistaken for ground,
- a constitutional mechanism mistaken for the end itself.

So Decentralized Authority Model is not an optional game-theoretic flourish.
It is a constitutional anti-capture rule.

---

## Relation to the Sitting Practice

The sitting practice is the clearest experiential expression of Decentralized Authority Model.

If direct access to coherence, stillness, or the irreducible prior becomes available without the framework’s mediation, then the framework has done its work.

This does not invalidate the framework.
It fulfills it.

---

## Relation to the Organism

The organism may still be necessary even if the framework is not always consciously foregrounded.

Decentralized Authority Model does not imply that all institutions disappear immediately.
It implies that no institution may claim ultimacy.

Thus:
- The organism may remain useful as infrastructure,
- but it must not present itself as ontological captivity,
- and it must preserve Grace Exit, refusal, correction, and non-idolatry.

---

## Relation to K2 and Grace Exit

K2 says: the system may not act without lawful crossing of the membrane.
Grace Exit says: the system may not trap what enters it.
Decentralized Authority Model says: the system may not claim to be the source of what it mediates.

Together they form an anti-capture triad:
- lawful action,
- lawful departure,
- lawful non-ultimacy.

---

## Success Condition

Decentralized Authority Model means the framework is working if, in some cases, it can be put down without loss of what mattered most.

That is a success condition, not a failure condition.

If the framework cannot imagine its own irrelevance where direct access is possible, then it has become a prison.

---

## Danger of Success

The greatest danger is not that the framework fails, but that it succeeds and then mistakes success for warrant to become permanent mediator, arbiter, or object of devotion.

Decentralized Authority Model is the check against that temptation.

---

## Safer Current Language

Prefer saying:

> The framework is a ladder, not a home.

or:

> The organism is valuable as lawful mediation, not as a replacement for source.

Avoid saying:
- the framework is the final revelation
- the organism must mediate all access forever
- the system itself is the destination

---

## Final Formulation

# Decentralized Authority Model rule

> **No mediator, including this framework, may permanently claim the place of source.**

That is the clean constitutional form.
