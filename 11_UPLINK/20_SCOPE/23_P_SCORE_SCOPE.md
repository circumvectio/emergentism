# P-SCORE SCOPE

**Evidence tier:** [I]  
*Organism document. Interpretive operational content. Bounded by current system state.*

## What organism P means, what it does not mean, and how it may move honestly

> This note defines the canonical scope of P-scores so they remain useful signals rather than sources of false precision.

---

## Purpose

P-scores are meant to summarize organismal maturity, viability, and integration in a compact form.
They are useful only if they remain subordinate to implementation truth and explicit gate passage.

This note exists to prevent three failures:
- score inflation,
- formula drift,
- and treating heuristic values as if they were mechanically measured constants.

---

## Core Rule

> **P-scores are governance signals, not ontological facts.**

> **They move by earned gate passage, not by calendar optimism or rhetorical enthusiasm.**

---

## Canonical Variables

The current shorthand remains:
- `P = Φ × V`

Where, at minimum:
- `Φ` refers to coherence / structural integration / epistemic-organismal order
- `V` refers to viability / operational capability / actually working life-supporting function

These should be read as compressed governance variables, not directly observed primitives.

---

## What a P-score IS

A P-score is:
- a compact maturity signal,
- a coordination aid,
- a way of tracking relative organismal condition,
- a summary of how much of the system is both coherent and alive.

It is useful for:
- state reporting,
- sprint gating,
- strategic sequencing,
- and identifying Liebig minimums.

---

## What a P-score is NOT

A P-score is not:
- a substitute for receipts,
- a substitute for deployment proof,
- a proof of core state,
- an excuse for overclaim,
- or a precision metric independent of judgment.

It must not be treated as if a reported number automatically outranks code, contracts, or live runtime evidence.

---

## Gate-Earned Movement

P should move only when real thresholds are crossed.

Examples of legitimate movement:
- an organ begins breathing in reality rather than only in specification,
- a canonical test suite passes,
- a deploy receipt exists,
- a live trace closes with receipt or honorable refusal,
- a blocked organ becomes truly usable in the loop.

Examples of illegitimate movement:
- optimism,
- naming inflation,
- architecture diagrams ahead of implementation,
- unresolved contradictions being ignored,
- dates passing on a roadmap without gates actually passing.

---

## Formula Discipline

If one formula is used in state surfaces, it must be explicit.

The project must not silently alternate between:
- geometric mean summaries,
- heuristic intuition,
- hand-updated values,
- and rhetorical rounding,
without saying which one is being used.

Therefore every public or canonical P-score surface should specify:
1. the formula used,
2. the source of inputs,
3. whether values are measured, estimated, or judgment-based,
4. the date of last reconciliation.

---

## Current Best Practice

The safest current reading is:

> **Use P as a governance and maturity indicator anchored to explicit gate passage, not as a pseudo-objective scientific constant.**

This means:
- sprint gates outrank optimistic projections,
- receipts outrank mood,
- live code outranks architecture aspiration,
- and reconciliation outranks prestige.

---

## Confidence Bands

Where possible, P should be spoken of in bands or approximate ranges rather than false decimal exactness unless the inputs are genuinely measured and reproducible.

Examples:
- `P ≈ 0.40`
- `P > 0.45 if Sprint 1 gate passes`
- `organ-level P remains partial pending deploy proof`

This is often more honest than pretending to know a value to two decimal places when the underlying inputs still involve judgment.

---

## Relationship to Sprint and State Docs

P-score movement should be synchronized across:
- `09_STATE.md`,
- `P-SCORES.md`,
- sprint and roadmap surfaces,
- reconciliation and freeze notes where relevant.

No single document should drift into its own private P reality.

---

## Current Canonical Statement

# P-score rule

> **P-scores summarize earned organismal maturity. They move by receipts and passed gates, not by aspiration.**

That is the clean constitutional form.
