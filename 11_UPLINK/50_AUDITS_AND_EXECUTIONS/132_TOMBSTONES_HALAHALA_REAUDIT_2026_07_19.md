## 5 · Final adjudication

All twelve candidate tombstones contain a genuinely dead strong form. Several
needed material narrowing or a stronger account of the surviving register:

- `D6≡D0` is dead as untyped ontological identity, not as every quotient-model
  equation;
- the foursome is honest only under C21’s fixed-point-inclusive frame-closure,
  not plain orbit closure;
- the signed three-substrate universal remains dead, but “martyr” alone is not
  a complete formal counterexample to substrate cardinality;
- the universal frame/operand ban dies while the unary removable-extension
  result remains valid;
- planetary association is verified and is a confound against
  convergence-as-proof, but is not itself proof of diffusion or causal
  genealogy.

Two combined different deaths and should be split: **seven forcedness** and
**Rosetta universality**. The mirror grammar remains a positive result.
