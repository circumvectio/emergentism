## Authority and safety boundary

The contribution surface is intentionally static. It does not contain a form,
payment integration, credential field, inference endpoint, or job queue. It
warns visitors never to paste or send a personal API key. A future compute
broker requires a separate server-side project, hard cost and tool limits,
retention controls, expiry, revocation, publication rules, and consequence
receipt.

This boundary was checked against current provider guidance: OpenAI states that
keys should not be shared or deployed in browsers, and Google likewise directs
production clients through protected server-side storage or a backend proxy:

- <https://help.openai.com/en/articles/5112595-best-practices-for-api-key-safety>
- <https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/api-key>

Sponsors fund search capacity only. They cannot choose a verdict, suppress a
null, or raise an evidence tier. Frontier-model output remains raw intake until
its native sources, definitions, assumptions, rivals, discriminator, kill,
survivor, provenance, cost, and review path are inspectable.
