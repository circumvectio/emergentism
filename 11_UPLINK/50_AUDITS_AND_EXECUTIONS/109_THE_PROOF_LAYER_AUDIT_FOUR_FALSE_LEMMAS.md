# 109 · The Proof-Layer Audit — four false lemmas the tiers did not catch

> Receipt 108 barred a fourth *coherence* audit and was right to: logic cannot
> separate `×` from `min`. But 108 itself named the one exception — *"only a
> **fact** can."* This pass looked only for facts: hand-checkable arithmetic and
> topology errors in the supporting lemmas. It found **four**, plus ten
> register-overreaches. **The spine holds at its honest tiers; four proofs under
> it are simply wrong and need repair, not re-tiering.**

## 1. What survives (stated first, per A7)

The load-bearing spine survived the attack at exactly the tiers canon assigns it:
`φ·ν = 1` as a **posited** conservation identity (not a theorem — canon already
says so); the **AM-GM** equatorial optimum `[A]` on the additive-budget reading;
the **bounded-vs-divergent** extraction distinction (the categorical break holds
even where the "attractor" wording does not — see §2.2); the **Möbius/Hopf**
frame closure; the **is/ought CONVERGENCE** at `[S/I]`; and **`η = 0` as a derived
conditional GATE** — untouched, because it never depended on the broken proof
below (§2.1). The framework does **not** collapse. The disease is one signature,
already 108's signature: *an earned weaker result written one modal notch too high.*

## 2. The four false lemmas (`[S]`, each hand-verifiable — the fact-class)

**[金] 2.1 · The strongest break — triadic N=3 "uniqueness = QED"**
`11_EFR_TRIADIC_STABILITY.md:163–182` + `21_TRIADIC_STABILITY_CORRESPONDENCE.md`.
**crack:** the `N≥5` branch of "N=3 is the **unique** stable count, QED" is
discharged solely by the lemma (11:165) *"any group with N≥4 elements has proper
subgroups."* **This is false.** `ℤ₅` (any prime-order group) has order ≥ 4 and, by
Lagrange, **no** nontrivial proper subgroup. One counterexample voids the
universal → voids the discharge → voids "QED." Compounded: P3 calls **S₃ "three
elements"** (S₃ has six). Doc 21's independent route stipulates `0×∞ = 1` into a
frame-algebra it **exempts from associativity** (21:87) so the value can be
assigned, then reads `1` back out as "the actual content of the theorem" —
*petitio principii*. **seam:** this is **spine-adjacent** (cited as "the
foundation of η=0"), but **η=0 does not fall** — canon already carries it as a
conditional gate (108 §1; extraction law; ΣΔP). What falls is only *"three is
uniquely forced, therefore extraction is impossible-by-count."* **Stage:** demote
the N=3 uniqueness proof `[S]→[C]`; re-anchor η=0's citation on the gate
derivation, not the count.

**[金] 2.2 · The Lotka–Volterra "attractor"** — `12_EFR_EXTRACTION_COEFFICIENT.md`.
**crack:** the classical LV system (12:107–108) is **conservative** — first
integral `V = δx − γ ln x + βy − α ln y` constant on every orbit → the interior
equilibrium is a **neutrally-stable centre** (purely imaginary eigenvalues,
closed periodic orbits), which has **no attractor and no basin**. So 12:135
`[S]` *"creates a basin of attraction"* and 12:165 *"converges to a finite
equilibrium"* are **false for the very equations proved over**; `η_sys` is
periodic and never converges — only its **time-average** = 1 (the LV averaging
identity). **seam:** the *categorical* bounded-vs-divergent break (§1) survives;
the word **"attractor" does not.** **Stage:** replace with "conservative centre;
`⟨η_sys⟩ = 1` by time-average."

**[金] 2.3 · S9 — "all paths on S² are helical, Proved from S² topology"**
`00_THE_HONEST_POSITION.md:154`. **crack:** universal impossibility ("you cannot
change latitude without rotating") at the strongest register. A **meridian**
(dφ = 0) changes latitude pole-to-pole with **zero** azimuthal rotation and is a
great-circle geodesic — the paradigm **non-helix**. One existential
counterexample refutes the universal. **Stage:** restrict to non-meridian paths,
or drop the universal + the "Proved" tag.

**[金] 2.4 · S2 — "algebraic closure O5 ⟹ simple-connectedness O3, Proved"**
`00_THE_HONEST_POSITION.md` S2 + `03_METHODOLOGY/00_WHY_THESE_AXIOMS.md`.
**crack:** false. A **torus** (genus 1) is a smooth projective algebraic curve
over `ℂ` (algebraically closed), compact and orientable — it satisfies O1/O2/O5
yet `π₁ = ℤ² ≠ 0`, violating O3. Only genus 0 is simply connected, and "algebraic
closure" does not force genus 0. Sibling row S1 shows the real elimination needs
**O4 (duality) + a minimal-dimension clause.** **Stage:** demote "Proved";
attribute the genus-0 cut to O4, not O5.
