## Proposition on trial (P)

> "Humans are polyphenic/polyphenotypic in the way ant castes are: humans divide into (hereditary/biological) castes that CORRESPOND to (a) the replicator stack Genotype → Epigenotype → Phenotype → Extended Phenotype → Memotype → Egregoreotype, and (b) the Rosetta L-rows (Śūdra..Ṛṣi / L2..L7). This should be admitted to the Emergentist canon as a discovery."

Standing posture: **refused.** Burden was on the amendment to overturn the standing ruling. It did not.

## Verdict: **REFUSED_WITH_FENCED_SURVIVOR** — consistent with KSC-24

P as filed is **refused** on four independent, each-sufficient grounds. The non-hereditary functional reading P strips down to **is already canon** (KSC-24 + ROSETTA_REPLICATOR § Polyphenotypic) and survives. Refusing P subtracts only the capture.

### Why P is refused

1. **Biology — [A]/[B], decisive.** The definitional core of caste polyphenism is a reproductive/sterile morph fixed by a developmental switch in a critical window. Humans have no reproductive caste, no discrete morphological caste, no caste-determination switch, no haplodiploidy, and are not eusocial under the strict definition. Human variation is continuous, polygenic, GxE-plastic, reversible, socially mobile; neoteny/retained plasticity is the **antithesis** of caste canalization.
2. **Category error — [S].** The replicator stack is a **within-organism** decomposition (every human instantiates all six layers at once — MF-281). P reads it as a **between-persons** partition (one layer per person). A decomposition-of-one cannot underwrite a taxonomy-of-many.
3. **Tier violation — [S].** "Admit as a discovery" is a silent **[I]→[B]** promotion barred by KSC-12 and the Master Rosetta non-transfer laws; no observation/test/custody exists to promote to.
4. **Kill Criterion #4 self-trips — [S].** P coheres **only** if the sphere is read as a hereditary ladder — the fence's own defined failure condition: *"refused, not repaired."*

Ratchet: equating a socially-enforced hereditary status system with a biological polyphenism is the category error that historically underwrote scientific racism. This **raises** the bar. **The epistemics is the ethics.**

Both prosecutors reached refuse; **both defenders conceded literal P is indefensible** and defended only a de-reified reformulation — which is the already-live functional reading, not P.

## Preserved dissent (Refusal 3 — no silent erasure)

The canon half-builds the reading: ROSETTA_REPLICATOR uses "polyphenotypic" in its own voice, and the Limit-B packet (CLOSED 2026-04-23) blessed a biological **Reading II** as "equally structural," with that document declared "Reading II, refined." Honest dissent: refusing P can look like the framework flinching from its own logic. **Answer:** Reading II is admitted only as a biological **column of layers within one organism** and only at `[I]`; the person-reading is fenced verbatim as "functional rather than taxonomic… not a biological classification." P is Reading II **plus** the two operators the fence removes — heritability and discovery-status. The seed being real is *why the fence is necessary*, not why P is admissible.
