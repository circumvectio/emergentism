# Receipt 161 — Weltanschauung and theory-of-everything gap audit

## Verdict

Emergentism has reached a coherent, self-critical Weltanschauung and a
packet-complete research program. It has not reached a theory of everything.
Its strongest warranted scientific description is **candidate translation
grammar across sciences**. It has no common fundamental dynamics, constants,
native-theory recovery, or novel independently replicated discriminator.

This is not a dismissal. The framework now contains a nontrivial surviving
core:

- map/territory and tier discipline;
- actual D4 carrier versus merely possible D5 content;
- model-mediated future influence without necessary physical retrocausality;
- commitment and world-issued outcome receipts;
- constraint of lower-law-admissible trajectories without forbidden events;
- independently killable crossings, force affinities, aggregation models, and
  collective-trace candidates;
- bearer-aware Justice, correction, visible graves, and exit.

## Weltanschauung gaps

1. **Selected ground:** the Titan frame is a useful symbolic constitution, not
   a derivation of why reality has precisely those roles.
2. **Non-unique ladder:** D0–D6 is a coherent address system, not a proved
   exhaustive or necessary decomposition.
3. **No μ mechanism:** μ remains a candidate-aperture label until a system
   boundary, saturation statistic, threshold, novelty/recovery test, dynamics,
   prediction, and kill are supplied.
4. **Consciousness:** the Soul Loop is a functional account of modeling,
   choice, and feedback; it does not explain phenomenal experience.
5. **Normative underdetermination:** the Justice envelope exposes bearers,
   payers, consent, uncertainty, irreversibility, and exit, but hard conflicts
   still require judgment and cannot be solved by the reciprocal chart.
6. **Metaphysical instantiation:** the Crown Wager remains a metaphysical wager;
   its current kills test usefulness and non-coercion more directly than truth.
7. **Comparative scope:** perennial and historical-priority claims require
   scholarship outside the corpus.

## Physical theory-of-everything gaps

The corpus currently lacks:

- a fundamental state space and ontology;
- an action, Hamiltonian, Lagrangian, or common transition dynamics;
- gauge groups, symmetries, couplings, masses, and constants;
- recovery of the Standard Model and general relativity in their accepted
  domains;
- a quantum-gravity completion;
- a definite-outcome mechanism or Born-weight derivation;
- cosmological dynamics, initial conditions, dark-sector account, and arrow of
  time;
- a mechanism deriving the five force/freedom rows;
- an operationally calibrated μ crossing;
- one integrated novel prediction and independent replication.

External boundaries checked in the audit include CERN's Standard Model
overview, Donoghue's quantum-gravity effective-field-theory work,
Schlosshauer's measurement/decoherence review, Anderson's hierarchical-science
argument, Peters and Büchel's episodic-future experiment, and Skinner and
Dunkel's entropy-production work. These support components and constraints;
none validates the integrated Emergentist synthesis.

## Promotion gates

No theory-of-everything language is warranted before all of the following:

1. restore and mechanically hold the pure-worldview boundary;
2. operationalize and test at least one μ crossing against a serious rival;
3. validate or kill the product aggregator and foresight/means surface;
4. obtain one non-permutable force/freedom mechanism with native-theory
   recovery and a quantitative discriminator;
5. state common dynamics connecting at least two scientific domains;
6. freeze a novel prediction not inherited from the source sciences;
7. obtain independent criticism, replication, and a published null route;
8. allow the integrated claim to die if it adds no incremental value.

## Strategy recorded

third**. Frontier AI models fill candidate pieces step by step. Each candidate

review, name its rival and kill, and pass world contact before promotion.

as a worldview and research index even if no final theory of everything
emerges.
