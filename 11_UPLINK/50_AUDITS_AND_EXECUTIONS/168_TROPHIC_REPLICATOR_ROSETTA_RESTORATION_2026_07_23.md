## 1. Result

The July compression fused four different coordinates and made the relational
non-extraction fence read like a denial of ecology. This pass restores the
founder correction:

> Cross-trophic consumption can be coupled ecological flow. Within human peer
> relations and among complementary Rosetta functions, hidden, decoupled, or
> ground-negating extraction remains refused. Seek cooperation within the same
> trophic stratum or functional guild; sexual selection is a bounded
> mating-domain exception, not a general licence.

This is a selected doctrine and strategic corollary. Founder direction fixes
its place in the framework but does not manufacture scientific proof.

No coordinate is a moral rank. Human rows are cooperating organs of shared
humanity and equal worth, never trophic prey for one another.
