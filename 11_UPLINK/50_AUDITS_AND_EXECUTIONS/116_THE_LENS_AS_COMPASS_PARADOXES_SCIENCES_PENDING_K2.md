K2 asked whether the sciences could be unified "in a simple diagram like
Feynman did." Ruling staged for countersign: **Feynman diagrams won because
they are a grammar that computes** — every line and vertex has strict rules
mapping to terms in a calculation — not because they are a picture. A "Burri

formalization (07B, in flight) supplies the vertex rules.
