Shipped fences (00_THE_SEED.md §6b): some-never-every · scaffold-vs-dynamics indexing · upward-success acknowledged · one-datum rule with Wigner (whose puzzle stays open) · no privileged lower description-seat (all describing is D5 ink) · μ-at-saturation stays `[C]` · kill = one documented lower-rung theory predicting a next-rung genesis phenomenon; registered standing pressure = D5's vN–M/Nash ledger (mathematics prima facie capturing choice-content), now cross-referenced on D5's page.
