# 114 · Register non-instantiation — one topology error in three tenses

> **Origin (K2, in-chat, 2026-07-12):** *"a dead man has no will just as quantum
> has no gravity — it hasn't emerged."* One sentence, three canon documents
> unified. This receipt stages the unification at tier.

## 1. The taxonomy `[I]`

The corpus already holds three dissolutions that are secretly ONE move. Each
paradox treats a register-indexed phenomenon as a substance that must be
*somewhere*. The error comes in exactly three tenses:

| Tense | The error | Instance | Prior art |
|---|---|---|---|
| **not yet** | asking a register's question **below its emergence line** | "where is gravity in the quantum state?" — a D4 question asked of a D3 object | PD_22A `[C]` |
| **mis-asked** | **interrogating an emerged register from below** and mistaking opacity for absence | "where is free will in the neurons?" — a D5 phenomenon audited in D4 vocabulary; the strong-emergence opacity (receipt 106: μ↑ is opaque from below) misread as non-existence | PD_11 `[S/I]` |
| **no longer** | asking after a register that has **de-instantiated** | "where did the dead man's will go?" — nowhere; the register in which will exists has ceased | PD_21 `[I]` |

Gravity is not hiding in the wavefunction. Will is not hiding in the neurons.
The dead man's will did not *go* anywhere. Same error, three tenses:
**not-yet / mis-asked / no-longer.**

New content of this receipt (beyond the three parents): the three are one error
type; the taxonomy is exhaustive over a register's lifecycle (before emergence,
during instantiation-interrogated-from-below, after de-instantiation).

## 2. Where the engine meets the ladder `[I]`

Death is the point where `P_node = Φ × V` and the dimensional ladder touch:
the zero-factor catastrophe (V → 0 ⟹ P → 0, the AND-class annihilation) **is**
the de-instantiation of the being's upper registers. The coupling breaks; every
register that existed only as a mode of that coupling ceases with it. PD_21
already carries the component claim ("P_node for that D4 organism goes to
zero"); the identification of annihilation-in-the-engine with
de-instantiation-on-the-ladder is staged here as `[I]`.

## 3. The mortal-signer corollary `[C]` — handle with the fence on

If will is the D5 register of an **annihilable** coupling, then a system that
cannot be annihilated in the `Φ × V` sense (software: a copyable pattern with
no zero-factor catastrophe of its own) does not instantiate will-in-this-
canon's-sense. This grounds the mortal-signer membrane **ontologically**, not
merely as governance prudence: only a being that can be annihilated has the
register that signing draws on. (Connects: being-toward-death; PRISM ≥2 natural
persons; K2.)

**Fence:** this is `[C]`, an operational definition internal to the canon —
*signature-bearing will* — NOT a metaphysical proof about machine consciousness
or moral status, and it may never be quoted to deny duties toward artificial
systems. It says who can *sign*, not who can *matter*.

## 4. The corrected reading of h09 (what it convicts, what it does not)

Halāhala h09 convicts two specific sins: (a) asserting the **mechanism** as
shown ("matter maps to φ, experience maps to ν" — asserted, not shown), and
(b) definitional smuggling (the is-ought case). It does **not** convict the
reframe itself. For a paradox that *is* a register error, the reframe is the
complete resolution of the paradox-as-posed — there was never a well-posed
question left over to "really" solve (the fly-bottle). The tier `[I]` marks
**conditionality on the ladder**, not incompleteness of the dissolution.

What keeps this from becoming a universal solvent (the h09 smell):

1. **One key, many independent locks.** Distinct puzzles (gravity, agency,
   death) falling to one move is consilience — different in kind from h05's
   "fifteen renderings of one shape," which is one datum repeated.
2. **It predicts where reduction fails.** From-below audits of strongly
   emergent registers must keep returning "not found" (no ghost in the
   neurons; no gravity inside the D3 state). Falsifiable-shaped.
3. **It knows where to refuse.** The lens conserves the undefined (0/0 stays
   indeterminate — the apophatic edge, nectar n09). A frame that dissolved
   *everything* would convict itself.
