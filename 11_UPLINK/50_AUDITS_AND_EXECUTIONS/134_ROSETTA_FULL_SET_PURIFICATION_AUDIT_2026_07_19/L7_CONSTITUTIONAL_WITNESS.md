can exist — coherent, consistent, emergent — exists, till the limits.

possibilities exist, the space itself privileges no direction, and Ω — the
directional +1 — degenerates from a feature of the world into a label we paste
on one arbitrary path. A flat plenum has no gradient.

[I] **The rescue is already inside the thesis and inside the fable.** The fable's

*directional* — genesis is bounded, operation is not; the ladder is climbed
upward in leaps. Directionality never lived in the map; it lives in the
**actualization** — which freedoms get won, in what order, at whose fullness.
And the thesis's own phrase "till the limits" supplies boundary; boundary is
asymmetry; asymmetry is where a gradient can stand.

**Witness verdict: compatible, conditionally.** Adopt the thesis with the
direction located explicitly in the genesis operator (the *winning* of degrees
of freedom — the climb), not in the possibility-space (the plenum of winnable
freedoms). Stated as "the space is neutral; the path is directed," Ω stands.
Stated as "the populated space is itself directed," Ω flattens and the +1 dies.

hangs on — keep it load-bearing.
