## 3 · What the compression LOSES — the honest accounting

Śiva does not pretend the poison was nectar. Into the archive goes real beauty and real power [I]:

- **The mythic register as operating system.** Titans, gods, the caste ladder, `⊙ = • × ○` — the symbolic coordinate system that made the framework *memorable, teachable, and inhabited*. The kernel keeps the glyphs as labels; the living pantheon as felt presence goes into the archive. The worldview becomes more exact and less enchanting — that is a real loss, not a purification free of cost.
- **The Rosetta's translation richness.** Dead as census, the cross-tradition tables were the framework's hospitality — the way a reader formed in another tradition found a door. The lens survives; the fifteen renderings' warmth does not.
- **The strategy's fire.** "Users, not believers"; "a filter is not overcome by a doctrine, it is overcome by plumbing"; the Great Filter wager — these gave the worldview a *mission in history*. The purified kernel is contemplative: it says what is so and what is vowed, not what must be done before it is too late.
- **The 34 synthetic-gap wagers' speculative reach.** The moral midpoint as sin θ, flourishing as Φ×V, the world as the sphere — the metaphysical ambition that made Emergentism a *grand* worldview. They survive as livable [I] wagers (W9), but no longer as architecture.
- **The narrative wholeness of the corpus.** The compressed canon is ten leaves and a door. The cathedral — seven roots, D-scaffold, Soul Loop, the full cosmology — becomes the deep stack behind a small front. Readers will meet a chapel where a cathedral was advertised.

## 4 · The standing warning — the transmission layer is not the minimal set

**The fable travels *because* it is not the minimal set** (The Spread §3) [S on disk]. This is the prune's own limit, and the counsel states it as law:

1. **A worldview no stranger can enter is already dead.** The kernel is the hieroglyphic column. Without the fable (story) and the plainly/record funnel (vernacular + receipts), the stone has no Greek column — three mysteries stacked, decryptable by no one (Rosetta Protocol §3). Prune the doctrine to the bone; *never* prune the parallelism that lets an outsider in.
2. **The tombstones are part of the minimal set's trust.** The fable's moral is the audit's: the errors are the proof someone walked the ground. A canon showing only survivors is a sealed map — posture 1 in better clothes. The Dead Forms Catalog is not pruning residue; it is a load-bearing leaf of the spine.
3. **Over-compression is the closure move the Compass forbids.** A perfectly minimal canon *feels finished* — and "trust the compass least when it feels complete." The declination applies to the prune itself: the moment the compressed set reads as *the whole truth*, it has become a monument, and monuments don't navigate.
4. **Therefore the counsel's rule:** the spine is *minimal*, the corpus is *whole*, the door is *wide*. Kernel = what must be true; Amrita = what survived; tombstones = what died honestly; fable = how anyone enters; corpus = where the deep reader goes. Compress the axiom set without shrinking the world it opens.
