## 1. The verdict

**AXIS_FUSION_BOTH.** The sources carry **both** readings on two orthogonal,
co-present axes; neither is a mis-encoding.

- **Emergence / μ axis (Reading A — the countersigned map):** D4 = potential
  (pre-collapse witness) → D5 = one **actual** enactment; μ₄ runs **UP**. Source
  of record: docs 34/27, each with an explicit correction note repudiating the
  older D4-as-actual mapping. *Emergence-actual = the collapsed enactment at D5.*
- **Action / operation axis (Reading B — Codex):** via `P_node = Φ × V` and the
  χ-commitment (the Soul Loop), D5 = Φ worldline-**foresight/possibility**,
  D4 = V **embodied means + receipt**; commitment flows **D5 → D4**.
  *Operation-actual = the embodied deed + receipt at D4.*

**"Actual" is register-indexed.** The two are not in conflict; they are the
framework's two registers, which the corpus *itself* names and forbids fusing.
