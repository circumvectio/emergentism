# Weltanschauung Kernel v0.2 — Emergentism only

> **Plato was partially right:** particulars are intelligible through structures that exceed any one instance. But those structures need not be separately existing Forms. The stronger Emergentist candidate is a world of **constraint-structured freedoms**: systems, relations, symmetries, invariants, laws, histories, resources, and boundaries that delimit how reality may vary. Stable “forms” are not the degrees of freedom themselves; they are invariants, equivalence classes, order parameters, attractors, or robust patterns formed **across constrained degrees of freedom**.
>
> **Everything coherent does not therefore exist actually.** Logical coherence admits a description; satisfiability admits a model; law and boundary conditions admit a physical possibility; dynamics admit reachability; realization gives actuality; robustness and macro-level autonomy license an emergence claim; embodiment and means give lived availability.

This is a Weltanschauung candidate, not a derivation from one equation. The formal kernel is thin and empty of world; the world-facing ontology remains a wager under test. Receipt 126 controls that separation.

## 1. The central ontological proposal `[I/C]`

Reality is approached as nested systems with constrained possibilities of variation. For a specified system `S`, a degrees-of-freedom claim is incomplete until it declares at least:

1. state or configuration space `X`;
2. independent variables and gauge redundancies;
3. constraints `C` and symmetries `G`;
4. dynamics or admissibility relation `T`;
5. initial and boundary conditions `B`;
6. resource, energy, entropy, information, and control costs `R`;
7. coarse-graining or macro-map `π`;
8. measurement and access relation `M`.

A degree of freedom is therefore not a substance. It is a typed capacity for independent variation in a specified system or model after constraints and redundancies are accounted for.

A stable form is better modeled as one or more of:

- an invariant under transformation;
- an equivalence class under a symmetry;
- a constraint-stabilized region of state space;
- an attractor or metastable regime where the dynamics warrants that language;
- an order parameter or macro-variable;
- a robust pattern preserved across perturbations and descriptions.

This proposal is compatible with S²/CP¹ as one model. It does not depend on S² being the ontology.

**Kill or demote if:** the proposal cannot distinguish coordinate choice from physical freedom; alternative ontologies explain the same evidence with fewer assumptions; “emergence” adds no tested constraint, autonomy, or predictive value; or “possible” is silently upgraded to “actual.”

## 2. Typed existence — the anti-modal-collapse rule `[S]` method

The verb **exists** must carry a register.

| Register | Minimum warrant | What it does not imply |
|---|---|---|
| Thinkable | Grammatically/intentionally describable | consistency |
| Logically admissible | no contradiction under stated logic | a satisfying model in a chosen theory |
| Mathematically existent | a model/object satisfying declared axioms | physical possibility |
| Model-admissible | point, region, or trajectory allowed by declared constraints | reachability or world contact |
| Nomologically possible | compatible with applicable physical laws | actual occurrence |
| Dynamically reachable | a lawful causal path reaches it from admissible conditions | stability |
| Actual | causally instantiated | robustness or emergence |
| Stable/robust | persists across a declared horizon and perturbation class | macro-level causal autonomy |
| Emergent | earns macro-level explanatory, predictive, or causal standing under explicit tests | new substance or lower-law violation |
| Livedly available | accessible to a finite embodied agent with means, timing, and disclosure | universal availability |

Accordingly:

> **Everything admitted by a specified possibility space exists in that space only as an admitted possibility.** Fewer possibilities are physically lawful, fewer reachable, fewer actual, fewer stable, fewer emergent, and fewer available to a finite life.

Unrestricted modal realism is not an Emergentist discovery and is not adopted here.
