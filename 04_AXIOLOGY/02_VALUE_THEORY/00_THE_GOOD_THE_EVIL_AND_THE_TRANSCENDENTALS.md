# The Good, the Harmful, and the Transcendentals

> **[金] Crack:** the predecessor derived good and evil from the sphere,
> `φν=1`, D5, or a single `η` direction. **Repair:** values are declared openly
> and consequential classifications remain bearer-complete and receipted.

Emergentism declares the good as the AND-class coupling of:

- **Beauty:** coherent form that does not hide its seams;
- **Truth:** faithful relation among model, action, and receipt;
- **Justice:** accountable non-extraction in which no affected bearer is erased
  for another's gain.

This is `[I]`, not a theorem of geometry. `φν=1`, `B≤1`, `P=ΦV`, D-registers,
Titans, and `etaObserved` do not entail it.

Actual agents, awareness, actions, harms, benefits, and receipts are
`D4 = actual`; represented alternatives are `D5 = merely_possible`. The
framework does not place goodness, evil, minds, or collectives in D5.

Prospective Justice requires complete affected-bearer coverage, accountable
authorization, consent, custody, payer and beneficiary visibility, physical
admissibility, truthful cost, reversibility where possible, exit, and contest.
Retrospective classification uses outcome receipts. `etaObserved=0` is
necessary but insufficient; `unknown` blocks a positive classification.

“Evil” should be reserved for severe, knowing, or structurally persistent
violations of that envelope, not used as a mathematical label for a pole,
coordinate, person, group, illness, or ordinary error. A theory that protects
its verdict from contrary receipts has ceased to be an ethic and become a cage.
