**Reciprocal closure.**

Reality is not inert matter plus accidental meaning.

**A finite expression of reciprocal closure.**

**The finite site of disclosure and action.**

The self is neither an isolated substance nor a nullity.

**The disclosure interface.**

### Beauty

**Beauty is coherence made perceivable.**

Beauty is the felt or seen appearance of integration, proportion, reciprocal fit, and non-contradictory order.

Beauty is not cosmetic ornament.
Beauty is the perceivable face of coherence.

In this compression:

- beauty leans toward `Φ`
- beauty peaks where balance becomes visible
- beauty is what right relation looks like from within experience

### Truth

**Truth is faithful disclosure of what is.**

Truth has two legitimate registers:

- **direct truth:** what is actually present when mediation falls quiet
- **public truth:** what survives inference, evidence, testing, and correction

Truth is not mere sincerity.
Truth is alignment between articulation and reality.

### Justice

**Justice is flourishing rightly arranged between beings.**

Justice is the moral and institutional form of flourishing.
It means:

- no hidden extraction
- no trapped being
- no structure preserving itself by degrading its substrate
- no relation that depends on severing sovereignty from action

Justice is a conjunctive envelope: complete accountable authorization,
non-extraction, explicit payers/beneficiaries/bearers, truthful evidence,
consent and custody, contestability, due process, reversibility where possible,
Grace Exit, honest physical cost, and preservation of every affected bearer's
independent standing. `η=0` is one necessary check, not the envelope itself.
