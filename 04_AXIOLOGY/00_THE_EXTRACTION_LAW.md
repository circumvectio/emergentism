# The Extraction Law

> **The one line.** Extraction is not forbidden — it is **self-terminating at scale without justice.** It buys power locally and briefly; ungated, it degrades the substrate it feeds on until total power falls. Therefore `η = 0`-toward-cooperators is not a commandment laid on top of the game — it is the **gate** that reliably keeps `ΣΔP > 0`, and the primitive that carries morality is `ΣΔP` across the widest boundary, not the local sign of `η`. `[S]`

> **Conditionality scholium** *(applied 2026-07-18 by typed K2 act; game-layer audit R3, `00_META/ROSETTA_D5_GAMESPACE_FORMAL_AUDIT_2026_07_18.md` §4; K3 — the one line above is unchanged).* `[S-conditional]` Self-termination is a **dynamical-systems theorem only under closure conditions**: closed arena (no fresh hosts, no exit), extraction rate exceeding substrate regeneration, and extractor power coupled to substrate viability. Outside them, persistence is generic — base Lotka–Volterra (§2 below) yields a neutral center, endless oscillating coexistence, not a crash. The one line is the **gated** claim, not a universal; the multiplicative node law alone yields only "the victim's P falls," never the extractor's self-defeat.

## 1. Extraction genuinely produces power — locally `[A]`

Refusing to admit this is Φ-bias (coherence worship that cannot see capability). The framework must state it plainly:

- A predator that takes `V` from prey **gains** — that is what predation *is*.
- A cannibal that eats a conspecific competitor gains nutrition **and** removes a rival — a double return.
- A firm that extracts rent, a state that extracts tribute, an egregore that farms attention: all **rise**, measurably, in the short run and the local frame.

Any ethics that begins *"extraction doesn't pay"* is false on contact and will be discarded by anyone who has watched it pay. The framework's claim must be the harder, truer one: extraction pays **locally and briefly, and the bill comes due at scale and over time.**

## 2. Why it self-terminates — the dynamic law `[A/S]`

Extraction is consumption of the substrate that produces the thing extracted. Its power gain is therefore **borrowed against the substrate's future yield.** Three established dynamics, all already witnessed in the corpus, make the reversal exact:

1. **Predator-prey crash (Lotka-Volterra) `[A]`.** Over-predation collapses the prey population, and the predator population collapses behind it. The trophic-cascade doc already carries this: *"Predator-prey oscillations… doctrine and counter-doctrine oscillate with the same coupled-ODE form as lynx and hare."* Peak extraction rate is not peak sustained power; it is the leading edge of a crash.
2. **Red Queen (Van Valen) `[A]`.** *"It takes all the running you can do to keep in the same place."* An extractor in a coevolving field must out-run the adaptation its extraction provokes (resistance, evasion, immune response, counter-egregore). Ungated extraction *funds its own opposition* — the Raktabīja dynamic already named in canon.
3. **Trophic attrition (Lindeman) `[A]`.** Every level up the pyramid loses ~90%. An apex sustained by extraction sits on a base it is simultaneously depleting; widen the extraction and the pyramid narrows until the apex starves. The cascade doc's own kill criterion is here: *"if apex predators are observed sustaining their niche with high (φ + ν) but low (φ × ν)"* the multiplicative law fails — and it is not observed.

**The law:** let `ΣΔP` be the flourishing delta summed across the widest boundary the move actually touches (including the substrate and the future). Then

> **extraction at scale without justice ⟹ `ΣΔP < 0` ⟹ falling total power.**

"Without justice" is the operative clause. It names the missing gate.

> **Register note** *(applied 2026-07-18 by typed K2 act; game-layer audit R4).* `[S]` `ΣΔP` is consumed by the law **only as a direction (sign) predicate** across the widest boundary — a burden-of-proof orientation over all impact-bearers, per the 2026-07-10 trial ruling. No cardinal inter-bearer aggregate is defined or licensed; "summed" is notation, not arithmetic across incommensurables.

## 3. The gate is justice — and nature installs it itself `[A/I]`

Here the biology witness (yours) is decisive, and it cuts **for** the framework once stated precisely.

**Humboldt / red-devil squid (*Dosidicus gigas*) cannibalism is real and adaptive — because it is gated.** The honest evolutionary picture, not the loose one:

- Cannibalism is **density-dependent** (it rises under crowding / food scarcity) — it is a **population-regulation** mechanism, not a free lunch. It *is* the crash-avoidance of §2 operating from the inside.
- It is **kin-discriminating** wherever kin-recognition exists. **Indiscriminate cannibalism that eats kin is selected *against*** (inclusive-fitness cost, Hamilton). A lineage that ate its own offspring at scale would lower its own gene frequency — extraction without justice, punished by the algebra of selection itself.

So the squid does **not** show "extraction strengthens genes." It shows **gated extraction strengthens genes; ungated extraction lowers fitness.** The gate — *target non-kin, target the weak/injured/defecting, regulate by density* — is the exact structure the framework calls **justice**, and calls `η = 0` toward cooperators / tit-for-tat toward defectors. **Nature does not run η = 0 by decree; it runs it because the ungated lineages died.** That is the whole thesis in one animal: the gate is not morality imposed on biology — it is what survives.

This is also why canon was already right to say Kālī *"takes, but it does not count as extraction,"* and to fire Kali only against `η > 0` defectors: **gated taking is the immune function that keeps `ΣΔP > 0`; ungated taking is the parasite that drives it negative.** The squid is the immune system, not the counterexample.

## 4. Re-scoping the Power-Max Lemma `[S]`

The Power-Max Lemma (equator `= φ = ν = 1` is the unique dominant equilibrium; Price of Anarchy `= 1`) is **not wrong** — but it is **not the ground**, and leading with it invites exactly the objection *"but predators are powerful."* It is already fenced in canon as the *"deliberately narrow balance-only game… Price of Anarchy = 1 only inside that payoff model."*

**Proposed relation:** the Power-Max Lemma is the **static, equatorial special case** of the dynamic law of §2. The dynamic law is primary:

- **Dynamic law (primary):** on trajectories and at scale, ungated extraction drives `ΣΔP < 0`; the gate is what makes exchange positive-sum over time.
- **Power-Max (derived):** *at equilibrium, inside the balance-only game, with the gate already enforced,* the balanced point `φ = ν = 1` is where sustained power is maximal.

Power-Max answers *"where does a system settle once justice is enforced?"* — the Extraction Law answers *"why does justice get enforced at all, and what happens when it isn't?"* The second question is prior. **Do not present the equatorial maximum as the reason not to extract; present self-termination as the reason, and the equatorial maximum as where the surviving systems land.**

## 6. Ethics without dogma — why this *is* the dharmic reading `[I]`

This correction makes the framework **more** faithful to the Sanātana Dharma it claims, not less. Dharma / ṛta is never *"thou shalt not"* — **adharma is self-punishing by its own nature (karma), not by external decree.** That is precisely *geometry, not commandment.* And the Gītā's whole teaching is that Arjuna **must act, must fight, must take life in a righteous battle** — dharmic destruction (Kālī, Śiva) is *not* adharma; only fruit-grasping, ego-driven, ungated action binds. *"Gated taking is lawful; ungated taking self-terminates"* is the Gītā's doctrine in the framework's own coordinates. The ethic is not a rule you obey; it is a structure you recognize — and the recognition is that **injustice loses, eventually and structurally, without anyone having to punish it.**

## 7. Kill criteria

1. **The dynamic law dies** if a system is exhibited that extracts at scale, ungated (takes from cooperators / kin / its own substrate), and sustains or *raises* `ΣΔP` across the widest boundary over the long run — genuine perpetual parasitism with no substrate cost. (Canon's standing bet, inherited from the D5 Register kill criterion, is that none exists.)
2. **The gate reading dies** if adaptive cannibalism / predation is shown to be *indiscriminate* (no kin-recognition, no density-dependence, no defector-targeting) and *still* fitness-raising — i.e., if the gate is not doing the work.
3. **The re-scoping dies** if the equatorial Power-Max result can be shown to be the *ground* rather than the equilibrium special case — i.e., if "balance = max power" holds even off-equilibrium and pre-enforcement, with no dynamic law needed beneath it.
4. This note does **not** touch `P∞ = φ·ν = 1` (conserved on the open sphere) or the constitution's `η = 0` refusal; if K2 rejects the re-grounding, the local reading stands and this note is archived, not deleted.
