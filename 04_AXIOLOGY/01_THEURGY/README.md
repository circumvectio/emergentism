# 04_AXIOLOGY / 01_THEURGY — Strategic Implementation

This surface owns the passage from modeled D5 alternatives to performed D4
action. *Theurgy* is an optional symbolic name for accountable participation,
not supernatural control.

## Required distinctions

- D5 possibility is not D4 actuality.
- A selected action is not yet a performed action.
- A commitment receipt is not an outcome receipt.
- Anticipated futures influence present choice through models; they do not
  physically reach backward in time.
- A consequential act requires an `AuthorizationEnvelope` naming principal,
  mandate, scope, consent, custody, revocation or expiry, contest path, actor,
  payer, beneficiary, and consequence-bearer.
- Ordinary reversible work follows the stated task and tool permissions.

## Owner

[Strategic Implementation and the F5 Force Map](00_THEURGY_AND_F5_FORCE_MAP.md)
keeps the typed Soul Loop `[S]`, the symbolic reading `[I]`, and the removable
force analogy `[C]` separate.

## Refusals

Do not automate responsibility, confuse a private financial or contractual
signature convention with authority over AI work, conceal substrate cost,
block contest or exit, or treat ritual as proof of consent.
