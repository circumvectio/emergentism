# Strategic Implementation and the F5 Force Map

**Scope:** pure Emergentist action theory. No organization, product, or
governance system supplies warrant here.

## 1. The boundary

D5 is the register of modeled possibilities, represented rankings, and
selection candidates. D4 is the register of actual modeling, ranking,
selection, embodied means, performed action, and factual record. A finite
agent does not make a future real by imagining it. It uses a fallible model of
possible futures to change its present choice distribution, then commits one
action through available means:

```text
modeled future field (D5) + embodied means (D4)
                    |
                    v
          accountable commitment
                    |
                    v
             performed act (D4)
                    |
                    v
          world-returned consequence
```

This is the Soul Loop's model-mediated future influence. It is not physical
retrocausality and it does not identify selection with quantum measurement.

## 2. The typed action interface `[S]`

The selector returns an action and an immediate commitment receipt:

\[
\chi_t:(X_t,\Omega_t,M_t,V_t,U_t)\rightarrow(a_t,q_t).
\]

The environment separately returns the next state and outcome receipt:

\[
(X_{t+1},r_{t+1})\sim K_t(\cdot\mid X_t,a_t,E_t).
\]

The loop may then update the model and the selection policy:

\[
(M_{t+1},G_{t+1})=
\operatorname{Loop}(M_t,G_t,q_t,r_{t+1}).
\]

Keeping `q_t` and `r_{t+1}` distinct prevents the chooser from manufacturing
its own consequence. It also exposes three inspectable gaps:

1. **Cognitive gap:** territory versus model.
2. **Execution gap:** selected intention versus performed act.
3. **Outcome gap:** expected consequence versus received consequence.

## 3. AuthorizationEnvelope `[S]`

Consequential coordinated action requires a typed accountability record:

```text
AuthorizationEnvelope =
  principal + mandate + scope + consent + custody
  + expiry_or_revocation + contest_path
  + actor + consequence_bearer
```

This is not a metaphysical ingredient and not a universal permission ritual.
It is the minimum information needed to ask who authorized a consequence, who
acted, who bears its cost, and how the act can be contested or stopped.
Ordinary inquiry, drafting, testing, and other reversible work remains governed
by the stated task scope and available tools.

## 4. Strategic implementation `[I]`

**Strategic implementation** is the disciplined practice of forming, tending,
and releasing a D5 coordination pattern through D4 acts while preserving:

- identifiable authorization and custody;
- informed consent and a real contest path;
- payer and beneficiary visibility;
- reversibility where possible and explicit irreversibility where not;
- Grace Exit;
- commitment and outcome receipts;
- correction when receipt and expectation diverge.

The word *theurgy* is retained as a symbolic translation for this practice. It
does not claim supernatural control. The practice succeeds when the agent can
act responsibly without needing the symbolic word.

## 5. The F5 correspondence `[C]`

`F5` is a conjectural force-like analogy for the selective pressure by which
modeled futures, shared traces, and coordination patterns reweight later
choices. It is not an established fifth physical force.

| Term | Operational reading | Tier |
|---|---|---|
| source | an agent or coupled set of agents able to model alternatives | `[I]` |
| field | the represented option field and shared trace | `[I]` |
| coupling | measurable sensitivity of later selections to that trace | `[B/S]` when measured |
| carrier | a commitment or outcome receipt that preserves the trace | `[I]` |
| work | D4 expenditure that changes reachable trajectories | `[S]` |
| force claim | a distinct, independently discriminable law beyond known mechanisms | `[C]` |

The carrier is therefore **not** a privileged signature type. Any auditable
trace may carry the relevant information if intervention on it measurably
changes later selection.

## 6. Justice boundary

Strategic implementation is lawful in the Emergentist value register only when
the Justice envelope holds and both sides of a coupled relation are preserved:

\[
\operatorname{Syntropic}(a)
\iff
\Delta_TW_i(a)>0\land\Delta_TW_H(a)>0\land J(a;i,H).
\]

The audit must name individual and whole, payer and beneficiary, consent,
custody, reversibility, and exit. Aggregate gain cannot launder the destruction
of one side. Voluntary sacrifice is a separate costly class and is never
mislabelled strict syntropy or demanded as proof of virtue.

## 7. Evidence and kill criteria

The typed D5-to-D4 boundary survives if the F5 analogy is removed. The F5 claim
must contract or die if any of the following holds:

1. interventions on represented futures or shared traces do not alter present
   action distributions beyond already specified mechanisms;
2. commitment and outcome receipts cannot be distinguished empirically;
3. the proposed coupling adds no prediction or compression beyond ordinary
   learning, signaling, institutional memory, and control theory;
4. the analogy requires forbidden physical trajectories or literal influence
   from a future event;
5. its use conceals a payer, blocks contest, or contracts another party's
   option cone without consent.

If criteria 1–3 hold, retain the Soul Loop and discard the force language. If
criteria 4–5 hold, reject the application.
